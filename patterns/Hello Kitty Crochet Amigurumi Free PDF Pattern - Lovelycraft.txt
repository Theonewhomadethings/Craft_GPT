hello kitty amigurumi crochet free pattern
designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn.
crochet hook: 2mm or 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
safety eyes size 5-6 mm
leg*2
start with white
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8- inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4-8. (5rnds) 24sc (24)
crochet the 2nd leg in the same way till rnd 8. do not
cut the yarn. join in 1st leg. continue to the body.
body
continue with blue
rnd 1. (on 1st leg) 24sc, (on 2nd leg) 24sc (48)
rnd 2-6. (5rnds) 48sc (48)
rnd 7. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 8-10. (3rnds) 42sc (42)
rnd 11. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 12. (pink) 36sc (36)
rnd 13. (white) 36sc (36)
rnd 14. (pink) (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 15. (white) 30sc (30)
rnd 16. (pink) 30sc (30)
rnd 17. (white) (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 18. (pink) 24sc (24)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
head
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8- inc (16)
rnd 3. blo (sc,inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*8 (48)
rnd 7. (2sc,inc)*16 (64)
rnd 8. (7sc,inc)*8 (72)
rnd 9-20. (12rnds) 72sc (72)
insert the safety eyes between rnd 13,14.
9sc apart.
rnd 21. (7sc,dec)*8 (64)
rnd 22. 64sc (64)
rnd 23. (6sc,dec)*8 (56)
rnd 24. (5sc,dec)*8 (48)
rnd 25. (4sc,dec)*8 (40)
rnd 26. (3sc,dec)*8 (32)
rnd 27. (2sc,dec)*8 (24)
rnd 28. (sc,dec)*8 (16)
rnd 29. 8- dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the sew the body to the head at rnd
3 of the head. (blo round)
ear*2
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4- inc (8)
rnd 3. (3sc,inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4. (4sc,inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5. (5sc,inc)*2 (14)
rnd 6. (6sc,inc)*2 (16)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
arm*2
rnd 1. 7sc in mr (7)
rnd 2. 7- inc (14)
rnd 3-9. (7rnds) 14sc (14)
rnd 10-15. (pink) (6rnds) 14sc (14)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
straps
make 36-40 chains. leave long tail and sew to the body with button.
embroidery nose and kitty mustach. make pink cheeks.
