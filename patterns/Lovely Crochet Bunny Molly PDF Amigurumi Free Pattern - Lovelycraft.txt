ears (make 2)
with grey yarn.
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4 inc (8)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*4 (12)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*4 (16)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*4 (20)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*4 (24)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*4 (28)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*4 (32)
rnd 9-15. 1 sc in each st (32)
rnd 16. (dec,14sc)*2 (30)
rnd 17-19. 1 sc in each st (30)
rnd 20. (dec,13sc)*2 (28)
rnd 21-23. 1 sc in each st (28)
rnd 24. (dec,12sc)*2 (26)
rnd 25-26. 1 sc in each st (26)
rnd 27. (dec,11sc)*2 (24)
rnd 28-29. 1 sc in each st (24)
rnd 30. (dec,10sc)*2 (22)
rnd 31-32. 1 sc in each st (22)
rnd 33. (dec,9sc)*2 (20)
rnd 34-35. 1sc in each st (20)
rnd 36. (dec,8sc)*2 (18)
rnd 37-38. 1 sc in each st (18)
rnd 39. (dec,7sc)*2 (16)
rnd 40-42. 1 sc in each st (16)
rnd 43. (dec,6sc)*2 (14)
rnd 44-45. 1 sc in each st (14)
don't stuff. cut yarn and fasten off with a long tail to sew.
arms (make 2)
with light gray yarn.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3-25. 1 sc in each st (9)
don't stuff. cut yarn and fasten off with a long tail.
legs (make 2)
with light gray yarn.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. blo 1 sc in each st (18)
rnd 5. 1 sc in each st (18)
rnd 6. (1sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 7-30. 1 sc in each st (12)
stuff through. fold in half and make 6 sc. to close both sides.
cut yarn and fasten off.
make a second leg, but do not fasten off the yarn, continue with the body.
body
connect the legs with 3 ch. and work the body.
rnd 31. 12 sc. in first leg, 3 sc. in the chains, 12sc in the second leg, 3sc. in the chains (30)
rnd 32. 2sc, inc, (4sc,inc)*5, 2sc (36)
rnd 33. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 34. 3sc, inc, (6sc,inc)*5, 3sc (48)
rnd 35-38. 1 sc in each st (48)
rnd 39. 3sc, dec, (6sc,dec)*5, 3sc (42)
rnd 40-44. 1 sc in each st (42)
rnd 45. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 46-48. 1 sc in each st (36)
rnd 49. 2sc, dec, (4sc,dec)*5, 2sc (30)
rnd 50-52. 1 sc in each st (30)
rnd 53. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 54. 1 sc in each st (24)
rnd 55. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 56-57. 1 sc in each st (18)
rnd 58. (1sc,dec)*6 (12)
stuff firmly as you go.
do not fasten off, continue with the head.
head
rnd 59. 1 sc in each st (12)
rnd 60. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 61. 1sc, inc, (2sc,inc)*5, 1sc (24)
rnd 62. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 63. 2sc, inc, (4sc,inc)*5, 2sc (36)
rnd 64. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 65. 3sc, inc, (6sc,inc)*5, 3sc (48)
rnd 66. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 67. 4sc, inc, (8sc,inc)*5, 4sc (60)
rnd 68-77. 1 sc in each st (60)
insert or embroider safety eyes between rounds 70 and 71, 8 stitches between. stuff through.
rnd 78. (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 79. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 80. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 81. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 82. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 83. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 84. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 85. (1sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 86. (dec)*6 (6)
stuff firmly. close the hole and fasten off.
sew the arms in round 57th.
sew the ears in round 6th from the top of the head.
dress
with yellow yarn.
make 19 ch. sc to the second ch. from the hook. we'll turning the work at the end of every round.
rnd 1. 18 sc (18)
rnd 2. blo (inc,7sc,inc)*2 (22)
rnd 3. 1sc,inc,5ch, skip 5 st, inc, 6sc, 5ch, skip, 5st, inc, 1sc (26)
rnd 4. blo, 1sc, (3sc,inc)*6, 1sc (32)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 6. blo, (4sc,inc)*8 (48)
rnd 7. (11sc,inc)*4 (52)
rnd 8. blo, (12sc,inc)*4 (56)
rnd 9. 1sc in each st (56)
rnd 10. blo, 1 sc in each st (56)
rnd 11. 1 sc in each st (56)
rnd 12. blo, 1 sc in each st (56)
rnd 13. 1 sc in each st (56)
rnd 14. blo, 1 sc in each st (56)
change to green yarn
rnd 15. 1 sc in each st (56)
change to yellow yarn
keep working with dc.
rnd 16. blo, (6sc,inc)*8 (64)
rnd 17. 1dc, in each st (64)
rnd 18. blo, 1dc, in each st (64)
rnd 19. 1dc, in each st (64)
rnd 20. blo, 1 dc, in each st (64)
rnd 21. (6dc,dec)*8 (56)
rnd 22. blo, (12dc,dec)*4 (52)
rnd 23. (11dc,dec)*4 (48)
rnd 24. blo, 1dc, in each st (48)
rnd 25. 1sc. in each st (48)
dress up the bunny and sew with yellow yarn to close both sides of the dress.
cut yarn and fasten off.
bow (make 2)
with green yarn.
make 22ch. dc. to the 3th ch. from the hook.
rnd 1. 20 dc. in each ch (20)
fold in half and make 3sc. to close both sides.
cut yarn and fasten off with a long tail to sew.
