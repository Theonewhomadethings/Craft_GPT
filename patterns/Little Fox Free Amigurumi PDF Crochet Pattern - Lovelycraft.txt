hello everyone, today i bring you a wonderful and completely free amigurumi crochet little fox pattern. your children will love this cute amigurumi fox toy that you can crochet step by step. if you wish, you can give a crochet toy gift to your loved ones. now, without further ado, let's get you to the pattern instructions.
we are with you with the little fox crochet toy. while crocheting the cute fox, orange and white were preferred as the main colors. you can choose these colors while working this cute fox with crochet step by step. follow the pattern instructions to complete the free amigurumi fox toy, it's pretty simple to make. for beginners, this pattern will be purely instructive.
little fox amigurumi free crochet pattern
designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn.
crochet hook: 2mm- 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
yes, friends, we first start the construction of the amigurumi fox toy from the head.
head
rnd 1: 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2: 8-inc (16)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4: blo 24sc (24)
rnd 5: (2sc,inc)*8 (32)
rnd 6: (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 7: (9sc,inc)*4 (44)
rnd 8-12: (5rnds) 44sc (44)
rnd 13: (9sc,dec)*4 (40)
rnd 14-15: (2rnds) 40sc (40)
rnd 16: (8sc,dec)*4 (36)
rnd 17: 36sc (36)
rnd 18: (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 19: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 20: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 21: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 22: 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
after completing the head part of the amigurumi fox, we can start to crochet its body step by step. this process may take a while, so be sure to rest.
body
insert the hook to blo round on head (rnd 4)
rnd 1: (orange) (3sc,inc)*2, (white) sc,inc,3sc,inc,2sc (orange) 3sc,inc,3sc,inc (30)
rnd 2: (orange) 13sc, (white) 8sc, (orange) 9sc (30)
rnd 3: (orange) 14sc, (white) 6sc, (orange) 10sc (30)
rnd 4: (orange) 15sc, (white) 4sc, (orange) 11sc (30)
rnd 5: (orange) (4sc,inc)*3,sc, (white) 2sc, (orange) sc, (inc,4sc)*2, inc (36)
rnd 6-8: (3rnds) 36sc (36)
rnd 9: (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 10-11: (2rnds) 30sc (30)
rnd 12: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 13: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 14: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 15: 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
after we knit the crocheted fox's body, we can move on to making the arms. you can crochet the ends using white yarn.
arm*2
start with white
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-3: (2rnds) 6sc (6)
change to orange
rnd 4-8: (5rnds) 6sc (6)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
in the same way, we crochet her ears using orange yarn and use white yarn at the ends.
ear*2
start with white
rnd 1: 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*2 (6)
change to orange
rnd 3: (2sc,inc)*2 (8)
rnd 4: (3sc,inc)*2 (10)
rnd 5: (4sc,inc)*2 (12), slst
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
leg*2
start with white
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6-inc (12)
change to orange
rnd 3: blo 12sc (12)
rnd 4: 3sc,3dec,3sc (9)
rnd 5: 3sc,2dec,2sc (7)
rnd 6-9: (4rnds) 7sc (7)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
after crocheting her legs, we will move on to the next step, which is the construction of her nose.
nose
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4: 12sc (12)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)*3 (15),slst
cut the yarn, and weave in the end.
tail
start with white
rnd 1: 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*2 (6)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 4: 9sc (9)
rnd 5: (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
change to orange
rnd 6-7: (2rnds) 12sc (12)
rnd 8: (2sc,dec)*3 (9)
rnd 9: 9sc (8)
rnd 10: (sc,dec)*3 (6)
rnd 11: 6sc, slst
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
after crocheting the nose, we make a cute tail and the crochet little fox amigurumi toy is completely ready. see you on the next free amigurumi pattern.
