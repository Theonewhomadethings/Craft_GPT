designer: tikho_toys_
materials and tools
yarn: alize lanagold fine (100g 390m) or any other with the same thread thickness.
the size of the bunny will be approximately 10 cm (excluding the ears).
hook: no. 2 or 1.75 (you can use another number, comfortable for yourself)
eyes: 5 mm beads.
dry pastel or acrylic tinting paints.
cotton swab (for tinting).
filling: holofiber.
large sewing needle for sewing parts.
scissors.
all details are crocheted in, only the head is sewn on.
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
tr: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
hands (2 parts)
1 rnd 6 sc in mr (6)
2 rnd 6 inc (12)
3 4 rnds 12sc
5 rnd 3 sc, 3 dec, 3 sc (9)
6 11 rnds 9 sc (6 rnd)
we fill only the bottom of the handle.
crochet 2 sc and bind off by crocheting 4 sc together.
at the end of 1 ch, we cut the thread, hide it.
legs (2 parts)
1 rnd make 6 ch, crochet from the 2nd loop from the hook: inc, 3 sc, 3 sc into the last loop, on the other side of the chain: 4 sc (12)
2 rnd 2 inc, sc, inc, sc, 3 inc, sc, inc, sc, inc (20)
3 5 rnds 20 sc (3 rows)
6 rnd 6 sc, 6 dec, 2 sc (14)
7 rnd 5 sc, 4 dec, 1 sc (10)
8 11 rnds 10 sc (4 rows)
we fill only the bottom of the leg.
work in 3 sc and bind off by crocheting 5 sc together.
at the end of 1 ch, we cut off the thread, hide it.
tail
1 rnd 6 sc in mr (6)
2 rnd 6 inc (12)
3 rnd 12 sc (12)
4 rnd (1 sc, dec) * 4 (8)
at the end of 1 ch, we cut the thread, hide it. we do not stuff.
body
1 rnd 8 ch, from the 2nd loop from the hook (inc, 2 sc) * 2, 3 sc into the last loop of the chain, crochet along the other side 2 sc, inc, 3 sc (18)
2 rnd 2 inc, 6 sc, 3 inc, 6 sc, inc (24)
3 rnd (3 sc, inc) * 6 (30)
next, we crochet the tail and legs.
4 rnd 23 sc, 4 sc along with the bottom of the tail, 3 sc (30)
5 rnd 5 sc together with the left leg, 10 sc, 5 sc together with the right leg, 3 sc, 4 sc only on the top of the tail, 3 sc (30)
6 rnd 30 sc (30)
7 rnd 5 sc, (1 sc, dec) * 3, 16 sc (27)
8 rnd 5 sc, (1 sc, dec) * 2, 16 sc (25)
9 rnd 25 sc (25)
10 rnd (3 sc, dec) * 5 (20)
11 rnd 20 sc (20)
12 rnd (8, dec) * 2 (18)
now we will crochet the handles.
13 rnd 1 sc, 4 sc with left hand, 5 sc, 4 sc with right hand, 4 sc (18)
14 rnd (1 sc, dec) * 6 (12)
at this stage, you need to fill the torso tightly.
15 rnd 6 dec (6), we tighten the loops, leave the end of the thread for sewing on the head.
ears (2 parts)
1 rnd 4 sc in mr (4)
next, crochet hdc, and increases with decreases.
2 rnd (1 hdc, inc) * 2 (6)
3 rnd (2 hdc, inc) * 2 (8)
4 rnd (3 hdc, inc) * 2 (10)
5 rnd (4 hdc, inc) * 2 (12)
6 rnd 12 hdc (12)
7 rnd (3 hdc, inc) * 3 (15)
8 rnd (4 hdc, inc) * 3 (18)
9 11 rnds 18 hdc (3 rows)
12 rnd (4 hdc, dec) * 3 (15)
13 rnd 15 hdc (15)
14 rnd (3 hdc, dec) * 3 (12)
15 rnd 12 hdc (12)
16 rnd (4 hdc, dec) * 2 (10)
17 rnd (3 hdc, dec) * 2 (8)
finishing the ear sc.
18 rnd 8 sc (8)
19 rnd crochet 4 sc together, 1 ch, hide the thread.
head
1 rnd 6 sc in mr (6)
2 rnd 6 inc (12)
3 rnd 12 sc (12)
4 rnd (1 sc, inc) * 6 (18)
5 rnd 18 sc (18)
6 rnd 6 sc, 6 inc, 6 sc (24)
7 rnd 6 sc, (1 sc, inc) * 6, 6 sc (30)
8 rnd 30 sc (30)
9 rnd (4 sc, inc) * 6 (36)
further, in 10 13 rows we crochet the ears, applying them with a pigtail inside (if you wish, you can just sew on)
10 rnd 13 sc, 1 sc together with 4th ear loop, 10 sc, 1 sc together with 1st ear loop, 11 sc (36)
11 rnd 13 sc, 1 sc together with 3rd ear loop, 10 sc, 1 sc together with 2nd ear loop, 11 sc (36)
12 rnd 13 sc, 1 sc together with 2nd ear loop, 10 sc, 1 sc together with 3rd ear loop, 11 sc (36)
13 rnd 13 sc, 1 sc together with 1st ear loop, 10 sc, 1 sc together with 4th ear loop, 11 sc (36)
14 rnd (4 sc, dec) * 6 (30)
if you are using eyes on a safety mount, insert them between the 5th and 6th rows at a distance of 6 increments of the 6th row.
15 rnd (3 sc, dec) * 6 (24)
16 rnd (2 sc, dec) * 6 (18)
17 rnd (1 sc, dec) * 6 (12)
we stuff the head tightly.
18 rnd 6 dec (6), we tighten the loops, we cut off the thread, we hide.
we tighten the eyes to the mouth, embroider the nose, tint.
sew the head to the body, tint the legs.
ready
