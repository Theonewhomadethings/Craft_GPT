let's crochet the tail and mane of the amigurumi unicorn pattern
mane
we are going to crochet two separate strips, one in lilac and the other in pink. each strip has 3 curls. we start by weaving with a slipped knot and we crochet 41 chains leaving one free chain and in the next we crochet the first sc increase, we continue crochet increases on the chains in total are 40 sc inc, without cutting we return the above to form the three curls of the strip. at the end of each strip we cut a long strand to sew at the back of the head on rounds 4 and 5.
tail
we are going to crochet two separate strips, one in lilac and the other in pink. each strip carries 2 curls. we start by weaving with a slip knot and we weave 31 chains leaving one free chain and in the next we rochet the first sc increase, we continue rochet increases on the chains in total are 30 sc inc, without cutting we rochet the chain again to weave the next curl and repeat the above until we form the three curls of the strip. we cut a long thread to sew on the back of the body on rounds 8 and 9.
