hello my dears, today i will share an amazing crochet bunny pattern. the bunny is made using velvet rope. that's why it has a fairly large size. bunny amigurumi pieces are very easy to make. head and body are made together. you will additionally make a crochet skirt. then you will have a magnificent bunny amigurumi. the only difference is the ears in terms of construction style. but overall it's an extremely easy free amigurumi pattern. now if you are ready, let's get started, let's crochet the bunny amigurumi step by step.
pdf plush sophie bunny amigurumi crochet free pattern
designer: yuni.toys
required materials
yarn himalaya dolphin baby: 1.5 skeins of the main color and 0.5 additional
yarn yarnart jeans: white color for tightening, pink color for muzzle decoration
hook: 3.5 or 4, for ears 5.5
marker or contrast yarn
eyes on a secure mount 16 mm
spout
eyelashes and glue (moment transparent)
thick eye needle
sewing needles for uniform attachment of the ears to the head
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
legs (2 parts)
we start crocheting with an additional color
1. 6 mr
2. 6 inc (12)
3. (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4. (2 sc, inc)*6 times (24)
5. (3 sc, inc)*6 times (30)
6-7. 30 sc (2 rows)
8. 9 sc, 6 dec, 9 sc (24)
9. 7 sc, 5 dec, 7 sc (19)
10. 6 sc, 3 dec, 7 sc (16)
change the yarn to the main color
11. 16 sc we crochet for the back half loop
12-14. 16 sc (3 rows)
we fill the leg with filler
15. (6 sc, dec)*2 times (14)
16-17. 14 sc (2 rows)
crochet another +2 sc.
fold in half and crochet 7sc.
handles (2 parts)
we start crocheting with the main color
1. 6 mr
2. 6 inc (12)
3. (2 sc, inc)*4 times (16)
4-5. 16 sc (2 rows)
6. 5 sc, 3 dec, 5 sc (13)
change the color of the yarn to an additional
7. 13 sc
8. 4 sc, 3 dec, 3 sc (10)
9-15. 10 sc (7 rows)
we fill only the lower part of the handle we crochet another +2 sc.
fold in half and crochet 5 sc.
ears (2 details)
we crochet with the main color
hook 5.5
1. 6 hdc in mr
2. 6 inc from hdc (12)
3. (1 hdc, inc)*6 times (18)
4. (2 hdc, inc)*6 times (24)
5. (3 hdc, inc)*6 times (30)
6-8. 30 hdc (3 rows)
9. (13 hdc, dec)*2 times (28)
10. (12 hdc, dec)*2 times (26)
11. (11 hdc, dec)*2 times (24)
12. 24 hdc
13. (4 hdc, dec)*4 times (20)
14. 20 hdc
15. (3 hdc, dec)*4 times (16)
16. (6 hdc, dec)*2 times (14)
17. 14 hdc
18. (5 hdc, dec)*2 times (12)
19-20. 12 hdc (2 rows)
21. (4 hdc, dec)*2 times (10)
22-24. 10 hdc (3 rows)
fold the edges together and crochet 5 sc.
fasten the yarn and leave a long end for sewing the ears to the head. ears are sewn in the center of the head.
body-head
we crochet the body and head in one piece.
we start crocheting the body with the main color
1. 6 mr
2. 6 inc (12)
3. (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4. (2 sc, inc)*6 times (24)
5. (3 sc, inc)*6 times (30)
6. (4 sc, inc)*6 times (36)
7. (5 sc, inc)*6 times (42)
change the color of the yarn to an additional
in the next row we crochet legs
8. 7 sc, 7 sc(leg), 14 sc, 7 sc(leg), 7 sc (42)
9. 42 sc crochet for the back half loop
10-11. 42 sc
12. (5 sc, dec)*6 times (36)
13. 36 sc
14. 13 sc, 6 dec, 11 sc (30)
15. 30 sc
16. (3 sc, dec)*6 times (24)
17. 24 sc
18. (2 sc, dec)*6 times (18)
in the next row we crochet handles
19. 4 sc, 5 sc (hand), 3 sc, 5 sc (hand), 1 sc
we fill the body well with filler, highlighting the tummy.
change the color of the yarn to the main one.
we begin to crochet a head
20. 18 sc
21. 18 inc (36)
22. (5 sc, inc)*6 times (42)
23. (6 sc, inc)*6 times (48)
24-27. 48 sc (4 rows)
28. (6 sc, dec)*6 times (42)
29. (5 sc, dec)*6 times (36)
30-31. 36 sc (2 rows)
32. (4 sc, dec)*6 times (30)
33. 30 sc
34. (3 sc, dec)*6 times (24)
35. (2 sc, dec)*6 times (18)
36. (1 sc, dec)*6 times (12)
37. 6 dec
we stuff bunny's head well.
we pay special attention to the neck, stuffing it very tightly so that the head does not dangle in different directions.
we tighten the hole with a needle with a thick eye.
glue the eyelashes to the eyes in advance
we insert the eyes between the 29th and 30th rows at a distance of 5sc.
we insert the nose between the 28th and 29th rows, in the middle of the eye.
we make a muzzle tightening
embroider eyebrows at level 31 and 35 in rows
tail
1. 6 mr
2. 6 inc (12)
3. (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4. 18 sc
5. (1 sc, dec)*6 times (12)
we crochet slst.
we fill the tail a little with filler. we fasten the tail to the body with the help of needles and sew between 3 and 7 rows.
skirt
we attach the yarn to the front side of the loop of the 9th row.
1. 3 ch, crochet in a circle, alternating 2 dc, 3 dc in each half loop. at the end of the row we make slst into the ch rise chain to connect the row.
2. 2 ch, 1 dc in each column of the previous row. at the end of the row we make slst into the ch rise chain to connect the row.
3. 2 ch, 1 dc in each column of the previous row. we crochet slst, fasten the yarn, cut off and carefully hide the tips in the body of the toy.
