materials
yarn: sports or light dk yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2mm 2.5mm)
black embroidery yarn
safety eyes size 5 mm
fiber filling
yarn needles
abbreviations
mr : amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
trc: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st : stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
k :knit (stitch)
let's start crocheting the amigurumi bunny pattern
head
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8- inc (16)
rnd 3. (1 sc, 1 inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (2 sc, 1 inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5-6. (2 rounds) 32 sc (32)
rnd 7. (3 sc, 1 inc)*8 (40)
rnd 8-10. (3 rounds) 40 sc (40)
rnd 11. (4 sc, 1 inc)*8 (48)
rnd 12-18. (7 rounds) 48 sc (48)
insert safety eyes between rounds 13,14. 7 st apart
rnd 19. (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 20. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 21. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 22. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 23. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 24. (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 25. 6 dec (6)
cut the yarn. leave a long tail for sewing
we crochet the head part of the amigurumi pattern. now we will crochet the legs
legs x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. 3 sc, 3 inc, 3 sc (12)
rnd 4. 12 sc (12)
rnd 5. 3 sc, 3 dec, 3 sc (9)
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
rnd 7. 12 sc (12)
rnd 8. (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd 9. 15 sc (15)
rnd 10. (4 sc, inc)*4 (18)
rnd 11. 18 sc (18)
rnd 12. 3 sc
cut the yarn, knit the 2nd leg in the same way until the round. eleven.
rnd 13. 16 sc
do not cut the yarn, make 6 chains, join 1 leg st. continue with the body.
after knitting the legs of the amigurumi rabbit, we knit the body part without cutting the threads. the color of the yarn is white.
body
rnd 1. 9 sc, inc, 8 sc, inc, 4 sc, inc, 8 sc, inc, 9 sc, inc, 4 sc, inc (54)
rnd 2-4. (3 rounds) 54 sc (54)
rnd 5. 9 sc, dec, 24 sc, dec, 17 sc (52)
rnd 6. 52 sc (52)
rnd 7. 8 sc, dec, 24 sc, dec, 16 sc (50)
rnd 8. 50 sc (50)
rnd 9. 7 sc, dec, 24 sc, dec, 15 sc (48)
rnd 10-11. (2 rounds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 12. (6 sc, dec)*3, turn red, (6 sc, dec)*3 (42)
rnd 13-14. 42 sc (42)
rnd 15. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 16-17. (2 rounds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 18. (4 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 19-20.(2 rounds) 30 sc (30)
rnd 21. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 22-24. (3 rounds) 24 sc (24)
rnd 25. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 26. 18 sc (18)
cut the yarn, leave a length to sew.
we have completed the body part of the amigurumi bunny. now we crocheet the arms and ears.
arms x 2
start with white yarn
rnd 1. 5 sc in mr (5)
rnd 2. 5 inc (10)
rnd 3-6. (4 rounds) 10 sc (10)
change to red
rnd 7-19. (13 rounds) 10 sc (10)
cut the yarn, leave the tail long to sew
ears x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4. (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd 5. (4 sc, inc)*3 (18)
rnd 6-8. (3 rounds) 18 sc (18)
rnd 9. (7 sc, dec)*2 (16)
rnd 10. (6 sc, dec)*2 (14)
rnd 11. (5 sc, dec)*2 (12)
rnd 12. (4 sc, dec)*2 (10)
rnd 13. (3 sc, dec)*2 (8)
rnd 14. (2 sc, dec)*2 (6)
we crochet the scarf of the amigurumi rabbit pattern. after completing the free pattern, you can put some makeup on the face part.:)
bufanda (tejido)
catch 8 st on needle
rnd 1. 8-k
rnd 2. until you get 20 cm long. repeat round 1.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi bunny.
