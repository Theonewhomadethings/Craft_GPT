body
use the red heart yarn. work in a continous spiral. mark the beginning of the lines with a piece of yarn.
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-5. 18sc (18)
do not cut the yarn of the second leg. chain 8 and connect the other leg with a sc
rnd 6. 18 sc around the leg, 8 sc into the chains, 18 sc around the other leg, 8 sc into the other side of chains (52)
rnd 7-9. 52 sc (52)
rnd 10. 8sc, inc, 7sc, inc, 3sc, inc, 4sc, inc, 7sc, inc, 18sc (57)
rnd 11. 8sc, inc, 8sc, inc, 2sc, inc, 6sc, inc, 2sc, inc, 7sc, inc, 18sc (63)
rnd 12-26. 63sc
rnd 27. 20sc, inv-sc2tog, 14sc, inv-sc2tog, 25sc (61)
rnd 28. 61sc (61)
rnd 29. 10sc, inv-sc2tog, 37sc, inv-sc2tog, 10sc (59)
rnd 30. 18sc, inv-sc2tog, 17sc, inv-sc2tog, 20sc (57)
rnd 31. 18sc, inv-sc2tog, 15sc, inv-sc2tog, 20sc (55)
rnd 32. 18sc, inv-sc2tog, 13sc, inv-sc2tog, 20sc (53)
rnd 33. 18sc, inv-sc2tog, 11sc, inv-sc2tog, 20sc (51)

nose
work in one sc the ones in bracket
chain 5
rnd 1. (start from the second chain from hook) 3sc, (4sc), 3sc, (4sc),slst (14)
rnd 2. 3sc, (2sc), (2sc), 5sc, (2sc), (2sc), 2sc, slst (18)
rnd 3. 3sc, (2sc), (2sc) (2sc), 7sc, (2sc), (2sc), 4sc, slst (22)
rnd 4. 4sc, (2sc), 10sc, (2sc), 6sc, slst (24)
arms (x2)
rnd 1. 6sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. 1sc, inc, (2sc, inc)*5, 1sc (24)
rnd 5-7. 24sc (24)
rnd 8. 1sc, inv-sc2tog, (2sc, inv-sc2tog)*5, 1sc
rnd 9-16. 18sc (18)
fold in half, work 9sc in both side
ears (x2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-5. 18sc (18)
fold in half, work 9sc in both side
