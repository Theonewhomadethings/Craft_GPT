we're starting to make baby yoda's favorite robe. amigurumi pattern is almost finished.
robe
work in rows (turning chain in every row)
beige:
r 1. 28 ch
r 2-5. 28 sc
brown:
r 6. 28 sc
r 7. 6 sc, 3 sl st, 10 sc, 3 sl st, 6 sc
r 8. 6 sc, 3 ch, skip 3 stitches, 10 sc, 3 ch, skip 3 stitches, 6 sc
r 9-15. 28 sc
work border of sc along the edge of robe.
sleeves
work in a spiral
holes between rows 7 and 8 consist of 10 loops (3 up, 3 down, 2 left, 2 right). join threat to middle down loop.
brown: 1-2. 10 sc
biege: 3-4. 10 sc
assemble:
sew all pieces in their places. put the robe on baby yoda. sew the collar.
