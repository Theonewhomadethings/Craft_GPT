crochet christmas penguin amigurumi free pattern

materials and tools
cotton blend yarn alize cotton gold (yardage 330 m / 100 g (361 yds / 3.5 02]) main colors: black 60, white 55, orange 83, pink 518. for the hat two matching colors, such as red and white; blue and green; blue and light blue.
crochet hook 2 mm (us steel hook 4, uk 212].
polyester fiberfil for stuffing.
safety eyes, diameter 6 mm (0.24 in) 2 pieces.
soft pompom ball, diameter 15- 18mm.
tapestry needle with a large eye.
the finished toy will be about 9 cm (3.5 in) tall.
abbreviations used
amr adjustable magic ring
st(s) stitch(es)
ch chain
sl-st slip stitch
sc single crochet (us)
inc increase (2 sts worked into the same stitch)
dec decrease (2 stitches crocheted off together)
hdc half double crochet (us)
dc double crochet (us)
tr treble crochet (us)
blo work through the back loop only
flo work through the front loop only
(sc, inc) times repeat instructions in parenthesis. numbers in parenthesis at the end of a row or round indicate the stitch count after completion of the row or round.
head and body
worked in one piece from the front to back. will be great if you have two balls of black yarn. if there is only one, either prepare a small ball of yarn in advance or use the other end of the current ball.
begin with white yarn. stuff as you go.
rnd 1: 6 sc into an amr (6)
rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, 1 inc) 6 times (18)
rnd 4: (2 sc, 1 inc) 6 times (24)
rnd 5: (3 sc, 1 inc) 6 times (30)
rnd 6: (4 sc, 1 inc) 6 times (36)
change color to black.
rnd 7: blo 1 sl-st each into every stitch (36)
rnd 8: blo (5 sc, inc) 6 times (42)
rnd 9: (6 sc, inc) 6 times (48)
place crocheting the body aside for a short time. join new working yarn in black to the unused loop of the first stitch of rnd 7 and work around: 15 sc, 1 hdc, 1 dc, 2 tr, 1 dc,1 hdc, 15 sc. cut the working yarn and secure the end. using black yarn sew two middle treble crochets to the white part of the head as if continuing these crochets by 2 rounds towards the center. using orange yarn embroider the beak as shown in the picture. insert the safety eyes on the same level as the beak line. using pink yarn embroider the cheeks. take up the body again.
rnds 1015: 6 rounds total 1 sc each into every st. (48)
rnd 16: (6 sc, dec) 6 times (42)
rnd 17:(5 sc, dec) 6 times (36)
rnd 18: (4 sc, dec) 6 times (30)
rnd 19: (3 sc, dec) 6 times (24)
rnd 20: (2 sc, dec) 6 times (18)
rnd 21: (1 sc, dec) 6 times (12)
rnd 22: 6 dec (6)
put more stuffing. pull the remaining opening closed, and hide the tail inside.
wings (make 2)
worked in black yarn.
rnd 1: 6 sc into an amr (6)
rnd 2: (1 sc, inc) 6 times (9)
rnds 3-4: 2 rounds total 1 sc each into every stitch (9)
don't stuff the wigs. fold the piece in half, and crochet the edges of both layers together with sc.
cut the yarn, leaving an end enough for seaming.
paws ( make 2)
worked in orange yarn.
crochet a chain of ch2.
work into the 1st stitch: 2 hdc, ch1., sl-st, ch1, 2 hdc, ch1., sl-st. cut the yarn, leaving an end enough for seaming.
tail
using black yarn work into an amr: (ch2, 1 bobble*, ch2, sl-st) repeat 5 times. tighten the magic ring firmly. cut the yarn, leaving an end enough for seaming. *bobble work 3 unfinished dc's into the same stitch and crochet them off together.
hat
begin from the top with color 1.
rnd 1: 6 sc into an amr (6)
rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc) 6 times (18)
rnd 4: 1 sc each into every st. (18)
rnd 5: (2 sc, inc) 6 times (24)
rnd 6: 1 sc each into every stitch (24)
rnd 7: (3 sc, inc) 6 times (30)
change to color 2.
rnd 8: 1 sc each into every stitch (30), sl-st in the next st., turn work.
rnd 9: ch3, dc into every back half loop.
cut the yarn, leaving an end enough for seaming.
fasten off. sew the soft pompom ball on the top.
1. sew the wings, paws and tail.
2. sew the hat on the head, adding a little fiberfill inside it before closing the hole.
