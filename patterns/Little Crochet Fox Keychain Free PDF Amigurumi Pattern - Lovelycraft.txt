little crochet fox keychain free pdf amigurumi pattern
designer
instagram: handmadebylacey
you will need:
2 mm crochet hook
6 mm safety eyes
abbreviations
rnd: round
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
sl st: slip stitch
sc: single crochet
dc: double crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
()*: repeat till the end of round
(): total number of stitches in the round
head
orange colour
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc, inc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, inc (30)
rnd 6. 4 sc, inc (36)
rnd 7-10. sc in all
change colour to white
rnd 11-12. sc in all
attache safety eyes between row 9-10 with 6 sc apart.
rnd 13. 4 sc, dec (30)
rnd 14. 3 sc, dec (24)
rnd 15. 2 sc, dec (18)
rnd 16. 1 dec, 10 sc, 1 dec, 4 sc (16)
body
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc, inc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, inc (30)
rnd 6-10. sc in all
rnd 11. 4 sc, dec (25)
rnd 12. 3 sc, dec (20)
rnd 13. sc in all
rnd 14. 3 sc, dec (16)
rnd 15-16. sc in all
nose
white yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. sc in all
inner ear
white
rnd 1. 5 sc in mr
ears
start with black yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
change colour to orange
rnd 2. 1 sc, inc (9)
rnd 3. 2 sc, inc (12)
rnd 4. 3 sc, inc (15)
rnd 5-7. sc in all
legs
ch 5, start in second ch from hook
rnd 1. 3 sc, 3 sc in last ch, continue working an oval round, 2 sc, inc
rnd 2. inc, 2 sc, 3 inc, 2 sc, 2 inc
rnd 3. inc, 3 dec, 4 sc, dec
rnd 4. 4 sc, 2 dec, 4 sc
rnd 5-10. sc in all
rnd 11. 3 sc, dec
arms
black yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. sc in all
change to colour orange
rnd 3-8. sc in all
tail
white yarn
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc, 3 sc (5)
rnd 3. (inc)*3, 2 sc (8)
change colour to orange
rnd 4. 1 sc, inc (12)
rnd 5-7. sc in all
rnd 8. 1 sc, dec (8)
rnd 9. 2 sc, dec (6)
rnd 10. sc in all
belly
white yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, inc (18)
