amigurumi puppy pattern is very easy to make. the body and head are crocheted together. you can make the eyes of the amigurumi pattern using plastic accessories. you can crochet the eyebrows and muzzle of the amigurumi dog pattern using black yarn when the free pattern is completed. you can make this wonderful pattern for amigurumi today. the amigurumi toy has hair on top of its head. for this hair, you can use threads that you do not use at home. you can choose the thread color for the hair as you wish. do not forget to add the puppy amigurumi pattern to your favorite list.

materials and tools:
1. sport / 5 ply (12 wpi) yarn. i used alize cotton gold. the mane color beige (67), for sweater color terracotta (89), for nose black (60). for ears and tail alize superlana klasik brown544. but you can use any yarn to create a puppy. let's experiment!
2. the hook 2.00 mm. or other size for your yarn
3. two black halfbeads 8 mm in diameter or the black beads
4. the moment glue.
5. stuffing (hollofayber)
let's start crocheting the puppy amigurumi pattern.
body-head
begin with the mane color. stuff in the course of crocheting.
rnd 1. 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) 6 (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc) 6 (36)
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc ) 6 (42)
rnd 8-14. 7 rows 1 sc in each st around (42)
change the color to the color of the sweater
rnd 15-24. 10 rows 1 sc in each st around (42)
change the color to the mane color
rnd 25-29. 5 rows 1 sc in each st around (42)
rnd 30. (5 sc, dec) 6 (36)
rnd 31-34. 4 rows 1 sc in each st around 36)
rnd 35. (4 sc, dec) 6 (30)
rnd 36-38. 3 rows 1 sc in each st around (30)
rnd 39. (3 sc, dec) 6 (24)
rnd 40. (6 sc, dec) 3 (21)
rnd 41. (5 sc, dec) 3 (18)
fasten off and cut the yarn.
we completed the body and head of the amigurumi pattern together. now let's crochet the nose part. black thread was used while making the nose part. you can use different colored yarn if you want. if you want to prepare a christmas gift, you can use red or green yarn.
nose:
crochet with the black color.
rnd 1. 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) 6 (24)
rnd 5-6. 2 rows 1 sc in each st around (24)
rnd 7. (6 sc, dec) 3 (21)
leave enough thread for sewing. before closing the opening, put the stuffing in the nose. attach the nose to the head as the lid to the bottle. fix it with the pins (attention!) to the penultimate 40th row of the body and sew it.
before closing the opening add some more stuffing. wrap the black thread around the base of the nose, fasten off, hide the end of the thread inside the detail.
let's crochet the ears of the puppy free amigurumi pattern. brown yarn is preferred for the ears. you can use different colored yarn if you want. you can make colorful ears like a rainbow.
ears (2 pcs)
crochet with the brown color
rnd 1. 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) 6 (18)
rnd 4. (5 sc, inc) 3 (21)
rnd 5-7. 3 rows 1 sc in each st around (21)
rnd 8. (5 sc, dec) 3 (18)
rnd 9-11. 3 rows 1 sc in each st around (18)
rnd 12. (4 sc, dec) 3 (15)
rnd 13-15. 3 rows 1 sc in each st around (15)
rnd 16. (3 sc, dec) 3 (12)
rnd 17-18. 2 rows 1 sc in each st around (12)
rnd 19. (2 sc, dec) 3 (9)
fold the ear in half, leave enough thread for sewing. you don't need to stuff them
let's crochet the legs of the amigurumi puppy. i wrote how to make the front and back legs step by step.
legs:
front legs (2 pcs)
crochet with the mane color
rnd 1. 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3-8. 6 rows 1 sc in each st around (12)
leave enough thread for sewing. put the stuffing
hind legs (2 pcs)
crochet with the mane color
rnd 1. 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (3 sc, inc) 3 (15)
rnd 4-7. 4 rows 1 sc in each st (15)
leave enough thread for sewing. put the stuffing if you want your sirup to stand, then crochet all the legs similarly as the front legs or as the hind legs.
let's crochet the tail of the puppy amigurumi pattern.
tail:
crochet with the mane color
rnd 1. 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc) 3 (9)
rnd 3-5. 3 rows 1 sc in each st (9)
rnd 6. inc, 8 sc (10)
rnd 7-9. 3 rows 1 sc in each st (10)
leave enough thread for sewing. put the stuffing.
assembly and decoration:
1. mark the places for the eyes with the pint at the level of the 30-31 rows and slightly deepen them, pulling them to each other with a couple of stitches.
2. glue the halfbeads to the places for the eyes or sew the beads.
3. make the eyelids above the eyes with the black thread, with the white thread under the eyes. embroider the brows and the mouth.
4. sew the ears and the tail
5. sew the front legs and hind legs so as the puppy can sit or stand.
6. attach the cuts of the threads to the top of the head.
congratulations, you have completed the puppy amigurumi pattern.
