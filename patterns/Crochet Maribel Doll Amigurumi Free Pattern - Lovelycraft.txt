abbreviations
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
dc : double crochet
dcinc: double crochet increase
sl-st : slip stitch
ch : chain
rnd: round
st/sts: stitch/stitches
flo :use the front loops only
blo :use the back loops only
(..) :total number of stitches after the round/ row.
() x . times:repeat in structions in parentheses x times
reverse single crochet: reverse single crochet simply means to crochet from left to right side of the round. you can use this technique to make borders on your works. chain 1 on the right side to turn your work. insert your hook into the right of the stitch you have just worked. make single crochets in this way from left to right and do not forget to stretch your yarn when crocheting. reverse single crochet until the end of the round.
materials
yarnart jeans / skin color (color code 73)
yarnart jeans / white (color code 62)
yarnart jeans / brown (color code 40)
alize cotton gold / blue (color code 40)
three different colors of yarn for embroideries
7 mm security eyes
polyester fiberfill
tapestry needle for sewing
2.5mm crochet hook
scissors
legs( with skin color)x 2
begin with the legs, we will make the body by joining two legs.
rnd 1: 6sc into the magic ring
rnd 2: incx6 (12)
rnd 3-10: 12sc
rnd 11: decx2, 3sc, incx2, 3sc(12)
rnd 12: 12sc
rnd 13: decx2, 3sc, incx2, 3sc (12)
rnd 14-19: 12sc
rnd 20: 12sc
rnd 21: work in blo 12sc
you have finished the first leg. crochet the second leg in the same way. when you have finished round 21, make 2 single crochets into the stitches of the second leg to reach the middle. it is important to make the tips of the feet look at each other. do not break yarn ch6 to join the two legs.
body
rnd 22:join the two legs by chaining 6(36)
rnd 23-29: 36sc
rnd 30-33: 36sc
rnd 34:work blo 36sc
rnd 35: 36sc
rnd 36: work blo 36sc
rnd 37: 36sc
rnd 38: (4sc, dec)x6 times (30)
rnd 39-40: 30sc
rnd 41: (3sc, dec)x6 times (24)
rnd 42: (2sc, dec)x times (18)
rnd43-44: 18sc
you have finished the body. do not break the yarn, we will crochet the head.
(for the ruffles of the underpants; with white color work into the blo stitches of round 21 and (ch1,1sc) x 12 times. repeat this for two legs.)
head (skin color)
rnd 45: (2sc, inc)x6 times (24)
rnd 46: (3sc,inc)x6 times (30)
rnd 47:(4sc, inc)x6 times (36)
rnd 48:(5sc,inc)x6 times (42)
rnd 49: (6sc, inc)x6 times (48)
rnd 50:(7sc,inc)x6 times(54)
rnd 51-55: 54sc
rnd 56:(7sc,dec)x6 times (48)
rnd 57-60: 48sc
rnd 61: (6sc,dec)x 6 times (42)
rnd 62:(5sc,dec)x6 times (36)
rnd 63: 36sc
rnd 64:(4sc,dec)x 6 times (30)
rnd 65:(3sc, dec)x 6 times (24)
rnd 66:(2sc,dec)x 6 times (18)
rnd 67: (1sc,dec)x 6 times(12)
rnd 68: (dec)x 6 times (6) fasten off
arm (skin color ) x 2
rnd 1: 5sc into the magic ring
rnd 2: inc x5 (10)
rnd 3-5: 10sc
rnd 6: dec, 6sc, dec(8)
rnd 7-17: 8sc
rnd 18:work flo inc x 8 (16)
rnd 19-20: 16sc
rnd 21: dec x 8 (8)
rnd 22: dec x 4 (4) fasten off.
place the security eyes between round 55-56 with a distance of 9 stitches between them. leave a long tail after finishing crocheting the arms. stuff them firmly and sew them to the body by closing the holes. sew the arms on the body where the neck section begins.
do not forget to sew them by hiding the color changes
hair (brown)
rnd 1:6sc into the magic ring
rnd 2: inc x 6 (12)
rnd 3: (1sc, inc) x 6 times (18)
rnd 4: (2sc, inc)x6 times (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)x 6 times (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)x 6 times (36)
rnd 7: 36sc
rnd 8:(5sc,inc)x 6 times(42)
rnd 9: (6sc,inc) x 6 times (48)
rnd 10-15: 48sc
to make the border of the hair:ch2,turn and make single crochet into the 3rd stitch from your hook. crochet all the rounds in the same way.
rnd16: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (46sc)
rnd17: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (44sc)
rnd18: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (42sc)
rnd19: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (40sc)
rnd20: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (38sc)
rnd21: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rds titich from your hook (36sc)
rnd22: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (34sc)
rnd23: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (32sc)
rnd24: ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (30sc)
to make hair look wavy on the back;ch2 and make 1sc into the next stitch, repeat this until the end of the round.
rnd25: (ch2,1sc)x30 times (30sc) fasten off.
leave a long tail and break the yarn. we will sew the hair on the head.
rnd 1: 6sc into the magic ring
rnd 2: inc x6 (12)
rnd 3: (1sc, inc) x 6 times (18)
rnd 4: (2sc, inc)x 6 times (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)x 6 times (30)
rnd 6-10: 30 sc
rnd 11: (3sc, dec)x 6 times (24)
rnd 12: (2sc, dec)x 6 times (18)
rnd 13: (1sc, dec) x 6 times (12)
rnd 14: dec x 6 times (6) fasten off.
rnd 1: 6sc into the magic ring
rnd 2: inc x 6 times (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,inc)x 6 times (18)
rnd 4-7: 18sc
rnd 8: (1sc,dec) x 6 times(12)
rnd 9: dec x 6 times (6) fasten off.
sew the big buns on the right and left sides of the hair as shown in the pictures. stuff them lightly, they should look curved. sew the small buns under the big ones and slightly back of the head.
inner part of the skirt (with white color)
we will crochet the inner part of the skirt by joining our yarn into the blo stitches of rnd34 on the body. crochet the whole skirt as dc.
rnd 1: (2dc, dcinc) x 12 times (48)
rnd2-4: 48dc
rnd 5: (3dc,dcinc) x 12 times (60)
rnd 6-10: 60dc
to make the borders on the inner part of the skirt; yarn over, skip two stitches and insert your hook into the 3rd stitch from your hook and make one dc and make 5 more dc into the same stitch. repeat until the end of the round. there will be one last stitch slst into this last stitch. fasten off.
upper part of the skirt ( with blue)
we will crochet the upper part of the skirt by joining our yarn into the blo stitches of rnd36 on the body.
crochet the whole skirt as dc.
rnd 1: 1dc, 1dcinc around (54)
rnd 2-4: 54dc
rnd 5: 2dc, 1dcinc (72)
rnd 6-11: 72dc fasten off.
after crocheting and sewing make embroideries on dress and front part of the hair as you can see from the pictures. you can use three different colors of yarn.
