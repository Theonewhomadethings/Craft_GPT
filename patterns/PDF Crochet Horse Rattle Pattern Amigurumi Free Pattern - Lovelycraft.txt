let's start crocheting the amigurumi horse rattle pattern. are you ready?
horse rattle pattern
wood hoop cover:
rnd 1. 25 ch
rnd 2. start on 2nd chain, 24 sc, 1 ch air and turn
rnd 3. repeat until the turn 10 (24st)
leave a long strand to sew the cover to the hoop.
we are going to crochet the head part of the amigurumi horse rattle pattern
head
ecru color
rnd 1. 6 sc stitch in magic ring
rnd 2. inc x 6 (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc, 1 inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. 24 sc
rnd 6. (3 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 7-8. 30 sc
rnd 9. 9 sc, 1 pc, 9 sc, 1 pc, 10 sc (30 st)
rnd 10-11. 30 sc
rnd 12. (3 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (24)
rnd 13-14. 24 sc
color beige
rnd 15. (3 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 16. 30 sc
rnd 17. (4 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (36)
rnd 18. 36 sc
rnd 19. (5 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 20-24. 42 sc
rnd 25. (5 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 26. (4 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (30)
rnd 27. (3 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (24)
rnd 28. (2 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (18)
rnd 29. (1 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (12)
rnd 30. (6 dec) (6)
fasten off.
we are going to knit the ears and mouth of the amigurumi horse rattle pattern
ears
(make two beige)
rnd 1. 5 sc in magic ring
rnd 2. (5 inc) (10)
rnd 3-6. 10 sc
close and leave a long thread to sew to the head
white stain
6 ch
start in 2nd ch, 4 sc, 3 sc together i continue to the next side, 4 sc fasten off.
muzzle
30 ch for muzzle and close, lift with 30 ch more and close in one of the muzzle ends.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi horse rattle pattern.
