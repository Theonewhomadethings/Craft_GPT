are our materials ready?
let's start making baby groot amigurumi pattern.
to make the baby groot amigurumi toy, we'll start with the legs.
legs:
rnd 1) 6 sc in mr 6
rnd 2) 2 sc, 1 inc, 3 sc 7
rnd 3) 7 sc 7
fo, make another leg.
the legs and body of the amigurumi pattern are crocheted together.
body:
2 ch from second leg, join legs.
rnd 1) 18 sc
rnd 2) 3 sc, 1 inc, 8 sc, 1 inc, 5 sc 20
rnd 3-6) 20 sc 20
rnd 7) 3 sc, 1 dec, 8 sc, 1 dec, 5 sc 18
rnd 8) 3 sc, 1 dec, 7 sc, 1 dec, 4 sc 16
rnd 9) (2 sc, 1 dec)*4 12
do not fo. stuff body.
after the body part of the amigurumi free pattern is completed, let's crochet the head part.
head:
continue from the last row of body.
rnd 1) flo (1 inc, 1 sc)*6 18
rnd 2) (1 inc, 2 sc)*6 24
rnd 3) (1 inc, 3 sc)*6 30
rnd 4-9) 30 sc 30. add safety eyes in row 6.
rnd 10) blo (1 dec, 3 sc)*6 24
rnd 11) (1 dec, 2 sc)*6 18
stuff head.
rnd 12) (1 dec, 1 sc)*6 12
rnd 13) 6 dec.
fo.
join thread to front loops in row 10.
rnd 10a) 1 ch, 1 hdc, 2 dc, 1 hdc, 1 sc, 1 ss, 1 sc, 2 hdc, 2 dc, 2 hdc, 1 sc, 1 ss, 1 sc, 1 hdc, 1 dc, 1 hdc, 1 cs, 1 ss, 1 ch, 1 hdc, 4 dc, 2 hdc, 2 sc. join the row by ss.
let's crochet the arms of the amigurumi toy.
hands:
rnd 1) 6 sc in mr 6
rnd 2-4) 6 sc 6
rnd 5) 1 dec, 4 sc 5
vines:
make chains (i made 57, you may make more or less). assemble: sew hands and vines to the body. embroider the mouth. embroider green leaves.
congratulations, you have completed the baby groot amigurumi toy.
