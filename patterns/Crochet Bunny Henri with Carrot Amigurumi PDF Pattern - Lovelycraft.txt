hello my dears. i know you love crochet bunny patterns. today we are adding a new one to the bunny amigurumi patterns. as you know, we have quite a large archive of bunny amigurumi. you can find any type of amigurumi you want. today's is a very easy amigurumi pattern. the fact that it consists of easy parts shortens the production time of amigurumi. the most important point here is that the arms and legs are done in the same way. this way, you can finish it in a very short time. carrot is added as an accessory. i think it looks pretty cool. now if you're ready, let's get start.
free crochet bunny henri with carrot amigurumi pdf pattern
designer: les.mailles.du.rocher
materials
cotton ricorumi (white, orange, green)
crochet hook: 3mm
yarn needle
fiberfill for stuffing
security eyes (5 or 6 mm)
black embroidery thread
stitch marker
scissors
abbrevations
rnd: round
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
sl st: slip stitch
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
hdc: half double crochet
blo: back loops
flo: front loops
0*: repeat till the end of round
[ ]: total number of stitches in the round
body
rnd 1: mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2 : (inc)* [12]
rnd 3 : (1 sc, inc)* [18]
rnd 4 : (2 sc, inc)* [24]
rnd 5 : (3 sc, inc)* [30]
rnd 6 : blo (2 sc, inc, 2 sc)* [36]
rnd 7 12 : 36 sc [36]
rnd 13 : (dec, 4 sc) x3, dec, 16 sc [32]
rnd 14 15 : 32 sc [32]
rnd 16 : (dec, 3 sc) x3, dec, 15 sc [28]
rnd 17 18 : 28 sc [28]
stuff.
rnd 19 : (dec, 2 sc) x3, dec, 14 sc [24]
rnd 20 : 24 sc [24]
rnd 21 : (2 sc, dec)* [18]
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing to the head.
head
rnd 1 : mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2 : (inc)* [12]
rnd 3 : (1 sc, inc)* [18]
rnd 4 :18 sc [18]
rnd 5 : (2 sc, inc)* [24]
rnd 6 : 24 sc [24]
rnd 7 : (3 sc, inc)* [30]
rnd 8 : 30 sc [30]
rnd 9 : (2 sc, inc, 2 sc)* [36]
rnd 10 16 : 36 sc [36]
insert the safety eyes btw rnds 9 & 10 with approx 16 stitches apart.
rnd 17 : (dec, 4 sc)* [30]
rnd 18 : (3 sc, dec)* [24]
rnd 19 : (2 sc, dec)* [18]
stuff.
rnd 20 : (dec, 1 sc)* [12]
rnd 21: (dec)* [6]
fasten and close.
ears (x2)
rnd 1: mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2 : (sc, inc)* [6]
rnd 3 : (1 sc, inc)* [9]
rnd 4 : 9 sc [9]
rnd 5 : (2 sc, inc)* [12]
rnd 6 : (3 sc, inc)* [15]
rnd 7 :15 sc [15]
rnd 8 : (4 sc, inc)* [18]
rnd 9 13 : 18 sc [18]
rnd 14 : (4 sc, dec)* [15]
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing.
fold the ear in half and sew both sides together.
arms and legs (x4)
rnd 1: mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2 : (inc)* [12]
rnd 3- 4: 12 sc [12]
rnd 5 : (2 sc, dec)* [9]
rnd 6 : 9 sc [9]
rnd 7 : (1 sc, dec)* [6]
dont stuff. fasten off and close, take out the thread to the inside and sew to the body (or glue).
tail
rnd 1: mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2 : (inc)* [12]
rnd 3 : (1 sc, inc)* [18]
rnd 4 : (2 sc, inc)* [24]
rnd 5 9 : 24 sc [24]
rnd 10 : (2 sc, dec)* [18]
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing. stuff and sew to the body.
carrot
take a 2.5mm hook if you want a small size.
start in orange.
rnd 1: mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2: 6 sc [6]
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc)* [9]
rnd 4: 9 sc [9]
rnd 5: (2 sc, inc)* [12]
rnd 6 10: 12 sc [12]
rnd 11: blo (dec)* [6]
change color to green and crochet in front loops of next round (the remaining back loops will be used to close the hole at the end)
rnd 12: flo [(ch 6, crochet along the ch: 2 sc, 3 slst), 1 slst in next stitch] x6
fasten off and hide the yarn.
cut orange yarn and leave a tail to close, using back loops of last round. you can bring out the thread at the bottom of the carrot.
