valentine's day plush teddy bear pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: odessaviktorytoy
abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
sl st slip stitch
materials
yarn: velvet 50gr/ 300 m
eyes 3 mm
the teddy bear's height is 9 cm.
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. 1sc,inc (18)
rbd 4. 2sc,inc (24)
rnd 5. 3sc,inc (30)
rnd 6. 4sc,inc (36)
rnd 7. 5sc,inc (42)
rnd 8-14. 42 sc (42)
rnd 15. 5sc,dec (36)
rnd 16. 4sc,dec (30)
rnd 17. 3sc,dec (24)
rnd 18. 2sc,dec (18)
fill with fiberfill.
rnd 19. 1sc,dec (12)
rnd 20. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn, fasten.
nose & tail
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. 12 sc (12)
cut the yarn, fasten.
ears (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-4. 12 sc (12)
rnd 5. fold the piece in half and crocheted in half
edge 6 sc.
cut the yarn, fasten, leave the end for
sewing.
arms (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. inc, 2sc, inc, 2sc (8)
rnd 3-15. 8 sc (8)
rnd 16. fold in half, tie for two edges 4 sc.
cut the yarn, fasten.
legs (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 ch, we start crocheted from the second loop from the hook: 4sc, 3sc in 1 loop, 3sc, inc
rnd 2. inc, 3sc, 3inc, 4sc, inc (17)
rnd 3. 6sc, inc, (1sc,inc)*2, 6sc (20)
rnd 4. 7sc, inc, (2sc,inc)*2, 6sc (23)
rnd 5. 23 sc (23)
rnd 6. 7sc, dec, (2sc,dec)*2, 6sc (20)
rnd 7. 5sc, 4dec, 7sc (16)
rnd 8. 4sc, 4dec, 4sc (12)
rnd 9-10. 12 sc (12)
stuff the detail. fasten the yarn, cut.
body
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. 1sc,inc (18)
rnd 4. 2sc,inc (24)
rnd 5. 3sc,inc (30)
rnd 6. 4sc,inc (36)
rnd 7. 5sc,inc (42)
rnd 8. 6sc,inc (48)
rnd 9-13. 48 sc (48)
rnd 14. 6sc,dec (42)
rnd 15. 42 sc (42)
rnd 16. 5sc,dec (36)
rnd 17. 36 sc (36)
rnd 18. 4sc,dec (30)
rnd 19. 30 sc (30)
rnd 20. 3sc,dec (24)
rnd 21-22. 24 sc (24)
assembly
sew on the ears and muzzle.
embroider nose and brows with black yarn.
glue the eyes.
embroider the squirrels of the eye with white yarn.
hands sew between 21-22 side by side.
heart
2 details
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. 12 sc (12)
at the first part, cut the yarn. do not cut off the second, we continue crochet. connect two parts with a connecting column.
rnd 4. 11sc, 1 piece, 11sc for the second part (22)
rnd 5. 22 sc (22)
rnd 6. (4sc,dec)*3, 4sc (19)
rnd 7. dec, 17sc (18)
rnd 8. (1sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 9. 12 sc (12)
rnd 10. 6 dec
rnd 11. 3 dec
cut the yarn, pull the hole. sew a heart on the teddy's paws.
