crochet joey the christmas boy amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: lieve_knutsels
start with brown, the color of joey's pants. fill the legs with fiberfill as you go.
rnd 1: magic ring sc 6 (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: blo sc 12 (12)
rnd 4-11: (8 rounds) sc around (12)
fasten off the first leg and set it aside. make the second leg and proceed with round 12.
rnd 12: with the second leg still on your hook, ch 3, make 12 sc around the first leg, 3 sc in the chains, 12 sc around the second leg, 3 sc in the chains. (30)
rnd 13: (sc4,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 14-17: (4 rounds) sc around (36)
change to red, the color of joey's sweater.
rnd 18: blo sc around (36)
rnd 19-20: sc around (36)
rnd 21: (sc4,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 22-23: sc around (30)
rnd 24: (sc3,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 25-26: sc around (24)
rnd 27: (sc2,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 28-29: sc around (18)
rnd 30: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 31: sc around (12)
change to skin color.
rnd 32: (inc)*12 (24)
rnd 33: (sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 34: (sc5,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 35-46: (12 rounds) sc around (42)
rnd 47: (sc5,dec)*6 (36)
place safety eyes between round 38-39, about 9 stitches apart.
rnd 48: (sc4,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 49: (sc3,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 50: (sc2,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 51: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 52: (dec)*6 (6)
fasten off, leaving a tail to close the little hole by sewing through the remaining six front loops with a tapestry needle.
turn your doll upside down. attach red yarn to the front loop of round 18, on the back of the doll and sc around.
joey's hat
using brown yarn:
rnd 1: magic ring sc 6 (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (sc2,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (sc3,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (sc4,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (sc5,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8: (sc6,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9-17: (9 rounds) sc around (48)
fasten off, leaving a long tail to sew the hat onto the head, later on. try the hat on for size and sew on the strands of hair, using some yarn and a tapestry needle.
ears (make 2)
using brown yarn:
rnd 1: magic ring sc 6 (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
don't fasten off. fold the ear in half opposite sides line up. make 3 sc to close the ear half way up. sew the hat onto the head. sew the ears onto the hat.
antlers (make 2)
using cream colored yarn:
piece 1
rnd 1: magic ring sc 6 (6)
rnd 2-9: (8 rounds) sc around (6)
fasten off. leaving a tail for sewing.
piece 2
repeat round 1-4 of piece 1.
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing.
sew piece 2 onto piece 1 to make one antler. sew both antlers onto joey's hat.
arms (make 2)
start with yarn in skin color.
rnd 1: magic ring sc 8 (8)
rnd 2-4: (3 rounds) sc around (8)
change to red
rnd 5-11: (7 rounds) sc around (8)
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing. sew the arms to either side of the body.
scarf
make a foundation chain in the desirable length. make 1 row of dc.
fasten off.
embroider some details on joey's sweater. wrap joey's scarf around his neck.
embroider a little nose and some make up blush. and that's it!
