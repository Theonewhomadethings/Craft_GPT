slst: slip stitch

..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
are you ready to make the amigurumi seagull pattern? lets start!
body
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc in ea of next 6 sc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)* rep 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)* rep 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)* rep 6 (30)
rnds 6-13. 30 sc (30)
rnd 14. 2 sc; adjust the body shape: ch 4, inc into 2nd ch from the hook, sc to end of chain, do not turn; sc in ea of next 28 sc on body.
rnd 15. 2 sc, do not turn, sc in ea of next 2 chhalf loops, inc into ea of next 2 ch-half loops; do not turn, sc in ea of next 32 sc.
continue in the round:
rnd 16. 6 sc, inc in next sc, 33 sc (41) rnd 17. 41 sc (41)
rnd 18. 6 sc, dec, 33 sc (40)
rnd 19. 40 sc (40)
rnd 20. 5 sc, dec, 2 sc, dec, 29 sc (38)
rnd 21. 23 sc, dec, 2 sc, dec, 9 sc (36)
rnd 22. (4 sc, dec)* rep 6 (30)
rnd 23. (3 sc, dec)* rep 6 (24)
rnd 24. (2 sc, dec)* rep 6 (18)
we have completed the body part of the seagull amigurumi pattern. now we knit the legs
legs x 2
pull 2 threads inside the body (at the bottom) symmetrically. make a knot from ws. create the cord by twisting the thread clockwise, then fold in two, let the cord twist itself counterclockwise. rep for other leg.
body (continue)
rnd 23. (1 sc, dec)* rep 6 (12)
rnd 24. (dec)* rep 6 (6)
fill in the body with some stuffing. fasten off.
we crochet the amigurumi seagull's beak.
beak
rnd 1. 5 sc in mr
rnds 2-3. sc in ea of 5 sc (5)
sew the beak to the head.
we crochet the legs of the amigurumi seagull. now we knit the feet.
pies x 2
rnd 1. 5 sc in mr
rnd 2. sc in ea of next 5 sc (5)
rnd 3. inc in ea of next 5 sc (10)
rnds 4-7. 10 sc (10)
pull the twisted cord (leg) inside the feet detail. make a knot from ws. sew the hole. rep for other feet.
after completing the feet of the amigurumi toy, we crochet the wings.
wings x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc in ea of next 6 sc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)* rep 6 (18)
rnds 4-5. 18 sc (18)
rnd 6. (1 sc, dec)* rep 6 (12)
rnd 7-8. 12 sc (12)
rnd 9. (dec)* rep 6 (6)
rnd 10. 6 sc, si st. fill in with some stuffing. fasten off. sew the wings to the body.
embroider the eyes. make the scarf and hat or some other clothes (optionally).
