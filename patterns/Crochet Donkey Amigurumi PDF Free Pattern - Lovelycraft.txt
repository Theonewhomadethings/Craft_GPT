easy crochet donkey amigurumi free pattern
designer: varry_crochets
necessary materials
-yarn yarnart jeans;
-filling (holofiber, synthetic fluff);
eyes on a secure mount;
-hook number 2;
needle for stitching parts;
tailor's pins;
floss yarns for embroidering a spout.
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
trc: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
we start from the head to crochet the amigurumi donkey.
head
1. 6 sc in mr
2. inc *6 (12)
3. (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4. (2 sc, inc)*6 times (24)
5. (3 sc, inc)*6 times (30)
6. (4 sc, inc)*6 times (36)
7. (5 sc, inc)*6 times (42)
8. (6 sc, inc)*6 times (48)
9. (7 sc, inc)*6 times (54)
10-12. 54sc (3 round)
13. (10 sc, inc)*4 times, 10 sc (58)
14-15. 58sc (2 round)
16. 3 sc, (12 sc, inc)*4 times, 3 sc (62)
17-18. 62 sc (2 round)
19. 1 sc, (9 sc, inc)*6 times, 1 sc (68)
20. 1 sc, (10sc, inc)*6 times, 1sc (74)
21-26. 74 sc (5 rounds)
27. 1 sc (10sc, dec)*6 times, 1sc (68)
28. 1 sc (9sc, dec)*6 times, 1sc (62)
29. 1 sc (8sc, dec)*6 times, 1sc (56)
30. (6 sc,dec)*7 times (48)
31. 1 sc (4 sc,dec)*8 times (41)
32. 1 sc (3 sc,dec)*8 times (33)
33. 1 sc (2 sc,dec)*8 times (25)
after crocheting the head of the donkey amigurumi, we crochet the muzzle part.
muzzle
1. 6 sc in mr
2. inc *6 times (12)
3. (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4. (2 sc, inc)*6 times (24)
5. (3 sc, inc)*6 times (30)
6. (4 sc, inc)*6 times (36)
7. (5 sc, inc)*6 times (42)
8. (6 sc, inc)*6 times (48)
9-11. 48 sc (3 round)
while sewing on the muzzle, it must be filled with filler.
then we make the ears of the crochet donkey amigurumi.
ears
1. 6 sc in mr
2. (1 sc, inc)*3 times (9)
3. (2 sc, inc)*3 times (12)
4. (3 sc, inc)*3 times (15)
5. (4 sc, inc)*3 times (18)
6-16. 18 sc (11 rounds)
17. (4 sc, dec)*3 times (15)
18. (3 sc, dec)*3 times (12)
19. (2 sc, dec)*3 times (9)
fold in half, crochet together 5 sc
now legs, we will crochet the legs of the amigurumi donkey together with donkey's body. it may take some time to crochet this piece.
legs
we collect a chain of 7 ch, starting with the 2nd ch
1. 5 sc, 3 sc on the last st, on the back of the chain 4 sc, inc (14)
2. inc, 4 sc, 3 inc, 4 sc, 2 inc (20)
3. 1 sc, inc, 5sc, inc, 3 sc, inc, 5 sc, inc, 2 sc (24)
4. 2 sc, inc, ( 5 sc, inc)*3 times, sc (28)
5. 2 sc, 2 inc, 5 sc, 2 inc, 4 sc, 2 inc, 6 sc, inc, 1 sc, inc, 2 sc (36)
6. 36 slst
7-10. 36 sc
11. 10 sc, 6 dec, 12 sc, dec (29)
12. 10 sc, 4 dec, 9 sc, dec (24)
13. 8 sc, 4 dec, 8 sc (20)
14. 8 sc, 2 dec, 8 sc (18)
change the color of the yarn, fill the leg tightly
15. (2 sc, inc)*6 times (24)
16-18. 24 sc (3 round)
19. (3 sc,inc)*6 times (30)
20-22. 30 sc (3 round)
23. (4 sc, inc)*6 times (36)
24-25. 36 sc
we crochet 9 sc (this is necessary so that our slst is on the side of the leg). fix slst and cut the yarn.
we also crochet the second leg, but we do not cut the yarn, we continue to crochet the body.
(in the last row of the second leg, i did not tie 7 sc, so that the working yarn would be on the side of the leg)
we crochet 2 ch, attach to the 1st leg
26. 36 sc, 2 sc along a chain of ch, 36 sc,2 sc along a chain of ch(76)
27. (18 sc, inc)*4 times (80)
28-30. 80 sc
31. 19 sc, 2 inc, 19 sc, inc, 19 sc, 2 inc, 17 sc, inc. (86)
32-36. 86 sc
37. dec, (12 sc, dec )*6 times (79)
38-39. 79 sc
40. (11sc, dec)*6 times, 1 sc (73)
41-42. 73 sc
43. (10 sc,dec)*6 times, 1 sc (67)
44-45. 67 sc
46. ( 9sc, dec)*6 times, 1 sc (61)
47-48. 61 sc
49. (8 sc, dec)*6 times, 1 sc (55)
50. 55 sc
51. (7 sc, dec)*6 times, 1 sc (49)
52. 49 sc
53. (6 sc, dec)*6 times, 1 sc (43)
54. 43 sc
55. (5 sc, dec)*6 times, 1 sc (37)
56. 37 sc
57. (4 sc, dec)*6 times, 1 sc (31)
58. 31 sc
59. (3 sc, dec)*6 times, 1 sc (25)
attach slst and cut the yarn. (the yarn must be left longer)
finally, we crochet the hands of the amigurumi donkey.
hands
1. 6 sc in mr
2. inc *6 (12)
3. (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4. (2 sc, inc)*6 times (24)
5-7. 24 sc
change yarn color
8. 8 sc, 4 dec, 8 sc (20)
9. 8 sc, 2 dec, 8 sc (18)
10-20. 18 sc
21. (4 sc, dec)*3 times (15)
22-23. 15 sc
24. (3 sc, dec)*3 times (12)
25-30. 12 sc
we fill the handles tightly only at the beginning, fold in half, crochet 6 sc.
