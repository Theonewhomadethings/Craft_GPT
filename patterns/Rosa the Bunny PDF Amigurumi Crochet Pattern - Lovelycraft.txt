materials
hook size 2.5mm
drops safran yarn
embroidery thread
yarn needle
safety eyes
stitch marker
fiber fill
scissors
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
()*n repeat in braces n times
st.st slip stitch
w white
p pink
if our materials are ready, then let's start making the little bunny. we start making the amigurumi pattern from the head.
head pink
1. mr 6sc (6)
2. 6inc (12)
3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
4. (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
5. (3sc, inc)*6 (30)
6. 2sc, inc (4sc, inc)*5, 2sc (36)
7. (5sc, inc)*6 (42)
8. 3sc, inc (6sc, inc)*5, 3sc (48)
9. 48sc (48)
insert the eyes (5mm) between r12-13, 6stitches apart. sew the mouth as you wish
10. 23sc, white 2sc, pink 23sc (48)
11. 22sc, w 4sc, p 22sc (48)
12. 21sc, w 6sc, p 21sc (48)
13. 20sc, w 8sc, p 20sc (48)
14-15. w 48sc (48)
16. 3sc, dec (6sc, dec)*5, 3sc (42)
17. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
18. 2sc, dec (4sc, dec)*5, 2sc (30)
cut the yarn & fasten off
rosa the bunny head part is finished. now we start making the leg and body.
leg => body pink
1. mr 5sc (5)
2. 5inc (10)
3-4. 10sc (10)
fasten off, cut the yarn. crochet the other leg, make ch 5 and join two legs
5. 10sc + 5sc + 10sc + 5sc (30)
6. 2sc, inc (4sc, inc)*5, 2sc (36)
7. 36sc (36)
8-12. 11sc, w 10sc, p 15sc (36)
13. 2sc, dec, 4sc, dec, 2sc, w 2sc, dec, 4sc, p dec, (4sc, dec)*2, 2sc (30)
14. 10sc, w 7sc, p 13sc (30)
fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing.
let's make the ears and arms of the cute rosa the bunny amigurumi toy.
ear x2 pink
1. mr 6sc (6)
2. 6inc (12)
3-5. 12sc (12)
6. (2sc, dec)*3 (9)
7. 9sc (9)
fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing
arm x2 pink
1. 5sc (5)
2. 5inc (10)
3-6. 10sc (10)
fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing
you have finished the cute little bunny.
