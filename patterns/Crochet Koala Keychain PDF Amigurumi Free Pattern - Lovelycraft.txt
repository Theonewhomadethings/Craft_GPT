crochet koala keychain pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: handmadebylacey
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
sl st slip stitch
ch chain
materials
2 mm crochet hook
6 mm safety eyes
stuffing
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc,inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc,inc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc,inc (30)
rnd 6. 4 sc,inc (36)
rnd 7-12. sc in all
attache safety eyes between row 9-10 with 8st apart.
rnd 13. 4 sc,dec (30)
rnd 14. 3 sc,dec (24)
rnd 15. 2 sc,dec (18)
rnd 16. 1 dec, 10 sc, 1 dec, 3 sc (16)
body
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc,inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc,inc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc,inc (30)
rnd 6-10. sc in all
rnd 11. 4 sc,dec (25)
rnd 12. 3 sc,dec (20)
rnd 13. sc in all
rnd 14. 3 sc,dec (16)
rnd 15-16. sc in all
nose
with black yarn
ch 5, start from second ch from hook
rnd 1. inc, sc 2, 3 sc in last st, 3 sc on other side (10)
rnd 2. sc in all
ears (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc,inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc,inc (24)
rnd 5-8. sc in all
inner ear
with white yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr, ch1 turn
rnd 2. 1 sc in all
arms (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 1-8. sc in all
legs (make 2)
ch 5 and start from second ch from hook
rnd 1. 3sc, 3sc in same st, 2sc, inc
rnd 2. inc, 2sc, 3inc, 2sc, 2inc
rnd 3. 4sc, 3dec, 4sc, dec
rnd 4. 4sc, 2dec, 4sc
rnd 5-10. sc in all
rnd 11. (3sc,dec)*
hair for koala
around the magic ring attach some strings then cut as short as you want.
peach
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 1sc,inc (9)
rnd 3. 2sc,inc (12)
rnd 4. 2sc,inc (16)
rnd 5. 3sc,inc (20)
rnd 6. 3sc,inc (25)
rnd 7-8. sc in all
rnd 9. 3sc,dec (20)
rnd 10. 2sc,dec (15)
stuff then sew the hole closed then don't cut yarn nsert into row 3 then pull tight and fasten off.
leaf
ch 4, sc in all, sl st in last,
ch 4, sc in all, sl st in last,
ch 4, sc in all, sl st in last,
then sew on top of the peach.
your cute koala crochet amigurumi keychain is ready. congratulations!
