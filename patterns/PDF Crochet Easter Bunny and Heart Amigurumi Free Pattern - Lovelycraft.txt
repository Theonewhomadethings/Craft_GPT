pdf crochet easter bunny and heart amigurumi free pattern
designer: rnata6744
materials
2,5 mm hook
cotton yarn (i used cotton baby "soft" from "alize); yellow, blue
safety eyes 6,0 mm
black embroidery thread
stuffing
yarn needle
stitch marker
i used black thread
abbreviations
ch chain
sc single crochet
st/sts stitch/ stitches
inc increase
dec decrease
slst slip stitch
fl front loop
head
use the blue yarn and 2,5 mm hook
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3. (inc, sc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. sc around (24)
rnd 6. (5sc, inc) x 4 (28)
rnd 7. sc around (28)
rnd 8. (inc, 6sc) x 4 (32)
rnd 9. sc around (32)
rnd 10. 2sc, (inc, 7sc) x 3, inc, 5sc (36)
attach the safety eyes between rounds 9 and 10 with 13st between
rnd 11-16. sc around (36), 6 rounds
rnd 17. (dec, 4sc) x 6 (30)
rnd 18. (dec, 3sc) x 6 (24)
start to stuff the head firmly
rnd 19. (dec, 2sc) x 6 (18)
rnd 20. (dec, sc) x 6 (12)
stuff the head firmly. close the opening.
how to close the end of the round: with a darning needle, insert the remaining yarn through all the fl of the stitches on the last round. pull all together at once and sew to hide the yarn inside the part you are working on.
body
use the blue yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3. (inc, sc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. (inc, 3sc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6. 2sc, (inc, 4sc) x 5, inc, 2sc (36)
rnd 7. (inc, 8sc) x 4 (40)
rnd 8-14. sc around (40), 7 rounds
rnd 15. (dec, 8sc) x 4 (36)
rnd 16-17. sc around (36), 2 rounds
rnd 18. (dec, 7sc) x 4 (32)
rnd 19-20. sc around (32), 2 rounds
rnd 21. (dec, 6sc) x 4 (28)
rnd 22-23. sc around (28), 2 rounds
rnd 24. (dec, 5sc) x 4 (24)
rnd 25. sc around (24)
stuff the body firmly. cut the yarn, leave a tail for sewing.
ears (make2)
use the blue yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc) x 3 (9)
rnd 3. sc around (9)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) x 3 (12)
rnd 5. sc around (12)
rnd 6. (3sc, inc) x 3 (15)
rnd 7. sc around (15)
rnd 8. (4sc, inc) x 3 (18)
rnd 9-13. sc around (18), 5 rounds
rnd 14. (dec, 4sc) x 3 (15)
rnd 15. sc around (15)
don't stuff the ears. cut the yarn, leave a tail for sewing.
fold the ear in half and sew two sides together using the needle
arms (make2)
use the blue yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc) x 3 (9)
rnd 3. sc around (9)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) x 3 (12)
rnd 5-6. sc around (12), 2 rounds
rnd 7. (dec, 2sc) x 3 (9)
rnd 8-20. sc around (9), 13 rounds
don't stuff the arms. cut the yarn, leave a tail for sewing.
tail
use the blue yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc) x 3 (9)
rnd 3. (2sc, inc) x 3 (12)
rnd 4. (3sc, inc) x 3 (15)
rnd 5-8. sc around (15), 4 rounds
rnd 9. (dec, sc) x 5 (10)
stuff the tail lightly. close the opening in the same way as the head.
legs (make2)
use the blue yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3. sc around (12)
rnd 4. (3sc, inc) x 3 (15)
rnd 5-7. sc around (15), 3 rounds
rnd 8. (dec, 3sc) x 3 (12)
rnd 9-11. sc around (12), 3 rounds
rnd 12. (dec, 2sc) x 3 (9)
stuff the legs lightly. close the opening in the same way as the head. cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
assembly
sew the ears to the head between rounds 15 and 18
embroider the nose using the black thread
sew the head to the body.
sew the arms to the body in diagonal as show on photo 21,22.
you shound sew the legs to body between rounds 4 and 10 in the end of leg and in the middle part as show on photos 23, 24, 25.
sew the tail to the body to the same rounds as the legs.
as last, sew the arms to the heart
heart
use the yellow yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3. (3sc, inc) x 3 (15)
rnd 4. sc around (15)
make two pieces and don't cut the yarn of the second. we are going to connect two pieces together. insert your hook in the next stitch of first and crochet sc. it will be the first st of new round and new beginning. put your stitch marker here.
with remaining tail of yarn close the hole between the pieces later.
rnd 5. two pieces together: 15sc around first piece, 15sc around second piece (30)
rnd 6. sc around (30)
rnd 7. (dec, 4sc) x 5 (25)
rnd 8. sc around (25)
rnd 9. (dec, 3sc) x 5 (20)
rnd 10. sc around (20)
rnd 11. (dec, 2sc) x 5 (15)
start to stuff the heart
rnd 12. sc around (15)
rnd 13. (dec, sc) x 5 (10)
stuff the heart lightly. close the opening.
lace: ch 60
turn the lace around neck and make a bow.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi easter bunny.
