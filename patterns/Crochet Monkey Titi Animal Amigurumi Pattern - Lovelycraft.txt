crochet monkey titi animal amigurumi pattern
designer: kediperiatolyesi
copyright owner: lovelycraft.com
abbreviations
ch: chain
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
slst: slip stitch
hdc: half double crochet
st/sts: stitch/stitches
dc: double crochet
tr: triple crochet
blo: back loops only
flo: front loops only
bobble: bobble stitch
( ): the parentheses at the end of the row give the total number of stitches of that round.
[ ]x.. : repeat instructions x times
materials
alize cotton gold tweed / beige (color code 262)
alize cotton gold / cream (color code 67)
yarnart jeans/ pink (color code 18)
6mm safety eye (black)
for embroidery: black thread
fiberfill
pins
size 2mm or 2.5mm crochet hook
stitch marker
scissors
legs (with beige color yarn and make 2)
use a different color long yarn piece to mark beginning of round while crocheting legs.
after 9th round foot part will take its shape. then crochet st at its sideways and front section. after that, legs will be completed by crocheting sc in next 12 st between 10-18 rounds.
when first leg is finished, fasten off and crochet second leg. after second leg finished do not fasten off yarn and join two legs with chain. then continue crochet the body part.
start with legs. after crocheting front foot part of leg, at 6th round heel part will be crocheted. lastly, after 9th round is completed rest of the rounds will be crocheted and legs will be completed.
don't forget following instruction images and notes!
join two legs and crochet the body as a single piece.
rnd 1 start 6sc in a magic ring and join with beige sl st (6) please look through "techniques section" to learn about "magic ring" join with a slst. do not forget to use your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3-5 sc in all 12 sts (3 rounds)
rnd 6 ch 1, turn, sc in next 7st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (7)
rnd 7 ch 1, turn, sc in next 5st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (5)
rnd 8 ch 1, turn, sc in next 3st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (3)
rnd 9 ch 1, turn, sc in next 3st, (sideways) sc in next 2st, (in front) sc in next 5st, (sideways) sc in next 2st (12)
rnd 10-18 sc in all 12 sts (9 rounds)
**stuff the leg.
after completing the legs and feet of our amigurumi monkey pattern, we can start knitting the body part.
body (with beige color yarn)
after crochet legs, start crocheting body part. before joining legs make sure legs look toward inside. while joining legs with chain, fold leg at the middle. when crochet up to waist part of body start filling polyester fiberfill. after body is finished fasten off and leave a long tail.
please follow instruction images!
rnd 19 to join two legs; ch 6,join to the middle of the other leg with a sl st and sc in the next 12 sts of the leg, flo sc over the ch 6 stitches,sc in the next 12 sts, and flo sc over the ch 6 sts (36)
rnd 20-21 sc in all 36 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 22 [sc in the next 5 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 23 [sc in the next 6 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 24-27 sc in all 48 sts (4 rounds)
rnd 28 [sc in the next 6 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 29-30 sc in all 42 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 31 [sc in the next 5 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 32 sc in all 36 sts
rnd 33 [sc in the next 4 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 34 sc in all 30 sts
rnd 35 [sc in the next 3 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 36 sc in all 24 sts
rnd 37 [sc in the next 2 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 38-40 sc in all 18 sts (3 rounds)
**stuff the body.
we have completed the body part of our amigurumi pattern, now we can move on to the head part.
head (with beige color yarn)
at head part, when reached the 17th round, start crocheting cheeks. there will be two protrusions at both sides of head. use a different color long yarn piece to mark beginning of round. therefore you will increase and decrease stitches at right location. after 23rd round, insert the safety eyes at this point. to find the center point fold the head at the center from cheek sides so safety eyes can be symmetrically inserted. after finish the head, fill the head with polyester fiberfill and continue with creating head form. please follow instructions images and all notes about crochet steps.
place the safety eyes between 16 and 17 with 8 stitches gap between them.
rnd 1 start 6 sc in a magic ring and join with beige slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4 [sc in the next 2 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5 [sc in the next 3 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 6 [sc in the next 4 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 7 [sc in the next 5 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 8 [sc in the next 6 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 9-16 sc in all 48 sts (8 rounds)
rnd 17 [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times, sc in the next 12 sts, [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times, sc in the next 12 sts (60)
rnd 18-19 sc in all 60 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 20 [sc in the next st, dec] repeat 6 times, sc in the next 12 sts, [sc in the next st, dec] repeat 6 times, sc in the next 12 sts (48)
rnd 21 [sc in the next 6 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 22 [sc in the next 5 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 23 [sc in the next 4 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 24 [sc in the next 3 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 25 [sc in the next 2 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (18)
**stuff the head.
we knitted the head of our amigurumi monkey model, now let's start knitting its mouth with crochet.
mouth (with cream color yarn)
after the mouth making is finished, we cut our rope long. we determine the location with our proof pins, under the two eyes. be careful not to slide the part when sewing. fill the interior with fiber before the end of sewing. then you can sew the cheeks and process your mouth with your black embroidery thread. please do not forget to follow the images and rows!
rnd 1 start 6 sc in a magic ring and join with cream slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 sc in the next 3 sts, [inc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 3 sts, [inc] repeat 3 times (18)
rnd 4 sc in the next 6 sts, [inc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 6 sts, [inc] repeat 3 times (24)
rnd 5 sc in the next 6 sts, [hdcinc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 10 sts, [hdcinc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 2 sts (30)
rnd 6-7 sc in all 30 sts (2 rounds)
after we have knitted the mouth of our amigurumi monkey toy, we can move on to the next step to complete the cheek patches.
rnd 1 start 6 sc in a magic ring and join with pink slst (6) break the yarn leaving a long tail for sewing.
after completing the cheek patches, let's move on to the ears of our amigurumi monkey character.
ears (with beige color yarn and 2 make)
the lower parts of the ears will be sewn to the 17th row and 7 rows from the eyes.
determine their places with rehearsal needles without sewing.
make sure both are symmetrical.
please do not forget to follow the images and rows!
rnd 1 start 6 sc in a magic ring and join with beige slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4 [sc in the next 2 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5-6 sc in all 24 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 7 [sc in the next 2 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 8 sc in all 18 sts
rnd 9 [sc in the next st, dec] repeat 6 times (12)
let's start knitting the arms after the ears of our amigurumi monkey character.
arms (with beige/cream color yarn and 2 make)
fasten off and leave a long tail to sew arms to the body. place arms at shoulder section of body as narrow section of arms are looking towards the outside (shoulder section of body is 3 rounds below from head). please follow up the instruction images.
rnd 1 start 5 sc in a magic ring and join with cream slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (10)
rnd 3-5 sc in all 10 sts (3 rounds)
rnd 6 dec, sc in the next 6st, dec (8)
rnd 7 sc in all 8 sts
rnd 8-23 sc in all 8 sts (16 rounds)
rnd 24 [dec] repeat 4 times (4)
the arms of our amigurumi pattern are now ready, now let's knit the tail and fix it to our character.
tail (with beige color yarn and 2 make)
the tail will be sewn behind the body.
note that you are centering the body before sewing.
please do not forget to follow the images and rows!
with colour beige
rnd 1 start 4 sc in a magic ring and join with beige slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (8)
rnd 3-34 sc in all 8 sts (35 rounds)
rnd 35 8sc fold and combine by sc and cut the string long
we are done with the tail, now we move on to the hat of our amigurumi pattern.
hat (with pink color yarn)
the hat will be knitted as "dc".
you will finish each row with a slst. to get to the top, you need to do ch 2.
in the 8th row, ear cavities will be created.
in the 12th row, it will be made to vote on the edge of the hat. there will be a total of 8 slices.
after the lace production is finished, it will be sewn to the bottom of the hat.
please do not forget to follow the images and rows!
rnd 1 start 10 dc in a magic ring and join with pink slst.
rnd 2 ch 2, dcinc in all sts (20)
rnd 3 ch 2, [dc in the next st, dcinc] repeats 10 times, slst (30 dc)
rnd 4 ch 2, [dc in the next 2 sts, dcinc] repeats 10 times, slst (40 dc)
rnd 5 ch 2, dc in the next 35 sts
rnd 6-7 ch 2, turn, dc in the next 35 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 8 ch 2, turn, dc in the next 5 sts, ch 5 (skip 4st and go into the 5th st), dc in the 8 sts, dcinc, dc in the 8 sts, ch 5 (skip 4st and go into the 5th st), dc in the 5 sts
rnd 9-11 ch 1, turn, sc in the next 36 sts (3 rounds)
rnd 12 to make borders of the hat; ch 2 (at the beginning make ch and do not repeat later), yarn over the hook one time and make dc in 2nd st. in same st make 5 dc. then skip 1 st and sc in 2nd st.
make 5 dc in same st, skip 1 st, sc in 2nd st. .repeat as same order.
rnd 1 ch 71
rnd 2 start in the second chain from the hook, sc in the 70 sts (70)
the hat of our amigurumi monkey character is also ok, now we can move on to the pink crochet dress.
dress (with pink color yarn)
crochet dress separately from body.
rnd 1 ch 29
rnd 2 start in the second chain from the hook, sc in the 28 sts (70)
**preparation of sleeve separation
rnd 3 ch 1, turn, sc in the next 5 sts, ch 8 (skip 4st and go into the 5th st), sc in the next 10 sts, ch 8 (skip 4st and go into the 5th st), sc in the next 5 sts
rnd 4-7 ch 1, turn, sc in the next 36 sts (4 rounds)
rnd 8 ch 2, turn, blo [dcinc] repeat 38 times (72dc)
**crochet body section of dress.
rnd 9-11 ch 2, turn, dc in the next 72 sts (3 rounds)
rnd 12 to make borders of the hat; ch 2 (at the beginning make ch and do not repeat later), yarn over the hook one time and make dc in 2nd st. in same st make 5 dc. then skip 1 st and sc in 2nd st.
make 5 dc in same st, skip 1 st, sc in 2nd st. .repeat as same order.
congratulations, you have completed the monkey titi amigurumi pattern.
