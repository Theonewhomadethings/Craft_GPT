let's start crocheting the billie eilish amigurumi doll.:)
foot / leg
white yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc- mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. 12 sc
rnd 4. 12 sc
rnd 5. 4 sc, 2 dec, 4 sc (10)
rnd 6. 4 sc, 1 dec, 4 sc (9)
change to light skin color yarn
rnd 7-11. 9 sc (5 rows)
change to white yarn
rnd 12-14. 9 sc (3 rows)
first leg. sew the yarn
second leg 15. 9 sc + 2 ch *join the legs* (next stitch is on leg 1) + 9 sc + 2 sc inside the 2 ch next stitch is the beginning of the mark of the rows
*put fiber filler*
after completing the foot and leg of the amigurumi baby, we crochet the body part.
body
rnd 16-18. 22 sc (3 rows)
rnd 19. 5 sc + 1 dec + 9 sc + 1 dec + 4 sc (20)
rnd 20. 4 sc + 1 dec + 8 sc + 1 dec + 4 sc (18)
rnd 21-24. 18 sc (4 rows)
change to light skin color yarn
*put fiber filler*
rnd 25. 1 sc, 1 dec
rnd 26. 6 dec
rnd 27. 6 sc
we finish the body part of the amigurumi doll pattern. now we crochet the sleeves.
arm ( x 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc- mr
rnd 2-12. 6 sc
*dont put fiber filler*
put the wire in one of arms pass it through the body until it reach the other side where the other arm will be, put the wire in the other arm and sew it
head
rnd 1. 6 sc- mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, 1 inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc, 1 inc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, 1 inc (30)
rnd 6. 4 sc, 1 inc (36)
rnd 7. 5 sc, 1 inc (42)
rnd 8-14. 42 sc (7 rows)
put the eyes between row 11 and 12 with a 7 sc distance, and embroider it with white, black and light blue yarn (pic).
embroider the nose like in the picture between rows 10 and 11
rnd 15. 5 sc, 1 inc (36)
rnd 16. 4 sc, 1 inc (30)
rnd 17. 3 sc, 1 inc (24)
rnd 18. 2 sc, 1 inc (18)
rnd 19. 1 sc, 1 inc (12)
rnd 20. 6 dec
sew the yarn
we are going to crochet the hair of the amigurumi billie eilish doll. light yellow yarn is preferred for her hair.
hair
light yellow yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc- mr
rnd 2. 6 inc
rnd 3. 1 sc, 1 inc
rnd 4. 2 sc, 1 inc
8 locks of hair: 1 sc + 21 ch + start on second ch 20 sc + 1 slst
1 locks of hair: 1 sc + 11 ch + start on second ch 10 sc + 1 slst
6 locks of hair: 1 sc + 6 ch + start on second ch 5 sc + 1 slst
1 locks of hair: 1 sc+ 11 ch + start on second ch 10 sc + 1 slst
8 locks of hair: 1 sc + 21 ch + start on second ch 20 sc + 1 slst
sew the yarn
with pins puts the pieces before gluing (photos)
then with craft glue, glue the locks of hair
let's crochet the cardigan of the amigurumi toy. yellow yarn is preferred for cardigans.
cardigan
cream
rnd 1. 31 ch
rnd 2. start on secod ch do 30 sc + 1 ch and turn
rnd 3. blo 30 sc + 1 ch and turn
rnd 4. 30 sc + 1 ch and turn
rnd 5. 5 sc + 5 ch skip 5 sc and do 10 sc + 5 ch skip 5 sc and do again 5 sc + 1 ch and turn
rnd 6-15. 30 sc + 1 ch and turn
rnd 16. 30 sc
+3 sc inside the same stitch nor contour all the cardigan with sc and do slst when you reach the end (photo)
slevees
rnd 1. connect the yarn in the sleeve space and do 12 sc
rnd 2-5. 12 sc (4 rows)
assembly
glue the hair, apply the blush on the cheeks and drees the cardigan on billie.
