designer
instagram: yammi_art
materials
yarn "hello" 100%-cotton (125m/50g)
hook 2,5 mm
black thread for embroidery of the eyes
a little bit pink yarn for the nose
holofiber
scissors, needles, tailor's pins
abbreviations
ar amigurumi magic ring
mr amigurumi magic ring
app application
ll lift loop
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
sl-st : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
bl : bobble stitch do 5dc all in the same stitch and then yarn over and pull through all the loops on your hook (the original loop + 1 loop for each partially completed dc).
ears (2 times)
1 rnd: 6 sc in mr
2 rnd: (1 sc, inc)x3 (9)
3 rnd: (2 sc, inc)x3 (12)
4-18 rnds: 12 sc (15 rounds)
fold the edges together, then in half, and sew the edges. fasten off, leave the tip for sewing.
head
1 rnd: 6 sc in mr
2 rnd: (1 sc, inc)x3 (9)
3 rnd: incx9 (18)
4 rnd: (2 sc, inc)x6 (24)
5 rnd: (3 sc, inc)x6 (30)
6 rnd: (4 sc, inc)x6 (36)
7 rnd: (5 sc, inc)x6 (42)
8 rnd: (6 sc, inc)x6 (48)
9-16 rnds: 48 sc (8 rounds)
17 rnd: (6 sc, dec)x6 (42)
18 rnd: (5 sc, dec)x6 (36)
19 rnd: (4 sc, dec)x6 (30)
20 rnd: (3 sc, dec)x6 (24)
21 rnd: (2 sc, dec)x6 (18)
stuff with holofiber. fasten off, leave the tip for sewing.
hands (2 times.) (don't stuff)
start with gray yarn:
1 rnd. 6 sc in mr
2 rnd. (1 sc, inc)x3 (9)
3-4 rnds. 9 sc (2 rounds)
change the color to the color of the pajamas:
5-14 rnds. 9 sc (10 rounds)
fold the edges together and crochet 4 sc for both sides of the hand, fasten off, leave the tip for sewing.
legs (stuff during the crocheting process)
start with a gray yarn:
1 rnd. 6 sc in mr
2 rnd. incx6 (12)
3-5 rnds. 12 sc (3 rows)
change the color to the color of the pajamas:
6-12 rnds. 12 sc (7 rounds)
cut the thread at the first leg, fasten it. we crochet the second leg in the same way, but we do not cut the thread, do ch 3 and attach the first leg.
body
1 rnd. 12 sc around the first leg, 3 sc along the chain, 12 sc around the second leg, 3 sc along the chain (from the other side) (30)
2 rnd. (4 sc, inc)x6 (36)
3 rnd. (5 sc, inc)x6 (42)
4-12 rnd.42 sc (9 rounds)
13 rnd. (5 sc, dec)x6 (36)
14 rnd. 36 sc
15 rnd. (4 sc, dec)x6 (30)
16 rnd. 30 sc
17 rnd. (3 sc, dec)x6 (24)
change the color to gray:
18 rnd. 24 sc
19 rnd. (2 sc, dec)x6 (18)
stuff the body with holofiber. fasten of hide the tip inside.
assembling
sew hands on the sides of the body (at the 17th row), sew the ears to the head near the crown, embroider eyes (at the level of 12 rows) and a nose (at the level of 14-15 rows). sew the head to the body.
congratulations! our bunny is ready!
