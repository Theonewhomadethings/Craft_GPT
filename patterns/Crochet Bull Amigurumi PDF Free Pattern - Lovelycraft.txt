crochet bull amigurumi pdf free pattern
designer: crochet__friend
materials
main color yarn (yarnart jeans color 07)
cream yarn (yarnart jeans color 05)
brown yarn (yarnart jeans color 40)
yarn of any color for panties
hook 1.75 or 2 mm
eyes on safe mount 6 mm
black and white thin thread for muzzle decoration
holofiber or any other filler
scissors and needle
abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
tch turning chain stitch
tbl through the back loop
head
yarn of the main color. we stuff in the course of crochet.
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr
rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8: 42 sc
in the next row, make holes for safe peephole (if you use sew-on or glue eyes, then knit 42 sc without changes).
rnd 9: 6 sc, 1 ch, skip 1 loop, 4sc, 1 ch, skip 1 loop, 30 sc (42)
rnd 10: 42 sc
rnd 11: 6 inc, 6 sc, 6 inc, 24 sc (54)
rnd 12-16: 54 sc (5 rnds)
at this stage, you can insert the eyes.
rnd 17: (2 sc, dec)*3, 6 sc, (2 sc, dec)*3, 24 sc (48)
rnd 18: (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 19: (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 20: (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
cut the thread and fasten off. leave a long tip for sewing.
ears (2 pcs)
yarn of the main color.
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr
rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
cut the thread and fasten off.
leave long tail for sewing.
fold the ear in half again and sew 2 loops on both edges.
muzzle
cream yarn:
5 tch, crochet in the second from the hook.
rnd 1: 3 sc, 3 sc in one loop, turn and crochet on the second half of the chain 2 sc, 2 sc in one loop (10)
rnd 2: 1 inc, 2 sc, 3 inc, 2 sc, 2 inc (16)
rnd 3: (3 sc, inc)*4 (20)
rnd 4: 20 sc
cut the thread and fasten off. leave a long end for sewing.
horns (2 pcs)
brown yarn.
rnd 1: 4 sc in mr
rnd 2: 4 sc
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc)*2 (6)
rnd 4: 6 sc
rnd 5: (2 sc, inc)*2 (8)
cut the thread and fasten off. leave long tail for sewing.
arms (2 pcs)
brown yarn.
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr
rnd 2: (1 sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: 9 sc
change the yarn to the main color.
rnd 4-13: 9 sc (10 rnds)
fold in half and crochet 4 sc for both walls. cut the thread.
sew the muzzle right under the eyes.
slightly stuffing in the process.
then sew on the horns, stepping back from the beginning of knitting 4 rows.
slightly bend the tips of the horns inward.
sew ears directly under the horns.
with a thin white thread, embroider the whites of the eyes.
in the 6th row, embroider eyebrows with black thread.
embroider the nostrils on the muzzle with the same thread. (refer to photo)
legs & body
we stuff in the course of crocheting.
brown yarn.
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr
rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: 12 sc
change color to main.
rnd 4: 12 sc
change the yarn to the color of the pants.
rnd 5-7: 12 sc (3 rnds)
cut the thread at one leg.
tie the second, but do not cut the thread.
crocheting 2 more sc on the second leg so that when the first leg is attached, the color transition step remains on the inside.
make 4 ch and attach one leg to the other.
note:
now the beginning of the row will be on the back!
rnd 8: 12 sc on one leg, 4 sc on a chain of ch, 12 sc on the other leg, 4 sc on a chain of ch (32)
rnd 9: (7 sc, inc)*4 (36)
rnd 10: (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 11: (6 sc, inc)*6 (48)
rnd 12-19: 48 sc (8 rnds)
change the yarn to the main color.
rnd 20: 3 tbl, 48 sc
rnd 21: (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 22: (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 23-25: 36 sc (3 rnds)
rnd 26: (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
in the next row, crocheting handles.
rnd 27: 4 sc, 4 sc crocheting, catching the loops of the arm and body, 10 sc, 4 sc with a handle, 8 sc (30)
cut the thread and fasten off.
sew the head to the body.
now let's make the pants.
in the 20th row of the body, mark 8 half loops in the middle.
attach the thread of the color of the panties and crochet 8 sc for half loops of the 20th row.
next, crochet in turning rows another 3 rows of 8 sc, making 1 ch of lifting at the beginning.
now we will tie the edge of the resulting part, as if going down to the beginning of the row (see photo).
tie the pants along the remaining half loops of the 20th row.
tie the edge of the part, now going up.
without cutting the thread, make a strap from ch.
i have got 20 ch. your quantity may vary.
the length of the strap should be enough to throw back and sew to the top of the pants.
i have them criss-cross.
attach thread to the other side of the panties and make a second strap.
sew them to the top of the pants.
tail
at the back of the bull, attach the thread of the main color and crochet a chain of 7 ch.
cut the thread and fasten.
make 3 identical pieces of brown yarn, fold them in half and thread through the last ch of the tail.
pass the ends through the resulting loop and tighten.
cut to fit long.
