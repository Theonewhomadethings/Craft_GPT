designer
instagram: tu.broche.original

abbreviations
ch chain
mr magic
sc single crochet
inc increase in single crochet
dec decrease
slst slip stitch
blo back loop only
flo front loop only
skill level: easy level
head
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc) x6 (18)
rnd 4. 1sc, inc, (2sc, inc) x5, 1sc (24)
rnd 5. 3sc, inc) x6 (30)
rnd 6. 2sc, inc, (4sc, inc)x5, 2sc (36)
rnd 7. (5sc, inc) x6 (42)
rnd 8. 42 sc
rnd 9. (6sc, inc)x6, (48)
rnd 10-13. 48 sc
rnd 14. 12sc, 8 ch, 28sc (leave a total of 8sc from the previous row without crocheting, start crocheting at the next stitch)
rnd 15-17. 48sc
rnd 18. 3sc, dec, (6sc, dec)x5, 3sc (42)
rnd 19. (5sc, dec) x6 (36)
rnd 20. 2sc, dec, (4sc, dec)x5, 2sc (30)
rnd 21. (3sc, dec) x6 (24)
rnd 22. 1sc, dec, (2sc, dec)x5, 1sc (18)
safety eyes between round 12 and 13 with 12 sc of separation between eyes.
trunk
rnd 1. in the 16sc that we leave the hole, we do 18sc (make an extra sc on each side of the trunk so that a hole is not visible)
rnd 2. 18 sc
rnd 3. (7sc, dec) x2 (16)
rnd 4-5. 16sc
rnd 6. (6sc, dec) x2 (14)
rnd 7-8. 14sc
rnd 9. (5sc, dec) x2 (12)
rnd 10-11. 12sc
rnd 12. (4sc, dec) x2 (10)
rnd 13. 10sc
fasten off
so that the trunk stays up, i have put a pipe cleaner so that the trunk can be shaped.
body
rnd 23. 1sc, inc, (2sc, inc)x5, 1sc (24)
rnd 24. (3sc, inc) x6 (30)
rnd 25. 30sc
rnd 26. 2sc, inc, (4sc, inc)x5, 2sc (36)
rnd 27-30. 36 sc
rnd 31. 2sc, dec, (4sc, dec)x5, 2sc (30)
rnd 32. (3sc, dec) x6 (24)
rnd 33. 1sc, dec, (2sc, dec)x5, 1sc (18)
rnd 34. (1sc, dec) x6 (12)
rnd 35. 6 dec (6)
fill and close.
ears (make 2)
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc)x 6 (18)
rnd 4. 1sc, inc (2sc, inc)x 5, 1sc (24)
rnd 5. (3sc, inc)x 6 (30)
rnd 6-8. 30sc
rnd 9. 2sc, inc (4sc, inc) x5, 2sc (36)
we fold the ear in half and close with 18sc
legs (make 2)
rnd 1. 5sc in mr (5)
rnd 2. 5 inc (10)
rnd 3. (4sc, inc)x 2 (12)
rnd 4. 12 sc blo
rnd 5-7. 12sc
rnd 8. (4sc, dec)x 2 (10)
rnd 9-11. 10 sc
tail
make 11 ch, 10 slst leave long thread to sew.
fangs (make 2)
rnd 1. 5sc in mr
rnd 2-4. 5sc
leave a long thread to sew.
assembling
ears: count 6 stitches and put the needle to bend the ear a little. we will place the ears between round 5 and 6 of the head and finish between round 17 and 18.
hind legs: between turns 30 and 31 of the body, at the level of the ears.
front legs: between turns 22 and 23, at eye level.
tail: between lap 31 and 32 of the body from the back, right in the center.
fangs: just below the trunk.
congratulations, you've completed the cute crochet elephant pattern, regards!
