crochet miss gnome amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: gewkat58
materials
fingering weight yarn in green, red, white, a colour for the skin, gold and orange. for orange (hair) you can also use acrylic or wool.
any hook size to match your yarn
stitch marker to mark the beginning of the rounds
stuffing material
scissors
tapestry needle
abbreviations
mr magic ring
st(s) stitch(es)
ch chain
sl st slip stitch
blsc back loop single crochet
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
fo fasten off
the pattern is worked in a continuous spiral unless otherwise noted.
you need to know how to make the crab stitch (reverse single crochet).
body
(with red yarn)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8-13: 42sc (42)
rnd 14: 42 blsc (42)
rnd 15-19: 42sc (42)
rnd 20: (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 21: 36sc (36)
rnd 22: (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 23: 30sc (30)
rnd 24: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 25: 24sc (24)
start stuffing
rnd 26: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 27: 18sc (18)
rnd 28: (1sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 29: 12sc (12)
complete stuffing
rnd 30: (dec)*6 (6)
fo leaving a long tail for sewing. sew through the front loops only and pull tight to close the hole. weave in the ends of the yarn.
continue with the dress by attaching red yarn in any st of the front loops of round 13 of the body. hold the doll upside down.
round 1 of dress: 3 chains (count as a st) and 1 dc in one st, 2 dc in each st around (84sts); sl st in 3rd ch to join the round.
round 2-4 of dress: 3 chains (count as a st), 83 dc (84sts); sl st in 3rd ch to join the round.
change to golden yarn
round 5 of dress: crab stitch x84 (84sts).
make a sl st and fo. weave in the ends.
feet (make 2)
(with green yarn)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3-6: 9sc (9); stuff lightly only the bottom part of the foot.
fo leaving a long tail for sewing. press the hole flat and sew both parts together. don't cut the rest of the yarn.
hat
(with green yarn)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-7: 6sc (6)
rnd 8: (2sc,inc)*2 (8)
rnd 9-12: 8sc (8)
rnd 13: (3sc,inc)*2 (10)
rnd 14-17: 10sc (10)
rnd 18: (4sc,inc)*2 (12)
rnd 19-20: 12sc (12)
rnd 21: (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
rnd 22: 15sc (15)
rnd 23: (4sc,inc)*3 (18)
rnd 24: 18sc (18)
rnd 25: (5sc,inc)*3 (21)
rnd 26: 21sc (21)
rnd 27: (6sc,inc)*3 (24)
rnd 28: 24sc (24)
rnd 29: (7sc,inc)*3 (27)
rnd 30: (8sc,inc)*3 (30)
rnd 31: (9sc,inc)*3 (33)
rnd 32: (10sc,inc)*3 (36)
rnd 33: (11sc,inc)*3 (39)
rnd 34: (12sc,inc)*3 (42)
rnd 35: (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 36: (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 37: (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 38: 60sc (60)
change to golden yarn
rnd 39: crab stitch x60 (60); make a sl st and fo. weave in the ends.
optional: decorate the hat with golden yarn.
nose
(with skin colour yarn)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: 12sc (12)
rnd 4: (sc,dec)*4 (8)
stuff the nose.
fo leaving a long tail. sew through the front loops only and pull tight to close the hole. don't cut the rest of yarn.
left hand
(with skin colour yarn)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: 9sc (9)
rnd 4: 3 hdc in one st, 8sc (11)
rnd 5: 2dec,7sc (9)
stuff a little bit, change to red colour and continue with the arm;
arm
rnd 6: 9blsc (9)
rnd 7-10: 9sc (9)
fo leaving a long tail of yarn.
right hand
(with skin colour yarn)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: 9sc (9)
rnd 4: 8sc,3hdc in one st (11)
rnd 5: 7sc,2dec (9)
stuff a little bit, change to red colour and continue with the arm.
apron
with white yarn. the apron will be worked in rows.
row 1: 3ch (count as a st), 7dc in mr (8), turn.
row 2: 3ch and 1dc in same stitch, (2dc in next stitch)*7 (16), turn.
row 3: 3ch, 2dc in next stitch, (1dc, 2dc in next stitch)*7 (24), turn.
row 4: (3ch, skip one st, sl st)*11, 3ch, slst in 3rd ch.
fo and weave in the ends.
with white yarn chain 25. attach the chain to the beginning of the upper (straight)
part of the apron and make 2sc in each dc. continue by making 25ch.
fo and weave in the ends.
optional: decorate the apron with golden yarn.
hair
cut the orange yarn in 10 pcs about 30 cm each. fold each one in half and with a hook pass them, between rounds 19-20, through the 5th st left of the nose and make a slip knot.
repeat 4 more times in the 6th, 7th, 8th and 9th st.
repeat on the right side of the nose.
tie them with a bow each and cut the hair in the desired length. you can also braid the hair if you like.
assembly
sew the feet onto the body on round 3 (to 5) leaving 3 sts apart.
sew the nose between rounds 18-20.
sew the arms between rounds 19-20 right beside the hair.
sew the hat onto the body.
