abbreviations
ch: chain
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
hdc: half double crochet
dc: double crochet
tr: treble /triple stitch
hdcinc: half double crochet increase
hdcdec: half double crochet decrease
dcinc: double crochet increase
dcdec: double crochet decrease
slst: slip stitch
flo: use the front loops only
blo: use the back loops only
bobble: bobble stitch
(..): total number of stitches after the round / row.
[] x . times: repeat instructions in parentheses x times
finished toy is around 25 cm height.
materials
yarnart jeans/ skin (color code 73)
yarnart jeans/ cream (color code 03)
yarnart jeans/ red (color code 66)
yarnart jeans/ light grey (color code 49)
yarnart jeans/ dark grey (color code 46)
yarnart jeans/ yellow (color code 35)
yarnart jeans/ black (color code 53)
size 2.5mm crochet hook
12mm safety eye (black)
black wire glasses (8cm)
electrical wire 1,5mm (10cm)
for embroidery: black thread
fiberfill
pins
stitch marker
legs
(with black color yarn)
rnd 1 chain 6
rnd 2 starting from the second chain; 4 sc, 4 sc into one stitch, (continue with other side of the chain), 3 sc, 1 inc (13)
rnd 3 [1 inc], 3 sc, [1 inc] repeats 4 times, 3 sc, [1 inc] repeat 2 times (20)
rnd 4 [1 sc, 1 inc], 3 sc, [1 inc], 3 sc, [1 sc, 1 inc] repeat 4 times, 3 sc, [1 sc, 1 inc] repeat 2 times (27)
rnd 5 blo 27 sc
rnd 6-7 sc in all 27 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 8 sc in the next 6 st, (dec) repeat 7 times, sc in the next 7 st (20)
rnd 9 sc in the next 5 st, (dec) repeat 5 times, sc in the next 5 st (15)
rnd 10 (3 sc, 1 dec) repeat 3 times (12)
change to skin color.
**stuff the legs.
rnd 11 blo 12 sc
rnd 12-15 sc in all 12 sts (5 rounds)
change to dark grey color.
**stuff the legs.
rnd 16 12 sc
rnd 17 blo 12 sc
rnd 18-26 sc in all 12 sts (9 rounds)
shoes
(with black color yarn)
on the blo stitches of legs in rnd 11;
rnd 1 ch 1, 12 sc, slst (12)
trouser leg
(with dark grey color yarn)
on the blo stitches of legs in rnd 17;
rnd 1 ch 1, (1 sc, 1 dec) repeat 6 times, slst (18)
rnd 2-4 sc in all 18 sts (3 rounds)
body
continue with dark grey color
rnd 27 to join two legs; 2 sc along the left leg, ch 6, join to the middle of the right leg with a slst and 12 sc of the leg, flo sc over the ch 6 stitches, 10 sc (36)
rnd 28-35 sc in all 36 sts (8 rounds)
change to light grey color.
rnd 36 blo 36 sc
rnd 37-40 sc in all 36 sts (4 rounds)
rnd 41 (4 sc, 1 dec) repeat 6 times (30)
**stuff the body.
rnd 42-43 sc in all 30 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 44 (3 sc, 1 dec) repeat 6 times (24)
change to cream color.
rnd 45 blo 24 sc
rnd 46 24 sc
change to skin color.
rnd 47 blo 24 sc
rnd 48 (2 sc, 1 dec) repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 49-50 sc in all 18 sts (2 rounds)
**stuff the body.
**a electrical wire will be inserted to keep the neck straight
belt
(with dark grey color yarn)
on the blo stitches of body in rnd 36;
rnd 1 ch 1, 36 slst (36)
sweater collar
(with light grey color yarn)
on the blo stitches of body in rnd 45;
rnd 1 ch 1, 24 slst (24)
shirt collar
(with cream color yarn)
on the blo stitches of body in rnd 47;
rnd 1 ch 2, (1sc, 1 inc) repeat 7 times, (1 hdc, 1 dc, 1 tr into one stitch), ch 1,
(1 tr, 1 dc, 1 hdc into one stitch), 3 slst, (1 hdc, 1 dc, 1 tr into one stitch), ch 1,
(1 tr, 1 dc, 1 hdc into one stitch), 1 sc, 1 inc, 1 sc
**when the body process is finished, embroider with red and yellow thread as in the images.
arm
(with skin color yarn) make 8
rnd 1 5 sc in the magic ring (5)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (10)
rnd 3-5 10 sc (2 rounds)
rnd 6 (1 dec, 3 sc) repeats 2 times (8)
rnd 7-8 8 sc (2 rounds)
change to cream color.
rnd 9-21 8 sc (13 rounds)
rnd 22 (1 inc) repeats 4 times (4)
head
(with skin color yarn)
rnd 1 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 (1 sc,1 inc) repeats 6 times (18)
rnd 4 (1 sc, 1 inc, 1 sc) repeats 6 times (24)
rnd 5 (3 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (30)
rnd 6 (2 sc, 1 inc, 2 sc) repeats 6 times (36)
rnd 7 (5 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (42)
rnd 8 (3 sc, 1 inc, 3 sc) repeats 6 times (48)
rnd 9 (7 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (54)
rnd 10 (4 sc, 1 inc, 4 sc) repeats 6 times (60)
rnd 11 (9 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (66)
rnd 12-18 66 sc (7 rounds)
rnd 19 27 sc, ch 1(skip 1 st and go in 2rd st), 10 sc, ch 1(skip 1 st and go in 2rd st), 27 sc (64)
rnd 20-23 66 sc (4 rounds)
rnd 24 (9 sc,1 dec) repeats 6 times (60)
rnd 25 (8 sc,1 dec) repeats 6 times (54)
rnd 26 (7 sc,1 dec) repeats 6 times (48)
rnd 27 (1 dec) repeats 24 times (24)
rnd 28 (2 sc,1 dec) repeats 6 times (18)
** attach safety eyes to ch1 in the 19th row where we did it.
**stuff the head
magician's robe
(with black color yarn)
row 1 ch 29
row 2 starting from the 2nd stitch from hook crochet 5 sc, ch 7, (skip 4st and go into the 5th st), 10 sc, ch 7, (skip 4st and go into the 5th st), 5 sc
row 3 ch 1, turn, 5 sc, flo sc over the ch 7 stitches, 10 sc, flo sc over the ch 7 stitches, 5 sc (34)
row 4 ch 1, turn, 2 sc, 1 inc, 10 sc, 1 inc, 6 sc, 1 inc, 10 sc, 1 inc, 2 sc (38)
row 5-6 ch 1, turn, 38 sc (2 rows)
row 7 ch 1, turn, 2 sc, 1 inc, 12 sc, 1 inc, 6 sc, 1 inc, 12 sc, 1 inc, 2 sc (42)
row 8-30 ch 1, turn, 42 sc (23 rows)
magician robe sleeve
(with black color yarn)
**we tie our rope to the magician's robe body
rnd 1 ch1, 14sc
rnd 2-14 14 sc (13 rounds)
magician robe collar
(with black color yarn)
rnd 1 ch1, 28sc
hair
(with black color yarn)
rnd 1 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 (1 sc,1 inc) repeats 6 times (18)
rnd 4 (1 sc, 1 inc, 1 sc) repeats 6 times (24)
rnd 5 (3 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (30)
rnd 6 (2 sc, 1 inc, 2 sc) repeats 6 times (36)
rnd 7 (5 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (42)
rnd 8 (3 sc, 1 inc, 3 sc) repeats 6 times (48)
rnd 9 (7 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (54)
rnd 10 (4 sc, 1 inc, 4 sc) repeats 6 times (60)
rnd 11 (9 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (66)
rnd 12-15 66 sc (4 rounds)
rnd 16 (ch 7, turn, crochet on chain from the 2nd stitch: 6 sc; 2 sc) repeats 13 times,
(ch 13, turn, crochet on chain from the 2nd stitch: 12 sc; 2 sc) repeats 21 times
** sew the wig on the head after finishing the curls.
pin the bottoms of curls as you can see in the pictures below.
pin and sew as you enjoy them
scarf
(with red color yarn)
row 1 ch 7
row 2 starting from the 3nd stitch from hook crochet 5 hdc
row 3 ch 2, turn 5 hdc
change to yellow color.
**crochet two rows of colors and switch
row 4-55 ch 2, turn 5 dc (52 rows)
