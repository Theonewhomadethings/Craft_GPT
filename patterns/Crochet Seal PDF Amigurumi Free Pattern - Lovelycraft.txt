abbreviations
rnd round
mr magic ring
ch chain
sl st slip stitch
sc single crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
()* repeat till the end of round
materials
cotton ricorumi: light grey (003), dark grey (080), yellow (071)
crochet hook: 3mm and 2.5mm
6 mm safety eyes, you may need use smaller/ larger eyes, depending on hook/ yarn size.
yarn needle
black embroidery thread
fiber fill for stuffing
scissors
size with the materials above will be about 9 cm.
head & body
stuff as you go along your work.
in light grey.
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (6)
rnd 2. (inc)* (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 7-9. 36 sc (36)
rnd 10. (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 11-14. 42 sc (42)
insert the security eyes between rnd 10 and 11.
rnd 15. (5sc,dec)* (36)
rnd 16. (4sc,dec)* (30)
rnd 17. (3sc,dec)* (24)
rnd 18. (2sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 19. (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 20. 24 sc (24)
rnd 21. (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 22-24. 30 sc (30)
rnd 25. (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 26-29. 36 sc (36)
rnd 30. (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 31. 42 sc (42)
rnd 32. through back loops only (5sc,dec)* (36)
finish to stuff.
rnd 33. (2sc,dec)* (27)
rnd 34. (1sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 35. (dec)* (9)
rnd 36. (1sc,dec)* (6)
fasten off and close.
embroider the cheeks in pink embroidery thread, and nose, eyebrows and whiskers with black one.
muzzle
in dark grey.
rnd 1. mr, 4sc (4)
rnd 2. (inc)* (8)
sew the muzzle between the eyes.
flippers (make 2)
in light grey.
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)* (9)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)* (12)
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)* (15)
rnd 5-7. 15 sc (15)
rnd 8. (3sc,dec)* (12)
rnd 9-10. 12 sc (12)
rnd 11. (2sc,dec)* (9)
flatten the opening of the foot and sc in next 4 stitches through both layers to close the opening. fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing. sew the flippers in the side of the body.
tail
little flippers (x2)
rnd 1. mr, 4sc (4)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)* (6)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)* (9)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* (12)
rnd 5. 12 sc (12)
rnd 6. (3sc,inc)* (15)
rnd 7. (3sc,dec)* (12)
rnd 8. (2sc,dec)* (9)
rnd 9. (1sc,dec)* (6)
don't cut the second flipper.
now join the little flippers by crocheting 1 sc in the first one (this sc will be the first sc of the next round).
rnd 10. 1 sc (join), 11 sc (12)
rnd 11. 3 sc, inc, 5 sc, inc, 2 sc (14)
rnd 12. 1 sc, dec, 6 sc, dec, 3 sc (12)
rnd 13. 12 sc (12)
rnd 14. (1sc,inc)* (18)
rnd 15. (2sc,inc)* (24)
fasten off and leave a long tail.
sew the tail in the back of magnum the seal.
