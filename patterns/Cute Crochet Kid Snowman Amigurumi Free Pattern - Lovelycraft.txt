cute kid crochet snowman amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: polinkowyzakatek
materials
yarn (yarnart jeans)
crochet hook 2,5 mm
safety eyes 3 mm
2 black buttons
soft stuffing
needle for sewing
scissors
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
ch chain
inc increase
dec decrease
() repeat until the end of the round
let's start the construction of the cute crochet snowman from the head first.
head
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8: (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9-15: 48 sc
rnd 16: (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 17: 42 sc
rnd 18: (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 19: 36 sc
rnd 20: (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 21: 30 sc
rnd 22: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 23: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 24: (1sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 25: (dec)*6 (6)
close the opening.
place the safety eyes between rnd 12 and 13. leaving 10 stitches between them.
after completing the head part of the crochet snowman amigurumi character, let's crochet the carrot nose.
carrot nose
rnd 1: 3sc in mr (3)
rnd 2: (inc)*3 (6)
rnd 3: 6 sc
after making the snowman's carrot-looking nose, let's complete his arms.
arms (make 2)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3-15: 9 sc
fold the opening and crochet both sides together.
we have completed the arms of the snowman, next are the legs, let's crochet them one by one.
legs (make 2)
legs 1
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: 18 sc
rnd 5: 3dec, 12sc (15)
rnd 6: 15 sc
leg 2
repeat rnd 1-6 but not cut the yarn. make 5 chain stitches and join both legs.
after crocheting the legs, we can start making the snowman's body.
body
rnd 1: 15sc (around 1 leg)
+ 5 sc in the chains
+ 15 sc (around 2 leg)
+ 5 sc in the chains- (40)
rnd 2-5: 40 sc
rnd 6: (7sc,inc)*5 (45)
rnd 7-12: 45 sc
rnd 13: (7sc,dec)*5 (40)
rnd 14: 40 sc
rnd 15: (6sc,dec)*5 (35)
rnd 16: 35 sc
rnd 17: (4sc,dec)*5 (30)
rnd 18: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 19: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 20-21: 18 sc
fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing. stuff the body.
you've completely finished the snowman, congratulations. now let's crochet the accessories. we can achieve a more cute look with a hat and scarf.
you can dress the snowman as you like.
my snowman is dressed in a hat and scarf made of scheepjes our tribe yarn.
*needles 2,5 mm
hat
cast on 52 stitches
rnd 1: 52 knit
rnd 2: 52 purl
repeat rows 30 times
bind- off all stitches
scarf
cast on 6 stitches
rnd 1: 6 knit
repeat rows 80 times.
