little and easy hippo amigurumi free crochet pattern
designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
etsy: beary_bearnita_
ravelry: beary_bearnita_
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2.5 mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5 mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
leg*2
rnd 1: 7 sc in mr (7)
rnd 2: 7-inc (14)
rnd 3-4: (4 rnds) 14 sc (14)
make the 2nd leg in the same way. don't cut the yarn, make 4 chains, join in 1st leg. continue to the body.
we crocheted the legs. body is next. now let's crochet the body of this cute hippo amigurumi.
body
rnd 1: (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 2: (6 sc, inc)*6 (48)
rnd 3-11: (9 rnds) 48 sc (48)
change to green
rnd 12: (10 sc, dec)*4 (44)
rnd 13-15: (3 rnds) 44 sc (44)
rnd 16: (9 sc, dec)*4 (40)
rnd 17: 40 sc (40)
change to grey,
rnd 18: (8 sc, dec)*4 (36)
rnd 19: 9 sc, inc, (3 sc, inc)*4, 10 sc (41)
rnd 20: 10 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*4, 10 sc (46)
rnd 21: 46 sc (46)
rnd 22: 17 sc, inc, (5 sc, inc)*2, 16 sc (49)
rnd 23-24: (2 rnds) 49 sc (49)
rnd 25: 17 sc, dec, (5 sc, dec)*2, 16 sc (46)
rnd 26: 46 sc (46)
rnd 27: 11 sc, dec, (4 sc, dec)*4, 9 sc (41)
rnd 28: 16 sc, dec, 8 sc, dec, 13 sc (39)
insert the eyes at round 28,29. 6 sc apart.
rnd 29-32: (4 rnds) 39 sc (39)
rnd 33: (11 sc, dec)*3 (36)
rnd 34-35: (2 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 36: (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 37: (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 38: (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 39: (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 40: 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end
it takes some time to crochet the body. you can get some rest. then let's crochet the arms of this cute hippo.
arm*2
rnd 1: 5 sc in mr (5)
rnd 2: 5-inc (10)
rnd 3-5: (3 rnds) 10 sc (10)
change to green
rnd 6-12: (7 rnds) 10 sc (10)
slst. cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
after crocheting the hippo's arms, we will finally crochet its ears.
ear*2
rnd 1: 5 sc in mr (5)
rnd 2: 5 sc (5)
rnd 3: 4 sc, slst in last stitch
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
little hippo is ready.
