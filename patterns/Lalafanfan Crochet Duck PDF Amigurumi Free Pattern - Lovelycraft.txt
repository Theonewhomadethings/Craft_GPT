lalafanfan crochet duck pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: yuliya_knitting11
materials
yarn art dolce main (761) 1 skein, cheeks (759), beak (778)
yarn art suit jeans(75)
1 skein, white for the bag
eyes 10mm
filler
cotton yarn for sewing details.
crochet hook no: 4,5 mm
abbreviations
mr: magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
tr: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (4sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6sc, inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9-14. 48 sc (6 rows)
rnd 15. (6sc, dec)* 6 (42)
rnd 16. (5sc, dec)* 6 (36)
rnd 17. (4sc, dec)* 6 (30)
rnd 18. (3sc, dec)* 6 (24)
rnd 19. (2sc, dec)* 6 (18)
rnd 20. (1sc, dec)* 6 (12)
rnd 21. (dec)*6 (6)
cut the thread, pull off the loops, fasten and hide.
arms (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
rnd 4-6. 15 sc (3 rows)
rnd 7. (3sc,dec)*3 (12)
fill the arm, fold in half and crochet both sides of the 6 sc together.
legs and body
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (inc)* 6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc) * 6 (18)
rnd 4-7. 18sc (4 rows)
cut the thread, fasten. we also crochet the second one, but we don't cut the thread anymore.
we connect the legs.
rnd 8. immediately connect with the first leg, 18sc on the first, 18sc on the second (36)
rnd 9-14. 36 sc (6 rows)
rnd 15. (10sc, dec)* 3 (30)
rnd 16. (9sc, dec)* 3 (30)
rnd 17. (8sc, dec)* 3 (27)
rnd 18. (7sc, dec)* 3 (24)
rnd 19-20. 24sc
cut the thread and fasten.
cheeks
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. 18 sc
beak
top part.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)*6 (12)
lower
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
connect the two parts and sew together from the inside sides of the 6 sc.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 sc
hat
we crochet with double crochet.
rnd 1. 12 dc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*12 (24)
rnd 3. (inc,1dc)*12 (36)
rnd 4. (inc,2dc)*12 (48)
rnd 5. (inc,3dc)*12 (60)
rnd 6. (inc,4dc)*12 (72)
rnd 7-10. 72 dc (4 rows)
rnd 11. in each loop we crochet inc from dc
rnd 12. (inc, 1dc) * alternate to the end.
ears (make 2)
rnd 1. 8 dc in mr
rnd 2. turn crocheting 2ch, inc * 8 (16)
the dress
rnd 1. 49ch, from the second loop from the hook 48sc
rnd 2. turn crochet 3 ch lifting, 7 dc, dial 10 ch and skip 10 sc, 14 dc, dial 10 ch and skip 10sc, 7 dc (48)
rnd 3. turn crochet ch, (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 4. turn 3ch, 54 dc
rnd 5. turn ch, (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 6. turn 3ch, 60 dc
rnd 7. turn ch, (9sc,inc)*6 (66)
rnd 8. in each loop we crochet inc from sen
rnd 9. (inc, 1 dc) * alternate to the end
rnd 10. (inc, 2 dc) * alternate to the end.
with the same thread we pass to the side of the dress and crochet sc, crochet loops for buttons.
bag
we crochet the same two parts.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)* 6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
on one thread cut off, fasten on the second leave. we crochet further for the back loops 20sc, cut the thread more authentically to sew with the first part. we sew with the first part also for the back loops.
71 ch, from the second loop from the hook we crochet 70 sc, sew to the bag.
lock 12 ch, sc 6 loops from the hook we crochet 7sc.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi pattern.
