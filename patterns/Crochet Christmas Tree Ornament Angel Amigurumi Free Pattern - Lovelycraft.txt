crochet christmas tree ornament angel amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: rnata6744
materials
2.5mm hook
cotton yarn (alize baby cotton soft)
(sky blue, light brown, cream and white)
safety eyes 6.00 mm
stuffing
yarn needle
stitch marker
the finished angel is approximately 12 cm tall from top to bottom. it may vary through depending on the kind of yarn and hook size used.
you can also use any color of yarn.
feel free to add accessories to your very own christmas ornaments.
abbreviations
ch chain
sc single crochet
st/sts stitch/ stitches
inc increase
dec decrease
slst slip stitch
fo fasten off
head
use the cream yarn.
rnd 1: start 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (inc,sc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (inc,2sc)*8 (32)
rnd 6: sc around (32)
rnd 7: (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 8-16: sc around (40), 9 rounds
attach the eyes between rounds 13 and 14 with 9 st in between (img 1).
rnd 17: (dec,3sc)*8 (32)
start to stuff the head.
rnd 18: (dec,2sc)*8 (24)
rnd 19: (dec,4sc)*4 (20)
stuff the head firmly (img2).
leave a long tail of yarn for sewing.
fasten off.
body
use the sky blue yarn.
rnd 1: start 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (inc,sc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (inc,2sc)*8 (32)
rnd 6: (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 7-10: sc around (40), 4 rounds
rnd 11: (dec,8sc)*4 (36)
rnd 12-13: sc around (36), 2 rounds
rnd 14: (dec,7sc)*4 (32)
rnd 15-16: sc around (32), 2 rounds
rnd 17: (dec,6sc)*4 (28)
start to stuff the body.
rnd 18-19: sc around (28), 2 rounds
rnd 20: (dec,5sc)*4 (24)
rnd 21-22: sc around (24), 2 rounds
rnd 23: (dec,4sc)*4 (20)
stuff the body firmly (img 3).
cut the yarn leaving long tail for sewing.
hat with hair
use the light brown yarn.
rnd 1: start 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (inc,sc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (inc,2sc)*8 (32)
rnd 6: sc around (32)
rnd 7: (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 8-15: sc around (40), 8 rounds
rnd 16: sl st in each st, (img 3) (40)
don't cut the yarn, we continue to make the hair.
work with the back loops of last round (img 4). insert your hook in back loop of slip stitch and ch 3 (img 5). start in second chain from hook: 2sc (img 6). make a sl st in next loop. repeat it 40 times (img 7,8,9).
fasten off.
binding: lay the hat with hair away from you. insert your hook through the stitch between round 14 and 15 (img 10). pick up the sky blue yarn (img 11).
insert the hook again in the next stitch (img 12) and pull it through the stitch of hat and the loop on hook (img 13). insert the hook again in the next stitch and pull up the yarn across the stitch and the loop on hook.
tip: do it more loosely as you can. make sure, that your slip stitch chain is not twisted. repeat it in each stitch around the hat (img 14).
you can make a second line of binding using a different color. do it in the same way as first between rounds 13 and 14 of hat (img 15).
halo
using the light yellow yarn.
ch 17. join in the first chain and work around. make a slip stitch in each chain (img 16). cut the yarn leaving a long tail for sewing.
arm (make 2)
using the cream yarn.
rnd 1: start 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3-4: sc around (9), 2 rounds
rnd 5: 5sc,2dec (7)
rnd 6-10: sc around (7), 5 rounds
change to yarn to sky blue.
rnd 11: sc around (7)
rnd 12: blo sc around (7)
rnd 13-17: sc around (7), 5 rounds
cut the yarn leaving a tail for sewing. don't stuff the arms.
sleeves: lay the arm with cream part away from you. find the last front loop of round 12. insert the hook and pick up the yarn (img 17). make a starting chain. insert your hook again in the same loop and make inrease (img 18).
rnd 1: (inc(in each front loop))*7, (img 19) (14)
work around the arm, don't join at the end.
rnd 2-3: sc around, (img 20) (14), 2 rounds
fasten off.
wings (make 2)
use the cream yarn.
first piece:
rnd 1: start 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
rnd 4-6: sc around (15), 3 rounds
cut the yarn and leaving a tail.
second piece:
rnd 1: start 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3-4: sc around (12), 2 rounds
don't cut the yarn, we continue to connect the two pieces together.
lay the two pieces together as shown on photo 21. insert your hook in the next st of first piece, after fasten off and make a sc (img 23,23). it will be the first stitch of the new round and wing. put your stitch marker here.
rnd 5: two pieces together: 15 sc around first piece, 12 sc around second (27)
rnd 6: (dec,7sc)*3 (24)
rnd 7: sc around (24)
rnd 8: (dec,6sc)*3 (21)
rnd 9: sc around (21)
rnd 10: (dec,5sc)*3 (18)
rnd 11: sc around (18)
rnd 12: (dec,4sc)*3 (15)
rnd 13: sc around (15)
rnd 14: (dec,3sc)*3 (12)
crochet 3 sc more after finished round 14 to bring the tail of yarn in the corner of wing. cut the yarn leaving a long tail for sewing. don't stuff the wings.
sew the halo to hat over rounds 5 to 12 (img 26).
sew the head to body using the tail of body (img 27,28).
sew the arms to body between rounds 22 and 23 and connect them together (img 29).
at last, sew the wings to body on the back side and connect them few rounds forward (img 30,31,32).
crochet christmas tree ornament angel ready, merry christmas.
