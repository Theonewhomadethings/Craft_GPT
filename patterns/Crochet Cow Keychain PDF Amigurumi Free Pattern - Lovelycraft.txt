you will need:
2 mm crochet hook
6 mm eyes
abbreviations
rnd: round
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
sl st: slip stitch
sc: single crochet
dc: double crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
()*: repeat till the end of round
(): total number of stitches in the round
let's start the crochet cow pattern from the head first.
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc, inc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, inc (30)
rnd 6. 4 sc, inc (36)
rnd 7-12. sc in all
attache safety eyes between row 9-10 with 6 st apart.
rnd 13. 4 sc, dec (30)
rnd 14. 3 sc, dec (24)
rnd 15. 2 sc, dec (18)
rnd 16. 1 dec, 10 sc, 1 dec, 4 sc (16)
after completing the crochet cow's head, we crochet its body.
body
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc, inc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, inc (30)
rnd 6-10. sc in all
rnd 11. 4 sc, dec (25)
rnd 12. 3 sc, dec (20)
rnd 13. sc in all
rnd 14. 3 sc, dec (16)
rnd 15-16. sc in all
you have completed the crochet cow's head and body parts. now let's do a patch on his head.
head patch
black
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, inc (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc, inc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, inc (30)
rnd 6. 4 sc, inc (36)
now we will make crochet cow's ears. they will be one black and one white. just like with cows.
ears (make 2)
1 black
1 white
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, inc (18)
pinch together and sew on head.
we've completed the cow's ears, now we're making a patch on her belly.
belly patch
black
rnd 1. 6 sc in ring
rnd 2. inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, inc (18)
now it's the cow's horns, let's embroider two cute horns.
horns
rnd 1. 4 sc in ring
rnd 2. 1 sc, inc (6)
rnd 3-5. sc in all
after finishing the cow's horns, let's make the legs.
legs
ch 5 and start in second ch from hook.
and continue working around ch.
rnd 1. 3 sc, 3 sc in last st, now continue on other side of, ch, 2 sc, inc
rnd 2. inc, 2 sc, 3 inc, 2 sc, 2 inc
rnd 3. 4 sc, 2 dec, 4 sc, dec
rnd 4. 4 sc, 2 dec, 4 sc
rnd 5-10. sc in all
rnd 11. 3 sc, dec
after completing the crochet cow's legs, let's crochet its nose.
nose
ch 7 and start in second ch from hook.
continue working around ch
rnd 1. inc, 4 sc, 3 sc in last ch, 4 sc, 1 sc in last st
rnd 2. (inc)*2, 4 sc, (inc)*3, 4 sc, inc
rnd 3. sc in all
we come to the last step, the arms, now we are making the cute crochet cow's arms and our keychain is completely ready.
arms (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in ring
rnd 2-8. sc in all
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi pattern.
