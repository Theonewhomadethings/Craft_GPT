designer
instagram: nat__knits
materials
3-color thread: main, brown, beige
8mm safety eyes
hook # 2
silicone fleece for filling
pair of scissors
upholstery needle
black thread to embroider the eyebrows
the final size with the material suggested is -19 cm.
abbreviations
sl slip stitch
sc single crochet
inc increase in single crochet
mr magic ring
dec decrease
let's start knitting the amigurumi giraffe pattern.
are you ready?
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc)*10 (40)
rnd 7-10. 40 sc (4 rows)
rnd 11. (4 sc, inc)*8 (48)
rnd 12-16. 48 sc (5 rows)
rnd 17. (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 18. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 19. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 20. (sc, dec)*10 (20)
rnd 21. (2 sc, dec)*5 (15)
cut the thread, leave long end to sew
we have completed the head part of the amigurumi pattern. now we knit the horns.
horns
in brown color
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4. 6 dec (6)
change to main color
5-7. 6 sc (3 rows)
cut the thread, leave long end to sew
we knit the nose and ears of the amigurumi giraffe pattern
snout
in beige
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (3 sc, 3 inc)*2 (18)
rnd 4. 18 sc
cut the thread, leave long end to sew
ears
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4 inc (8)
rnd 3. (3 sc, inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4. (4 sc, inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5. (5 sc, inc)*2 (14)
rnd 6-7. 14 sc (2 rows)
rnd 8. 7 dis (7)
fold and join both sides with 3 sc. cut the thread, leave long end for sewing. embroider the nose and eyebrows. place the eyes between rows 12 and 13 with a distance between them 7 sc.
we knit the hands and legs of the amigurumi giraffe
hands
in brown color
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd 4. 15 sc
rnd 5. (sc, dec)*5 (10)
change to main color
rnd 6. (3 sc, dec)*2 (8)
rnd 7-15. 8 sc (9 rows)
fold and join both sides with 4 sc. cut the thread, leave long end for sewing.
legs
in brown color
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. 18 sc
rnd 5. 6 sc, 3 dec, 6 sc (15)
rnd 6. 5 sc, 3 dec, 4 sc (12)
change to main color
rnd 7-10. 12 sc (4 rows)
rnd 11. (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
cut the yarn on the first leg. work an additional 5 to 6 sc on the second leg and begin to weave the body.
we knit the part of the body of the amigurumi pattern. let's combine the parts we prepare into the body part.
body
chain 2 and join to the first leg
rnd 1. (15 sc, 2 sc on the two chains)*2 (34)
rnd 2. 15 sc, 2 inc, 17 sc (36)
rnd 3-7. 36 sc (5 rows)
rnd 8. (10 sc, dec)*3 (33)
rnd 9. 33 sc
rnd 10. (9 sc, dec)*3 (30)
rnd 11. 30 sc
rnd 12. (8 sc, dec)*3 (27)
rnd 13. 27 sc
rnd 14. (7 sc, dec)*3 (24)
rnd 15-16. 24 sc (2 rows)
rnd 17. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 18. (4 sc, dec)*3 (15)
rnd 19-21. 15 sc ( 3 rows)
close and cut the thread
stains x 4
rnd 1. 1, 6 sc en mr (6)
rnd 2. 1, 6 sc en mr 2, 6 inc (12)
rnd 3-4. 1, 5 sc en mr 2, 5 inc (10)
sew the spots to the body
tail
ch 20 a chain and sl st in the first ch from hook. crochet then 1 eg in each remaining chain. cut the thread and leave long end for sewing. for the tail brush, cut colored threads brown and tie them to one end.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi giraffe pattern.
