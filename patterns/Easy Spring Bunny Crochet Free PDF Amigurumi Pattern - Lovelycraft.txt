hello my dears. i'm going to share a pretty easy crochet bunny pattern. if you're ready, let's start. it is quite easy to make. an amigurumi that you can easily do with basic crochet knowledge. in addition, the feet, body and head are easily made as a whole. then you add the arms and ears. you don't stuff the bunny amigurumi's ears. it will look very nice if you do it firmly. please don't forget to follow for more free amigurumi patterns. if you're ready, let's start! let's crochet the bunny amigurumi step by step.
pdf easy spring bunny crochet free amigurumi pattern
designer: ami.rina_toys
materials
knitted from "yanart. jeans", with ears 17-18 cm.
hook
needle
scissors
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
legs body head
we start with the legs. stuff with sewing.
1. 6 sc in mr
2. 6 inc 12 sc
. (3 sc, 1inc)*3 15 sc
4-6. 3 rows 15 sc
slst, cut the yarn (first leg).
similarly, we crochet the second leg, but do not cut the yarn. we make 3 ch, connect the second leg to the first and crochet.
7. 15 sc in the first leg, 3 sc in a chain of 3 ch, 15 sc in the second leg, 3 sc in a chain of 3 ch. we get 36 sc. here it is desirable to put a marker.
8. (5 sc, 1 inc)*6 42 sc
9-12. 4 rows 42 sc
13. (12 sc, 1 dec)*3 39 sc
14-15. 2 rows 39 sc.
16. (11 sc, 1 dec)*3 36 sc
17. 1 row 36 sc
18. (10 sc, 1 dec)*3 33 sc
19. 1 row 33 sc
20. (9 sc, 1 dec)*3 30 sc
21. 1 row 30 sc
22. (3 sc, 1 dec)*6 24 sc
23. 1 row 24 sc
24. (2 sc, 1 dec)*6 18 sc
25. (1 sc, 1 dec)*6 12 sc
26. (1 sc, 1 inc)*6 18 sc
27. (2 sc, 1 inc)*6 24 sc
28. (3 sc, 1 inc)*6 30 sc
29. (4 sc, 1 inc)*6 36 sc
30. (5 sc, 1 inc)*6 42 sc
31. (6 sc, 1 inc)*6 48 sc
32-37. 6 rows 48 sc
i sewed eyes, if on a secure fastening, insert between about 35-36 rows.
38. (6 sc, 1 dec)*6 42 sc
39. (5 sc, 1 dec)*6 36 sc
40. (4 sc, 1 dec)*6 30 sc
41. (3 sc, 1 dec)*6 24 sc
42. (2 sc, 1 dec)*6 18 sc
43. (1 sc, 1 dec)*6 12 sc
44. 6 dec
pull the hole with a needle, cut off the yarn, hide the tip of the yarn inside the head.
make a muzzle. embroider nose and eyebrows.
ears
crochet 2 identical details. don't stuff.
1. 6 sc in mr
2. 6 inc 12 sc
. (3 sc, 1 inc)*3 15 sc
4. 1 row 15 sc
5. (4 sc, 1 inc)*3 18 sc
6-10. 5 rows 18 sc
11. (4 sc, 1 dec)*3 15 sc
12-14. 3 rows 15 sc
15. (3 sc, 1 dec)*3 12 sc
16. 1 row 12 sc
17. (2 sc, 1 dec)*3 9 sc
crochet behind both sides 4 sc leave the yarn for sewing.
tail
1. 6 sc in mr
2. 6 inc 12 sc
3. 1 row 12 sc
4. 6 dec 6 sc
arms
crochet two identical pieces. stuff moderately.
1. 5 sc in mr
2. 5 inc 10 sc
3-12. 10 rows 10 sc
13. (3 sc, 1 dec)*2 8 sc
fold, crochet for both sides 3-4 sc.
leave the yarn for sewing.
sew on the ears, arms and tail.
