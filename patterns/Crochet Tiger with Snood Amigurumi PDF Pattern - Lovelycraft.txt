hello my dears. today i am going to share a cute crochet tiger pattern that is designed quite nicely. the tiger amigurumi, whose picture you have seen, is crocheted very tightly. so i can say that it looks pretty nice. a nice harmony was achieved by using white, red and black colors. i can say that making amigurumi is difficult and takes a long time. because it really consists of a lot of loops. the fact that it looks small is completely related to the rope used. if you're ready now, let's get start.
crochet tiger with snood amigurumi free pdf pattern
designer: mauvepony
materials:
1. yarn of three primary colors: red, white (or milky), black (or dark brown). and one additional color for snood.
2. hook number 2.
3. holofiber filler.
4. plastic eyes on a safe mount with a diameter of 6 mm.
5. needle, scissors, crocheting marker (i have a paper clip), sewing pins.
level: intermediate.
finished tiger amigurumi size 15 cm tall
special instructions:
stuff the head and body gradually in the course of crocheting. stuff the muzzle before sewing. stuff the upper and lower paws to half. do not stuff ears and tail.

snood
1) make 20 ch + 2 tch, turn, crochet 20 hdc
2) 2 tch + 20 hdc blo, starting from the third loop
crochet turning rows (according to the pattern of the second row) until the length of the snood is enough to wrap it around the neck of the tiger amigurumi. approximately 33-36 rows. 6 .
