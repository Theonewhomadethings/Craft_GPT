materials
1 ball of natural cotton swan 6 strands of coats chain in lilac, purple and orange.
1 ball of natural cotton swan 3 ends of coats chain in black and white.
needle number 3.5 mm
scissor
wool needle
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase in single crochet
dec decrease
let's start crocheting the amigurumi cat pattern
head
in lilac color and with a 3.5 mm needle we start with a magic ring
rnd 1. mr from 6 stitching (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, 1 inc. repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4. 1 sc for each base stitch (18)
rnd 5. 2 sc, 1 inc. repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 6. 1 sc for each base stitch (24)
rnd 7. 3 sc, 1 inc. repeat 6 more times (30)
rnd 8-11. 1 sc for each base stitch (30)
rnd 12. 7 sc, 3 inc, 12 sc, 3 inc, 5 sc (36)
rnd 13. 7 sc, (1 sc, 1inc) x3, 12 sc, (1 sc, 1 inc) x3, 5 sc (42)
rnd 14-16. 42 sc
rnd 17. 7 sc, (1 sc, 1 dec) x3, 12 sc, (1 sc, 1 dec) x3, 5 sc (36)
rnd 18. 7 sc, 3 dec, 12 sc, 3 dec, 5 sc (30)
flatten the head on a surface leaving inc, from row 12 to the sides. place the safety eyes between rows 9 and 10 leaving 6 stitch of separation fill the head with silicone fleece continue weaving the body.
we have completed the head of the amigurumi cat pattern, now crochet the body part
body
we continue with the same color
rnd 19. 15 dec (15)
rnd 20. blo: 14 sc, 1 inc (16)
rnd 21. 15 sc, 1 inc (17)
rnd 22. 16 sc, 1 inc (18)
rnd 23. 1 sc for each base stitch (18)
rnd 24. 2 sc, 1 inc. repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 25-27. 1 sc for each base stitch (24)
rnd 28. blo 24 sc
rnd 29-30. 24 sc
fill with silicone fleece
rnd 31. 12 dec
rnd 32. 6 dec
close and hide the thread
we crocheting the legs and arms of the amigurumi cat
legs (blank)
rnd 1. mr from 6 stitch (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. blo: 1 sc for each base stitch (12)
change the color to light gray
rnd 4. blo: 1 sc for each base stitch (12)
rnd 5. 2 sc, 4 dec, 2 sc (8)
rnd 6-10. 1 sc for each base stitch (8)
fill with fleece only the part of the foot. close and leave thread to sew.
take into account that when sewing the leg, the part of the 4 dec of round 5 has to stitch upwards.
arms (in light gray)
rnd 1. mr from 8 stitch (8)
rnd 2-7. 1 sc for each base stitch (8)
fill with silicone fleece. close and leave thread to sew.
we crocheting the ears and the tail of the amigurumi pattern
ears (with gray color)
rnd 1. magic ring 5 sc
rnd 2. 5 sc
rnd 3. 1 inc at each stitch (10)
rnd 4. 10 sc
we close and sew
tail (in gray)
hook the needle in the lower center back and crocheting a chain of 11 stitches. skip a stitch and from the second chain we crocheting: 6 inc, 4 sc. close and hide the thread
we crocheting the skirt and the hat of the amigurumi cat.
skirt (with violet color)
we make a slip knot and hook it in the front strand of the row 28. we go up with 3 ch, we weave 5 double crochet together at each base stitch. we change to orange. we weave *1sc, 2 cad in the air*
repeat from to each base stitch. fasten off and hide the threads
hat (with violet color)
rnd 1. mr from 6 sc
rnd 2-4. 6 sc
rnd 5. 1 sc, 1 inc (9)
rnd 6-8. 9 sc
rnd 9. 2 sc, 1 inc (12)
rnd 10. 3 sc, 1 inc (15)
rnd 11. 4 sc, 1 inc (18)
rnd 12. 5 sc, 1 inc (21)
rnd 13. 6 sc, 1 inc (24)
rnd 14. 7 sc, 1 inc (27)
rnd 15. blo 8 sc, 1 inc (30)
rnd 16. 2 sc, 1 inc
rnd 17. 3 sc, 1 inc
we change to orange 18th. we weave *1sc, 2 ch in the air*
repeat from to each base stitch
we close and hide the threads. with orange color we are going to weave a chain guard around the hat. we make a slip knot and hook it in the row 14. we are going to work in slip stitch all the way around. we close and hide the threads. termination. we embroider the eyes and the nose. we hide the threads.
congratulations, you have completed the cat anne amigurumi pattern.
