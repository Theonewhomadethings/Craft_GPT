crochet dinosaur rattle pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: yar.yarn
materials
yarn (semi-cotton, cotton) milky and blue (i have yarnart jeans color 03 and azure color gazzal organic baby cotton)
threads (iris) black
hook 2.0 mm
wooden ring (outer diameter 65 mm)
ringer
knitted needle with a blunt tip
holofiber
abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
sl st slip stitch
dc double crochet
tch turning chain stitch
head
blue thread.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. 1sc, 1inc (18)
rnd 4. 2sc, 1inc (24)
rnd 5. 3sc, 1inc (30)
rnd 6-8. 30 sc (30)
rnd 9. 4sc, 1inc (36)
rnd 10. 36 sc (36)
rnd 11. 5sc, 1inc (42)
rnd 12. 42 sc (42)
rnd 13. 6sc, 1inc (48)
rnd 14-18. 48 sc
rnd 19. 6sc, 1dec (42)
rnd 20. 42 sc (42)
rnd 21. 5sc, 1dec (36)
rnd 22. 4sc, 1dec (30)
fill with holofiber, put a caller.
rnd 23. 3sc, 1dec (24)
rnd 24. 2sc, 1dec (18)
rnd 25. 1sc, 1dec (12)
thread attach and cut leaving the end for sewing length 20cm if required, add holofiber.
muzzle
blue thread.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. 1sc, 1inc (18)
rnd 4. 18 sc (18)
thread attach and cut, leave the end for sewing length 15cm.
clamp for ring
collect 10 ch with a thread blue (number of loops depends on the thickness of the ring and yarn, therefore, connecting 3-4 rows, you need try the clamp on the ring. he must sit very tight!).
turn the work and starting from the 2nd loop crochet 9 sc
1rw and crochet back 9 sc so continue to crochet for the length you need. i have 32 rows.
fix the thread and cut leaving the end for sewing.
tail
blue thread.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 sc (6)
rnd 3. 1sc, 1inc (9)
rnd 4. 2sc, 1inc (12)
rnd 5. 3sc, 1inc (15)
rnd 6. 15 sc (15)
rnd 7. 4sc, 1inc (18)
rnd 8. 18 sc (18)
rnd 9. 5sc, 1inc (21)
rnd 10. 21 sc (21)
fasten and cut the thread, leaving an end for sewing 20 cm long.
crests
large (4pcs)
milk thread.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 sc (6)
rnd 3. 1sc, 1inc (9)
rnd 4. 2sc, 1inc (12)
fasten and cut the thread, leaving the end for sewing 10 cm long.
medium (1pcs)
milk thread.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 sc (6)
rnd 3. 1sc, 1inc (9)
fasten and cut the thread, leaving the end for sewing 10 cm long.
small (1pcs)
milk thread.
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 1sc, 1inc (6)
fasten and cut the thread, leaving the end for sewing 10 cm long.
assembly
muzzle, filling with holofiber, sew to the front of the head.
large combs (3pcs) sew to the back of the head from the center down. do not fill with holofiber!
embroider eyes and nose with black thread. embroider eyebrows with blue thread.
sew the collar to the ring. turn the seam inside the ring.
sew the head to the ringlet sew the tail to the ringlet (do not fill with holofiber!) sew the middle comb to the clamp, the small one to the tail (do not fill with holofiber!).
crochet dinosaur rattle is ready!
