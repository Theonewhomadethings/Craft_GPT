materials
thread; white, turquoise, red, black
plastic eye
fiber for filling
scissors, crochet
abbreviations
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
ch chain
blo back loop only
flo front loop only
head and body
white color.
rnd 1. 6 sc into a double magic loop = 6
rnd 2. 6 inc = 12
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 = 18
rnd 4. (1 sc, inc, 1 sc)*6 = 24
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 = 30
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc, 2 sc)*6 = 36
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc)*6 = 42
rnd 8. (3 sc, inc, 3 sc)*6 = 48
rnd 9-13. 48 sc = 48 (5 rows)
rnd 14. 19 sc, 2 ch, skip 2 stitches, 6 sc, 2 ch, skip 2 stitches, 19 sc = 48
rnd 15-19. 48 sc = 48 (5 rows)
put the safety eyes.
rnd 20. (2 sc, dec, 2 sc)*8 = 40
rnd 21. (dec, 3 sc)*8 = 32
start stuffing the head and continue stuffing as you progress.
rnd 22. (1 sc, dec, 1 sc)*8 = 24
rnd 23. (3 sc, inc)*6 = 30
rnd 24. (2 sc, inc, 2 sc)*6 = 36
rnd 25. (5 sc, inc)*6 = 42
rnd 26. (3 sc, inc, 3 sc)*6 = 48
rnd 27. (7 sc, inc)*6 = 54
rnd 28-39. 54 sc = 54 (12 rows)
rnd 40. (dec, 7 sc)*6 = 48
rnd 41. (2 sc, dec, 2 sc)*8 = 40
rnd 42. (dec, 3 sc)*8 = 32
start stuffing the body and continue stuffing as you progress.
rnd 43. (1 sc, dec, 1 sc)*8 = 24
rnd 44. (dec, 1 sc)*8 = 16
rnd 45. 8 dec = 8
fasten off.
hands (2 pieces)
turquoise color.
rnd 1. 6 sc into a double magic loop = 6
rnd 2. 6 inc = 12
rnd 3-5. 12 sc = 12 (3 rows)
stuff the hands.
cocoa color.
rnd 6. blo: (dec, 1 sc)*4 = 8
rnd 7-14. 8 sc = 8 (8 rows)
rnd 15. 4 sc short row
fold piece in half and sew connecting both sides 3 sc. fasten off.
turquoise color.
working in the flo of the 6th row: 12 sc = 12
fasten off. sew the hands.
legs (2 pieces)
turquoise color.
rnd 1. 8 sc into a double magic loop = 8
rnd 2. 8 inc = 16
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*8 = 24
rnd 4. blo: 24 sc = 24
rnd 5-6. 24 sc = 24 (2 rows)
rnd 7. (dec, 1 sc)*8 = 16
rnd 8. 8 dec = 8
stuff the legs. fasten off. sew the legs.
hat
turquoise color.
rnd 1. 6 sc into a double magic loop = 6
rnd 2. 6 inc = 12
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 = 18
rnd 4. (1 sc, inc, 1 sc)*6 = 24
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 = 30
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc, 2 sc)*6 = 36
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc)*6 = 42
rnd 8. (3 sc, inc, 3 sc)*6 = 48
rnd 9-16. 48 sc = 48 (8 rows)
ears
(2 pieces, distance between the ears 16 stitches).
rnd 1-4. 8 sc = 8, ch, flip over (4 rows)
rnd 5. dec, 4 sc, dec = 6, ch, flip over
rnd 6. 6 sc = 6, ch, flip over
rnd 7. dec, 2 sc, dec = 4, ch, flip over
rnd 8. 4 sc = 4
fasten off.
visor
silver color. join the thread between the ears, skipping 1 stitch from the ear.
rnd 1-5. 14 sc = 14, ch, flip over (5 rows)
rnd 6. dec, 10 sc, dec = 12, ch, flip over
sc around the entire piece. fasten off. sew the hat and visor.
nose
persimmon color.
rnd 1. 6 sc into a double magic loop = 6
rnd 2. 6 sc = 6
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc, 1 sc)*2 = 8
fasten off. sew the nose.
embroider eyebrows and smile using black color. embroider snowflake using turquoise color.
scarf
red color.
75 ch, starting from the second ch from the hook
rnd 1. 74 sc = 74
fasten off. wear the scarf.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi snowman pattern.
