crochet elephant melanie doll amigurumi free pattern
designer: @kediperiatolyesi
copyright owner: lovelycraft.com
finished toy is around 20 cm height.
materials
yarnart jeans/ grey (color code 49)
yarnart jeans/ pink (color code 18)
alize cotton gold / tan (color code 89)
6mm safety eye (black)
1 cm wood button (2 piece)
for embroidery: black thread
fiberfill
pins
size 2mm or 2.5mm crochet hook
stitch marker
scissors
abbreviations
ch: chain
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
slst: slip stitch
hdc: half double crochet
dc: double crochet
st/sts: stitch/stitches
tr: triple crochet
blo: back loops only
flo: fornt loops only
bobble: bobble stitch
( ) : the parentheses at the end of the row give the total number of stitches of that round.
[ ]x.. : repeat instructions x times
legs (with grey color yarn and make 2)
rnd 1 start 6sc in a magic ring and join with grey sl st (6) please look through "techniques section" to learn about "magic ring" join with a slst. do not forget to use your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3-5 sc in all 12 sts (3 rounds)
make heel part
rnd 6 ch 1, turn, sc in next 7st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (7)
rnd 7 ch 1, turn, sc in next 5st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (5)
rnd 8 ch 1, turn, sc in next 3st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (3)
join foot part and crochet leg
rnd 9 ch 1, turn, sc in next 3st, (sideways) sc in next 2st, (in front) sc in next 5st, (sideways) sc in next 2st (12)
rnd 10-18 sc in all 12 sts (9 rounds)
**stuff the leg.
we complete the leg parts of our amigurumi elephant pattern, then we can move on to the body part.
body (with grey color yarn)
with grey colour
rnd 19 to join two legs; ch 6,join to the middle of the other leg with a sl st and sc in the next 12 sts of the leg, flo sc over the ch 6 stitches,sc in the next 12 sts, and flo sc over the ch 6 sts (36)
rnd 20-21 sc in all 36 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 22 [sc in the next 5 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 23 [sc in the next 6 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 24-27 sc in all 48 sts (4 rounds)
rnd 28 [sc in the next 6 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 29-30 sc in all 42 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 31 [sc in the next 5 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 32 sc in all 36 sts
rnd 33 [sc in the next 4 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 34 sc in all 30 sts
rnd 35 [sc in the next 3 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 36 sc in all 24 sts
rnd 37 [sc in the next 2 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 38-40 sc in all 18 sts (3 rounds)
**stuff the body.
as you can see in the pictures, after crocheting the body part step by step, we can move on to the head part of our elephant pattern.
head (with grey color yarn)
rnd 1 start 6 sc in a magic ring and join with grey slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4 [sc in the next 2 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5 [sc in the next 3 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 6 [sc in the next 4 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 7 [sc in the next 5 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 8 [sc in the next 6 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 9-16 sc in all 48 sts (8 rounds)
rnd 17 [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times, sc in the next 12 sts, [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times, sc in the next 12 sts (60)
rnd 18-19 sc in all 60 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 20 [sc in the next st, dec] repeat 6 times, sc in the next 12 sts, [sc in the next st, dec] repeat 6 times, sc in the next 12 sts (48)
rnd 21 [sc in the next 6 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (42)
place the safety eyes between 17 and 18 with 9 stitches gap between them.
rnd 22 [sc in the next 5 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 23 [sc in the next 4 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 24 [sc in the next 3 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 25 [sc in the next 2 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (18)
**stuff the head.
after completing the head part of our amigurumi pattern, let's place the eyes and start knitting the ear part.
ears (with grey color yarn and 2 make)
rnd 1 start 10 dc in a magic ring and join with grey slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 dcinc in all sts (20dc)
rnd 3 [dc in the next st, dcinc] repeat 10 times (30)
rnd 4 [dc in the next 2 sts, dcinc] repeat 10 times (40)
rnd 5 double up and slst in the next 20 sts
**do not stuff the fiberfill. break the yarn leaving a long tail for sewing.
cheek patches (with pink color yarn and 2 make)
rnd 1 start 6 sc in a magic ring and join with pink slst (6) break the yarn leaving a long tail for sewing.
after crocheting her ears on her head, we will start making the trunk of our amigurumi elephant.
trunk (with grey color yarn)
rnd 1 start 4 sc in a magic ring and join with grey slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (8)
rnd 3-7 sc in all 8 sts (5 rounds)
rnd 8 [sc in the next 3 sts, inc] repeat 2 times (10)
rnd 9-10 sc in all 10 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 11 [sc in the next 4 sts, inc] repeat 2 times (12)
rnd 12-13 sc in all 12 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 14 [sc in the next 5 sts, inc] repeat 2 times (14)
rnd 15 [sc in the next 6 sts, inc] repeat 2 times (16)
rnd 16 [sc in the next 7 sts, inc] repeat 2 times (18)
rnd 17 sc in all 18 sts
rnd 18 sc in the next 9 sts, hdc in the next 3 sts, dc in the next 3 sts, hdc in the next 3 sts (18)
**do not stuff the fiberfill.
arms (with grey color yarn and 2 make)
rnd 1 start 5 sc in a magic ring and join with grey slst. insert your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (10)
rnd 3-5 sc in all 10 sts (3 rounds)
rnd 6 dec, sc in the next 6st, dec (8)
rnd 7-17 sc in all 8 sts (11 rounds)
rnd 18 [dec] repeat 4 times (4)
**stuff the arms.
after completing the trunk of our amigurumi elephant step by step, now we move on to that wonderful red crochet dress.
dress (with tan color yarn)
rnd 1 ch 46
rnd 2 start in the second chain from the hook, sc in the 45 sts, slst (45)
rnd 3-4 ch 1, turn, sc in the next 45 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 5 ch 1, blo sc in the next 45 sts
rnd 6 ch 2, [dcinc] repeat 45 times (90)
rnd 7-9 ch 2, dc in the next 90 sts (3 rounds)
** make 5 dc in same st, skip 1 st, sc in 2nd st. repeat as same order.
inner part of the skirt;
** we will crochet the inner part of the skirt into the blo stitches of round 5.crochet the skirt as dc
rnd 1 ch 2, [dcinc] repeat 45 times (90)
rnd 2-4 ch 2, dc in the next 90 sts (3 rounds)
** make 5 dc in same st, skip 1 st, sc in 2nd st. repeat as same order.
shoulder straps of dress;
rnd 1 ch 23
rnd 2 start in the second chain from the hook, sc in the 22 sts
rnd 3 ch 3, turn, [trinc] repeat 22 times (44)
