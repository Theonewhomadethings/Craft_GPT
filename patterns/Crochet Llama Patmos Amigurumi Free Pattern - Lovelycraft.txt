abbreviations
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
st/sts: stitch/stitches
hdc: half double crochet
sl-st : slip stitch
ch : chain
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo :crochet into back loops only
bl : bobble stitch do 5dc all in the same stitch and then yarn over and pull through all the loops on your hook (the original loop + 1 loop for each partially completed dc).
reverse single crochet: crochet the stitch from left to right. this technique is most often used to make edges look smooth and neat. make a chain to turn back from font side. crochet a single crochet one before from last stitch. then continue in the same way through the right side of crochet while slightly flexing yarn. crochet single crochet until end of the row.
(..): total number of stitches after the round.

legs (use white color and make 2)
start with legs. after crocheting front foot part of leg, at 8th round heel part will be crocheted. lastly, after 11th round is completed rest of the rounds will be crocheted and legs will be completed.

join two legs and crochet the body as a single piece.
rnd 1: start 6 sc in a magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) repeat 6 times (12)
rnd 3-7: sc in each st around (12)

rnd 8: ch 1, turn, sc in next 7st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (7)
rnd 9: ch 1, turn, sc in next 5st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (5)
rnd 10: ch 1, turn, sc in next 3st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (3)

rnd 12-22: sc in each st around (12)

after 11th round foot part will take its shape. then crochet st at its sideways and front section. after that, legs will be completed by crocheting sc in next 12 st between 12-22 rounds.
when first leg is finished, fasten off and crochet second leg. after second leg finished do not fasten off yarn and join two legs with chain. then continue crochet the body part.

tress (use white color yarn)
start crocheting tresses after the head is completed. join the yarn with a sl-st into the first stitch of blo ( at the top of head where the magic ring is started) at head. crochet all tresses as inc single crochet (make 2sc in each ch).
after first tress is completed, make 1 sl-st and repeat the process. as total there are 17 tresses! join the yarn with sl-st at the top point of head.
ch 16, (skip 1st st and start crocheting 2nd st.) inc and repeat 15 times, 1 sl-st, ch 16, .(repeat up to last st)

shape the head
when completed the crocheting the head and placed the eyes, fill the head with polyester fiberfill. instruction images will be helpful for filling process of polyester fiberfill. make sure that enough polyester fiberfill is placed inside the head without changing the head form/shape.
after completing all steps which are mentioned above, sew the mouth. while sewing the mouth, leave a small gap and fill the inside of mouth with polyester fiberfill. then sew the gap part of mouth. before sewing the mouth, by using pins place on head and check it location.

if it is preferred, tresses can be loosely sewed at the end point on the head.

poncho
use 5 different color yarns base on your preferences. poncho will be crocheted as rectangular and it will be sew by folding from front sides as seen instruction images. after single crochet all edges, start edge lace.
make sure sew the half of it otherwise it will not pass through head.
rnd 1: ch 61
rnd 2: sc in each chain (start from 2nd st) (60)
switch other color
rnd 3:ch 2, turn, dc in each st (60)
rnd 4: ch 1, turn, sc in each st (60)
rnd 5: ch 1, turn, sc in next 2st, (1 bl, sc in next 2st) repeat 19 times, sc in next st
rnd 6: ch 1, turn, sc in each st (60)
rnd 7: ch 1, turn, sc in each st (60)
rnd 8: ch 2, turn, sc in each st (60)

rnd 10: ch 1, sc in each st (60)
rnd 11: ch 2, turn, dc in each st (60)
fold the poncho as seen instruction images and sew by aligning bottom section. use first color of poncho and single crochet around of poncho. if you make (sc inc, repeat 3 times) at front corner, there will be no tension. and lastly crochet edge lace and finish poncho.
