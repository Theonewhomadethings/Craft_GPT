materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2.5 mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
safety eyes size 5-6 mm
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
head
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3 sc, inc)*7, 2 sc (40)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)*8 (48)
rnd 7-18. (12 rnds) 48 sc (48)
insert the safety eyes at round 13,14. 8sc apart
rnd 19. (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 20. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 21. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 22. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 23. blo (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 24. (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 25. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end
scarf
make 51 chains, sc, 48-hdc, sc (50)
fasten off
body
insert the hook into round 23 of the head
(blo round)
rnd 1. 24 sc (24) (pic 0)
rnd 2. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 3. 30 sc (30)
rnd 4. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 5-7. (3 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 8. (6 sc, dec)*4, 4 sc (32)
rnd 9-11. (3 rnds) 32 sc (32)
rnd 12. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
rnd 13. (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 14. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end
nose
rnd 1. make 3 chains, inc, 4 sc in last chain, inc (8)
rnd 2. inc, 2 sc, 2-inc, 2 sc, inc (12)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
arm*2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3-10. (8 rnds) 9 sc (9)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
leg*2
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3-4. (2 rnds) 16 sc (16)
rnd 5. 5 sc, 3-dec, 5 sc (13)
rnd 6. 5 sc, dec, 6 sc (12)
rnd 7-11. (5 rnds) 12 sc (12)
rnd 12. 6-dec (6)
fasten off, leave long tail for sewing to the body with a button.
ear*2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. turn your hook, 1-chain, 6 sc (6)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
baby teddy is ready.
