crochet monkey pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: aquariwool
materials
yarn color:
(a) white: yarnart jeans 03 (50g, 160m)
(b) brown: yarnart jeans 70 (50g, 160m)
(c) red: yarnart jeans 51 (50g, 160m)
other materials:
crochet hook size 2- 2.5 mm
knitting needles size 3.5- 4 mm
1 wooden button, 1 pair of safety eyes 10 mm
tapestry needles, pins, stitch markers, scissors
abbreviations
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
hdc half double crochet
sl st slip stitch
mr magic ring
dc double crochet
tr treble crochet
bobble bobble stitch (4 hdc in the same st)
blo back loop only
flo front loop only
rnd round
sts stitches
k-knit
p-purl
finished size approx: 12 cm
legs (make 2)
yarn a
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
break the yarn, make the second leg in the same way, but don't break the yarn this time. connect 2 legs together: sc 15 (leg 1)+ sc 15 (leg 2)= 30.move to the body part, change to yarn b.
body
yarn b
rnd 1. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 2-7. 36 sc (6 rows) (36)
rnd 8. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 9. 30 sc (30)
rnd 10. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 11. 24 sc (24)
rnd 12. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 13. 18 sc (18)
break the yarn with a long rest. stuff.
arms (make 2)
yarn a
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3, change to yarn b (9)
rnd 3-7. 9 sc (5 rows) (9)
rnd 4. dec 1, sc 7 (8)
rnd 5. dec 1, sc 6 (7)
rnd 6. dec 1, sc 5 (6)
break the yarn with a long rest. stuff slightly. sew 2 edges together.
head
yarn a
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10. 54 sc (54)
rnd 11. (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 12-19. 60 sc (8 rows) (60)
rnd 20. (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 21. 54 sc (54)
rnd 22. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 23. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 24. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 25. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 26. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 27. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later. stuff.
hat
yarn b
rnd 1-9. crochet rnd 1-9 of the head (6)
rnd 10. (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 11-17. 60 sc (7 rows), you can add 1 more rnd if you find the head still too small (60)
rnd 18. sc 15, hdc 2, (dc 3), hdc 2, sc8, hdc 2, (dc 3), hdc 2, sc 8, hdc 2, (dc 3), hdc 2, sc 14 (66)
rnd 19. sc 15, hdc 3, (dc, tr, dc), hdc 3, sc 8, hdc 3, (dc, tr, dc), hdc 3, sc 8, hdc 3, (dc, tr, dc), hdc 3, sc 14 (72)
crochet along the hat with sc stitch. break the yarn with a long rest to sew later.
inner ears (make 2)
yarn a
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)*6 (16)
break the yarn with a long rest.
outer ears (make 2)
yarn b
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. 24 sc (24)
rnd 6. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
break the yarn with a long rest. put the inner ear on the outer ear and sew 2 pieces together.
tail
yarn b
rnd 1. 7 sc in mr (7)
rnd 2-21. 7 sc (20 rows) (6)
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later.
scarf
yarn c, using knitting needles.
rnd 1. cast on 6 st (6)
rnd 2. k6 (6)
rnd 3. p6 (6)
repeat rnd 2 and 3 till you reach 14 cm then cast off, break the yarn, hide the remaining yarn.
use the tapestry needle to attach the wooden button to the scarf. that's done.
