materials
black cotton yarn

synthetic cotton
safety eyes and nose (the latter can also be embroidered)
wire
galvanized
pliers
wool needle
pair of scissors
adhesive tape (preferably masking tape)
note: if you notice that the fabric is too tight or you can see the filling, change to a crochet hook more according to your yarn
abbreviations
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
let's start crochetting the amigurumi kitten pattern.
we crochet the part of the body and the head of the amigurumi pattern.
head and body
black yarn
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. inc at 12 sc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)x6 (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)x 6 (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)x 6 (36)
rnd 7-12.36 sc
rnd 13. (4 sc, dec) x 6 (30)
rnd 14. (3 sc, dec) x 6 (24)
rnd 15. 2 sc, dec) x 6 (18)
in this part we already place the safety eyes (between round 9 and 10 and the nose (if we do not have it, we can embroider it at the end) we fill lightly.
we create the support: we take a
wire approx 35 cms high and bend the tip with pliers (see images)
we cover it with duct tape
we put it inside the head and fill firmly
we keep weaving around the wire always
rnd 16. (sc, dec) x 6 (12)
rnd 17. (3 sc, dec) x 2, 2 mp (10)
rnd 18. (3 sc, dec) x 2 (8)
rnd 19-23. 8 sc
rnd 24. (3 sc, dec) x 2 (10)
rnd 25. (4 sc, dec) x 2 (12)
rnd 26. (3 sc, dec) x 3 (15)
rnd 27. (4 sc, dec) x 3 (18)
rnd 28. (5 sc, dec) x 3 (21)
rnd 29-30. 21 sc
rnd 31. 8 sc, here we will leave the wire outside and then weave the tail, (see images) 13 sc (21) fill.
rnd 32. (5 sc, dec) x 3 (18)
rnd 33. (sc, dec) x 6 (12)
rnd 34. 6 dec (6) cerrar
let's crochet the tail of the amigurumi cat pattern
tail
black thread
we measure the length of the wire destined for the tail, it should measure approx 12 cms long, we cut the excess
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2-23. 4 sc
(the number of turns depends on the length of the wire destined for the tail)
we crochet the legs of the amigurumi kitten pattern.
paws x 2
black yarn
rnd 1. 5sc in mr (5)
rnd 2. 5 sc
rnd 3. 2 sc, dec, sc (4)
rnd 4-5. 4 sc
sew to the body
we are going to crochet amigurumi cat ears. the outer part of the ears is crocheted with black yarn. the inner part of the ears is crocheted with pink yarn.
ears
we crochet only 1 piece since it will have a hat, in black thread
rnd 1. 3sc in mr (3)
rnd 2. sc, inc, sc (4)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x 2 (6)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) x 2 (8)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) x 2 (10)
paint or embroider the inner part of the ear with pink.
we are going to crochet the hat of the amigurumi toy for halloween.
hat
yarn of the color you like the most, you can also intersperse color as i did
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. (sc, inc) x 2 (6)
rnd 3. 6 sc
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) x 2 (8)
rnd 5. 8 sc
rnd 6. (3 sc, inc) x 2 (10)
rnd 7. 10 sc
rnd 8. (4 sc, inc) x 2 (12)
rnd 9-10. 12 sc
rnd 11. (5 sc, inc) x 2 (14)
rnd 12. (6 sc, inc) x 2 (16)
rnd 13. (7 sc, inc) x 2 (18)
the next round we crochet it taking only the front strand
rnd 14. (3 sc, inc) x 4, 2 sc (22)
rnd 15. (3 sc, inc) x 5, 2 sc (27)
rnd 16. (2 sc, inc) x 9 (36)
sew to the head
hat details:
color of choice 23 ch, from the second ch from the hook we crochet 21 sc
details:
with white thread we add the edge of the eyes.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi kitten pattern.
