designer
instagram: ngoc_linh_0805
materials
yarn: jeans (yarnart) color 03 for head & body
crochet hook size 2.0mm
one pair of black safety eyes size 8mm
fiberfill, stitch marker, scissors, yarn needle
black embroidery thread to make the mouth
abbreviations
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
slst: slip stitch
rnd(s): round (s)/ rows
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
blo: back loop only
flo: front loop only
dc: double crochet
fo: fasten off
head
rnd 1: 6sc into mr (6)
rnd 2: 6inc (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (sc,inc,sc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (2sc,inc,2sc)*6 (36)
rnd 8: (3sc,inc,3sc)*6 (48)
rnd 9-11: (3rnds) 48sc
rnd 12: (11sc,inc)*4 (52)
rnd 13-15: (3rnds) 52sc
rnd 16: (6sc,inc,6sc)*4 (56)
rnd 17: 56sc
rnd 18: 23sc, maker for the first eye, 9sc, maker for the second eye, 24sc
rnd 19: 12sc, (2sc,inc)*4, 8sc, (inc,2sc)*4, 12sc (64)
rnd 20-23: (4rnds) 64sc
rnd 24: (3sc,dec,sc)*8 (56)
rnd 25: (5sc,dec)*8 (48)
rnd 26: (2sc,dec,2sc)*8 (40)
rnd 27: (3sc,dec)*8 (32)
rnd 28: (sc,dec,sc)*8 (24)
fo. leave a long tail for sewing. stuff the head with fiberfill.
yes, friends, now the head part of our amigurumi pattern is finished, let's move on to the legs.
legs (make 2)
the first leg:
rnd 1: 6sc into mr (6)
rnd 2: 6inc (12)
rnd 3-5: (3rnds) 12sc
rnd 6: 3sc, (sc,inc)*3, 3sc (15)
rnd 7-13: (7rnds) 15sc
rnd 14: 3sc, (sc,inc,sc)*3, 3sc (18)
rnd 15-16: 18sc
change dres's color yarn:
rnd 17-18: 18sc, slst. fasten off.
make the second leg: repeating round 1-18 but do not cut the yarn as we join the legs together to make the body. stuff both legs firmly.
remember friends legs will build the body!!
body
from second leg make a chain of 1 ch.
rnd 19: 18sc on next 18 stitches of first leg (start from the slst of first leg), sc on the chain, 18sc on 18 stitches of second leg, sc on the other side of the chain. (38)
continue working the body:
rnd 20: (5sc,inc)*3, sc, (5sc,inc)*3,sc (44)
rnd 21: (3sc,inc,3sc)*3,sc, (3sc,inc,3sc)*3,sc (50)
rnd 22-24: (3rnds) 50sc
rnd 25: sc, (2sc,dec,2sc)*8,sc (42)
rnd 26: 42sc
change body's color yarn:
rnd 27: blo 42sc
rnd 28: 42sc
rnd 29: sc, (3sc,dec)*8,sc (34)
rnd 30-31: (2rnds) 34sc
rnd 32: 2sc, (sc,dec)*10,2sc (24)
rnd 33: 24sc, slst.
leave a long tail for sewing. stuff the head with fiberfill.
and the body part of our amigurumi rabbit pattern has been completed. we can move on to her dress.
dress
rnd 1: at rnds 27 of body: flo and 3ch, (dc,2dc in one stitch). repeat on () more 21 times, slst.
rnd 2: 3ch, (2dc,2dc in one stitch)*21, slst. have 84 stitches in total.
rnd 3-4: (2rnds)3ch, 84dc, slst.
rnd 5: 1ch, 84sc, slst.
we have completed the dress of our amigurumi bunny character, we can start making her arms.
arms (make 2)
rnd 1: 5sc into mr (5)
rnd 2: 5inc (10)
rnd 3-5: (3rnds) 10sc
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)*2 (12)
rnd 7-10: (4rnds) 12sc
rnd 11: (sc,dec)*4 (8)
do not stuff, flatten and fold the top of the arm, sew the 2 opposed stitches.
fo. leave a long tail for sewing.
the arms of our amigurumi bunny character are also completed, next are those cute ears.
ears (make 2)
rnd 1: 5sc into mr (5)
rnd 2: 5inc (10)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*5 (15)
rnd 4-6: (3rnds) 15sc
rnd 7: (3sc,dec)*3 (12)
rnd 8-9: (2rnds) 12sc
rnd 10: (sc,dec)*4 (8)
do not stuff, flatten and fold the top of the ear, sew the 2 opposed stitches.
fo. leave a long tail for sewing.
the ears of our amigurumi rabbit character are also completed, we have crocheted the pattern step by step. it's ready now.
