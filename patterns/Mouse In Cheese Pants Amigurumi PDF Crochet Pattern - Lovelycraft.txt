materials:
yarns in three colors: gray, light yellow and yellow, (i use alize cotton gold 330m/100g)
2 mm crochet hook (or another convenient hook for you)
eyes 6 mm
toys stuffing
stitch maker
scissors
black thread and needles
abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc single crochet increase
dec single crochet decrease
blo working in back loops only
st stitch
slst slip stitch
(.)*x repeat x times
() number of stitches in the round.
toy gets filled while crochet
let's start making the cheese pants amigurumi mouse pattern
let's crochet the body and head of the amigurumi toy
head and body
start at the top oh the head(in off- gray)
rnd 1. 6 sc in a mr (6)
rnd 2. inc x 6 (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. 30 sc (30)
rnd 7. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 8. 36 sc (36)
rnd 9. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 10. 42 sc (42)
rnd 11. (6 sc, inc)*6 (48)
rnd 12. 48 sc (48)
rnd 13. (7 sc, inc)*6 (54)
rnd 14-19. (6 rounds) 54 sc (54)
rnd 20. (7 sc, dec)*6 (48)
rnd 21. (4 sc, dec)*8 (40)
insert safety eyes between rounds 14 and 15, 7 stitches apart.
rnd 22. (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 23. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
continue the body
rnd 24-26. (3 rounds) 24 sc (24)
rnd 27. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 28. 30 sc (30)
change to light yellow yarn.
rnd 29. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 30. blo- (2 sc, inc)*12 (48)
rnd 31-40. (10 rounds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 41. (4 sc, dec)*8 (40)
rnd 42. 40 sc (40)
rnd 43. (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 44. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
stuff the body
rnd 45. (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 46. dec*8 (8)
faster off leaving a yarn tail, tighten and pull the thread inside the body.
we have completed the body and head part of the amigurumi pattern. now let's crochet the arms and legs.
arms x 2
(in off gray)
rnd 1. 6 sc in a mr (6)
rnd 2-9. (8 rounds) 6 sc (6)
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing. don't stuff.
legs x 2
(in off gray)
rnd 1. 8 sc in a mr (8)
rnd 2-5. (4 rounds) 8 sc (8)
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing.
if you want your mouse to stand, then stuff the legs
-if you want your mouse to be seated, stuff only half of the legs.
let's crochet the big ears of the amigurumi toy.
ears x 2
(in off gray)
rnd 1. 8 sc in a mr (8)
rnd 2. inc*8 (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5-8. (4 rounds) 32 sc (32)
rnd 9. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
rnd 10-11. (2 rounds) 24 sc (24)
rnd 12. (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 13-14. (2 rounds) 16 sc (16)
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing. don't stuff.
let's crochet the tail of the amigurumi mouse and the pants.
tail
(in off gray)
ch 30
rnd 1. start in second ch from hook, 29 slst in all loops (29)
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing.
spots
(in off yellow)
3 different sizes
spot one:
rnd 1. 5 sc in a mr (5)
rnd 2. inc*5 (10)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*5 (15)
-slst in next st;
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing
spot two:
rnd 1. 5 sc in a mr (5)
rnd 2. inc*5 (10)
slst in next st;
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing.
spot three:
rnd 1. 6 sc in a mr (6)
slst in next st;
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing.
assembly and finishing touches
upper line of the pants:
find round 29 on the body, where you left out the front loops, crochet the upper line of the pants.
rnd 1. (3 sc, 3 sc in one st)*9 (54)
slst in next st.
tighten and pull the threads inside the body.
-embroider the nose and mouth
sew the spots, arms, legs and tail to the body
sew ears to the head
your mouse in cheese pants amigurumi pattern is ready.
