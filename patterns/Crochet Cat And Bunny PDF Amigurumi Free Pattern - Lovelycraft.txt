material
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
leg*2
start with white yarn
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6-inc (12)
rnd 3: (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-5: (2 rnds) 18 sc (18)
rnd 6: (5 sc, inc)*3 (21)
rnd 7-9: (3 rnds) 21 sc (21)
rnd 10: (6 sc, inc)*3 (24)
rnd 11-12: (2 rnds) 24 sc (24)
break the yarn. crochet the 2nd leg in the same way. do not break the yarn join first leg and continue to the body.
body
start with white yarn
rnd 1: (on 1 st leg) (7 sc, inc)*3, (on 2nd leg) (7 sc, inc)*3 (54)
rnd 2-3: (2 rnds) 54 sc (54), change color to make the shirt
rnd 4-8: (5 rnds) 54 (54)
rnd 9: (7 sc, dec)*6 (48)
rnd 10-11: (2 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 12: (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 13-14: (2 rnds) 42 sc (42)
rnd 15: (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 16-17: (2 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 18: (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 19-20: (2 rnds) 30 sc (30)
rnd 21: (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 22: blo 24 sc (24)
break the yarn. leave long tail for sewing.
bunny head
start with white yarn
rnd 1: 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2: 8 inc (16)
rnd 3: (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4: blo (2 sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5: (3 sc, inc)*2, (sc, inc)*8, (3 sc, inc)*2 (44)
rnd 6: (4 sc, inc)*2, (2 sc, inc)*8, (grey) (4 sc, inc)*2 (56)
rnd 7: (grey (5 sc, inc)*2, (white) (7 sc, inc)*3, 6 sc, (grey) sc, inc, (5 sc, inc)*2 (64)
rnd 8: (grey) 16 sc, (white) 29 sc, (grey) 19 sc (64)
rnd 9: (grey) 18 sc, (white) 25 sc, (grey) 21 sc (64)
rnd 10: (grey) 19 sc, (white) 23 sc, (grey) 22 sc (64)
rnd 11: (grey) 20 sc, (white) 21 sc, (grey) 23 sc (64)
rnd 12: (grey) 21 sc, (white) 19 sc, (grey) 24 sc (64)
rnd 13: (grey) 22 sc, (white) 17 sc, (grey) 25 sc (64)
rnd 14: (grey) 23 sc, (white) 15 sc, (grey) 26 sc (64)
rnd 15: (grey) 7 sc, dec, 6 sc, dec, 2 sc, dec, 3 sc, (white) 6 sc, dec, 5 sc, (grey) 3 sc, dec, 5 sc, dec, 6 sc, dec, 7 sc (57)
rnd 16: (grey) 16 sc, dec, 4 sc, (white) 4 sc, dec, 4 sc, (grey) 4 sc, dec, 19 sc (54)
rnd 17: (grey) 15 sc, dec, 5 sc, (white) 7 sc, (grey) 5 sc, dec, 18 sc (52)
rnd 18: (grey) 14 sc, dec, 6 sc, (white) 5 sc, (grey) 6 sc, dec, 17 sc (50)
rnd 19: (grey) 22 sc, (white) 3 sc, (grey) 25 sc (50)
rnd 20: (grey) 23 sc, (white) 1 sc, (grey) 26 sc (50)
rnd 21-23: (3 rnds) 50 sc (50)
rnd 24: (8 sc, dec)*5 (45)
rnd 25: 45 sc (45)
rnd 26: (3 sc, dec)*9 (36)
rnd 27: (2 sc, dec)*9 (27)
rnd 28: (sc, dec)*9 (18)
rnd 29: 9-dec (9)
break the yarn and weave in the end. insert the eyes at round 15, 16. 10 stitches apart.
cat head
crochet cat head in the same way till round 17.
rnd 18: (black) 22 sc, (white) 5 sc, (black) 25 sc (52)
rnd 19: (black) 23 sc, (white) 3 sc, (black) 26 sc (52)
rnd 20: (black) 24 sc, (white) 1 sc, (black) 27 sc (52)
rnd 21-22: (2 rnd) 52 sc (52)
rnd 23: (11 sc, dec)*4 (48)
rnd 24: (4 sc, dec)*8 (40)
rnd 25: (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 26: (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
rnd 27: (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 28: 8-dec (8)
break the yarn and weave in the end
arm*2
start with white yarn
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-5: (3 rnds) 12 sc (12)
change the color
rnd 6-20: (15 rnds) 12 sc (12)
break the yarn. leave long tail for sewing.
collar
start from rnd 22 on the body (the blo round)
make 2 chains
rnd 1: (2 dc, 1 dc-inc)*8, (32) 2 ch
rnd 2: (2 dc, 1 dc-inc)*8 (40), slst
break the yarn and wea ve in the end
cat ears
rnd 1: 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2: 4-inc (8)
rnd 3: (3 sc, inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4: (4 sc, inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5: (5 sc, inc)*2 (14)
rnd 6: (6 sc, inc)*2 (16)
rnd 7: (7 sc, inc)*2 (18)
rnd 8: (8 sc, inc)*2 (20), slst
break the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
bunny ears
rnd 1: 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2: 4-inc (8)
rnd 3: (3 sc, inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4: (4 sc, inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5: (5 sc, inc)*2 (14)
rnd 6: (6 sc, inc)*2 (16)
rnd 7-8: (2 rnds) 16 sc (16)
rnd 9: (6 sc, dec)*2 (14)
rnd 10: 14 sc (14)
rnd 11: (5 sc, dec)*2 (12)
rnd 12: 12 sc (12)
rnd 13: (4 sc, dec)*2 (10)
break the yarn. leave long tail for sewing
cat and bunny ready for you.
