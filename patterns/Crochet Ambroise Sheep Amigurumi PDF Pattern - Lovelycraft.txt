hello my dears. today i will share a beautiful crochet sheep pattern that you can use as a keychain. as you know, there are quite a lot of crochet keychain patterns on our site. if you want to examine other crochet keychain patterns, you can check our category. today's sheep amigurumi is a very cute and extremely simple model. there are a few points to note. other than that, you can do it easily. i think the hardest part of this amigurumi is making the wool part. after doing this part carefully, you will have completed the amigurumi.
crochet ambroise sheep amigurumi pdf free pattern
designer: mes.mignonneries
materials
cotton ricorumi : white (001), beige (054), pink (065)
crochet hook : 3mm
6mm safety eyes. you may need to use smaller/larger eyes, depending on hook/yarn size.
yarn needle
black embroidery thread
fiberfill for stuffing
scissors
size with the materials above will be about 9cm
abbreviations
rnd: round
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
sl st: slip stick
sc: single crochet
dc: double crocket
hdc: half double crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
head and body
stuff as you go along your work. in white.
rnd 1: mr, 6sc [6]
continue through back loops only.
rnd 2 : (inc)* [12]
rnd 3 : (1 sc, inc)* [18]
rnd 4 : (2 sc, inc )* [24]
rnd 5 : (3 sc, inc) * [30]
rnd 6 : (4 sc, inc) * [36]
rnd 7 : 36 sc [36]
continue into the two loops.
rnd 8 9 : 36 sc [36]
rnd 10 : (5sc, inc)* [42]
rnd11 14 : 42 sc [42]
insert the security eyes between rnd 10 and 11.
rnd 15 : (5 sc, dec) * [36]
rnd 16 : (4 sc, dec) * [30]
rnd 17 : (3 sc, dec) * [24]
rnd 18 : (2 sc, dec) * [18]
continue in white and through back loops only.
rnd 19 : (2 sc, inc)* [24]
rnd 20 : 24 sc [24]
rnd 21 : (3 sc, inc)* [30]
rnd 22 24 : 30 sc [30]
rnd 25 : (4 sc, inc)* [36]
rnd 26 29 : 36 sc [36]
rnd 30 : (5 sc inc)* [42]
rnd 31 : 42 sc [42]
rnd 32 : (5 sc, dec)* [36]
finish to stuff.
continue into the two loops.
rnd 33 : (2 sc, dec)* [27]
rnd 34 : (1 sc, dec)* [18]
rnd 35 : (dec)* [9]
rnd 36 : (1 sc, dec)* [6]
fasten off and close.
embroider nose and eyelashes with black embroidery thread.
sheep's wool
next you will crochet the wool using remaining flo from rnd 2 to rnd 7 and rnd 19 to rnd 31. with white yarn crochet as follow : (ch 4, sl st in next stitch)*
ears (x2)
inner ears (x2)
in pink
rnd 1: mr, 4 sc (4)
rnd 2 : (inc)* (8)
outer ears (x2)
in beige.
rnd 1: mr, 4 sc (4)
rnd 2 : (inc)* (2)
at the end of rnd 2, do not fasten off. we will now crochet the inner and outer ears together. place the backs of the inner and outer ears together.
rnd 3 : continuing from the end of rnd 2 of the outer ear, ch 1, sc in each of next 8 st through both the inner and outer ear layers.
sew the ears.
arms
in beige.
rnd 1 : mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2 : (1 sc , sc)* [6]
rnd 3 9 : 6 sc [6]
sew the ears and arms to the body
