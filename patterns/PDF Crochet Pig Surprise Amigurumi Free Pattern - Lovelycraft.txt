hands (make 2) / legs (make 2)
pink for the pig, blue for the sheep
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8 sc (8) back loop only
light pink for the pig, white for the sheep
rnd 3-8. 8 sc (8) 6 rows
the hands/ the legs are not filled too tightly with the stuffing material only up to row 4. flatten opening and crochet over both edges with 4 sc. fasten the thread, cut it off.
ears (make 2)
for sheep
white
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1 sc, inc) * 3 (9)
flatten opening and crochet over both edges with 1-2 sc
for pig
light pink
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (2 sc, inc) * 2 (8)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc) * 4 (12)
flatten opening and crochet over both edges with 6 sc.
the ears are not stuffed.
body
light pink for the pig, blue for the sheep.
the marker is perpendicular at the back of the body. if necessary, crochet another 1-2 sc and move the marker.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc) * 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) * 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) * 6 (30)
rnd 6. 5 sc, 4 sc together with the leg, 12 sc, 4 sc together with the leg, 5 sc (30)
rnd 7. 30 sc (30)
rnd 8. 30 sc (30) back loop only
rnd 9. (3 sc, dec) * 6 (24)
rnd 10. 24 sc (24)
rnd 11. 4 sc, 4 sc together with the hand, 8 sc, 4 sc together with the hand, 4 sc (24)
rnd 12. 24 sc (24)
rnd 13. (2 sc, dec) * 6 (18)
rnd 14. 18 sc (18)
rnd 15. (1 sc, dec) * 6 (12)
the body is filled very tightly with the stuffing material in the process of crocheting. insert the joint, and then crochet the decreases until the opening is tight around the leg of the joint. fasten the thread, cut it off.
head
light pink for the pig, white for the sheep.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc) * 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) * 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) * 6 (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc) * 6 (36)
put the head on the joint and lock the joint.
rnd 7-12. 36 sc (36) 6 rows
the head is filled very tightly with the stuffing material in the process of crocheting. insert the eyes between rows 9 10, leave 5 sc between the eyes from edge to edge. embroider the white parts near the eyes, the arrows for eyes (eyelashes). embroider a sheep's nose between rows 8-9, a sheep's mouth between rows 5- 8, a sheep's eyebrows in the 11th row. embroider a pig's mouth in the 5th row; the pig's eyebrows between rows 11-12 (see the photo below).
*use sewing threads for embroidery the eyebrows, the mouths, the arrows for eyes (eyelashes). use yarn. yarnart jeans for embroidery the white parts near the eyes.
rnd 13. (4 sc, dec) * 6 (30)
rnd 14. (3 sc, dec) * 6 (24)
rnd 15. (2 sc, dec) * 6 (18)
rnd 16. (1 sc, dec) * 6 (12)
rnd 17. 6 dec (6)
tighten the remaining stitches with a needle and the rest of the thread.
cap
blue. for the sheep
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc) * 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) * 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) * 6 (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc) * 6 (36)
rnd 7-10. 36 sc (36) 4 rows
if necessary, crochet 1-2 more rows. fasten the thread, cut it off.
pig's nose
light pink
rnd 1. 3 ch (3)
rnd 2. starting from 2nd chain from hook 1 sc, 3 sc in one loop.
on the reverse side of the chain inc, slst.
fasten the thread, cut it off. use black sewing thread to embroider stripes (nostrils). the pig's nose is not stuffed.
tail
for pig
light pink
rnd 1. 7 ch (7)
rnd 2. starting from 2nd chain from hook (3 sc in one loop) * 6 (18)
fasten the thread, cut it off.
for sheep
white
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
fasten the thread, cut it off.
skirt
fasten the thread, cut it off.
for sheep. white.
in the 8th row, crochet front loop only (3 dc in one loop) * 30 (90)
bow
white for sheep, blue- for pig
rnd 1. 6 ch (6)
rnd 2. starting from 2nd chain from hook 5 sc (5)
rnd 3-4. 5 sc (5) 2 rows
fasten the thread, cut it off. pull the thread in the center and fasten the thread, giving the shape of a bow.
pants
for pig
rnd 1. in the 8th row, crochet front loop only 30 sc (30)
rnd 2-5. 30 sc (30) 4 rows
divide the received part in half: 7 ch, slst at the opposite side of the pants.
crochet the pant leg:
rnd 6. 22 sc (22)
then, according to the same scheme, we crochet the pant leg. fasten the thread and cut it.
strap (make 2) 15 ch, fasten each strap in front and back of the pants (you can see the photo in the "assembling the toy" section).
assembling the toy
for pig: sew on the ears between rows 12-16. sew the pig's nose between rows 8-10. sew a bow on the neck. sew a tail on the pants.
sew the horns between rows 14-15. sew the ears between rows 11-12, under the horns. sew the hands between rows 10-13. sew the legs between rows 3-6. sew the tail in the 6th row.
for sheep: put the cap on the head two rows above the eyes. sew the ears on the cap in the lower rows. sew the bow on the side above the ear. sew the tail from behind under the skirt.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi pattern.
