materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2.5 mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
stitch markers
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
body
start with grey color
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (36)
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. 2 sc, inc, (6 sc, inc)*5, 4 sc (48)
rnd 9. 48 sc (48)
rnd 10. (7 sc, inc)*6 (54)
rnd 11-13. (2 rnds) 54 sc (54)
rnd 14. (8 sc, inc)*6 (60)
rnd 15. 60 sc (60)
rnd 16. (9 sc, inc)*6 (66)
rnd 17. (grey) 28 sc, (white) 8 sc, (grey) 30 sc (66)
rnd 18. (grey) (10 sc, inc)*2, 3 sc, (white) 7 sc, inc, 6 sc, (grey) 4 sc, inc, (10 sc, inc)*2 (72)
rnd 19. (grey) 26 sc, (white) 17 sc, (grey) 29 sc (72)
rnd 20. (grey) 25 sc, (white) 19 sc, (grey) 28 sc (72)
rnd 21. (grey) 24 sc, (white) 21 sc, (grey) 27 sc (72)
rnd 22. (grey) 23 sc, (white) 23 sc, (grey) 26 sc (72)
rnd 23. (grey) 22 sc, (white) 25 sc, (grey) 25 sc (72)
rnd 24. (grey) 22 sc, (white) 25 sc, (grey) 25 sc (72)
rnd 25-28. (grey) 22 sc, (white) 26 sc, (grey) 24 sc (72) (4 rnds)
rnd 29-31. (grey) 23 sc, (white) 25 sc, (grey) 24 sc (72) (3 rnds)
rnd 32. (grey) 24 sc, (white) 23 sc, (grey) 25 sc (72)
rnd 33. (grey) (10 sc, dec)*2, sc, (white) 9 sc, dec, 10 sc, (grey) dec, (10 sc, dec)*2 (66)
rnd 34. (grey) 25 sc, (white) 17 sc, (grey) 24 sc (66)
rnd 35. (grey) (9 sc, dec)*2, 4 sc, (white) 5 sc, dec, 8 sc, (grey) sc, dec, (9 sc, dec)*2 (60)
rnd 36. (grey) 26 sc, (white) 10 sc, (grey) 24 sc (60)
rnd 37. (grey until end) (8 sc, dec)*6 (54)
rnd 38. (7 sc, dec)*6 (48)
rnd 39. (2 sc, dec)*12 (36)
rnd 40. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 41. blo (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 42. (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 43. 6-dec (6)
leave long tail and weave in the end.
ear*2
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*2 (6)
rnd 3. (2 sc, inc)*2 (8)
rnd 4. (3 sc, inc)*2 (10)
rnd 5. (4 sc, inc)*2 (12)
rnd 6-7. 12 sc (12)
rnd 8. (4 sc, dec)*2 (10)
rnd 9. 10 sc (10)
rnd 10. (3 sc, dec)*2 (8)
rnd 11. (2 sc, dec)*2 (6)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
arm*2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-5. (2 rnds) 18 sc (18)
rnd 6. (7 sc, dec)*2 (16)
rnd 7-8. (2 rnds) 16 sc (16)
rnd 9. (6 sc, dec)*2 (14)
rnd 10-11. (2 rnds) 14 sc (12)
rnd 12. (5 sc, dec)*2 (12)
rnd 13. 12 sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
tail
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. 12 sc (12)
rnd 4. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 5. 18 sc (18)
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 7-9. (3 rnds) 24 sc (24)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing. embroidery nose and mouth and belly
totoro is ready now.
