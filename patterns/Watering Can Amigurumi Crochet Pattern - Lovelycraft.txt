watering can amigurumi crochet free pattern
designer
instagram: ann_lavka
tools & supplies
hook #2
cotton yarn- pink, blue, white, green
fiberfill
eyes 2 pieces
needle and thread
some black floss
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sts stitches
ch chain
sl st slip stitch
sc single crochet
inc increase (2 sc in one)
dec decrease (2 sc togerher)
dc double crochet
hdc half double crochet
()- repeat* times
let's start to crochet the amigurumi watering can
vessel:
rnd 1. 6 sc into mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6 sc, inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9. 48 sc back loop only
rnd 10-11. 48 sc
rnd 12. (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 13-16. 42 sc
rnd 17. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 18-21. 36 sc
rnd 22. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 23. 30 sc back loop only
rnd 24. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 25. 24 sc
rnd 26. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 27. (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 28. 6 dec (6)
we have completed the body part of the amigurumi pattern. let's make the spout part of the watering can
spout & rose:
start with white color
rnd 1. 6 sc into mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. 18 sc
change to blue color
rnd 5-6. 18 sc
rnd 7. (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 8-11. 12 sc
rnd 12. 1 inc, 11 sc (13)
rnd 13-16. 13 sc
remove the marker at the beginning of the row
rnd 17. 1 inc, 5 sc, 4 sl st, 2 sc, 4 dc, 1 sc, sl st
cut the end leaving a long tail for sewing
we have come to the most beautiful part of the amigurumi pattern. let's crochet our favorite flowers and decorate the watering can
flowers:
rnd 1. ch 3, 1 dc into third loop, ch 2, 1 sl st
rnd 2. ch 2 in the middle, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 sl st
rnd 3. ch 2, 1 dc, ch 2, sl st
fasten off and cut the ends, make 4 pink flowers and 4 white flowers.
leaf:
rnd 1. ch 4, 1 sc into the 2 loop from the hook, hdc, sl st into the last loop.
fasten off and cut the ends. make 5-8 leaves. sew the flowers on top
using thin thread and needle.
handle:
rnd 1. ch 22
rnd 2-3. 22 sc
fasten off, leave a long tail for sewing.
2 handle:
rnd 1. ch 5, join into a circle
rnd 2-19. 5 sc around
fasten off, leave a long tail for sewing.
cheeks:
make two pieces in pink color
rnd 1. 6 sc into mr
the eyes should be inserted between 6 and 7 row counting from the loops on the front of the base. sew on the nose and handles as shown in the photo. sew flowers and leaves on top in random order. sew on cheeks and embroider a smile with black thread.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi watering can
