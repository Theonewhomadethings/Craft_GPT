materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2mm 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
leg*2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5-6. (2rnds) 24sc (24)
change to black
rnd 7-13. (7rnds) 24sc (24)
cut the yarn, make the 2nd leg in the same way. do not cut the yarn.
make 6 chains.join in 1st leg and continue to the body.
body
rnd 1-2. (2rnds) 60sc (60)
change to yellow
rnd 3-7. (5rnds) 60sc (60)
rnd 8. (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 9-12. (3rnds) 54sc (54)
rnd 13. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 14-17. (4rnds) 48sc (48)
rnd 18. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 19-21. (3rnds) 42sc (42)
rnd 22. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 23-24. (2rnds) 36sc (36)
rnd 25. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 26-27. (2rnds) 30sc (30)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
head
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. blo (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*2,2sc,inc,3sc,inc
(3sc,inc,2sc,inc)*2,6sc,inc (51)
rnd 9. (7sc,inc)*2,3sc,inc,4sc,inc,
(4sc,inc,3sc,inc)*2,7sc,inc (60)
rnd 10. 27sc,inc,5sc,inc,4sc,inc,6sc,inc,
14sc (64)
rnd 11-17. (7rnds) 64sc (64)
rnd 18. 21sc,dec,4sc,dec,5sc,dec,4sc,dec,6sc,
dec,3sc,dec,9sc (58)
rnd 19. 26sc,dec,4sc,dec,3sc,dec,4sc,dec,13sc (54)
rnd 20-28. (9rnds) 54sc (54)
insert the eyes between round 19-20 6sc apart.
rnd 29. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 30. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 31. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 32. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 33. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 34. (sc,dec)*8 (16)
rnd 35. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn, weave in the end. sew the head to the body at the blo round
on the head (rnd 6)
arm*2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
rnd 4-5. (2rnds) 15sc (15)
change to yellow
rnd 6-17. (12rnds) 15sc (15)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
ear*2
rnd 1. 7sc in mr (7)
rnd 2. turn yoyr hook, 7sc,slst. (7)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
scarf
cast on 46 stitches
rnd 1. 1k,1p,1k,1p untill the end (46)
2-20. repeat first round.
place the scarf on the bear and sew both edges together. sew the button on scarf.
