crochet cute teddy bear pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: tashkatoys
materials
himalaya dolphin baby yarn or other analog plush yarn (1/2 just skein)
semi- cotton nose thread
eyes: 10 mm
crochet hook: 4.5 mm and 3 mm (for nose)
needle, needle tailors
scissors
fiberfiller
the height of the teddy bears amigurumi turns out to be 17-18 cm, however, depending on the yarn and hook you choose, your density, the growth the toy may be different.
the head, tail ears and nose are sewn on. the paws are all knitted in, the nose can be replaced with a plastic one with a secure attachment.
arms (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 sc
rnd 3. color change and 6 sc
rnd 4. 2sc, dec, 1sc, inc (6)
rnd 5-7. 6 sc
rnd 8. 1sc, half and 3sc
legs (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. color change and 12 sc
rnd 4. 12 sc
rnd 5. 3sc,(dec)*3, 3sc (9)
rnd 6. 2sc,(dec)*2, 3sc (7)
(not necessary, but so that the right and left legs are visible, on the second leg we knit the opposite row: 3sc, (dec)*2, 2sc. similarly in the 9th row)
rnd 7-8. 7 sc
rnd 9. 3sc,dec,2sc (6)
rnd 10. 1sc, half and 3sc
ears
rnd 1. 5sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*5 (10)
rnd 3. 10 sc
tail
rnd 1. 6sc in mr
rnd 2. 6sc
nose
crochet from yarnart jeans 3mm hook
rnd 1. 6sc in mr
rnd 2. inc, 1sc, inc, 3sc (8)
rnd 3. 2sc, (inc,2sc)*2 (10)
body
rnd 1. 6sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-7. 18 sc (in row 6 we knit legs: 1sc, 3sc with leg, 6sc, 3sc with leg, 5sc)
rnd 8. (1sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 9. 12sc
rnd 10. (3sc,dec)*2 (10)
rnd 11. 10sc (knit arms: 1sc,3sc with arms, 3sc, 3sc with arms)
rnd 12. 2sc and 10 slst
we sew the head to the body, fixing it with knitting needles.
sew on the tail between area of 6-47 rows.
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3. inc, 4sc, (inc)*2, 4sc, inc (16)
rnd 4. color change and 16 slst
rnd 5. (inc)*6, 10sc (22)
rnd 6. 5sc, (inc)*2, 15sc (24)
rnd 7-10. 24 sc
rnd 11. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 12. (1sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 13. (dec)*6
we fill with filler during knitting.
we insert the eyes in the 5th row at a distance of 5sc (approximately the 3rd and 9th loops from the beginning of the row)
we make a tie, sew on the nose and embroider the muzzle.
note: since the creator of the amigurumi pattern did not share the sweater pattern of the teddy bear, we did not include it in the description. when the creator shares, the amigurumi pattern description will be updated.
