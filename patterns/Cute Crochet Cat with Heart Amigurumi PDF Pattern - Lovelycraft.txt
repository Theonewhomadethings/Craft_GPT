hello my dears. i am with you again today to share a wonderful cat amigurumi pattern. i haven't shared a crochet cat pattern for a long time. today i would like to make a nice presentation to you with a beautiful cat amigurumi pattern. today's free amigurumi pattern consists of cat and heart patterns. you can find these two patterns later in the article. as for the construction, it is a very easy pattern. now if you're ready, let's get start. let's crochet the cat amigurumi step by step.
pdf cute crochet cat with heart amigurumi pattern
designer: beary_bearnita_
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2mm-2.5mm
safety eyes size 5-6mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
head
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) * 8 (24)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) * 8 (32)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3sc, inc) * 7, 2sc (40)
rnd 6. (4sc, inc) * 8 (48)
rnd 7. 48sc (48)
rnd 8. 2sc, inc, (5sc, inc) * 7, 3sc (56)
rnd 9. 56sc (56)
rnd 10. (6sc, inc) * 8 (64)
rnd 11-16. (6 rnds) 64sc (64)
rnd 17. (7sc, inc) * 8 (72)
rnd 18-20. (3 rnds) 72sc (72)
rnd 21. (7sc, dec) * 8 (64)
rnd 22. 64sc (64)
rnd 23. (6sc, dec) * 8 (56)
rnd 24. (5sc, dec) * 8 (48)
rnd 25. (4sc, dec) * 8 (40)
rnd 26. (3sc, dec) * 8 (32)
rnd 27. (2sc, dec) * 8 (24)
rnd 28. blo (sc, dec) * 8 (16)
rnd 29. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
leg * 2
start with white
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc) * 6 (12)
rnd 3. (2sc, inc) * 6 (18)
rnd 4. (5sc, inc) * 3 (21)
rnd 5-6. (2 rnds) 21sc (21)
change to pink
rnd 7. 21sc (21)
cut the yarn, crochet the 2nd leg in the same way. do not cut the yarn, join in 1st leg and crochet the body.
body
rnd 1. 42sc (42)
rnd 2. (6sc, inc) * 6 (48)
rnd 3. 48sc (48)
rnd 4. (7sc, inc) * 6 (54)
rnd 5-10. (6 rnds) 54sc (54)
rnd 11. (7sc, dec) * 6 (48)
rnd 12-16. (5 rnds) 48sc (48)
rnd 17. (6sc, dec) * 6 (42)
rnd 18-19. (2 rnds) 42sc (42)
rnd 20. (5sc, dec) * 6 (36)
rnd 21-22. (2 rnds) 36sc (36)
rnd 23. (4sc, dec) * 6 (30)
rnd 24. 30sc (30)
rnd 25. (3sc, dec) * 6 (24)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail, sew to the head at the blo round.
arm * 2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-5. (3 rnds) 12sc (12)
change to pink
rnd 6-17. (12 rnds) 12sc (12)
cut the yam, leave a long tail for sewing
ear * 2
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4-inc (8)
rnd 3. (3sc, inc) * 2 (10)
rnd 4. (4sc, inc) * 2 (12)
rnd 5. (ssc, inc) * 2 (14)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing
heart
first piece
1. 6sc in mr (6)
2. 6-inc (12)
3. (sc, inc) * 6 (18)
4. (5sc, inc) * 3 (21)
cut the yarn, crochet 2nd piece in the some way. do not cut the yam, join to 1st piece
5-6. 42sc (42)
7. (5sc, dec) * 6 (36)
8. 36sc (36)
9. (4sc, dec) * 6 (30)
10. 30sc (30)
11. (3sc, dec) * 6 (24)
12. 24sc (24)
13. (2sc, dec) * 6 (18)
14. (sc, dec) * 6 (12)
15. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn, fasten off.
