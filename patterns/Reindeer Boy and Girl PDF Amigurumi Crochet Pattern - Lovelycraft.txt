abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
flo front loop only
let's start making an amigurumi reindeer pattern:)
first, let's crochet the girl reindeer pattern.
girl pattern
leg (make 2)
with beige yarn
rnd 1. mr 6 (magic circle) (6)
rnd 2. inc all around (12)
rnd 3-6. sc all around (12)
don't cut the yarn when you'll finish the second leg. continue on the second leg: crochet 2 ch from the second leg & join it with the first leg.
body
with the beige yarn
rnd 1. 12 sc on the first leg, 2 sc in next 2 ch, 12 sc on the second leg, 2 sc in next 2 ch (opposite side) (28)
rnd 2. 12 sc, 2 inc, 14 sc (30)
rnd 3. sc all around (30) back loops
rnd 4-8. sc all around (30)
rnd 9. (3 sc dec) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 10-11. sc all around (24)
rnd 12. (2 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (18)
continue with beige yarn
rnd 13. sc all around (18)
we crocheted amigurumi reindeer legs and body. now let's crochet the skirt part. red thread is used for the skirt.
skirt
join red yarn to the body rnd 3:
rnd 1. flo (sc, inc)*repeat to the end of rows
rnd 2. sc in each st to the end of row
after completing the skirt part, let's crochet the head part and the ears.
head
with beige yarn
rnd 1. mr 6 (magic circle) (6)
rnd 2. inc all around (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 8-13. sc all around (42)
rnd 14. (5 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 15. (4 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 16. (3 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 17. (2 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 18. sc all around (18)
ears (make 2)
with beige yarn
rnd 1. mr 5 (magic circle) (5)
rnd 2. sc all around (5)
rnd 3. inc all around (5)
rnd 4. (4 sc, inc) repeat 2 times (12)
rnd 5-7. sc all around (12)
rnd 8. (4 sc, dec) repeat 2 times (10)
let's crochet the mouth and nose of the amigurumi pattern. if you are preparing the amigurumi toy for christmas, i recommend using red and white yarn.
muzzle

nose
