crochet mouse pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: marizzatoys
materials
sport / 5 ply yarn white (yarnart jeans 62), yellow (yarnart jeans 67), beige (yarnart jeans 07)
brown or black thread to embroider eyebrows
safety eyes 7mm
crochet hook 2,00 mm
stuffing (polyester fiber fill)
with these materials, the finished crochet mouse is 17 cm tall.
abbreviations
mr magic ring
st stitch
ch chain
sl st slip stitch
sc single crochet
tr treble crochet
inc increase (crochet 2 sc in one loop)
dec decrease (crochet 2 sc together)
(sc, inc) x n repeat those in brackets n-times
first we make the legs of the amigurumi.
left leg
start crocheting with white yarn.
stuff as you go.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. 5 sc, inc, 6 sc (13)
rnd 4. 6 sc, inc, 6 sc (14)
rnd 5. 7 sc, inc, 6 sc (15)
rnd 6. 7 sc, inc, 7 sc (16)
rnd 7. 7 sc, inc, 8 sc (17)
rnd 8. 8 sc, inc, 8 sc (18)
rnd 9. 18 sc
change yarn to yellow color:
rnd 10-11. 18 sc (2 rounds)
fasten off, cut the yarn.
after making the left leg, we make the right leg.
right leg
start crocheting with white yarn.
stuff as you go.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. 11sc, inc (13)
rnd 4. 12sc, inc (14)
rnd 5. 13sc, inc (15)
rnd 6. 14sc, inc (16)
rnd 7. 15sc, inc (17)
rnd 8. 16sc, inc (18)
rnd 9. 18sc
change yarn to yellow color:
rnd 10-11. 18 sc (2 rounds)
don't cut the yarn, we will continue with the body.
after making the legs, we make the body of the amigurumi mouse.
body
rnd 1. crochet 8 sc on the right leg: make 3 chain: 18 sc on the left leg: 3 sc on the chain: 18 sc on the right leg: 3 sc on the chain: put the marker here (42)
rnd 2. (6sc,inc) repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 3-9. 48 sc (7 rounds), change the yarn to white color:
rnd 10. 48 sc
rnd 11. (6sc,dec) repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 12-13. 42 sc (2 rounds)
rnd 14. (5sc,dec) repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 15-16. 36 sc (2 rounds)
rnd 17. (4sc,dec) repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 18-19. 30 sc (2 rounds)
rnd 20. (3sc,dec) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 21-22. 24 sc (2 rounds)
fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing.
stuff the body firmly, especally in the neck.
after making the body of the amigurumi, we make the head.
head
start crocheting with beige yarn.
stuff as you go.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. 6 dec (6), change the yarn to white color:
rnd 4. 6 sc (6)
rnd 5. (sc,inc) repeat 3 times (9)
rnd 6. (2sc,inc) repeat 3 times (12)
rnd 7. (3sc,inc) repeat 3 times (15)
rnd 8. (4sc,inc) repeat 3 times (18)
rnd 9. 6sc, 6inc, 6sc (24)
rnd 10. 6sc, (sc,inc) repeat 6 times , 6sc (30)
rnd 11. 6sc, (2sc,inc) repeat 6 times , 6sc (36)
rnd 12. (5sc,inc) repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 13. (6sc,inc) repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 14. (7sc,inc) repeat 6 times (54)
insert the eyes in round 10, inside the first and sixt increase.
rnd 15-22. 54 sc (8 rounds)
rnd 23. (7sc,dec) repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 24. 3sc, dec, (6sc,dec) repeat 5 times, 3sc (42)
rnd 25. (5sc,dec) repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 26. 2sc, dec, (4sc,dec) repeat 5 times, 2sc (30)
rnd 27. (3sc,dec) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 28. sc, dec, (2sc,dec) repeat 5 times, sc (18)
rnd 29. (sc,dec) repeat 6 times (12)
rnd 30. 6 dec (6)
make sure that the head is stuffed firmly, especially in the muzzle.
close the opening and hide the yarn inside.
embroider the eyebrows between rounds 10-11.
sew the head to the body between rounds 10-18.
we have now completed the construction of large parts. next up are the arms.
arms
crochet with white yarn.
stuff halfway.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3-17. 12 sc (15 rounds)
fasten off leaving a long tallfor sewing.
sew the arms in round 20.
now we will make the tail.
tail
with white yarn make 16 ch.
crochet 15 sc on the chain.
fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing.
sew the tail on the back between rounds 5-6.
let's make the ears now.
ears
crochet with white yarn.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4. sc, inc, (2 sc, inc) repeat 5 times, sc (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. 30 sc
fold the ear in half and crochet 1 sc grabbing the both sides.
fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing.
sew the ears where you want them to be.
i sew them right after round 18.
after you make the bow tie, it will be over.
bowtie
crochet with beige yarn.
make a magic ring and crochet in it: 3 ch, 2 tr, 3 ch, sl st, 3 ch, 2 tr, 3 ch, sl st.
tighten the magic ring and cut the thread, leaving a long tail for sewing.
roll the yarn around the center of the bowtie.
sew the bowtie in round 21.
straps
with yellow yarn make 35 ch (or as you need).
crochet 34 sc on the chain.
fasten off, leavig a long tail for sewing.
sew the straps to the body, crossing them on the back.
