designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc hald double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn.
crochet hook: 2mm- 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
leg*2
start with white yarn
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-7. (4 rnds) 18sc (18)
cut the yarn, make the 2nd leg in the same way till rnd 7.
do not cut the yarn, make 2 chains, join in 1st leg.
continue to the body.
body
rnd 1. (4sc,inc)*8 (48)
rnd 2. (11sc,inc)*4 (52)
rnd 3-6. (4rnds) 52sc (52)
change to pink
rnd 7. blo 52sc (52)
rnd 8-11. (4rnds)52sc (52)
rnd 12. (11sc,dec)*4 (48)
rnd 13-15. (3rnds) 48sc (48)
rnd 16. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 17. 42sc (42)
rnd 18. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 19. 36sc (36)
rnd 20. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
skirt
insert your hook to the blo round on the
body (rnd7) make 2 chains
rnd 1. (3dc,dc-inc)*13 (65)
rnd 2. 65-dc (65)
head
start with white yarn
rnd 1. 7sc in mr (7)
rnd 2. 7-inc (14)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*7 (21)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*7 (28)
rnd 5. sc,inc,(3sc,inc)*6, 2sc (35)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*7 (42)
rnd 7. 2sc,inc, (5sc,inc)*6,3sc (49)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*7 (56)
rnd 9. 3sc,inc, (7sc,inc)*6,4sc (63)
rnd 10. (8sc,inc)*7 (70)
rnd 11-25. (15rnds) 70sc (70)
insert the eyes between round 19, 20. 10sc apart.
rnd 26. (8sc,dec)*7 (63)
rnd 27. (7sc,dec)*7 (56)
rnd 28. (6sc,dec)*7 (49)
rnd 29. (5sc,dec)*7 (42)
rnd 30. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 31. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail and sew to the body.
ear*2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6- inc (12)
rnd 3. (5sc,inc)*2 (14)
rnd 4. (6sc,inc)*2 (16)
rnd 5-8. (4rnds) 16sc (16)
rnd 9. (6sc,dec)*2 (14)
rnd 10. 14sc (14)
rnd 11. (5sc,dec)*2 (12)
rnd 12. 12sc (12)
rnd 13. (4sc,dec)*2 (10)
slst. cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
arm*2
start with white yarn
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6- inc mr (12)
rnd 3-6. (4rnds) 12sc (12)
change to pink
rnd 7-15. (9rnds) 12sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
