designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn.
crochet hook: 2mm or 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
safety eyes size 5mm
leg*2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-4. 12sc (12)
cut the yarn.
make the 2nd leg in the same way till rnd 4.
do not cut the yarn, make 12ch, join in 1st leg.
continue to the body.
body
rnd 1-8. (8 rnds) 48sc (48)
change to color
rnd 9-18. (10 rnds) 48sc (48)
rnd 19. (10sc,dec)*4 (44)
change to color. continue to the head.
head
rnd 1. 44sc (44)
rnd 2. 11sc, inc, (3sc,inc)*4,16sc (49)
rnd 3. 17sc, inc, (4sc,inc)*2,21sc (52)
rnd 4-6. (3rnds) 52sc (52)
rnd 7. 17sc,dec, (4sc,dec)*2,21sc (49)
rnd 8. 17sc,dec, (3sc,dec)*2,20sc (45)
insert the safety eyes at round 8,9. 7sc apart.
rnd 9-13. (5rnds) 45sc (45)
rnd 14. (7sc,dec)*5 (40)
rnd 15-16. (2rnds) 40sc (40)
rnd 17. (3sc,dec)*8 (32)
rnd 18. (2sc,dec)*8 (24)
rnd 19. (sc,dec)*8 (16)
rnd 20. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
arm*2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-4. (2rnds) 12sc (12)
change to color
rnd 5-13. (9rnds) 12sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
ear*2
make 8ch, start to crochet from the 2nd stitch from hook. 5sc,
hdc, 4-hdc in last chain, hdc, 5sc, slst cut the yarn.
leave long tail for sewing.
