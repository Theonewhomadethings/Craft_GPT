cute unicorn amigurumi free crochet pattern
cute crochet unicorn has curly hair and horns. the most striking part of the toy is its wings on the back. this detail is well thought out. his tail is curly braided into curls. this model is done in the same way on her hair. it made the toy look cuter. you need to prepare the materials and make this toy immediately.
designer
instagram: anan.amilove
ravelry: anan.amilove
material:
1. yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn (i used jeans from yarnart).
2. hook: size 2.5mm
3. polyester fiberfill
4. stitch markers
abbreviations:
rnd: round
inc: increase
ch: chain
dec: decrease
sc: single crochet
sl st: slip stitch
hdc: half double crochet
size:
if you use yarn as me, the finish doll will be 30 cm / 11.81 inch (without horn)

sew the eyes between rows 22-23
ear (make 2)
rnd 1: 5 sc in the magic ring (5)
rnd 2: 5 sc (5)
rnd 3: 5 inc (10)
rnd 4: 10 sc (10)
rnd 5: (1 sc, inc)*5 (15)
rnd 6: 15 sc (15)
rnd 7: (2 sc, inc)*5 (20)
rnd 8: 20 sc (20)
rnd 9: (3 sc, inc)*5 (25)
rnd 10: 25 sc, sl st. (25)
fasten the yarn, leaving a long tail for sewing.
do not stuff the ear, flatten and fold the top of the ear, sew the 2 opposite stitches.
put in the ears between rows 27-32.
horn
rnd 1: 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2: 6 sc (6)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 4 5: 9 sc (2 rounds) (9)
rnd 6: (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
rnd 7 8: 12 sc (2 rounds) (12)
rnd 9: (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd 10 11: 15 sc (2 rounds) (15)
fasten off the yarn. leaving a long tail for sewing.
stuff the horn. put in the horns between rows 23-29.

rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (inc, 2 sc)*6 (24)

leg (make 2)
rnd 1: 6 sc in the magic ring (6)

swing (make 2)
rnd 1: 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: 12 sc (12)
rnd 4: (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 5: 18 sc (18)
rnd 6: (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 7 8: 24 sc (2 rounds) (24)
small feather
rnd 1: 6 sc and join first stitch to make a ring (6)
rnd 2: 6 sc, sl st. (6)
fasten off the yarn. weave in the yarn end
medium feather
rnd 1: sc in next 4 stitch and sc in next 4 opposite stitch to make a ring (8)
rnd 2 3: 8 sc (2 rounds) (8)
rnd 4: (2 sc, dec)*2, sl st (6)
fasten off the yarn. weave in the yarn end.
big feather
rnd 1: sc in next 10 remaining stitch (10)
rnd 2 4: 10 sc (3 rounds) (10)
rnd 5: (3 sc, dec)*2 (8)
rnd 6: (2 sc, dec)*2, sl st. (6)
fasten off the yarn. weave in the yarn end
hair (make 11)
make 68 ch, start third ch from hook: 66 hdc (66)
fasten off the yarn. leaving a long tail for sewing.
tail (make 4)
make 42 ch, start third ch from hook: 40 hdc (40)
fasten off the yarn. leaving a long tail for sewing.
heart
make magic ring: 2 ch, 2 hdc, 2 sc, 1 ch, 1 hdc, 1 ch, 2 sc, 2 hdc, 2 ch, sl st in the
magic ring. fasten off the yarn. leaving a long tail for sewing
