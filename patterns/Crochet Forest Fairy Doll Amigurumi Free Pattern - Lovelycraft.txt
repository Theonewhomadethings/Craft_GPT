abbreviations
ch: chain
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
slst: slip stitch
hdc: half double crochet
dc: double crochet
st/sts: stitch/stitches
tr: triple crochet
blo: back loops only
flo: fornt loops only
bobble: bobble stitch
( ) : the parentheses at the end of the row give the total number of stitches of that round.
[ ]x.. : repeat instructions x times
legs (with skin color yarn and make 2)
rnd 1 start 6sc in a magic ring and join with skin sl st (6) please look through "techniques section" to learn about "magic ring" join with a slst. do not forget to use your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3-15 sc in all 12 sts (13 rounds)
rnd 16 [dec] repeat 2 times, sc in next 3st, [inc] repeat 2 times, sc in next 3st (12)
rnd 17 sc in next 12sts
rnd 18 [dec] repeat 2 times, sc in next 3st, [inc] repeat 2 times, sc in next 3st (12)
rnd 19-28 sc in all 12 sts (13 rounds)
**stuff the leg.
body (with skin color yarn)
with skin colour
rnd 29 to join two legs; ch 8,join to the middle of the other leg with a sl st and sc in the next 12 sts of the leg, flo sc over the ch 8 stitches,sc in the next 12 sts, and flo sc over the ch 8 sts (40)
rnd 30-34 sc in all 40 sts (5 rounds)
rnd 35 [sc in the next 19 sts, inc] repeat 2 times (42)
rnd 36-40 sc in all 42 sts (5 rounds)

rnd 42-48 sc in all 36 sts (7 rounds)
rnd 49 [dec] repeat 18 times (18)
rnd 50-54 sc in all 18 sts (5 rounds)
**stuff the body.
