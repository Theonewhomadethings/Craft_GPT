crochet baby piglet similar to the cartoon character piglet. the colors are the same and it is a very cute amigurumi. the body part is knitted in dark and light pink colors. the arms, legs and face are knitted in a lighter pink tone. this wonderful doll is very suitable for home decoration. it will be great decor for every room of the house. don't forget to add the wonderful pattern to your favorite list.
pdf baby piglet amigurumi crochet free pattern
designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
ravelry: beary_bearnita_
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
blo back loops only
slst slip stitch
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm 2.5 mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
safety eyes size 5 mm
leg*2
start with skin color
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. 18 sc (18)
rnd 5. (5 sc, inc)*3 (21)
rnd 6-7. (2 rnds) 21 sc (21)
crochet the 2 nd leg in the same way till rnd 7. do not cut the yarn. join in 1 st leg.
continue to the body.
body
rnd 1. (6 sc, inc)*66 (48)
change to pink
rnd 2-4. (3 rnds)48 sc (48)
change to dark pink
rnd 5. 48 sc (48)
change to pink
rnd 6-7. 48 sc (48)
rnd 8. (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
change to dark pink
rnd 9. 42 sc (42)
change to pink
rnd 10. 42 sc (42)
rnd 11. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 12. 36 sc(36)
change to dark pink
rnd 13. 36 sc (36)
change to pink
rnd 14. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 15-16. (2 rnds) 30 sc (30)
change to dark pink
rnd 17. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
change to pink
rnd 18. 24 sc, slst
cut the yarn and leave long tail for sewing.
head
start with skin color
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*2, (sc, inc)*8, (3 sc, inc)*2 (44)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)*2, (2 sc, inc)*8, (4 sc, inc)*2 (56)
rnd 7-12. (6 rnds) 56 sc (56)
rnd 13. 18 sc, dec, (6 sc, dec)*2, 20 sc (53)
rnd 14. 17 sc, dec, (5 sc, dec)*2, 20 sc (50)
rnd 15. 16 sc, dec, (4 sc, dec)*2, 20 sc (47)
insert the safety eyes between rnd 14, 15.
7 sc apart
rnd 16-21. (6 rnds) 47 sc (47)
rnd 22. (6 sc, dec)*5, 7 sc (42)
rnd 23. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 24. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 25. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 26. (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 27. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
arm*2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-11. (9 rnds) 12 sc (12)
rnd 12. blo 12 sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
ear*2
start with pink
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*2 (6)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
rnd 5-7. (3 rnds) 12 sc (12)
rnd 8. (4 sc, dec)*2 (10)
rnd 9. (3 sc, dec)*2 (8)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing. embroidery nose and mouth. piglet is ready now.
