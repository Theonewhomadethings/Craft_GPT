designer: mwl_crochet
materials
yarn of the following colors: light brown, dark brown, white, yellow and black.
crochet hook 3.5 mm
stuffing
8 mm security eyes
wooden stick or chopstick to support the head.
stuff as you go, the arms don't need stuffing.
this pattern is a desing of made with love crochet. you can share it by giving credit to its original designer, you can sell the finished doll but this pattern cannot be sold.
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
trc: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
sl : slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
arms
light brown
r1. mr. 6 sc
r2. r12. 6 sc
r13. 1 sl, fold and join the sides with 2 sc.
make 2 arms
legs
white
r1. mr, 8 sc
r2. r6. 8 sc
switch to light brown
r7. r22. 8 sc
switch to white
r23. 8 sc
make an extra sc in the first leg so the color change doesn't show. continue the next leg but don't cut the yarn.
snout
light brown
r1. mr, 6 dc
r2 6 inc (12)
r3 1 sc, inc (18)
r4 18 sc
embroider the some lines with black yarn.
body
white
keep working on the second leg.
r1 3 sc, chain 3, join the legs, 8 sc, 3 sc, 5 sc (22)
r2 22 sc
r3 10 sc, inc, 10 sc, inc (24)
r4 r6. 24 sc
r7 blo (dec, 8 sc, dec, 12 sc) (22)
r8 22 sc
r9 dec, 7 sc, dec, 11 sc (20)
r10 20 sc
r11 dec, 6 sc, dec, 10 sc (18)
r12 18 sc
r13 (dec, 1 sc)*6 (12)
r14 join the arms in this round with 2 sc each,
leave 4 stitches between
them. (12)
r15 12 sc change to light brown
r14 6 dec (6)
r15 6 sc
head
light brown
r1. 6 inc (12)
r2 12 inc (24)
r3 (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
r4 2 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*5, 2 sc (36)
r5 (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
r6 3 sc, inc, (6 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (48)
r7- r16
place the eyes in rounds 11-12, leave 8 stitches between them. embroider a black line to make then look better. sew the snout in rounds 5 to 11, just in between the eyes.
r17 3 sc, dec, (6 sc, dec)*5, 3 sc (42)
r18 (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
r19 2 sc, dec, (4 sc, dec)*5, 2 sc (30)
r20 (3 sc, dec)*6 (24) place the sitick to support the head. make sure it is firm and keep stuffing as you close the head.
r21 1 sc, dec, (2 sc, dec)*5, 1 sc (18)
r22 (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
r23 6 dec (6)
close the head and cut the yarn.
dress
white yarn
go back to round 6, pass the yarn in one of the stitches in the back, chain 2 and cotinue. every row finishes with a sl and begins with chain 2 (does not count as a stitch).
row 1. 24 inc in dc (48)
row 2. (1 dc, inc)*24 (72)
row 3. row 4. 72 dc. cut the yarn and weave.
ears
light brown
r1. mr, 4 sc
r2 4 inc (8)
r3 (1 sc, inc)*4 (12)
r4 r7. 12 sc
r8. (1 sc, dec)*4 (8)
make 2 ears and sew them on the sides of the head in rows 16 and 17. as a guide you can count 6 stitches from the eyes to place the ears. emborider on the forehead 5 white "v" shapes.
horns
dark brown
small horn
r1. mr, 5 sc
r2. r5. 5 sc
cut the yarn and make 2
big horn
r1. mr, 5 sc
r2. r7. 5 sc
r8 join the smal horn, 10 sc
r9 10 sc
r10 5 dec (5) repeat the process. sew the horns 2 rounds up from the ears.
daisy flower
yellow
r1. mr, 4 sc, close with a sl and chain 1.
r2 4 inc, close with a sl changing to white
r3 chain 4, 2 sc, chain 1, 1 sl on the yellow part. repeart until you have 8 petals.
sew or glue the flower in front of one horn. you can also make a flower crown using a thinner yarn just make 3 of 4 and place them.
