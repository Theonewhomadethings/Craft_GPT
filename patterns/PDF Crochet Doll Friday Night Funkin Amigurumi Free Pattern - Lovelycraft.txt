abbreviations
sc single crochet
slst slip stitch
inc increase
dec decrease
blo back loop only
flo front loop only
ch chain
arms (x2) (skin)
rnd 1. mr 8 sc (8)
rnd 2-12. 8 sc (8)
to form the fingers
rnd 13. join the sc of the 8 go to 4 sc. to form the thumb, ch 4 and return with 3 sc
rnd 14. a row of 3 sc
rnd 15. 3 ch, and return with 2 sc for little finger and ch 4 for other fingers
after completing the arms of the amigurumi doll, let's crochet the body part.
body
legs x 2, skin color
rnd 1. 6 ch (6)
rnd 2. 4 sc, 3 sc in the same sc, 4 sc, 3 sc in the last stitch (14)
rnd 3. 1 inc, 3 sc, 3 inc, 3 sc, 2 inc (18)
rnd 4. 18 sc
rnd 5. 5 sc, 5 dec, 3 sc (13)
rnd 6. 6 sc, 1 dec, 5 sc (12)
rnd 7-14. 12 sc (12)
rnd 15. join both legs with 3 ch (30)
rnd 16-17. 30 sc (30)
rnd 18. 3 sc, 1 dec (24)
rnd 19-22. 24 sc (24)
rnd 23. 2 sc, 1 dec (18)
rnd 24-25. 18 sc (18)
rnd 26. 2 sc, 1 dec (12)
rnd 27-31. 12 sc (12)
rnd 32. 6 dec (6)
head (skin)
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (36)
rnd 7. (5 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 8. (6 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (48)
rnd 9-11. 48 sc (48)
rnd 12. (11 sc, 1 inc) x 4 (52)
rnd 13-15. 52 sc (52)
rnd 16. 4 sc, (2 sc, 1 inc) x 6, 36 sc (58)
rnd 17-20. 58 sc (58)
rnd 21. (12 sc, 1 dec) x 4, 1 sc (54)
rnd 22. (7 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (48)
rnd 23. (6 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (42)
rnd 24. (5 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 25. (4 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (30)
rnd 26. (3 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (24)
rnd 27. (2 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (18)
rnd 28. (1 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (12)
we have completed the head part of the amigurumi doll. now let's crochet ears
ears (skin)
rnd 1. mr 4 sc (4)
rnd 2. (1 sc, 1 inc) x 2 (6)
rnd 3. (2 sc, 1 inc) x 2 (8)
rnd 4-5. 8 sc (8)
rnd 6. join both sides (4 sc)
let's crochet amigurumi funkin doll's hair
hair (calypso or light blue)
each point is a lock of hair, make the chains and return with sc
rnd 1. 36 ch join with slst
rnd 2. 7 ch 6 sc, 4 ch 3 sc, 2 slst, 3 ch 2 sc, 7 ch 6 sc, (9 ch 8 sc) x 4, 7 ch 6sc, 3 ch 2 sc, 2 slst, 4 ch 3 sc, 7 ch 6 sc, 4 slst, (7 ch 6 sc) x 12, 4 slst
fix the hair on the head covering with the hat.
let's crochet amigurumi doll's clothes
pantolon (blue)
rnd 1. 36 ch (join with slst)
rnd 2-7. 36 sc
rnd 8. separate each leg with 18 sc
rnd 9-14. 18 sc (18)
side pocket: ch 7, knit 5 rows of 6 sc and glue to the trousers on each side
t-shirt (white)
rnd 1. 18 ch (join with slst)
rnd 2. (2 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 3-4. 24 sc (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6-8. 30 sc (30)
rnd 9. (4 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (36)
rnd 10-12.36 sc (36)
rnd 13. (5 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 14-15. 42 sc (42)
sleeve (white)
rnd 1. 16 ch (join with slst)
rnd 2. 16 sc (16)
rnd 3. 7 sc, 1 dec, 7 sc (15)
rnd 4. 15 sc (15)
rnd 5. 11 sc, ch and turn (11)
rnd 6. 1 dec, 7 sc, 1 dec, ch and turn (9)
rnd 7. 1 dec, 5 sc, 1 dec, ch and turn (7)
rnd 8. 1 dec, 3 sc, 1 dec, ch and turn (5)
leave long thread to sew to the body
let's crochet the amigurumi doll's hat and shoes.
hat (red)
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6. (6 sc, 3 sc in the same stitch) x 4 (36)
rnd 7. (8 sc, 3 sc in the same stitch) x 4 (44)
rnd 8. (10 sc, 3 sc in the same stitch) x 4 (52)
rnd 9. blo 52 sc (52)
rnd 10-14. 52 sc (52)
rnd 15-16. 20 slst, 25 sc, 7 sc
visor in blue, only one side of the hat
rnd 1. flo 13 sc
rnd 2-9. 13 sc (13)
fold in half and sew on row 1 of the brim
shoes (red)
rnd 1. 6 ch (6)
rnd 2. 4 sc, 3 sc in the same sc, 4 sc, 3 sc in the last stitch (14)
rnd 3. 1 inc, 4 sc, 3 inc, 4 sc, 2 inc (20)
rnd 4. 1 sc, 1 inc, 4 sc, (1 sc, 1 inc) x 3, 4 sc, (1 sc, 1 inc) x 2 (26)
rnd 5. blo 26 sc (26)
rnd 6. change to red 26 sc (26)
rnd 7. 1 dec, 5 sc, 5 dec, 6 sc, 1 dec (19)
rnd 8. 4 sc, 5 dec, 5 sc (14)
rnd 9. 14 sc (14)
rnd 10. 4 sc, ch and turn
rnd 11. 8 sc, ch and turn (8)
rnd 12. 8 sc (8) and finish off
rnd 13. take 1 stitch from the remaining 6 and make 2 rows of 6 sc
microphone (gray)
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4-6. 18 sc (18)
rnd 7. (1 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (12)
rnd 8. 6 dec (6)
rnd 9-14. 6 sc finish and close with a needle.
perform line embroidery with black thread.
terminations
embroider the eyes, eyebrows and hat detail with black thread. the mouth embroider with thread black and filled with white thread. detail of the embroidered shirt or red felt.
