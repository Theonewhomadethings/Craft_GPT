designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
materials
yarn: sport or dk llight weight yarn.
crochet hook: 2mm- 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
head
start with white yarn, crcohet carefully
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. blo (2sc,inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*2,(sc,inc)*7 (in black) (sc,inc), (3sc,inc)*2 (44)
rnd 6. (black) 10sc, (white) (2sc,inc)*6, 2sc (black) inc,2sc,inc,9sc,inc (53)
rnd 7. (black) 12sc, (white) 23sc, (black) 18sc (53)
rnd 8. (black) 14sc, (white) 20sc, (black) 19sc (53)
rnd 9. (black) 15sc, (white) 18sc, (black) 20sc (53)
rnd 10. (black) 16sc, (white) 16sc, (black) 21sc (53)
rnd 11. (black) 17sc, (white) 14sc, (black) 22sc (53)
rnd 12. (black) 13sc, dec, 3sc, (white) 5sc, dec, 5sc, (black) 3sc, dec, 18sc (50)
rnd 13. (black) 12sc, dec, 4sc, (white) 3sc, dec, 4sc, (black) 3sc, dec, 17sc (47)
insert the safety eyes at round 12, 13. 7sc apart.
rnd 14. (black) 11sc, dec, 5sc, (white) 6sc, (black) 5sc, dec, 16sc (45)
rnd 15. (black) 18sc, (white) 4sc, (black) 23sc (45)
rnd 16. (black) 19sc, (white) 2sc, (black) 24sc (45)
rnd 17. (black) 19sc, (white) 1-dec, (black) 24sc (44)
rnd 18-20. (3rnds) 44sc (44)
rnd 21. (9sc,dec)*4 (40)
rnd 22. 40sc (40)
rnd 23. (3sc,dec)*8 (32)
rnd 24. (2sc,dec)*8 (24)
rnd 25. (sc,dec)*8 (16)
rnd 26. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
body
start with orange yarn.
insert the hook into the blo round of the head (rnd 4)
rnd 1. 24sc along the blo round (24)
rnd 2. (2sc,inc)*8 (32)
rnd 3-4. (2rnds) 32sc (32)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 6-7. (2rnds) 40sc (40)
change to black
rnd 8. blo 40sc (40)
rnd 9-10. (2rnds) 40sc (40)
rnd 11. (3sc,dec)*8 (32)
rnd 12-13. (2rnds) 32sc (32)
rnd 14. (2sc,dec)*8 (24)
rnd 15. (sc,dec)*8 (16)
rnd 16. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
leg*2
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3-4. (2rnds) 16sc (16)
rnd 5. 5sc, 3-dec, 5sc (13)
rnd 6. 4sc, dec, sc, dec, 4sc (11)
cut the yarn, make the 2nd leg in the same way.
leave a long tail for sewing.
ear*2
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4-inc (8)
rnd 3. (3sc,inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4. (4sc,inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5. (5sc,inc)*2 (14)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
arm*2
rnd 1. 5sc in mr (5)
rnd 2. 5-inc (10)
rnd 3-5. (2rnds) 10sc (10)
change to orange
rnd 6-10. (5rnds)10sc (10)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
skirt
insert the hook to the blo round on the body (round 8)
chain 2, (dc,dc-inc)*18,2-hdc,2sc,slst.
hat
rnd 1. 5sc in mr (5)
rnd 2-7. (6rnds) 5sc (5)
rnd 8. 5-inc (10)
change to black
rnd 9. 10sc (10)
change to orange
rnd 10. 10sc (10)
rnd 11. (3sc,inc)*2,2sc (12)
rnd 12. 12sc (12)
change to black
rnd 13. 12sc (12)
change to orange
rnd 14. (4sc,inc)*2,2sc (14)
rnd 15. 14sc (14)
rnd 16. (5sc,inc)*2,2sc (16)
rnd 17. flo 16sc (16)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
pupmkin cat is ready..
