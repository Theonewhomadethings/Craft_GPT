hello my dears. i haven't shared a crochet deer pattern for a long time. today i think it's time to put an end to this. i will share a very cute and very large deer amigurumi pattern for you. the crochet deer amigurumi pattern has been created in detail. in this way, you can make deer amigurumi very easily. although the deer is large, it does not have many details. that's why it's made with basic crochet techniques. so i think you can do it for beginners. now if you are ready, let's crochet deer amigurumi step by step.
designer: crochet.edhappiness
materials
-yarn, i had a "children's novelty" you can use another, for example yarnart jeans.
colors: basic, white for the muzzle and to highlight the limbs.
a yarn for an embroidery of a nose and eyelashes.
eyes 14 mm
hook no: 2.5
sewing needle
pin for tracking rows
filler holofiber or sintepukh.
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
ears: 2 parts
1. 6 sc in mr or 2 ch 12 sc
2. 6 inc. 6 sc
3. 12 sc. 12 sc
4. 1 sc 1 inc * 6 -18 sc
5. -9. 18 sc. (5 rows) . 18 sc
10. 1 sc 1 dec *6 12 sc
11. 12. 12 sc (2 rows) 12 sc
we fold the ears in half and sew the 12th row and leave the yarn for sewing.
horns: 2 parts
for the horn, we crochet a process, we will connect it with the horn trunk.
1. 6 sc in mr or 2ch 6 sc
2. 2 sc 1 inc * 2. 8 sc
3. -7. 8 sc (5 rows). 8 sc
1. 6 sc in mr or 2ch 6 sc
2. 2 sc 1 inc * 2. 8 sc
3. -6. 8 sc (4 rows). 8 sc
we connect with the process of slst.
7. we crochet 7 sc and 1 inc in sl-st and more 7 sc. 16 sc
8. 16 sc. 16 sc
9. 2 sc 1 dec * 4. 12 sc
10. 12 sc. -12 sc
11. 1 sc 1 dec *4 8 sc
12. 15. 8 sc (4 rows). 8 sc
muzzle: 1 detail
1. 6 sc in mr or 2ch. 6 sc
2. 6 inc. 12 sc
3. 1 sc 1 inc * 6. -18 sc
4. 18 sc. 18 sc
5. 2 sc 1 inc * 6. 24
leave the yarn for sewing.
head: 1 detail
1. 6 sc in mr or 2ch. 6 sc
2. 6 inc. -12 sc
3. 12 sc -12 sc
4. 1 sc 1 inc *6 -18 sc
5. 2 sc 1 inc *6 -24 sc
6. 3 sc 1 inc *6 -30 sc
7. 4 sc 1 inc *6 -36 sc
8. 36 sc -36 sc
9. 5 sc 1 inc * 6 -42 sc
10. 42 sc. -42 sc
11. 6 sc 1 inc *6. -48 sc
12. 48 sc. 48 sc
13. 7 sc 1 inc *6 54 sc
14. 54 sc. -54 sc
15. 8 sc 1 inc *6. -60 sc
next will be crocheting the muzzle in beige, i will mark in brackets where exactly this beige color will be needed).
you want the beige columns in each row to go 1 sc less on each side. if there is some error with the columns at the beginning of the row, nothing terrible at the end of the row can be corrected, for this there are columns that i do not count with columns for strangleholds.
16. (beige) 12 sc, 48 sc 60 sc
17. 1 sc, (beige) 10 sc, 6 sc 1 dec *6, 1 sc 54 sc
18. 1 sc, (beige) 8 sc, 5 sc 1 dec * 6, 3 sc. 48 sc
19. 1 sc, (beige) 8 sc, 38 sc 48 sc
20. 1 sc, (beige) 6 sc, 41 sc 48 sc
21. 1 sc, (beige) 4 sc, 43 sc 48 sc
22. -23. 48 sc (2 rows). 18 sc
24. 6 sc 1 dec * 6. 42 sc
25. 5 sc 1 dec * 6. 36 sc
26. 36 sc. 36 sc
27. 4 sc 1 dec * 6. 30 sc
28. 3 sc 1 dec * 6. 24 sc
29. 2 sc 1 dec * 6. 18 sc
30. 1 sc 1 dec * 6. 12 sc
dec to the end of the row. we fix the yarn.
we insert the eyes on the 19-20th row, approximately 14 sc of the distance between them
i sew the muzzle blo (we fill it with a small amount of filler)
on beige, we embroider corners like mine.
we sew the horns parallel to each other for about 4-6 rows.
we sew the ears below two rows of horns.
then we make a small tightening of the eye.
eyelashes are embroidered with the same yarn as the nose.
tail: 1 piece
1. 6 sc in mr or 2 ch 6 sc
2. 6 inc -12 sc
3. 1 sc 1 inc *6. -18 sc
4. 1 sc 1 inc *6. -12 sc
5. 12 sc. -12 sc
6. 6 dec. -6 sc
leave the yarn for sewing
hands: 2 parts
we start crocheting with the color of hooves
1. 6 sc in mr or 2 ch 6 sc
2. 6 inc. 12 sc
3. 1 sc 1 inc *6 18 sc
4. -5. 18 sc (2 rows) 18 sc
6. 3 sc 1 dec 2 sc 1 dec 2 sc 1 dec 3 sc 1 dec 14 sc
7. 14 sc. 14 sc
change color to main
8. we crochet 14 sc blo 14 sc
9. 20. 14 sc (12 rows) 14 sc
we fill with filler and crochet for both sides. total 7 sc is obtained.
legs: 2 parts
we start crocheting with the color of hooves
1. 6 sc in mr or 2 ch 6 sc
2. 6 inc 12 sc
3. 1 sc 1 inc *6. 18 sc
4. 2 sc 1 inc *6. 24 sc
5. 6. 7. 24 sc (3 rows). 24 sc
8. 2 sc 1 dec * 6. -18 sc
9. -10. 18 sc (2 rows). -18 sc
11. 2 sc 1 dec *3, 6 sc. 15 sc
change color to main
12. we crochet a row of 15 sc blo
13. -25. 15 sc (15 rows). -15 sc
dec to the end of the row. leave the yarn for sewing.
body
1. 6 sc in mr or 2 ch. 6 sc
2. 6 inc. 12 sc
3. 1 sc 1 inc *6. 18 sc
4. 2 sc 1 inc *6. 24 sc
5. 3 sc 1 inc *6. 30 sc
6. 4 sc 1 inc *6. 36 sc
7. 5 sc 1 inc *6. 42 sc
8. 6 sc 1 inc *6. 48 sc
9. 7 sc 1 inc *6. 54 sc
10. 8 sc 1 inc *6. -60 sc
11. -12. 60 sc (2 rows). 60 sc
13. 8 sc 1 dec * 6. 54 sc
14. 7 sc 1 dec * 6. 48 sc
15. 6 sc 1 dec * 6. 42 sc
16. -17. 42 sc. (2 rows). 42 sc
18. 5 sc 1 dec * 6. 36 sc
19. 23. 36 sc (5 rows). 36 sc
24. 4 sc 1 dec * 6. 30 sc
25. 30 sc. 30 sc
26. 3 sc 1 dec * 6. 24sc
we crochet hands parallel to each other
27. hand 7 sc, 5 sc hand 7 sc, 5 sc. 24 sc
filling with fillers
28. 2 sc 1 dec * 6. 18 sc
29. 1 sc 1 dec * 6. 12 sc
30. decrease to the end sew the legs on the 8-10th row
