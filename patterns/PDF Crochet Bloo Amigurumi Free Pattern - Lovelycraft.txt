abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase, 2 sc in the same sc
dec decrease, 2 sc in a single sc
flo-front loop only
body
rnd 1. 6mr (6)
rnd 2. 6inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc) x6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) x6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc, inc) x6 (30)
rnd 6. (4sc, inc) x6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc, inc) x6 (42)
rnd 8-32. 42sc (42)
rnd 33. (5sc, dec) x6 (36) flo
rnd 34. (4sc, dec) x6 (30)
rnd 35. (3sc, dec) x6 (24)
rnd 36. (2sc, dec) x6 (18)
rnd 37. (1sc, dec) x6 (12)
rnd 38. 6 dec (6)
we have completed the body part of the amigurumi bloo pattern. now let's crochet the arms.
arms x2
use blue color
rnd 1. 6mr (6)
rnd 2. 6inc (12)
rnd 3-6. 12sc (12)
rnd 7. (2sc, dec) x3 (9)
rnd 8-9. 9sc (9)
