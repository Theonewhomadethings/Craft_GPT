crochet little kid with tiger hat free amigurumi pattern
designer
instagram: donglaihandmade
materials
hook: 2,5mm
yarn: sport yarn
needle and thread for sewing eyes,nose
abbreviations
mr: magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
st: stitch
slst: slip stitch
let's start the construction of the cute crochet amigurumi doll from the head part.
head
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (45c, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7-10: 36sc (36)
rnd 11: 12sc, (inc, 2sc)*4, inc, 11sc (41)
rnd 12-13: 41sc (41)
rnd 14: 12sc, (dec, 2sc)*4, dec, 11sc (36)
rnd 15: 36sc (36)
rnd 16: (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 17: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 18: (2sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 19: (sc, dec)*6 (12)
sew the eyes on r10.
after completing the head part of the crochet character, we will crochet the legs and body part.
legs & body
rnd 1: 5sc in mr (5)
rnd 2: 5inc (10)
rnd 3-7: 10sc
chain 2, join 2 legs
rnd 8: 10sc, 2sc in ch, 10sc, 2sc in ch (24)
rnd 9: (3sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 10-12: 30sc (30)
rnd 13: (3sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 14-16: 24sc (24)
rnd 17: (2sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 18-19: 18sc (18)
rnd 20: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 21: 12sc (12)
after completing the leg and body part, let's crochet her arms and fix them to her body.
arms
rnd 1: 5sc in mr (5)
rnd 2-8: 5sc (5)
rnd 9: crochet on both side 2sc.
sew 2 arms on r20.
we finished the cute crochet toy kid's arms and fixed them to the body. now we will make your trousers first, then your shirt.
trouser
chain 32, join to a circle
rnd 1-6: 32sc (32)
chain 2 to divide 16 sts each pant
rnd 1: 18sc (18)
make the second pant same as the first pant.
shirt
chain 18, join to a circle.
rnd 1: (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 2: (3sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 3: 5sc, chain 3, skip 5sts, 9sc, chain 3, skip 5sts, 6sc (26)
rnd 4: (4sc, inc)*5, sc (31)
rnd 5: 2sc, (inc, 5sc)*4, inc, 4sc (36)
rnd 6: 36sc (36)
sew the arms first then put the shirt on before sew the head.
after completing the crochet shirt, let's crochet the tiger patterned red hat.
hat
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (5sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8-15: 42sc (42)
as the last part, we make ears and nose for the hat, after which the pattern is completely finished.
ears for hat
make 2 pcs
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*4
stop here
nose for hat
make 2 pcs
8sc in mr (8)
slip to the first st.
the cute amigurumi character is ready, have fun.
