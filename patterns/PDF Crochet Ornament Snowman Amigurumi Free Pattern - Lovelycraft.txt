crochet ornament snowman amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: rin.meow21
materials
hook size 2.5mm
drops safran yarn
embroidery thread
yarn needle
safety eyes
stitch marker
fiber fill
scissors
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
()*n repeat in braces n times
sl st slip stitch
ch chain
hdc half double crochet
blo back loop only
flo front loop only
head & body
(white)
rnd 1: mr 8 sc (8)
rnd 2: 8inc (16)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4: (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 5: (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 6: (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 7-11: 42sc (42)
rnd 12: (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 13: 36sc (36)
rnd 14: (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 15: (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 16-18: 48sc (48)
rnd 19: (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 20-21: 42sc (42)
rnd 22: blo 42sc (42)
rnd 23: (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 24: (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 25: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 26: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 27: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 28: 6dec (6)
weave in the rest of yarn. insert the eyes between r 8-9. or embroider the eyes.
hat
(black)
rnd 1: mr 6sc (6)
rnd 2: 6inc (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: blo 24sc (24)
rnd 6: 24sc (24)
rnd 7: (yellow) 24sc (24)
rnd 8: 24sc (24)
rnd 9: (black) 24sc (24)
rnd 10: flo 24inc (48)
fasten off and cut the yarn.
scarf
(turquoise)
make 64 ch; at the second ch from hook. crochet 63 hdc.
