abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
slst slip stitch
sc single crochet
inc single crochet increase 2 single crochet in 1 stitch
dec single crochet invisible decrease sc 2 stitch together
dc double crochet
blo crochet through back loop only
flo crochet through front loops only
materials
crochet hook suitable with your yarns
yarns; light pink, pink, gray
sewing needle, scissor
i used sport yarn 2 mm with hook size 2,5 mm
you can use the same pattern to make smaller or larger toy by using finer or bulkier yarn
head
yarn gray
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc, 2 sc) x 6 (36)
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 8. (3 sc, inc, 3 sc) x 6 (48)
rnd 9-19. 48 sc (48)
rnd 20. (3 sc, dec, 3 sc) x 6 (42)
rnd 21. (5 sc, dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 22. (2 sc, dec, 2 sc) x 6 (30)
rnd 23. (3 sc, dec) x 6 (24)
cut the yarn. hide yarn tail inside the head.
ears x2
yarn gray
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 sc (6)
rnd 3. 6 inc (12)
rnd 4. 12 inc (12)
rnd 5. (sc, inc) x 6 (18)

rnd 7. (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 8-17. 24 inc (24)

rnd 19. (5 sc, dec) x 6 (15)
rnd 20. (4 sc, dec) x 6 (12)
rnd 21. (3 sc, dec) x 6 (9)
rnd 22. (2 sc, dec) x 6 (6)
together 5 sc
cut the yarn. leaving a long tail for asembly.
headband
yarn pink ch 75: turn 74 sc
flower
yarn pink
small flower
mr: (ch3, 3dc, ch3, sc) x 4
big flowers x2
mr: (ch5, 4tr, ch5, sc) x 4
sew eyes and nose
body
yarn light pink
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc, 2 sc) x 6 (36)
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 8. (3 sc, inc, 3 sc) x 6 (48)
rnd 9-13. 48 sc (48)
change yarn gray
rnd 14. blo: (6 sc, dec) x 6 (42)
rnd 15-16. 42 sc (42)
rnd 17. (5 sc, dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 18-19. 36 sc (36)
rnd 20. (4 sc, dec) x 6 (30)
rnd 21-22. 30 sc (30)
rnd 23. (3 sc, dec) x 6 (24)
rnd 24-25. 24 sc (24)
cut the yarn. leaving a long tail for asembly.
dress
yarn pink
continue r13 of the body
rnd 14. ch3, (2 dc in 1 st, 3 dc in 1 st) x 23, dc, slst
rnd 15. ch3, (2 dc in 1 st, dc) x 57, slst
rnd 16-17. all dc
hands x2
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3-19. 12 sc (12)
4 together
cut the yarn. leaving a long tail for asembly

yarn gray
