espresso the crochet sheep pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: aquariwool
materials
yarn color:
(a) brown: yarnart jeans 70 (50g, 160m)
(b) skin color: yarnart jeans 87 (50g, 160m)
other materials:
crochet hook size 2- 2.5 mm
knitting needles size 3.5- 4 mm
1 wooden button
tapestry needles, pins, stitch markers, scissors
abbreviations
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
hdc half double crochet
sl st slip stitch
mr magic ring
dc double crochet
tr treble crochet
bobble bobble stitch
blo back loop only
flo front loop only
rnd round
sts stitches
the finished crochet sheep size is about 13 cm.
legs (make 2)
yarn b
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
break the yarn, make the second leg in the same way, but don't break the yarn this time.connect 2 legs together: 15 sc (leg 1)+ 15 sc (leg 2)=30. move to the body part, change to yarn a, turn (remember to turn here to crochet the bobble stitch in the body part, so that the bobble can pop out clearly.)
body
yarn a
rnd 1. ch 2 (count as 1 hdc), bobble 1, (hdc 1, bobble 1)*14, slst to the 1st ch (30)
rnd 2. sc 30, slst to the 1st ch (30)
rnd 3. ch 2, (count as 1 hdc), bobble 1, (hdc 1, bobble 1)*14, slst to the 1st ch (30)
rnd 4. (3sc,dec)*6, slst to the 1st sc (24)
rnd 5. ch 2, (count as 1 hdc), bobble 1, (hdc 1, bobble 1)*11, slst to the 1st ch (24)
rnd 6. (2sc,dec)*6, slst to the 1st sc (18)
rnd 7. ch 2, (count as 1 hdc), bobble 1, (hdc 1, bobble 1)*8, slst to the 1st ch (18)
rnd 8. sc 18 (18)
break the yarn with a long rest. stuff.
arms (make 2)
yarn b
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3, change to yarn a (9)
rnd 3-7. sc 9 (5 rows) (9)
rnd 4. dec 1, sc 7 (8)
rnd 5. dec 1, sc 6 (7)
rnd 6. dec 1, sc 5 (6)
break the yarn with a long rest. stuff slightly. sew 2 edges together.
head
yarn b
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10. 54 sc (54)
rnd 11. (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 12-19. 60 sc (6 rows) (60)
rnd 20. (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 21. 54 sc (54)
rnd 22. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 23. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 24. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 25. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 26. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 27. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later. stuff.
hat
yarn a
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (bobble 1, hdc 1)*6 (12)
rnd 3. 12 inc (24)
rnd 4. (bobble 1, hdc 1)*12 (24)
rnd 5. (1sc,inc)*12 (36)
rnd 6. (hdc 1, bobble 1)*18 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (bobble 1, hdc 1)*21 (42)
rnd 9. 42 sc (42)
rnd 10. (bobble 1, hdc 1)*21 (42)
rnd 11. 42 sc (42)
rnd 12. (bobble 1, hdc 1)*21 (42)
rnd 13. 42 sc (42)
rnd 14. (bobble 1, hdc 1)*21 (42)
rnd 15. 42 sc (42)
break the yarn with a long rest tow sew later.
use brown thread to embroider the eyes and mouth.

ears (make 2)
yarn b
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3-10. 9 sc (8 rows) (9)
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later. do not stuff. use the tapestry needle to sew 2 edges together and roll it over and sew to the hat (photo below).
