crochet bear baby rattle pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: deynega_yuly
materials
yarn- yarnart jeans
crochet hook size: 2 mm
wooden ring diameter 6 cm
rattle
safety eyes size 3 mm
toy filler
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
ch chain
let's start with the crochet rattle, first of all, from the head.
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring
rnd 2. 6 inc 3 (1 sc, inc)*6 times
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*6 times
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 times
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)*6 times
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc)*6 times
rnd 8-14. 42 sc
rnd 15. (5 sc, dec)*6 times
rnd 16. (4 sc, dec)*6 times
rnd 17. (3 sc, dec)*6 times
insert safety eyes size 3 mm between 11th and 12th rows at 6-7 sts apart.
at this stage stuff the head and insert a rattle inside.
rnd 18. (2 sc, dec)*6 times
rnd 19. (1 sc, dec)*6 times
rnd 20. 6 dec
cut off the thread, leave enough length for sewing head to the ring. pull the opening.
after crocheting the head of the cute teddy bear rattle, let's move on to the ears.
ears (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring
rnd 2. 6-inc
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 times
we have also completed the ears of the crocheted teddy bear rattle. now, there is the muzzle part.
muzzle
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring
rnd 2. 6- inc
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 times
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 times
rnd 5. 24 sc
rim on the ring
cast on a chain of 24 ch. from the second ch from the hook 23 sc. now start each row with 1 ch, work in turning rows, crochet 8 rows in total.
but keep in mind that the thickness of the ring may differ from mine, so you may need to crochet more or fewer rows.
when the rim is done, wrap it around the ring and connect the edges with slip stitches.
collar
rnd 1.cast on a chain of 20 ch. from the second ch from the hook crochet increases of sc into each ch to the end of the row, 1 ch, turn.
rnd 2. increases of sc, 1 ch, turn
rnd 3. sc into each st, 1 ch, turn
rnd 4. sc into each st
sew edges of the collar around the rattle's neck.
