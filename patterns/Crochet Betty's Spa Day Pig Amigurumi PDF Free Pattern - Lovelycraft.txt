let's start knitting the amigurumi betty pattern.
we knit the body and head of the amigurumi pig pattern together.
head and body in peach
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6-10. 30 sc
*insert safety eyes r7 & stuff head only*
rnd 11. (3 sc, dec) x 6 (24)
rnd 12. (2 sc, dec) x 6 (18)
rnd 13. (sc, dec) x 6 (12)
rnd 14. 12 sc
rnd 15. (sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 16. (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 17. 24 sc (24)
rnd 18. (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
fo leave a tail for sewing
we have completed the body of the amigurumi pig. now we knit the arms.
arms x2
in light brown
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. (inc) x 4 (8)
rnd 3. blo 8 sc (8)
change to peach
rnd 4-9. 8 sc (8)
stuff lightly and flatten.
through both sides close with 4 sc.
we knit the ears and nose of the amigurumi toy.
ears x 2 in peach
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4 sc (4)
rnd 3. (inc) x 4 (8)
rnd 4. 8 sc (8)
rnd 5. (2 sc, dec) x 2 (6)
nose
ch 70
(in 2nd ch from hook begin)
rnd 1. slst, 68 sc, slst fo
we knit the headband of the amigurumi pattern.
headband in pink
ch 70
(in 2nd ch from hook begin)
rnd 1. slst, 68 sc, slst fo
soap
ch 4 (in 2nd ch from hook begin)
rnd 1. inc, sc, 3 sc in last stitch, 2 sc, slst to first sc fo
loofah
ch 5 in 2nd ch from hook begin)
rnd 1. 5sc in each ch fo
curl up and sew through middle.
we sew the parts of the betty pattern from the amigurumi pig to the body part.
assembly betty pig
sew ears to the top of head between round 4 & 5.
with black thread stitch some eyelashes on eyes.
sew nose in between the eyes, 1 row below.
sew 2 stitches in pink for nostrils.
sew the arms to the body.
sew the soap to one hand and the loofah to the other hand.
once the bath is completed, before the bubbles, sew betty into the centre of the bath.
we are going to knit the bath part of the amigurumi toy. you can choose the color of the wool of the amigurumi bath pattern as you wish. yellow and blue colors would look great.
bath legs x4 in light blue
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4 sc (4)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x2 (6)
bath in light blue
ch 11 (in 2nd ch from hook begin)
rnd 1. inc, 8 sc, (3 sc in last stitch), 9 sc (22)
rnd 2. (inc) x2, 8 sc, (inc) x3, 8 sc, inc (28)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x 2,8 sc, (sc, inc) x 3, 9 sc, inc (34)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) x 2,8 sc, (2 sc, inc) x 3, 10 sc, inc (40)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) x 2,8 sc, (3 sc, inc) x 3, 11 sc, inc (46)
rnd 6. blo 46 sc (46)
rnd 7-10. 46sc(46)
rnd 11. 6 sc, inc, 15 sc, inc, 6 sc, inc, 15 sc, inc (50)
rnd 12. 7 sc, inc, 16 sc, inc, 7 sc, inc, 16 sc, inc (54)
rnd 13. 54 sc(54)
rnd 14. flo 54 sc (54)
rnd 15. blo 54 sc (54)
rnd 16. blo 54 sc (54)
rnd 17. blo and into blo of r14 54 sc (54)
rnd 18. (blo 7 sc, dec) x 6 (48)
rnd 19. (6 sc, dec) x 6 (42)
*begin to stuff the bath and continue till closed.
rnd 20. (5 sc, dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 21. (4 sc, dec) x 5 (30) fo and leave a tail for sewing
rnd 22. sew across both sides in front loops only to close.
assembly
sew bath feet on to the bottom of the bath in the corners.
sew betty pig in the centre of the bath.
bubbles
join white yarn in first front loop of round 18.
rnd 1. ((pc, sc) x3, pc, dec) x6 (48)
rnd 2. ((pc,sc) x3, dec) x6 (42)
rnd 3. ((pc,sc) x2, pc, dec) x6 (36)
rnd 4. ((pc, sc) x2, dec) x6 (30)
fo and sew onto and around pig belly.
