little crochet piggy free pdf amigurumi pattern
designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2.5 mm
black embroidery
safety eyes size 5 mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviations
mr: magic ring
sc: single crochet
hdc: half double crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
flo: front loop only
slst: slip stitch
head
start with pink yarn
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. blo (2sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3sc, inc)*7, 2sc (40)
rnd 6. (4sc, inc)*8 (48)
rnd 7-11. (5 rnds) 48sc (48)
rnd 12. (10sc, dec)*4 (44)
rnd 13-14. (2 rnds) 44sc (44)
rnd 15. (9sc, inc)*4 (40)
rnd 16-17. (2 rnds) 40sc (40)
rnd 18. (8sc, dec)*4 (36)
rnd 19. (4sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 20. (3sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 21. (2sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 22. (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 23. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
body
start with white yarn
insert the hook into the blo round of the head (rnd 4)
rnd 1. (2sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 2-3. (2 rnds) 32sc (32)
rnd 4. (7sc, inc)*4 (36)
rnd 5-6. (2 rnds) 36sc (36)
change to pink
rnd 7. 36sc (36)
rnd 8. (4sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 9-10. 30sc (30)
rnd 11. blo (3sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 12. (2sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 13. (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 14. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
insert the hook into the blo round on the body (rnd 11) slst along the blo round.
leg (make 2)
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. blo 12sc (12)
rnd 4. 3sc, 3-dec, 3sc (9)
rnd 5. 4sc, dec, 3sc (8)
rnd 6-9. (4 rnds) 8sc (8)
cut the yarn, make the 2nd leg in the same way.
leave a long tail for sewing.
ear (make 2)
rnd 1. 6sc inc mr (6)
rnd 2. (2sc, inc)*2 (8)
rnd 3. (3sc, inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4. (4sc, inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5. 12sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
arm (make 2)
rnd 1. 6 in mr (6)
rnd 2-3. (2 rnds) 6sc (6)
change to white
rnd 4-8. (5 rnds) 10sc (10)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
nose
chain 4, start in the 2nd chain from the hook 3sc in the first chain, 2sc in next 2 chains, 3sc in last chain, 2sc in other side of the chain, slst.
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing. little piggy is ready..
