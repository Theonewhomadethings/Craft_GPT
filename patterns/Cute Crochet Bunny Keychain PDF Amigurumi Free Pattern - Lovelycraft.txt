today we are adding a new one to the amigurumi bunny patterns. this free bunny amigurumi pater is translated from russian. we have prepared a very nice pattern for you. amigurumi is designed as a keychain. therefore, it is not very suitable for a toy. very simple materials were used as colors and yarns. so you can crochet easily. crochet bunny amigurumi patterns have recently started to be used as key chains. you can use this crochet bunny as a key chain.
a very nice shade of green is used. the structure of the mouth and nose is quite simple. the defining element in amigurumi bunny patterns is their ears. you can see how cute the ears are in this free amigurumi pattern. without further ado, let's start crocheting.

designer
instagram: brooch_cott
materials and tools used:
yarn half cotton 25g / 160 m (i used yarnart jeans, dividing the yarn into two parts), (you can also use alize cotton gold fine or yarnart cotton soft)
suitable hook (i have it 1.25 mm);
eyes or beads;
yarn for spout embroidery;
needle;
filler for soft toys.
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
let's start making cute keychain bunny free pattern
head
we start knitting the head from the muzzle
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. inc*6 (12)
rnd 3. sc*12 (12)
rnd 4. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 5. 3 sc, (sc,inc)*6, 3 sc (24)
rnd 6. 4 sc, inc, (2sc,inc)*5, 4sc (30)
rnd 7. 3 sc, (3 sc, inc)*6 , 3 sc (36)
rnd 8. 5 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*5, 5 sc (42)
rnd 9-14. sc*42 (42)
rnd 15. (5sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 16. sc*36 (36)
rnd 17. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 18. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 19. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 20. (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 21. dec*6 (6)
cut off the yarn, tighten the hole and hide the tip.
we finished the head part of the cute keychain bunny amigurumi toy. now let's start making the ears
ears (2 parts)
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. inc*6 (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-9. sc*18 (18)
rnd 10. (4sc, dec)*3 (15)
rnd 11-12. sc*15 (15)
rnd 13. (sc, dec)*3 (12)
rnd 14-15. sc*12 (12)
rnd 16. (2sc, dec)*3 (9)
rnd 17-18. sc*9 (9)
rnd 19. (sc, dec)*3 (6)
cut off the thread, leaving a long end for sewing.
we proceed to the design of the head (we fix all the yarns in the lower part of the head, so where the body will be attached):
1. we make tightening and sew on the eyes, placing them between the 5th and 6th rows (between the eyes about 7 sc from the 5th row)
2. we embroider the nose
3. sew on the ears, approximately between 11 and 12 rows
after finishing the ears of the cute keychain bunny amigurumi pattern, let's crochet the body part.
body
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. inc*6 (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6-8. sc*30 (30)
rnd 9. 4 sc, (dec, 2 sc)*5, dec, 4 sc (24)
rnd 10-11. sc*24 (24)
rnd 12. (2sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 13-14. sc*18 (18)
rnd 15. (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 16. dec*6 (6)
cut the yarn and pull off the hole with a needle, hide the end of the yarn.
legs (2 parts)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. inc*6 (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. sc*18 (18)
rnd 5. 3 sc, dec*6, 3 sc (12)
rnd 6. 3 sc, dec*3, 3 sc (9)
rnd 7. sc*9 (9)
rnd 8. (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
rnd 9-11. sc*12 (12)
rnd 12. dec*6 (6)
cut the yarn and pull off the hole with a needle, hide the end of the yarn.
arms (2 parts)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. inc*6 (12)
rnd 3. (2 sc, dec)*3 (9)
rnd 4-8. sc*9 (9)
rnd 9. (sc, dec)*3 (9)
cut the yarn and pull off the hole with a needle, hide the end of the yarn.
check out the pictures below to see how the cute keychain bunny pom-pom tail will look.
tail (you can use a pompom)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. sc*6 (6)
cut the yarn, leaving the long end to be sewn on.
assembly
1. sew the body to the head with a yarn fastening.
2. sew on the legs and handles with yarn fastening
3. sew the tail to the body
! for thread fastening, you need to use a strong yarn.
cute keychain bunny ready now.
