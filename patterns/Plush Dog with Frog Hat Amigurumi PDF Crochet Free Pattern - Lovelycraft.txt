materials
bernat pipesqueak (size 5) yarn in the color "whitey white"
worsted weight (size 4) yarn in color green
4.5 mm hook and 2.75 mm hook
6mm and 7mm pairs of safety eyes
black, pink, and red felt fabric
black embroidery thread
fabric glue
scissors
yarn and sewing needles
stuffing
stitch marker
sewing pins
abbreviations
sc single crochet
ch chain
inc increase
dec decrease
st stitch
mr magic ring
3 inc* sc 3 in one st
head
start with bernat pipsqueak yarn and 4.5 mm hook;
rnd 1: sc 6 in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (sc2,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (sc3,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (sc4,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (sc5,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8-9: sc 42 (42)
rnd 10: (sc6,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 11: sc 48 (48)
rnd 12: sc 18, (sc,inc)*3, sc 18, (sc,inc)*3 (54)
rnd 13-16: sc 54 (54)
rnd 17: sc 18, (sc,dec)*3, sc 18, (dec,sc)*3 (48)
rnd 18: (sc6,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 19: (sc5,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 20: (sc4,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 21: (sc3,dec)*6 (24)
stuff head
rnd 22: (sc2,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 23: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 24: dec*6 (6)
fo, cut the yarn tail and sew remaining hole closed.
ears (make 2)
start with bernat pipsqueak yarn and 4.5 mm hook;
rnd 1: sc 6 in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*6 (12)
rnd 3: sc 12 (12)
rnd 4: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 5-8: sc 18 (18)
don't stuff.
fo, cut long yarn tail to sew remaining opening closed and to sew ears onto head.
body
start with bernat pipsqueak yarn and 4.5 mm hook;
rnd 1: sc 6 in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (sc2,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (sc3,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (sc4,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (sc5,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8: (sc6,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9: (sc15,inc)*3 (51)
rnd 10-13: sc 51 (51)
rnd 14: (sc15,dec)*3 (48)
rnd 15: (sc6,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 16: sc 42 (42)
rnd 17: (sc12,dec)*3 (39)
stuff body.
fo, cut long yarn tail to sew head onto body.
arms/ tail (make 3)
start with bernat pipsqueak yarn and 4.5 mm hook;
rnd 1: sc 6 in mr (6)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: sc 9 (9)
stuff lightly.
fo, cut long yarn tails to sew arms/ tail onto body.
legs (make 2)
start with bernat pipsqueak yarn and 4.5 mm hook;
rnd 1: sc 6 in mr (6)
rnd 2: (inc2,sc)*2 (10)
rnd 3-5: sc 10 (10)
stuff legs.
fo, cut long yarn tails to sew legs onto body.
muzzle
start with bernat pipsqueak yarn and 4.5 mm hook;
rnd 1: ch 7, starting from 2nd chain from the hook, sc5, 3 inc*, turn to work
on other side of chain, sc4, 3 inc* (15)
rnd 2: sc 5, inc, 3 inc*, inc, sc 4, inc, 3 inc*, inc (23)
rnd 3: sc 23 (23)
stuff lightly.
fo, cut long yarn tail to sew muzzle onto face.
frog hat cap piece
start with green yarn and 2.75 mm crochet hook;
rnd 1: sc 6 in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: sc,inc, (sc2,inc)*5, sc (24)
rnd 5: (sc3,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: sc2, inc, (sc4,inc)*5, sc2 (36)
rnd 7: (sc5,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8: sc3, inc, (sc6,inc)*5, sc3 (48)
rnd 9: (sc7,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10: sc4, inc, (sc8,inc)*5, sc4 (60)
rnd 11: (sc9,inc)*6 (66)
rnd 12: sc5, inc, (sc10, inc)*5, sc5 (72)
rnd 13-14: sc 72 (72)
rnd 15: (sc11,inc)*6 (78)
rnd 16: sc 78 (78)
rnd 17: sc6, inc, (sc12, inc)*5, sc6 (84)
rnd 18: hdc 84 (84)
fo, cut long yarn tail to sew hat onto head.
frog hat eyes (make 2)
start with green yarn and 2.75 mm crochet hook;
rnd 1: sc 6 in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: sc, inc, (sc2,inc)*5, sc (24)
rnd 5: (sc3,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6-7: sc 30 (30)
rnd 8: (sc8,dec)*3 (27)
rnd 9: (sc7,dec)*3 (24)
stuff eyes.
fo, cut long yarn tails to sew eyes onto cap piece.
assembly
1: use remaining yarn tail from body and sew body onto head.
2: flatten ears in half and use remaining yarn tails to sew across the opening shut.
use the remaining tails to sew flatten ears on the sides of head.
3: use remaining yarn tails from arms and sew arms onto upper half of body.
4: use remaining yarn tails from legs and sew legs onto bottom half of body.
5: use remaining yarn tail from muzzle and sew muzzle onto face.
6: cut out a small, rounded- edge triangle out of black felt fabric and glue onto muzzle as a nose.
7: insert 6 mm safety eyes one round above the muzzle with about 1.65 inches apart.
8: cut two small ovals out of pink felt fabric and glue them onto face as blush.
9: use remaining yarn tail from tail and sew onto back of the body.
10: use remaining yarn tail from frog eyes and few frog eyes onto frog base cap between
rnd 4 and 12 of cap.
11: insert 7mm safety eyes into frog eyes between rnds 6 and 7.
12: use black embroidery thread to embroider a small smile at around rnd 12 of frog cap.
13: cut two small ovals out of red felt fabric and glue them onto frog cap as blush for the frog.
14: use remaining yarn tail from frog cap and sew entire frog cap onto dog head.
