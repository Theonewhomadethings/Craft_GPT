the tofu crochet tiger pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: mes.mignonneries
abbreviations
mr magic ring
rnd round
ch chain
sl st slip stitch
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
()* repeat till the end of round
[] total number of stitches in the round
materials
cotton ricorumi: orange (025), black (060), white (001), red (029), yellow (063), gold
crochet hook: 3 mm
6 mm safety eyes
yarn needle
black embroidery thread
fiberfill for stuffing
scissors
head & body
stuff as you go along your work.
in orange.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. (inc)* [12]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [18]
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* [24]
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* [30]
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)* [36]
rnd 7-9. 36 sc [36]
rnd 10. (5sc,inc)* [42]
rnd 11-14. 42 sc [42]
insert the security eyes between rnd 10 and 11.
rnd 15. (5sc,dec)* [36]
rnd 16. (4sc,dec)* [30]
rnd 17. (3sc,dec)* [24]
rnd 18. (2sc,dec)* [18]
rnd 19. (2sc,inc)* [24]
rnd 20. 24 sc [24]
rnd 21. (3sc,inc)* [30]
rnd 22-24. 30 sc [30]
rnd 25. (4sc,inc)* [36]
rnd 26-29. 26 sc [36]
rnd 30. (5sc,inc)* [42]
rnd 31. 42 sc [42]
rnd 32. through back loops only (5sc,dec)* [36]
finish to stuff.
rnd 33. (2sc,dec)* [27]
rnd 34. (1sc,dec)* [18]
rnd 35. (dec)* [9]
rnd 36. (1sc,dec)* [6]
fasten off and close.
muzzle
in white
rnd 1. mr, 4sc [4]
rnd 2. (inc)* [8]
sew the muzzle between the eyes.
embroider the nose and eyebrows with black embroidery thread.
ears (make 2)
in black
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
in orange
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)* [9]
rnd 3-4. 9 sc [9]
arms (make 2)
in orange
rnd 1. mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2. (1sc,sc)* [6]
rnd 3-7. 6 sc [6]
sew the ears and arms to the body.
tail
in orange
rnd 1. mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2. (1sc,sc)* [6]
rnd 3-15. 6 sc [6]
embroider the tiger's stripes on the head, body, arms and tail with black embroidery thread.
chinese hat
in red
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. (inc)* [12]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [18]
rnd 4. 18 sc [18]
in yellow
rnd 5. 18 sc [18]
embroider hat decoration with yellow thread and hat details with gold one.
