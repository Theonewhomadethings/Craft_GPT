first of all, hello everyone. today i will share a very cute mouse amigurumi pattern. a big thank you to studio.wow_toys, the owner of the design. you can find details about this cute amigurumi mouse in the continuation of the article.
this mouse amigurumi pattern is pretty simple. depending on the yarn you use, the shape of the amigurumi you knit may change. i recommend that you take care to use the same materials.

etsy: studiowowtoysua
materials
grey yarn sport (12 wpi)
beige yarn sport (12 wpi)
white yarn sport (12 wpi)
black yarn sport (12 wpi)
hook 2.00 mm
stuffing
needle for sewing on parts
marker or contrast thread (optional)
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
ch chain stitch
dc half double crochet
** n repeat sequence in brackets n times
() amount of stitches after the row
legs
beige yarn
1. 6 mr
2. 6 inc (12)
3. *2 sc, 1 inc* 4 (16)
4. 16 sc
change color to grey
5. *3 sc, 1 inc* 4 (20)
6. 20 sc
7. *4 sc, 1 inc* 4 (24)
8. 24 sc
9. "5 sc, 1 inc* 4 (28)
cut the yarn. don't close the loop
crochet second leg similarly. don't cut yarn
body
continue crochet the second leg
1. 28 sc by second leg, attach the first leg, 28 sc by first leg (56)
2-13. /12 rows/ 56 sc
14. *12 sc, 1 dec* 4 (52)
15. *11 sc, 1 dec* 4 (48)
16. *10 sc, 1 dec* 4 (44)
17. *9 sc, 1 dec* 4 (40)
18. *8 sc, 1 dec* 4 (36)
19. *7 sc, 1 dec* 4 (32)
cut the yarn, leave long end for sewing
to stuff
head
grey yarn
16 ch, start in 2nd loop from hook
1. 14 sc, 3 sc in 1 loop, by the other side of the chain 14 sc, 3 sc in 1 loop (34)
2. *14 sc, 3 inc* 2 (40)
3. 14 sc, *1 sc, 1 inc* 3, 14 sc, *1 sc, 1 inc* 3 (46)
4. 14 sc, *1 inc, 2 sc* 3, 14 sc, *1 inc, 2 sc* 3 (52)
5. 14 sc, *3 sc, 1 inc* 3, 14 sc, *3 sc, 1 inc* 3 (58)
6-15. /10 rows/ 58 sc
16. 23 sc, 1 dec, 27 sc, 1 dec, 4 sc (56)
17. *12 sc, 1 dec* 4 (52)
18. *11 sc, 1 dec* 4 (48)
19-23. /5 rows/ 48 sc
24. *6 sc, 1 dec* 6 (42)
25. *5 sc, 1 dec* 6 (36)
26. *4 sc, 1 dec* 6 (30)
27. *3 sc, 1 dec* 6 (24) to stuff
28. *2 sc, 1 dec* 6 (18)
29. *1 sc, 1 dec* 6 (12)
30. 6 dec (6)
close the hole. hide the end of the yarn
arms
beige yarn
1. 6 mr
2. *1 sc, 1 inc* 3 (9)
3. 9 sc
change color to grey
4-14. /11 rows/ 9 sc
don't stuff
close detail in a half. crochet with single crochet, grabbing the both edges.
leave long end for sewing
crochet second arm similarly
eyes
white yarn
5 ch, start in 2nd loop from hook
1. 3 sc, 3 sc in 1 loop, by the other side of the chain 3 sc, 3 sc in 1 loop (12)
2. *3 sc, 3 inc* 2 (18)
3. 3 sc, *1 sc, 1 inc* 3, 3 sc, *1 sc, 1 inc* 3 (24)
4. 3 sc, *1 inc, 2 sc* 3, 3 sc, *1 inc, 2 sc* 3 (30)
cut the yarn. leave long end for sewing+
black yarn
1. 6 mr
2. 6 inc (12)
sew the black detail to the white detail.
crochet second eye similarly
ears
crochet 2 beige details and 2 grey details
don't cut yarn on grey details
1. 6 mr
2. 6 inc (12)
3. *1 sc, 1 inc* 6 (18)
4. *2 sc, 1 inc* 6 (24)
5. *3 sc, 1 inc* 6 (30)
6. *4 sc, 1 inc* 6 (36)
fold the beige and gray details and crochet together sc with gray yarn.
tail
beige yarn
40 ch, start in 2nd loop from hook 39 dc
assembly
1. sew head to the body
2. sew arms
3. sew the eyes down the center of the head
4. sew the ears at the top of the head
5. sew tail on the back
6. embroider the nose with beige yarn
