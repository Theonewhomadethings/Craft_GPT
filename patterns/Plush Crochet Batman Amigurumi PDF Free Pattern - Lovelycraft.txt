dear friends. today i will share an amazing crochet amigurumi pattern. the name of our crochet pattern for today is batman. i can say that it is a very realistic and easy to make amigurumi pattern. velvet rope is not preferred in crochet doll amigurumi patterns. however, velvet rope is used very often by russian designers. this doll batman pattern is one of them.
we will crochet doll batman amigurumi pattern. we start this process first from the hands of the crocheted amigurumi. then we work the other parts with crochet. then we do the merging. now let's crochet batman amigurumi step by step.
pdf plush crochet batman amigurumi free pattern
designer: anastasiya_skryaga
necessary materials:
1. himalaya dollpfin baby yarn black (311), gray (320), flesh (333) and a little yellow (313)
2. foamiran for eyes and emblems (white, yellow and black)
3. hook number 4
4. needle for sewing on parts with a blunt end
5. filler
6. hot glue
7. scissors
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
hands:
we start crocheting in black:
1: 2 ch and in the 2nd from the hook we crochet 6 sc (6)
2: 6 inc (12)
3-6: 12 sc, change the color to gray
7: (4 sc, dec)*2 (10)
8-16: 10 sc (9 rows), slst and cut the yarn.
we fill with holofiber for 2/3 hands
legs:
we start crocheting in black:
1: 6 ch and from the 2nd loop we crochet around the chain inc, 3 sc, 3sc on last loop, 4 sc on second side (12)
2: 2 inc, 3 sc, 3 inc, 3 sc, inc (18)
3: (sc, inc)*2, 3 sc, (sc, inc)*3, 4 sc, inc (24)
4-5: 24 sc + 3 sc at the end of the 5th row for offset
6: 7 sc, (sc, dec)*3, 8 sc (21)
7: 7 sc, 3 dec, 8 sc (18)
8: (sc, dec)*6 (12)
9: 12 sc, change the color to gray
10: 12 sc for blo (12)
11-16: 12 sc, and change the yarn to black
17: 12 sc. cut the yarn at the first leg, not at the second.
body:
we make 4 ch from the second leg and attach it to the 1st, so that the legs looked in one direction, and we crochet:
1: 12 scon the leg, 4 sc chain from ch, 12 sc on the leg, 4 sc on a chain of ch (32)
we fill the legs tightly with filler, and the body during crocheting.
2-3: 32 sc, change the color to yellow
4: (7 sc, inc)*4 (36)
5: (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
6: 42 sc, change the color to gray
7: 42 sc (42)
8: (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
9-10: 36 sc (36)
11: (4 sc, dec)* 6 (30)
12: 30 sc (30)
13: (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
14: 24 sc (24)
15: we crochet our hands (24)
16: (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
change the color to flesh and continue crocheting the head.
head:
1: 18 inc (36)
2: (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
3: (6 sc, inc)*6 (48)
4-7: 48 sc (4 rows)
change the color to black. stuff the head and neck tightly as we crochet.
8-11: 48 sc (4 rows)
12: (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
13: (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
14: (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
15: (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
16: (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
17: (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
18: 6 dec. pull off the opening and hide the end of the yarn.
ears:
1: 2 ch and in the 2nd from the hook 6 sc (6)
2: 6 sc (6)
3: (sc, inc)*3 (9)
4: (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
5: (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
6-7: 15 sc
8: 7 sc for both walls, ch
cut the yarn, leave it for sewing.
sew on the ears between the 9th and 12th rows of the head, so that they hold and do not fall.
cloak:
we crochet in black in pivoting rows.
at the beginning of ch set, leave the long end of the yarn to be sewn on.
1: 11 ch turn
2: 10 sc (10)
3: 10 hdc (10)
4: inc (we make from hdc), 8 hdc, inc (12)
5: 12 hdc (12)
6: inc, 10 hdc, inc (14)
7: 14 hdc (14)
8: inc, 12 hdc, inc (16)
9: 16 hdc (16)
10: inc, 14 hdc, inc (18)
11: 18 hdc (18)
12: inc, 16 hdc, inc (20)
13-20: 20 hdc (8 rows), ch
cut and hide the yarn.
assembly:
sew the cloak with the yarn that we left at the beginning, between the head and the body.
embroider the nose with black plush yarn in a triangle, as in the photo.
tie the legs in row 10 flo- 12 sc, and hide the yarn.
cut out eyes from foamiran in white, and an emblem from yellow and black (i found examples on the internet, drew on paper, cut out and cut out foamiran according to this form. glue on hot glue.
that's all, our batman is ready!
