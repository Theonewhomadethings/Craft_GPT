crochet zombie pumpkin mouse amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: mongoreto
pumpkin
rnd 1: 6 sc in a magic loop (6)
rnd 2: 6- inc (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,1inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,1inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,1inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,1inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (5sc,1inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8-13: sc in each stitch (42)
rnd 14: (5sc,1dec)*6 (36)
rnd 15: (4sc,1dec)*6 (30)
rnd 16: (3sc,1dec)*6 (24)
rnd 17: (2sc,1dec)*6 (18)
rnd 18: (1sc,1dec)*6 (12)
rnd 19: 6-dec (6)
sewing needle and shaping the segments.
ears (make 2)
rnd 1: 6 sc in a magic loop (6)
rnd 2: 6-inc (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,1inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-5: sc in each stitch (18)
rnd 6: (1sc,1dec)*6 (12)
rnd 7: 6-dec (6)
fasten off leaving a long tail to attach it.
arms (make 2)
rnd 1: 7sc in a magic loop (7)
rnd 2-8: sc in each stitch (7)
fasten off leaving a long tail to attach it.
legs (make 2)
rnd 1: 5 sc in a magic loop (5)
rnd 2: 5-inc (10)
rnd 3-5: sc in each stitch (10)
fasten off leaving a long tail to attach it.
stuff.
tail
20 ch, return with sl st.
snout
rnd 1: 6 sc in a magic loop (6)
rnd 2: (1sc,1inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: (2sc,1inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4: (3sc,1inc)*3 (15)
fasten off leaving a long tail to attach it.
embroider the nose.
hat
rnd 1: 8 sc in a magic loop (8)
rnd 2-5: sc in each stitch (8)
rnd 6: 8-inc (16)
rnd 7: (1sc,1inc)*8 (24)
finish lap 7 with a 32 point chain cradle, return crocheting 2 sc on each chain.
head
rnd 1: 6 sc in a magic loop (6)
rnd 2: 6-inc (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,1inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,1inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,1inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,1inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7-11: sc in each stitch (36)
rnd 12: (4sc,1dec)*6 (30)
rnd 13: (3sc,1dec)*6 (24)
rnd 14: (2sc,1dec)*6 (18)
fasten off leaving a long tail to attach it.
embroider eyes (tip: you can replace one of them with a button)
sew snout, ears and hat, stuff.
congratulations, your zombie pumpkin mouse amigurumi toy is ready!
