let's start crocheting the amigurumi teddy bear pattern.
ears
rnd 1. 6 sc in a mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (8 sc, inc)*2 (20)
rnd 5. 20 sc
fold the ears in half and make 10 sc, both sides together to close. fasten off leaving a long tail for sewin.
after crocheting the ears of the amigurumi teddy bear, let's crochet the nose part.
muzzle
rnd 1. 6 sc in a mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. 3 sc, (sc, inc)*3, 3 sc, (sc, inc)*3 (24)
rnd 5. 24 sc, ss.
fasten off leaving a long tail for sewing.
let's crochet the head part of the amigurumi teddy bear. brown yarn is used.
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in a mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc)*10 (40)
rnd 7-12. 40 sc
rnd 13. the increases are placed in the sides 4 sc, (2 sc, inc)*4,8 sc, (2 sc, inc)*4, 4 sc (48)
rnd 14-16. 48 sc
rnd 17. (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 18. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 19. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 20. (1 sc, dec)*10 (20)
rnd 21. (2 sc, dec)*5 (15)
ss. fasten off leaving a long tail for sewing. secure safety eyes between rounds 12 and 13 at a distance of 8 sc.
let's crochet the arms and legs of the amigurumi bear pattern
arms
rnd 1. 6 sc in a mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3-4. 12 sc
rnd 5. (2 sc, dec)*3 (9)
rnd 6-10. 9 sc
change color
rnd 11-12. 9 sc
fold the arms in half and make 4 sc, both sides together to close.
legs
rnd 1. 6 sc in a mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd 4. 15 sc
rnd 5. 6 sc, 2 dec, 5 sc (13)
rnd 6. 6 sc, dec, 5 sc (12)
rnd 7-9. 12 sc
on the first leg crochet 2 additional sc, ss. on the second leg crochet 5 additional sc and start working the body.
let's crochet the body part of the amigurumi toy. let's sew the parts we prepared to the body part.
body
chain 2, this will be the new round beginning, attach the first leg.
rnd 1. (12 sc, 2 sc on the chain)*2 (28)
rnd 2. 6 sc, inc, 13 sc, inc, 7 sc (30)
rnd 3-4. 30 sc
in the next two rows increases are placed in the middle.
rnd 5. 12 sc, (inc, sc)*3 , 12 sc (33)
rnd 6. 12 sc, (2 sc, inc)*3, 12 sc (36)
rnd 7. 3 dc in one stitch tail, should be in the back, 35 sc (36)
rnd 8-9. 36 sc change color
rnd 10. 36 sc
rnd 11. 36 sc blo
rnd 12. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 13-14. 30 sc
rnd 15. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 16. 24 sc
rnd 17. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 18. 18 sc
rnd 19. (4 sc, dec)*3 (15) ss
assembly
sew the ears, arms and muzzle. embroider a nose.
