materials
thread 3mm thick colored thread; black, red, yellow, green, pink, white
2.5 mm hook
plastic eye accessory
fiber
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
trc: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
you have to have basic knowledge of crochet, joining pieces.
easy level
let's start crocheting the frida amigurumi pattern
legs (x2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (1 sc, inc) x 3 (9)
rnd 3-12. 9 sc
after crocheting the legs of the amigurumi doll, we crochetthe body and head.
body and head
rnd 13. join with slst, and work 18 sc (9 sc in one leg and 9 sc in the other)
rnd 14-16. 18 sc
rnd 17. 7 sc, dec, 7 sc, dec (16)
rnd 18. 16 sc
rnd 19. 6 sc, dec, 6 sc, dec (14)
rnd 20. 14 sc
rnd 21. 5 sc, dec, 5 sc, dec (12)
rnd 22. 12 sc
rnd 23. 6 dec
rnd 24. 6 sc
rnd 25. 6 inc (12)
rnd 26. (1 sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 27. 1 sc, inc, (2 sc, inc) x 5, 1 sc (24)
8 mm safety eyes between rounds 33 and 34, 8 sc apart.
rnd 28. (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 29. 2 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc) x 5, 2 sc (36)
rnd 30. (5 sc, inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 31-36. 42 sc
rnd 37. (5 sc, dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 38. 2 sc, dec, (4 sc, dec) x 5, 2 sc (30)
rnd 39. (3 sc, dec) x 6 (24)
rnd 40. 1 sc, dec, (2 sc, dec) x 5, 1 sc (18)
rnd 41. (1 sc, dec) x 6 (12)
rnd 42. 6 dec (6)
close
we crochet the skirt and blouse part of the amigurumi doll.
skirt
leave about 30 cm of thread and start with
rnd 1. 25 ch, join with slst and 24 sc
rnd 2-3. 24 sc
rnd 4. (7 sc, inc) x 3 (27)
rnd 5-6. 27 sc
rnd 7. (8 sc, inc) x 3 (30)
rnd 8-9. 30 sc
rnd 10. (9 sc, inc) x 3 (33)
rnd 11-12. 33 sc, close and change color
rnd 13. (1 sc, 1 hdc)*,(1 hdc, 1 sc)*, 1 slst x 11
close.
blouse
rnd 1. 13 ch, 12 sc, ch and turn work (12)
rnd 2. inc, 12 sc, aum, ch and turn work (14)
rnd 3. inc, 14 sc, aum, ch and turn work (16)
rnd 4. inc, 16 sc, aum, ch and turn work (18)
rnd 5-7. 18 sc, ch and turn work
close with a slst and leave a long thread to sew
we crochet the floral decorations on the head of the amigurumi doll.:)
hair bun
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. 1 sc, (2 sc, inc) x 5, 1 sc (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6. 2 sc, (4 sc, inc) x 5, 2 sc (36)
rnd 7. (5 sc, inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 8-12. 42 sc
close and leave a long thread to sew to the head
fringe (x 2)
rnd 1. 17 ch, 6 slst, 2 sc, 8 dc, 2 ch open and close in the first ch
leave a long thread to sew flush with the hair
roete bun
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4. 1 sc, inc, (2 sc, inc) x 5, 1 sc (24)
rnd 5-8. 24 sc
rnd 9. 1 sc, dec (2 sc, dec) x 5, 1 sc (18)
close and leave a long thread to sew to the head
handkerchief
rnd 1. 33 ch, 32 sc from 2nd ch
rnd 2. 2 dec, 28 sc, 2 dec
rnd 3. 2 dec, 24 sc, 2 dec
rnd 4. 2 dec, 20 sc, 2 dec
rnd 5. 2 dec, 16 sc, 2 dec
rnd 6. 26 sc, bordering the entire contour, except the straight part of the handkerchief.
we crochet the arms of the amigurumi frida pattern
arms (x 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2-8. 6 sc
leave long thread to sew to the body
flowers (x 4)
rnd 1. ch 13, from the 2nd ch, 1 sc, (2 sc)*, (1 sc, 1 hdc)*, (2 hdc)*, (1 hdc, 1 dc)*, (*2 dc)* x 6, 1 dc
ch 3 open and join at the end to form a double crochet with those three chains.
we are going to crochet the ears of the amigurumi doll. you can change the color of the earrings on your ears as you wish.
ears (x 2)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
do not close, leave long thread to sew.
assembly and details
sew the hair to the head
sew the bow over the head.
sew the two fringes to the head, up to 6 pe.
sew the ears on rounds 33 and 34
embroider the nose and eyebrows
with colored beads and wire make the earrings
sew the skirt, blouse and arms to the body
place the flowers and the handkerchief, you can sew them with a little thread or glue them
with hot silicone.
congratulations, you have completed the frida amigurumi pattern.
