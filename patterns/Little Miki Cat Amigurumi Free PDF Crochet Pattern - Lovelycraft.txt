materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2.5 mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
safety eyes size 5 mm
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
head
start with white yarn, crochet carefully
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. blo (2 sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*2, (sc, inc)*7, (in grey) (sc, inc), (3 sc, inc)*2 (44)
rnd 6. (grey) 10 sc, (white)(2 sc, in)*6, 2 sc, (grey) inc, 2 sc, inc, 9 sc, inc (53)
rnd 7. (grey) 12 sc, (white) 23 sc, (grey) 18 sc (53)
rnd 8. (grey) 14 sc, (white) 20 sc, (grey) 19 sc (53)
rnd 9. (grey) 15 sc, (white) 18 sc, (grey) 20 sc (53)
rnd 10. (grey) 16 sc, (white) 16 sc, (grey) 21 sc (53)
rnd 11. (grey) 17 sc, (white) 14 sc, (grey) 22 sc (53)
rnd 12. (grey) 13 sc, dec, 3 sc, (white) 5 sc, dec, 5 sc, (grey) 3 sc, dec, 18 sc (50)
rnd 13. (grey) 12 sc, dec, 4 sc, (white) 3 sc, dec, 4 sc, (grey) 3 sc, dec, 17 sc (47)
insert the safety eyes at round 12, 13. 7 sc apart.
rnd 14. (grey) 11 sc, dec, 5 sc, (white) 6 sc, (grey) 5 sc, dec, 16 sc (45)
rnd 15. (grey) 18 sc, (white) 4 sc, (grey) 23 sc (45)
rnd 16. (grey) 19 sc, (white) 2 sc, (grey) 24 sc (45)
rnd 17. (grey) 19 sc, (white) 1-dec, (grey) 24 sc (44)
rnd 18-20. (3 rnds) 43 sc (43)
rnd 21. (9 sc, dec)*3 (40)
rnd 22. 40 sc (40)
rnd 23. (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 24. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
rnd 25. (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 26. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end
arm*2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-5. (3 rnds) 12 sc (12)
change to yellow
rnd 6-15. (10 rnds) 12 sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
leg*2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. 12 sc (12)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*4 (16)
rnd 5-6. (2 rnds) 16 sc (16)
cut the yarn make the 2nd leg in the same way till rnd 6.
don't cut the yarn. join in 1 st leg. continue to the body.
body
rnd 1. (3 sc, inc)*8 (40)
rnd 2. 10 sc, inc, 18 sc, inc , 10 sc (42)
rnd 3-9. (8 rnds) 42 sc (42)
rnd 10. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 11-12. (2 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 13. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 14-15. (2 rnds) 30 sc (30)
rnd 16. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 17. blo 24 sc (24)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
shirt
start with yellow. find blo round of the body (rnd 17) and start to crochet the shirt.
rnd 1. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 2. 30 sc (30)
rnd 3. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 4-5. (2 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 6. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 7-15. (9 rnds) 42 sc (42)
slst. cut the yarn and weave in the end
ear*2
start with grey
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4-inc (8)
rnd 3. (3 sc, inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4. (4 sc, inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5. (5 sc, inc)*2 (14), slst
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
hat
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3 sc, inc)*7, 2 sc (40)
rnd 6. (4-hdc, inc)*8 (48)
rnd 7-8. (2 rnds) 48 hdc (48)
rnd 9. 8 hdc, 8 chains, (skip 8 hdc), 9 sc, 8 chains, (skip 8 hdc), 15 hdc (48)
rnd 10. 11 hdc, inc, (5 hdc, inc)*3, 18 hdc (52)
rnd 11. 12 hdc, inc, (6 hdc, inc)*3, 18 hdc (56)
rnd 12. 54 hdc, 2 sc, slst (56)
little miki cat is ready.
