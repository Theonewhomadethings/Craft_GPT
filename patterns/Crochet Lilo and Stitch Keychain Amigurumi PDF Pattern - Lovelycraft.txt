i think it is a great solution to use buttons on your eyes while doing stitch doll amigurumi. such a design adds a very stylish air to the amigurumi. it is also quite gorgeous in the chosen color. i shared two differently made stitch pictures. i hope you like the crochet stitch amigurumi. now let's crochet the stitch amigurumi step by step.

designer: twinspsins
necessary materials
yarn pekhorka children's novelty (colors: 581
(main color), 351 (bow), 12 (hair), 02 (mouth).
hook 2 mm
buttons with a diameter of 1.5 cm
toy height 10 cm
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
trc: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
head
1rnd: 6 sc in mr
2rnd: inc*6 (12)
3rnd: (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4rnd: (2 sc, inc)*6 times (24)
5rnd: (3 sc, inc)*6 times (30)
6rnd: (4 sc, inc)*6 times (36)
7rnd: (5 sc, inc)*6 times (42)
8-16rnd: 42sc (9 rounds)
17rnd: (5 sc, dec)*6 times (36)
18rnd: (4 sc, dec)*6 times (30)
19rnd: (3 sc, dec)*6 times (24)
20rnd: (2 sc, dec)*6 times (18)
21rnd: (1 sc, dec)*6 times (12)
22rnd: dec*6 (6)
finish crocheting, fasten the yarn and hide.
we crocheted the head of the stitch amigurumi. now it's the body's turn.
body
1rnd: 6 sc in mr
2rnd: inc*6 times (12)
3rnd: (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4rnd: 18 sc
5rnd: (2 sc, inc)*6 times (24)
6rnd: (5 sc, inc)*4 times (28)
7-8rnd: 28sc (2 round)
9rnd: (5 sc, dec)*4 times (24)
10rnd: 24sc
11rnd: (2 sc, dec)*6 times (18)
12rnd: (1 sc, dec)*6 times (12)
13rnd: 12sc
14rnd: dec*6 (6)
finish crocheting, leave the yarn for sewing.
we crocheted the body of the stitch amigurumi.
hands (2pcs)
1rnd: 6 sc in mr
2rnd: inc*6 times (12)
3-6rnd: 12sc (4 round).
7rnd: (1 sc, dec)*4 times (8)
8-9rnd: 8 sc (2 round)
finish crocheting, leave the yarn for sewing.
the arms and legs of the doll amigurumi are very easy to make.
legs (2 pcs)
1rnd: 6 sc in mr
2rnd: inc*6 times (12)
3rnd: (1 sc, inc)*6 times (18)
4-7rnd: 18 sc (4 round)
8rnd: (1 sc, dec)*6 times (12)
9rnd: (1 sc, dec)*4 times (8)
finish crocheting, leave the yarn for sewing.
bow
19ch
1rnd: crochet froom the second loop sc, 18 sc (19)
2-8rnd: 19sc blo
fasten and hide the thread.
assembly
1) sew the head to the body.
2) sew hands between the first and second row from the head of the toy on the sides.
3) sew the legs between the sixth and ninth rows from the head of the toy.
4) between the fourth and sixth rows from the body, we embroider a smile on the toy.
5) at the level of 8 and 9 rows, sew on the eyes of the buttons.
6) sew a bow on the top of the head and attach some hair threads.
