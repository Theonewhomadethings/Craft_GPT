crochet doll frida kahlo amigurumi pdf pattern

abbreviations
ch: chain
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
hdc: half double crochet
dc: double crochet
tr: treble /triple stitch
hdcinc: half double crochet increase
hdcdec: half double crochet decrease
dcinc: double crochet increase
dcdec: double crochet decrease
slst: slip stitch
flo: use the front loops only
blo: use the back loops only
bobble: bobble stitch
(..): total number of stitches after the round / row.
[] x . times: repeat instructions in parentheses x times
materials
yarnart jeans/ skin (color code 73)
yarnart jeans/ cream (color code 03)
yarnart jeans/ red (color code 66)
yarnart jeans/ green (color code 52)
yarnart jeans/ pink (color code 59)
yarnart jeans/ blue (color code 55)
yarnart jeans/ purple (color code 50)
yarnart jeans/ black (color code 53)
size 2.5mm crochet hook
12mm safety eye (black)
electrical wire 1,5mm (10cm)
for embroidery: black thread
fiberfill
pins
stitch marker
legs (with skin color yarn)
rnd 1 chain 6
rnd 2 starting from the second chain; 4 sc, 4 sc into one stitch, (continue with other side of the chain), 3 sc, 1 inc (13)
rnd 3 [1 inc], 3 sc, [1 inc] repeats 4 times, 3 sc, [1 inc] repeat 2 times (20)
rnd 4 [1 sc, 1 inc], 3 sc, [1 sc, 1 inc] repeat 4 times, 3 sc, [1 sc, 1 inc] repeat 2 times (27)
rnd 5 blo 27 sc
rnd 6-7 sc in all 27 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 8 sc in the next 6 st, (dec) repeat 7 times, sc in the next 7 st (20)
rnd 9 sc in the next 5 st, (dec) repeat 5 times, sc in the next 5 st (15)
rnd 10 (3 sc, 1 dec) repeat 3 times (12)
**stuff the legs.
rnd 11-26 sc in all 12 sts (16 rounds)
**stuff the legs.
body
continue with skin color
rnd 27 to join two legs; 2 sc along the left leg, ch 6, join to the middle of the right leg with a slst and 12 sc of the leg, flo sc over the ch 6 stitches, 10 sc (36)
rnd 28-29 sc in all 36 sts (2 rounds)
change to cream color.
rnd 30-34 sc in all 36 sts (5 rounds)
rnd 35 blo 36 sc
rnd 36 sc in all 36
rnd 37 blo 36 sc
rnd 38-40 sc in all 36 sts (3 rounds)
rnd 41 (4 sc, 1 dec) repeat 6 times (30)
**stuff the body.
rnd 42-43 sc in all 30 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 44 (3 sc, 1 dec) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 45 sc in all 24
change to skin color.
rnd 46 blo 24 sc
rnd 47 (2 sc, 1 dec) repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 48 (1 sc, 1 dec) repeat 6 times (12)
rnd 49-50 sc in all 12 sts (2 rounds)
**stuff the body.
**a electrical wire will be inserted to keep the neck straight
head (with skin color yarn)
rnd 1 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 (1 sc,1 inc) repeats 6 times (18)
rnd 4 (1 sc, 1 inc, 1 sc) repeats 6 times (24)
rnd 5 (3 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (30)
rnd 6 (2 sc, 1 inc, 2 sc) repeats 6 times (36)
rnd 7 (5 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (42)
rnd 8 (3 sc, 1 inc, 3 sc) repeats 6 times (48)
rnd 9 (7 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (54)
rnd 10 (4 sc, 1 inc, 4 sc) repeats 6 times (60)
rnd 11-18 60 sc (8 rounds)
rnd 19 25 sc, ch 1(skip 1 st and go in 2rd st), 8 sc, ch 1(skip 1 st and go in 2rd st), 25 sc (58)
rnd 20-23 60 sc (5 rounds)
rnd 24 (8 sc,1 dec) repeats 6 times (54)
rnd 25 (7 sc,1 dec) repeats 6 times (48)
rnd 26 (4 sc,1 dec) repeats 8 times (40)
rnd 27 (3 sc,1 dec) repeats 8 times (32)
rnd 28 (2 sc,1 dec) repeats 8 times (24)
rnd 29 (2 sc,1 dec) repeats 6 times (18)
rnd 30 (1 sc,1 dec) repeats 6 times (12)
** attach safety eyes to ch1 in the 19th row where we did it.
**stuff the head
arm (with skin color yarn) make 8
rnd 1 5 sc in the magic ring (5)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (10)
rnd 3-5 10 sc (2 rounds)
rnd 6 dec, 6sc, dec (8)
rnd 7-17 8 sc (11 rounds)
change to cream color.
rnd18 flo (1 inc) repeats 8 times (16)
rnd 19-20 16 sc (2 rounds)
rnd 21 (1 inc) repeats 8 times (8)
rnd 22 (1 inc) repeats 4 times (4)
petticoat (with purple color yarn)
on the blo stitches of body in rnd 35;
row 1 (5 dc,1 dcinc) repeats 6 times, slst (42)
row 2-11 ch 2, 42 dc, slst (10 rows)
row 12 ch 1, (1 sc, ch 2) repeats 42 times, slst
skirt (with green color yarn)
on the blo stitches of body in rnd 37;
row 1 ch 2, (1 hdcinc) repeats 36 times, slst (72)
row 2 ch 2, (1 hdc, 1 hdcinc) repeats 36 times, slst (108)
row 3-14 ch 2, 108 hdc, slst (12 rows)
change to pink color.
row 15 ch 1, 108 sc, slst (108)
change to green color.
row 16 ch 1, 108 sc, slst (108)
row 17 ch 1, (1 sc, ch 3) repeats 108 times, slst
collar (with cream color yarn)
we will crochet the collar by joining our yarn into the blo stitches of round 46 on the body.
row 1 ch 2, (1 hdcinc) repeats 24 times, slst (48)
change to red color.
row 2 ch 1, (1 sc, ch 3) repeats 48 times, slst
skirt flowers (with pink color yarn) make 8
rnd 1 5 sc in the magic ring (5)
rnd 2 (1 sc, 1 dc, ch1, 1 dc, 1 sc) repeats 5 times
hair (with black color yarn)
rnd 1 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 (1 sc,1 inc) repeats 6 times (18)
rnd 4 (1 sc, 1 inc, 1 sc) repeats 6 times (24)
rnd 5 (3 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (30)
rnd 6 (2 sc, 1 inc, 2 sc) repeats 6 times (36)
rnd 7 (5 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (42)
rnd 8 (3 sc, 1 inc, 3 sc) repeats 6 times (48)
rnd 9 (7 sc, 1 inc) repeats 6 times (54)
rnd 10 (4 sc, 1 inc, 4 sc) repeats 6 times (60)
rnd 11-20 60 sc (10 rounds)
rnd 21 20 sc, 1 hdc, 2 dc, 3 tr, 2 dc, 1 hdc, 2 slst, 1 hdc, 2 dc, 3 tr, 2 dc, 1 hdc, 20 sc (60)
**sew the lower parts of the flowers. then fix it by sewing in the middle of the head as in the images
flowers (with red color yarn) make 6
rnd 1 ch 14
rnd 2 starting from the 3nd stitch from hook crochet; [1 dcinc] repeats 12 times (24)
cape edge (with blue color yarn)
row 1 ch 56
row 2 starting from the 5nd stitch from hook crochet 1 dc, (skip 2st and go into the 3th st), [1 dcinc, ch 2, 1 dcinc (skip 2st and go into the 3th st)] repeats 17 times
** crochet the next rounds in the ch space in the middle of the dc group
row 3-5 ch 2, turn, [1 dcinc, ch 2, 1 dcinc, (skip 3st and go into the 4th st)] repeats 18 times, 1 dc (3 rows)
row 6 ch 1, (1 sc, ch 3) repeats 55 times, slst
shoes (with blue color yarn)
rnd 1 chain 8
rnd 2 starting from the 2nd stitch from hook crochet 6sc on chain, 4 sc inc into last stitch, continue into the unused loops on the opposite side of the chain 5sc, 1 inc (17)
rnd 3 (1 inc), 5 sc, (1 inc) repeats 4 times, 5 sc, (1 inc) repeats 2 times (22)
rnd 4 (1 sc, 1 inc), 5 sc, (1 sc, 1 inc) repeat 4 times, 5 sc, (1 sc, 1 inc) repeat 2 times (31)
rnd 5 blo 31 sc
rnd 6-7 31 sc (2 rounds)
rnd 8 8 sc, (1 dec) repeats 7 times, 9 sc (24)
rnd 9 24 slst
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi frida kahlo.
