little bear amigurumi free pattern
the cute little bear amigurumi pattern is small in size. making the amigurumi bear pattern can be a bit of a challenge for you. be patient and continue to crochet. the yarn color used for the amigurumi toy is brown. yellow yarn is preferred in the body part. the body and legs of the free amigurumi pattern are crocheted together. her arms are crocheted using yellow yarn. her hands are crocheted using brown yarn. you can crochet the mouth and nose of the free pattern using black yarn after the pattern is completed. don't forget to add the cute little bear to your favorite list.

materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2,5 mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5 mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
let's start making the amigurumi crochet cute bear pattern
head
rnd 1. 7 sc in mr (7)
rnd 2. 7 inc (14)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*7 (21)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*7 (21)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3 sc, inc)*6, 2 sc (35)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)*7 (42)
rnd 7. 2 sc, inc, (5 sc, inc)*6, 3 sc (49)
rnd 8-12. (5 rnds) 49 sc (49)
rnd 13. (6 sc, inc)*7 (56)
rnd 14-17. (4 rnds) 56 sc (56)
rnd 18. (5 sc, dec)*8 (48)
rnd 19. 48 sc (48)
rnd 20. (4 sc, dec)*8 (40)
rnd 21. (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 22. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
insert the eyes at round 13, 14, 9 sc apart
cut the yarn, weave in the end.
after completing the head part of the cute bear amigurumi pattern, let's crochet the legs.
legs x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd 4. 15 sc (15)
cut the yarn, crochet the 2nd leg in the same way till rnd 4. do not cut the yarn, make 3 chains, join in 1 st leg. continue to the body.
let's crochet the body part of the amigurumi bear. in this part, i recommend using 2 different colors of yarn. you can make one color if you want.
body
rnd 1. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 2-6. (5 rnds) 42 sc (42)
change to yellow
rnd 7. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 8-9. (2 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 10. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 11-12. (2 rnds) 30 sc (30)
rnd 13. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 14. blo 24 sc (24)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing
we have completed the body part of the amigurumi pattern. now let's crochet the arms. you can use 2 different colored threads for the arms.
arm x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. 9 sc (9)
rnd 4-9. (6 rnds) 9 sc (9)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
let's crochet the ears of the cute bear free pattern
ear x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 sc (6)
rnd 3. 4 sc, dec (5)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing
let's crochet the collar part of the cute bear amigurumi pattern.
collar
insert the hook to blo round on the body. chain 4, slst in next stitch till end round. cut the yarn and weave in the end.
congratulations, you have completed the little bear amigurumi pattern.
