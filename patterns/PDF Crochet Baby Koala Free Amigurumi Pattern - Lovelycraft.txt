ch chain
sc single crochet

tr -triple crochet
inc sc increase
dec sc decrease
slst-stip stitch
let's start by crocheting the nose part of the amigurumi koala pattern.
nose
black cotton; 3 mm hook
mr: 1 ch, 1 sc, 1 ch, 1 hdc, 1 ch, 2 sc, 1 ch, 1 hdc, 1 ch, 1 sc
let's make the pacifier and tassel details of the amigurumi baby koala pattern
pacifier
liliac cotton. 1.7 mm hook
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 inc(12)
tassel
liliac cotton, 1.7 mm hook
mr: (4 ch, 4 tr, 4 ch, 1 slst)*2
let's crochet the eucalyptus leaf in the hand of the amigurumi koala pattern
eucalyptus leaf
green cotton; 3 mm hook
10 ch and turn
1 slst in the next 3 stitches,
3 sc in the next 3 stitches,
3 hdc in the next 3 stitches
let's crochet the body part of the amigurumi koala
body
grey cotton
rnd 1.6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, inc) (18)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) (24)
rnd 5. (3sc, inc) (30)
rnd 6-10. 30 sc
rnd 11. (3sc, dec) (24)
rnd 12. (2sc, dec) (18)
fill with fiberfill; sew nose and eyes
rnd 13. (1sc, dec) (12)
rnd 14. 12 sc
rnd 15. (1sc, inc) (18)
rnd 16. (2sc, inc) (24)
rnd 17. 24 sc
rnd 18. 24 sc
white cotton
rnd 19-21. 24 sc
rnd 22. (2sc, dec) (18)
rnd 23. (1sc, dec) (12)
rnd 24. dec (6)
cut the yarn and close the opening
let's crochet the ears and arms of the amigurumi koala
ears
grey cotton
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc) (18)
rnd 4. 18 sc
arms
grey cotton
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (1sc, inc) (9)
rnd 3. 9 sc
rnd 4. 9 sc
rnd 5. 7 sc, dec (8)
rnd 6-10. 8 sc
rnd 11. dec, 5 sc (7)
let's crochet the legs of the amigurumi koala
legs
grey cotton
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. 12 sc
rnd 4. 12 sc
rnd 5. (2sc, dec) (9)
rnd 6. 9 sc
rnd 7. 9 sc
rnd 8. 8 sc, dec (10)
rnd 9-11. 10 sc
rnd 12. 8, dec (9)
rnd 13. 7, dec (8)
sew all the parts togheter and embroider the ears hairs
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi baby koala pattern.
