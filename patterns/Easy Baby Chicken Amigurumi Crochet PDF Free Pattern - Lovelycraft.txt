baby chicken amigurumi free crochet pattern
designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc hald double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn.
crochet hook: 2mm- 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
safety eyes size 5-6mm
body
star with yellow yarn:
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3sc,inc)*7,2sc (40)
rnd 6-7. (2rnds) 40sc (40)
rnd 8. (4sc,inc)*8 (48)
rnd 9-10. (2rnds)48sc (48)
rnd 11. (5sc,inc)*8 (56)
rnd 12. (13sc,inc)*4 (60)
rnd 13-17. (5rnds) 60sc (60)
change to green:
rnd 18-21. (4rnds) 60sc (60)
rnd 22. (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 23. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 24. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 25. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 26. (sc,dec)*12 (24)
rnd 27. blo (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 28. (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 29. 6dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
beak (orange yarn)
big piece
make 6 chain, from ch2 make 1sc, 3hdc, 1sc (5), slst
leave long tail for sewing.
small piece
make 2 chains, insert the hook into first chain,
make 3sc, 1 chain, slst. cut the yarn, leave a long
tail for sewing.
wing*2
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4 inc (8)
rnd 3. (3sc,inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4. (4sc,inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5-9. (5rnds) 12sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
feet*2
make 2 chain, insert the hook into first chain,
make 3sc, 1 chain, slst. cut the yarn, leave a long tail
and sew to the body.
hat
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8 inc (16)
rnd 3-4. (2rnds) 16sc (16)
rnd 5. blo (sc,inc)*8 (24), slst
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
your baby chicken is now ready.. ? ?
