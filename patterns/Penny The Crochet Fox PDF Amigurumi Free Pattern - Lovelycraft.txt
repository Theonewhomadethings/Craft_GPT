penny the crochet fox pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: aquariwool
materials
yarn color:
(a) black: yarnart jeans 53 (50g, 160m)
(b) white: yarnart jeans 62 (50g, 160m)
(c) orange: yarnart jeans 23 (50g, 160m)
(d) green: alize cotton gold 610 (100g, 330m)
other materials:
crochet hook size: 2- 2.5mm
knitting needles size: 3.5- 4mm
1 wooden button
tapestry needles, pins, stitch markers, scissors
the crocheted fox size, complete with the specified materials, will be 13 cm.
abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
hdc half double crochet
sl st slip stitch
dc double crochet
tr treble crochet
bobble bobble stitch (4 hdc in the same st)
blo back loop only
flo front loop only
sts stitches
rnd round
p-purl
k knit
co-cast on
legs (make 2)
yarn c
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
break the yarn, make the second leg in the same way, but don't break the yarn this time. connect 2 legs together: sc 15 (leg 1)+ sc 15 (leg 2) = 30. move to the body part.
body
yarn c
rnd 1. (sc4,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 2-7. sc 36 (6 rows)
rnd 8. (sc4,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 9. sc 30 (30)
rnd 10. (sc3,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 11. sc 24 (24)
rnd 12. (sc2,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 13. sc 18 (18)
break the yarn with a long rest. stuff.
arms (make 2)
yarn a
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc1,inc)*3, change to yarn c (9)
rnd 3-7. sc 9 (5 rows)
rnd 4. dec 1, sc 7 (8)
rnd 5. dec 1, sc 6 (7)
rnd 6. dec 1, sc 5 (6)
break the yarn with a long rest. stuff slightly. sew 2 edges together.
head
yarn b
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10. sc 54 (54)
rnd 11. (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 12-19. sc 60 (8 rows)
rnd 20. (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 21. sc 54 (54)
rnd 22. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 23. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 24. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 25. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 26. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 27. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later. stuff.
hat
yarn c
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10. 54 sc (54)
rnd 11. (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 12-18. sc 60 (7 rows) (60)
rnd 19. sc 1, hdc 1, dc 26, hdc 1, sc 1, turn (30)
rnd 20. ch 1, sc 1, dec, hdc 3, dc 7, tr 4, dc 7, hdc 3, dec, sc 1, turn (28)
rnd 21. ch 1, sc 4, dec, hdc 3, dc 3, tr 4, dc 3, hdc 3, dec, sc 4, turn (26)
rnd 22. ch 1, dec 2, sc 2, hdc 2, dc 12, hdc 2, sc 2, dec 2, turn (24)
rnd 23. ch 1, 8 slst, sc 1, hdc 1, dc 4, hdc 1, sc 1, 8 slst, break the yarn (24)
rnd 24. join the yarn at the 8th slst from the last round, ch 1, dec, hdc 1, dc 2, hdc 1, dec, turn (6)
rnd 25. ch 1, dec, hdc 2, dec, turn (4)
rnd 26. dec 2 (2)
crochet along the hat with sc stitch. break the yarn with a long rest to sew later.
tail
yarn b
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. sc 2, inc 2, sc 2 (6)
rnd 3. sc 3, inc 2, sc 3 (8)
rnd 4. sc 4, inc 2, sc 4 (12)
rnd 5. sc 5, inc 2, sc 5, change to yarn c (14)
rnd 6. sc 6, inc 2, sc 6 (16)
rnd 7. sc 7, inc 2, sc 7 (18)
rnd 8. sc 18 (18)
rnd 9. (inc,2sc)*6 (24)
rnd 10-14. sc 24 (5 rows) (24)
rnd 15. (dec,2sc)*6 (18)
rnd 16-18. sc 18 (3 rows) (18)
rnd 19. (dec, 1sc)*6 (12)
rnd 20-22. sc 12 (3 rows) (12)
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later. stuff.
ears (make 2)
yarn a
rnd 1. 3 sc in mr (3)
rnd 2. 3 inc (6)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 5. (1sc,inc)*6, change to yarn c (18)
rnd 6. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 7-13. 24 sc (7 rows) (24)
break the yarn with a long rest, sew two edges together.
scarf
yarn d, using knitting needles.
rnd 1. co 6 st (6)
rnd 2. k 6 (6)
rnd 3. p 6 (6)
repeat rnd 2 and 3 till you reach 15 cm then cast off, break the yarn, hide the remaining yarn. use the tapestry needle to attach the wooden button to the scarf. that's done!
use yarn a to embroider the eyes, and the nose (the nose, embroider tight above the 2 last rnd of the hat).
use yarn a to embroider the mouth.
add some pink color for the cheeks if you like.
