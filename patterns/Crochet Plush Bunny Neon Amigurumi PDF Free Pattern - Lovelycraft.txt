designer
instagram: holodusya_knits
materials:
yarn, bobbin shinil of two colors 160m / 100g, any other plush yarn!
hook 4.0 and (6.0, for knitting ears);
eyes 14mm;
spout "heart" 12mm;
holofiber filler 50g.
abbreviations
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
tr: treble/triple crochet
trinc: work 2 tr sts intothe same st.
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
sl-st: slip stitch
ch: chain
st/sts: stitch/stitches
flo: front loop only.
blo: back loop only.
ears (2 parts):
rnd 1) 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2) (2sc, inc)2 (8)
rnd 3) (3sc, inc)2 (10)
rnd 4) (4sc, inc)2 (12)
rnd 5) (5sc, inc)2 (14)
rnd 6) (6sc, inc)2 (16)
rnd 7) (7sc, inc)2 (18)
rnd 8) (8sc, inc)2 (20)
rnd 9-19) 20sc (11 rows)
rnd 20) (8sc, dec)2 (18)
rnd 21) (7sc, dec)2 (16)
rnd 22) (6sc, dec)2 (14)
rnd 23) (5sc, dec)2 (12)
rnd 24) 12sc
rnd 25) 6sc (for both walls)
head:
white
rnd 1) 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2) (inc)6 (12)
rnd 3) (sc, inc)6 (18)
grey
rnd 4) (2sc, inc) 6 (24)
rnd 5) sc, inc,(3sc, inc)5, 2sc (30)
rnd 6) (4sc, inc)6 (36)
rnd 7-8) 36sc (2 row)
rnd 9) (we knit ears) 10sc,(6sc+ear), 4sc,(6sc+ear), 10sc (36)
rnd 10-11) 36sc (2 row)
rnd 12) (4sc, dec)6 (30)
rnd 13) (3sc, dec)6 (24)
rnd 14) (2sc, dec)6 (18)
rnd 15) (sc, dec)6 (12)
rnd 16) (dec)6 (6)
cut off the yarn, tighten the hole, hide the thread inward with using a needle.
upper legs (2 times):
white
rnd 1) 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2) (inc)6 (12)
rnd 3) 12sc (12)
gray
rnd 4) 12sc (12)
rnd 5) (dec)6 (6)
rnd 6-9) 6sc (4 row)
rnd 10) 3sc (for both sides)
we fix the slst, hide the yarn inward with a needle.
lower legs (2 times):
white
rnd 1) 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2) (inc)6 (12)
rnd 3) (sc, inc)6 (18)
rnd 4) 18sc
gray
rnd 5-6) 18sc (2 row)
rnd 7) 3sc, (dec)6, 3sc (12)
rnd 8) 4sc, (dec)2, 4sc (10)
rnd 9-12) 10sc+2sc (for symmetry) (4 row)
rnd 13) we knit for both sides of 5sc
we fix the slst, hide the yarn inside with a needle.
body:
rnd 1) 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2) (inc)6 (12)
rnd 3) (sc, inc)6 (18)
rnd 4) (2sc, inc) 6 (24)
rnd 5) sc, inc, (3sc, inc)5, 2sc (30)
rnd 6) (4sc, inc)6 (36)
rnd 7) 35sc, 5dc in one loop(tail) (36)
rnd 8) (we knit the lower legs) 6sc, (5sc+paw), 13sc, (5sc+paw), 7sc (36)
rnd 9) 36 sc
rnd 10) (4sc, dec)6 (30)
rnd 11) 30sc
rnd 12) (3sc, dec)6 (24)
rnd 13) 7sc, (dec)6, 5sc (18)
rnd 14) (sc, dec)6 (12)
rnd 15) (we knit the upper legs) 2sc, (3sc+ arm), 2sc, (3sc+ arm), 2sc (12)
rnd 16) (optional) 12sc.
