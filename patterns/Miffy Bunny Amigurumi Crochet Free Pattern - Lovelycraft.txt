miffy bunny amigurumi free crochet pattern
designer
instagram: beary_bearnita_
ravelry: beary_bearnita_
etsy: beary_bearnita_
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2.5 mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
safety eyes size 6 mm
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease ( invisible decrease)
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
now everything is ready. lets crochet miffy
leg*2
rnd 1: 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2: 8-inc (16)
rnd 3: (3 sc, inc)*4 (20)
rnd 4-8: (5 rnds) 20 sc (20)
cut the yarn, make the 2nd leg in the same way. don't cut the yarn. join in 1 st leg and continue to the body.
body
rnd 1: (4 sc, inc)*8 (48)
rnd 2-8: (7 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 9: (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 10-13: (4 rnds) 42 sc (42)
rnd 14: (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 15-16: (2 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 17: (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 18-19: (2 rnds) 30 sc (30)
rnd 20: (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 21: blo 24 sc (24), slst
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
head
rnd 1: 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2: 8-inc (16)
rnd 3: (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4: (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 5: (4 sc, inc)*6(36)
rnd 6: (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 7: (6 sc, inc)*6 (48)
rnd 8: (7 sc, inc)*6 (54)
rnd 9: (8 sc, inc)*6 (60)
rnd 10-20: (11 rnds) 60 sc (60)
rnd 21: (8 sc, dec)*6 (54)
rnd 22: (7 sc, dec)*6 (48)
rnd 23: 48 sc (48)
rnd 24: (4 sc, dec)*8 (40)
rnd 25: (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 26: (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
rnd 27: (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 28: 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end
arm*2
rnd 1: 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2: 8-inc (16)
rnd 3-6: (4 rnds) 16 sc (16)
change to orange
rnd 7-16: (10 rnds) 16 sc (16)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
ear*2
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4-5: (2 rnds) 12 sc (12)
rnd 6: (4 sc, dec)*2 (10)
rnd 7-8: (2 rnds) 10 sc (10)
rnd 9: (3 sc, dec)*2 (8)
rnd 10-11: (2 rnds) 8 sc (8)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
skirt
insert the hook to th blo round on the body (rnd 21). start to crochet the skirt in orange
rnd 1: (2 sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 2: 32 sc (32)
rnd 3: (3 sc, inc)*8 (40)
rnd 4-5: (2 rnds) 40 sc (40)
rnd 6: (4 sc, inc)*8 (48)
rnd 7-8: (2 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 9: (11 sc, inc)*4 (52)
rnd 10-11: (2 rnds) 52 sc (52)
rnd 12: (12 sc, inc)*4 (56)
rnd 13-18: (6 rnds) 56 sc (56)
slst.
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
carrot
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: inc*6 (12)
rnd 3-7: 12 sc (12)
rnd 8: 4 sc, 1 dec (10)
rnd 9: 10 sc (10)
rnd 10: 3 sc, 1 dec (8)
rnd 11: 8 sc (8)
rnd 12: 2 sc, 1 dec (6)
rnd 13: 1 sc, 1 hdc (4)
with a tapestry needle, close the 4 st. add green threads (leaves).
(carrot accessory designer: pipizzuellacrochet)
