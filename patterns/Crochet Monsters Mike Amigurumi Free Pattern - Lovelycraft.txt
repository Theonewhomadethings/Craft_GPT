designer

abbreviations
mr: magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
rnd: round
we start crocheting our one-eyed green monster amigurumi character, first of all, from the legs.
legs (make 2)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: 18sc (18)
rnd 5: 5sc,4dec,5sc (14)
rnd 6: 3sc,4dec,3sc (10)
rnd 7: (3sc,dec)*2 (8)
rnd 8-14: 8sc (8)
after crocheting the legs of the green overhead monster, let's move on to the construction of its arms.
arms (make 2)
rnd 1: 5sc in mr (5)
rnd 2: 5 inc (10)
rnd 3-4: 10sc (10)
rnd 5: (3sc,dec)*2 (8)
rnd 6-14: 8sc (8)
we have completed the arms and legs of the cute crochet amigurumi monster very nicely with crochet. now it's time to create one eye.
eye
rnd 1: 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2: (1sc,inc)*4 (12)
white yarn
rnd 3: (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
after finishing the cute green monster amigurumi character's eye with crochet beautifully, we are making his horns. remember, you will do 2 the same way.
horns (make 2)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (2sc,inc)*2 (8)
rnd 3: (3sc,inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4: (4sc,inc)*2 (12)
after completing the horns of the cute green one-eyed monster, it remains to make the body, which is the last part, and then the green one-eyed amigurumi monster is completely finished.
body
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6 inc (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8: (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9: (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10: (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 11-20. 60 sc(60)
rnd 21. (8sc, dec) x6 (54)
rnd 22. (7sc, dec) x6 (48)
rnd 23. (6sc, dec) x6 (42)
rnd 24. (5sc, dec) x6 (36)
rnd 25. (4sc, dec) x6 (30)
rnd 26. (3sc, dec) x6 (24)
rnd 27. (2sc, dec) x6 (18)
rnd 28. (1sc, dec) x6 (12)
rnd 29. 6 dec (6)
congratulations we have successfully completed the one eye green crochet monster toy. see you in the next free pattern.
