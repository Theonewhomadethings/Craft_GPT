scissors
for the pupils (eyes)
1,25 mm crochet hook
black mercerized yarn you use black felt for the pupils (eyes)
abbreviations
rnd = round
sc = single crochet
inc = increase/
dec = decrease
slst = slip stitch
sti sts = stitch/ stitches
ch = chain
blo = back loop only
flo = front loop only
() x = repeat instructions in the round bracket for the indicated number of times
[ ] = number of stitches in each round/ row
head
(start with black)
stuff firmly as you crochet.
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3: (inc) x 12 (24)
rnd 4: (3 sc, dec) x 6 (30)
rnd 5: 30 sc (30)
rnd 6: (4 sc, dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 7: (5 sc, dec) x 6 (42)
rnd 8-17: 42 sc (10 rounds) (42)
change color to blue
rnd 18: 6 sc, (inc) x 10, 10 sc, (inc) x 10, 6 sc (62)
rnd 19: 62 sc (62)
rnd 20: 6 sc, (1 sc, inc) x 10,10 sc, (1 sc, inc) x 10, 6 sc (82)
rnd 21: 82 sc (82)
rnd 22: 9 sc, (2 sc, inc) x 9,10 sc, (2 sc, inc) x 9, 9 sc (100)
rnd 23: 100 sc (100)
rnd 24: 41 sc, (2 sc, inc) x 6, 41 sc (106)
rnd 25-29: 106 sc (5 rounds) (106)
rnd 30: 41 sc, (2 sc, dec) x 6, 41 sc (100)
rnd 31: 9 sc, (2 sc, dec) x 9,10 sc, (2 sc, dec) x 9, 9 sc (82)
rnd 32: 6 sc, (1 sc, dec) x 10,10 sc, (1 sc, dec) x 10, 6 sc (62)
rnd 33: 6 sc, (dec) x 10, 10 sc, (dec) x 10, 6 sc (42)
rnd 34-37: 42 sc (4 rounds) (42)
rnd 38: (5 sc, dec) x 6 (36)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing.
body
(using blue)
stuff firmly as you crochet.
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) x 6 (36)
rnd 7: (5 sc, inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 8: (6 sc, inc) x 6 (48)
rnd 9: (7 sc, inc) x 6 (54)
rnd 10: 54 sc (54)
rnd 11: (8 sc, inc) x 6 (60)
rnd 12-15: 60 sc (4 rounds) (60)
rnd 16: 15 sc, (4 sc, inc) x 6, 15 sc (66)
rnd 17-23: 66 sc (7 rounds) (66)
rnd 24: 15 sc, (4 sc, dec) x 6, 15 sc (60)
rnd 25-27: 60 sc (3 rounds) (60)
rnd 28: (8 sc, dec) x 6 (54)
rnd 29: (7 sc, dec) x 6 (48)
rnd 30: (6 sc, dec) x 6 (42)
rnd 31-36: 42 sc (6 rounds) (42)
rnd 37: (5 sc, dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 38-44: 42 sc (7 rounds) (36)
fasten off.
nose
(using red)
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3: (3 sc, inc) x 3 (15)
rnd 4-6: 15 sc (3 rounds) (15)
rnd 7: (3 sc, dec) x 3 (12)
rnd 8: (3 sc, inc) x 3 (15)
rnd 9: 15 sc (15)
rnd 10: (3 sc, dec) x 3 (12)
rnd 11: (2 sc, dec) x 3 (9)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing. stuff the nose.
sclera
(make 2) (using blue)
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing.
cornea
(make 2) (using white)
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3: 1 sc, (inc) x 3, 3 sc, (inc) x 3, 2 sc (18)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing.
irish
(make 2) (using mint green)
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (1 sc, inc) x 3 (9)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing.
pupil
(make 2) (i use black mercerized yarn and 1.25 mm crochet hook you can use black felt for the pupils.
rnd 1. 6 sc in magic ring (6)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing.
sew cornea on the center of the sclera.
sew irish on the center of the cornea
sew pupil on the right side of the cornea
whiskers
(make 4) (using yellow)
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing.
tummy
(using white)
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) x 6 (36)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing.
hands
(consists of thumb and 3 fingers) thumb
(using white)
rnd 1: 5 sc in magic ring (5)
rnd 2-3: 5 sc (2 rounds) (5)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing. stuff the thumb.
fingers
(make 3) (using white)
rnd 1: 5 sc in magic ring (5)
rnd 2-4: 5 sc (3 rounds) (5)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing. stuff the thumb.
do not fasten off the 3 rd finger. now you will combine the 3 fingers and thumb.
stuff as you crochet
rnd 1: 1 sc in the next 2 stitches of 3 rd finger, take the 1 st finger and 1 sc in the next 3 stitches, take the 2 nd finger and 1 sc in the next 5 stitches, 1 sc in the next 2 stitches of 1 st finger, 1 sc in the last 3 stitches of 3 rd finger (15)
rnd 2: 15 sc (15)
rnd 3: 8 sc, join the thumb 5 sc, 1 sc in the next 7 stitches (20)
rnd 4: 20 sc (20)
rnd 5: (2 sc, dec) x 5 (15)
rnd 6: 15 sc (15)
rnd 7: (1 sc, dec) x 5 (10)
change color to blue
rnd 8-21: 10 sc (14 rounds) (10)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing.
ears
(using black)
rnd 1: 4 sc in magic ring (4)
rnd 2: (inc) x 4 (8)
rnd 3: 8 sc (8)
rnd 4: (3 sc, inc) x 2 (10)
rnd 5: 10 sc (10)
rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) x 2 (12)
rnd 7-8: 12 sc (2 rounds) (12)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing. stuff the ears.
tail
(using blue)
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6-8: 30 sc (3 rounds) (30)
rnd 9: (3 sc, dec) x 6 (24)
rnd 10-11: 24 sc (2 rounds) (24)
rnd 12: (2 sc, dec) x 6 (18)
rnd 13-15: 18 sc (3 rounds) (18)
rnd 16: (1 sc, dec) x 6 (12)
rnd 17-23: 12 sc (7 rounds) (12)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing. stuff the tail.
legs
(make 2) (using blue)
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) x 6 (12)
rnd 3-16: 12 sc (14 rounds) (12)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing. stuff the legs..
feet (start crochet the toes)
toes
(make 3) (using white)
rnd 1: 5 sc in magic ring (5)
rnd 2-4: 5 sc (3 rounds) (5)
fasten off, leaving long tail for sewing. stuff the toes. do not fasten off the 3 rd toe. now you will combine the 3 toes. stuff as you crochet.
rnd 1: 1 sc in the next 2 stitches of 3 rd toe, take the 1 st
toe and 1 sc in the next 3 stitches, take the 2nd toe and 1 sc in the next 5 stitches, 1 sc in the next 2 stitches of 1 st toe, 1 sc in the last 3 stitches of 3rd toe (15)
rnd 2: 15 sc (15)
rnd 3: (2 sc, inc) x 5 (20)
rnd 4-7: 20 sc (4 rounds) (20)
rnd 8: (2 sc, dec) x 5 (15)
rnd 9-11: 15 sc (3 rounds) (15)
rnd 12: (1 sc, dec) x 5 (10)
rnd 13: (dec) x 5 (5)
fasten off, leaving a long tail for closing the hole. thread the yarn end through a yarn needle. to close the head, insert the needle below front loop of the 5 stitches from the center to outside of the hole then pull through. fasten; weave in and cut the yarn.
sew the feet to the bottom of the legs.
assembly
1. sew ears on both sides of the head on round 4 to 6
2. sew eyes on round 7 to 17 of the head
3. sew nose on round 15 to 17 of the head
4. with black embroidery thread or mercerized yarn embroider the smile on round 22 to 26, about 22 stitches long.
5. with white yarn, embroider 2 teeth below the smile, 12 stitches apart.
6. sew the whiskers on both sides of the face: 1 st whisker on round 22 and 23 2 nd whisker 2 rounds below the 1 st whiskers.
7. sew the arms 1 round below the neck
8. sew the tummy on round 13 to 25
9. sew the tail on the back center of the body on round 14 to 17.
10. sew the legs to the bottom sides of the body on round 5 to 7.
