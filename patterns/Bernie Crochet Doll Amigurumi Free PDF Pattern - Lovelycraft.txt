()*n repeat in braces n times

let's start making the cute chibi bernie sanders free pattern
head & body cream
rnd 1. mr 8sc (8)
rnd 2. 8 inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (3 sc inc)*6 (30)
rnd 5. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 6. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 7-12. 42 sc (42)
rnd 13. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 14. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 15. (3sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 16. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 17. (green) blo (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 18. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 19. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 20. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 21-22. 42 sc (42)
rnd 23. (black) 42 sc (42)
rnd 24. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 25. 36 sc (36)
rnd 26. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 27. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 28. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 29. (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 30. 6 dec (6)
wave in the rest of yarn.
chibi bernie sanders amigurumi toy has small arms and legs. you have to be a little patient while crocheting these small pieces.
arm x2 white
rnd 1. mr 5 sc (5)
rnd 2. 5 inc (10)
rnd 3. (light brown) 10 sc (10)
rnd 4. (brown) 10 sc (10)
rnd 5. (light brown) 10 sc (10)
rnd 6-8. (green) 10 sc (10)
fasten off & cut the yarn
legs x2 brown
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3-4. (black) 12 sc (12)
fasten off & cut the yarn
ear x2 cream
rnd 1. mr 3 sc (3)
it looks like a semicircle.
sew fast it between r11-12. insert eyes (5mm) between r10-11, 5 stitches apart
