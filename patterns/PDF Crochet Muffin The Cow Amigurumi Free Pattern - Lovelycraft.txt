designer: meow.amigurumi
materials
thread; white, pink, light blue, red
fiber for filling
plastic eye accessory
scissors
abbreviations
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
mr magic ring
blo back loop only
st(s) stitch(es)
ch chain
head
use white yarn
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8 inc (16)
rnd 3. white: ( 1sc, 1inc) * 5, pink: ( 1sc, 1inc) * 2, white ( 1sc, 1inc) (24)
rnd 4. white: ( 1sc, 1inc, 1sc ) * 5, 1sc, pink: 1inc, 2sc, 1inc, white: 2sc, 1inc, 1sc (32)
rnd 5. white: ( 3sc, 1inc) * 5, pink: 3sc, 1inc, 3sc, 1inc, white: 3sc, 1inc ( 40)
rnd 6. white: 25 sc, pink: 10 sc, white: 5 sc ( 40)
rnd 7. white: 26sc, pink: 8sc, white: 6sc ( 40)
rnd 8. ( 1sc, 1inc, 3sc )*5, 1sc, 1inc, pink: 4sc, 1inc, white: 4sc, 1inc, 3sc( 48)
rnd 9-12. white: 34sc, pink: 4sc, white: 10sc (48)
rnd 13. white: 12 sc, ( 1sc, 1inc) * 12, 12sc ( 60)
rnd 14. white: 14 sc, pink: 2sc, white: 44 sc (60)
rnd 15. white: 13 sc,pink: 4 sc, white: 43sc (60)
rnd 16. white: 12 sc ,pink: 6 sc, white: 42 sc (60)
rnd 17. white: 12 sc , pink: 6 sc, white: 42 sc (60)
rnd 18. white: 13 sc ,pink: 1 dec, 1sc, 1dec , white: ( 1sc, 1dec) * 10, 12sc ( 48)
from here, you'll use white yarn
rnd 19. 48 sc (48)
rnd 20. (2sc, 1dec, 2sc) * 8 (40)
rnd 21. (3sc, 1dec) *8 (32)
rnd 22. (1sc, 1dec, 1sc) * 8 (24)
cut the thread leaving enough length for sewing. stuff with the fiberfill. make eyes socket,
place the safety eyes between rounds 12 and13 at about 10 sts apart.
horn
use pink yarn
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. (1sc, 1inc) * 2 (6)
rnd 3. (1sc, 1inc) * 3 (9)
cut and secure the yarn. sew the horn to the head.
ear
use light pink yarn
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. (1sc, 1inc) * 2 (6)
rnd 3. 6inc (12)
rnd 4. (3sc, 1inc) * 3 (15)
rnd 5-6. 15sc (15)
rnd 7. ( 3sc, 1dec) *3 (12)
cut the yarn. sew the ear to the head.
muzzle
use pink yarn
rnd 1. chain 5, start from the second st from the hook, 1inc, 3sc, 4sc in the last st, 3sc, 1inc
rnd 2. 2inc, 3sc, 4inc, 3sc, 2inc (22)
rnd 3. blo, 22 sc (22)
cut the yarn. sew the muzzle to the head.
bow
use blue yarn
chain 11
rnd 1. starting in second ch from hook, 10 sc, turn back (10 sts).
rnd 2-4. ch1, 10 sc, turn back (10 sts).
fasten off and leave long end for sewing. wrap the middle of the bow with a yarn a couple of times.
sew the bow to the head.
leg
use pink yarn
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. blo, 12 sc (12)
change to white yarn
rnd 4-6. 12 sc (12)
cut and secure the yarn.
make the other leg but don't cut the thread and continue working the body.
body
rnd 1. 12 sc of the second leg, chain 6 and join with the first leg, 12sc of the first leg,
6 sc in 6ch, place a stitch marker in the last sts (36)
rnd 2. 36 sc (36)
rnd 3. (5sc, 1inc) * 6 (42)
rnd 4. 42 sc (42)
rnd 5. (6sc, 1inc) * 6 (48)
rnd 6. white: 8sc, pink: 3sc, white: 37 sc (48)
rnd 7. white: 7sc, pink: 5sc, white: 36 sc (48)
rnd 8. white: 6sc, pink: 7sc, white: 35 sc (48)
rnd 9-10. white: 5sc, pink: 9sc, white: 34 sc (48)
rnd 11. white: 6sc, pink : 1dec, 5sc, white: 1sc, 1dec, (6sc, 1dec) * 4 (42)
rnd 12. white: 7sc, pink: 4sc; white : 31 sc (42)
rnd 13. white: 5sc, 1dec, 1sc, pink: 2sc, white: 2sc, 1dec, (5sc, 1dec) * 4 (36)
rnd 14-15. 36 sc (36)
rnd 16. (2sc, 1dec, 2sc) * 6 (30)
rnd 17. (3sc, 1dec) * 6 (24)
cut and secure the thread. stuff the body. sew the body to the head.
arm
use pink yarn
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc, 1inc) * 3 (9)
rnd 3. 9 sc (9)
change to white yarn
rnd 4-12. 9sc (9)
cut and secure the yarn. sew the arm to the body.
necklace
use blue yarn, chain 40.
cut and secure the yarn. sew the bell to the midle of the necklace. sew the necklace to the cow.
bag
use red yarn
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc, 1inc) * 6 (18)
rnd 4-5. 18sc (18)
rnd 6. (1sc, 1dec) * 6 (12)
rnd 7. (3sc in same st)* 12
cut and secure the yarn.
use yellow yarn to decor the bag.
use red yarn, chain 50, cut and secure the yarn. sew the chain to 2 side of the bag.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi cow pattern.
