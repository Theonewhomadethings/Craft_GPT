pdf little pig animal toy amigurumi free crochet pattern
designer: beary_bearnita_
materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2mm- 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
nose
make 4 chains, from ch2 make 2sc, sc, 4sc, skip 1ch, 2sc in last chain.
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
leg*2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-5. (2rnds) 18sc (18)
cut the yarn. make the 2nd leg in the same way. don't cut the yarn. make 6 chains.
join in 1st leg and continue to the body.
body
rnd 1. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 2. 10sc, inc, 27sc, inc, 16sc (56)
rnd 3-12. (10rnds) 56sc (56)
rnd 13. (12sc,dec)*4 (52)
rnd 14-19. (6rnds) 52sc (52)
rnd 20. (11sc,dec)*4 (48)
rnd 21. 48sc (48)
rnd 22. 11sc, (inc,3sc)*6, inc, 11sc, inc (56)
rnd 23. 56sc (56)
rnd 24. 12sc,(inc,4sc)*6, inc, 13sc (63)
rnd 25-29. (5rnds) 63sc (63)
rnd 30. 12sc, (dec,4sc)*6,dec, 13sc (56)
rnd 31. 21sc, (dec,3sc)*2,dec, 23sc (53)
rnd 32-37. (6rnds) 53sc (53)
insert the safety eyes at round 32,33. 6sc apart.
rnd 38. 51sc,dec (52)
rnd 39. (11sc,dec)*4 (48)
rnd 40. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 41. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 42. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 43. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 44. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 45. (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 45. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
arm*2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-13. (11rnds) 12sc (12)
cut the yarn, make the 2nd arm in the same way. leave long tail and sew to the body.
ear*2
rnd 1. 4sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4-inc (8)
rnd 3. (3sc,inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4. (4sc,inc)*2 (12)
rnd 5. (5sc,inc)*2 (14)
rnd 6. (6sc,inc)*3 (16)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
tail
make 17sc, from ch2 crochet 16sc. (16)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
bow
make 3 chains 3-hdc to the first chain, 2ch, slst. 2ch, 3-hdc to the first chain
(same chain of 3-hdc above), 2ch, slst. sew to the body.
little pig is ready..
