crochet plush bunny amigurumi free pattern
designer: danart_est
materials
1 skein of plush yarn (yarnart dolce or himalaya dolphin baby or bernat)
4mm crochet hook and 6mm crochet hook for ears!!!!!
10mm safety eyes and 10mm*11mm safety nose.
soft stuffing
thing long needle and needle with a large eyelet, scissors.
abbreviations
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
sl-st : slip stitch
ch : chain
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo :crochet into back loops only
bl : bobble stitch do 5dc all in the same stitch and then yarn over and pull through all the loops on your hook (the original loop + 1 loop for each partially completed dc).
legs
1r: 2ch. 6sc in the 2nd ch from the hook
2r: 6inc (12)
3-4r: 12sc (2 rounds)
5r: 4dec, 4sc (8)
6-10r: 8sc (5 rounds)
stuff %50. leave a little tail.
arms
1r: 2ch. 6sc in the 2nd ch from the hook
2r: (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
3-4r: 9sc (2 rounds)
5r: 3dec, 3sc (6)
6-8r: 6sc (3 rounds)
stuff %50. leave a little tail.
body
1r: 2ch. 6sc in the 2nd ch from the hook
2r: 6inc (12)
3r: (1sc, inc)*6 (18)
4r: (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
5r: 4sc with first leg, 8sc, 4sc with second leg, 8sc (24)
6-7r: 24sc (2 rounds)
8r: (2sc, dec)*6 (18)
9r: 18sc
10r: (4sc, dec)*3 (15)
11r: 2sc, 3sc with arm, 4sc, 3sc with second arm, 3sc (15)
12r: 15sc
stuff the body.
head
1r: 2ch. 6sc in the 2nd ch from the hook
2r: 6inc (12)
3r: (1sc, inc)*6 (18)
4r: (2sc, inc)*6 (24)
5r: (3sc, inc)*6 (30)
6-8r: 30sc (3 rounds)
9r: (2sc, dec)*6 (24)
10r: (2sc, dec)*6 (18)
11r: (4sc, dec)*3 (15)
12r: 15sc
stuff the head.
ears
1r: 2ch. 4sc in the 2nd ch from the hook
2r: 4inc (8)
3r: (2sc, inc)*2 (10)
4r: (4sc, inc)*2 (12)
5r: (5sc, inc)*2 (14)
6r: (6sc, inc)*2 (16)
7r: (7sc, inc)*2 (18)
8r: 18sc
9r: (dec, 7sc)*2 (16)
10r: (dec, 6sc)*2 (14)
11r: (dec, 5sc)*2 (12)
12r: (dec, 4sc)*2 (10)
13r: (dec, 3sc)*2 (8)
14r: (dec, 2sc)*2 (6)
15-16r: 6sc (2 rounds) +1sc in the last row.
set safety eyes between 6 and 7 rounds.
set safety nose between 7 and 8 rounds.
your amigurumi plush bunny toy is ready!,
