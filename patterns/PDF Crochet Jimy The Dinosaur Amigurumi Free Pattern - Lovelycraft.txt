let's start crocheting the amigurumi dinosaur pattern
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in a magic ring
rnd 2. 6 inc= 12sc
rnd 3. (1 sc- 1 inc)*6= 18sc
rnd 4. (2 sc- 1 inc)*6= 24sc
rnd 5. (3 sc- 1 inc)*6= 30sc
rnd 6. (4 sc- 1 inc)*6= 36sc
rnd 7. (5 sc-1 inc)*6= 42sc
rnd 8-13. (42 sc) 6 rounds
rnd 14. (6sc- 1 inc)*6= 48sc
rnd 15-20. (48 sc) 6 rounds
rnd 21. (6sc-1 dec)*6= 42sc
rnd 22. (5sc- 1 dec)*6= 36sc
rnd 23. (4sc- 1 dec)*6= 30sc
rnd 24. (3sc- 1 dec)*6= 24sc
attach the eyes between round 13 and 14 with 7 visible stitches in between.
we have completed the head part of the amigurumi dinosaur. now let's crochet the nose part
snout
make ch 7
rnd 1. 5 sc ,1 inc in the last ch, on the opposite side of the chain, make 5 sc= 12sc
rnd 2. 2 inc, 3 sc, 3 inc, 3 sc, 1 inc= 18sc
rnd 3. 2 inc, 6 sc, 3 inc, 6 sc, 1 inc= 24sc
rnd 4. 2 inc, 9 sc, 3 inc, 9 sc, 1 inc= 30sc
rnd 5-7. (30 sc) 3 rounds
let's crochet the sequins of the amigurumi pattern. you can crochet using any color yarn you want.
scales
rnd 1. 6 sc in a magic ring
rnd 2. 6 inc= 12 sc
rnd 3-4. (12 sc) 2 rounds
body
start using ocher color.
rnd 1. 6 sc in a magic ring
rnd 2. 6 inc= 12sc

rnd 6. (4 sc- 1 inc)*6= 36sc
rnd 7. (5 sc-1 inc)*6= 42sc
rnd 8. (6 sc- 1 inc)*6= 48sc
rnd 9-12. (48 sc) 4 rounds
change green color.
rnd 13-14. (48 sc) 2 rounds
rnd 15. (6 sc- 1 dec)*6= 42sc

rnd 19. (5 sc-1 dec)*6= 36sc

rnd 23. (4 sc- 1 dec)*6= 30sc

we have completed the body part of the amigurumi dinosaur. now let's crochet legs
legs
rnd 1. 6 sc in a magic ring
rnd 2. 6 inc= 12 sc
rnd 3. (2 sc- 1 inc)*4= 165c
rnd 4. (16 sc) blo
rnd 5-9. (16 sc) rounds
let's crochet the arms and tail of the dinosaur amigurumi pattern
arms
start using ocher color.
rnd 1. 6 sc in a magic ring
rnd 2. 6 inc = 12 sc
rnd 3. (12 sc) blo
rnd 4-8. (12 sc) 5 rounds
change to green
rnd 9-11. (12 sc) 3 rounds
flatten the end of arms and close both sides with 5 sc.
tail
rnd 1. 62 sc in a magic ring
rnd 2. (2 sc- 1 inc)* 2= 8 sc
rnd 3. (8 sc)
rnd 4. (3 sc- 1 inc)*2= 10 sc
rnd 5. (10 sc)
rnd 6. (4 sc- 1 inc)* 2= 12sc
rnd 7. (12 sc)
rnd 8. (5 sc- 1 inc) *2= 14sc
rnd 9. (14 sc)
rnd 10. (6 sc- 1 inc)* 2= 168c
rnd 11. (16 sc)
rnd 12. (7 sc- 1 inc)* 2= 18sc
rnd 13. (18 sc)
rnd 14. (8 sc-1 inc)* 2= 20sc
