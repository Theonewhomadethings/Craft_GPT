let's start from the head part to make our crochet dinosaur amigurumi pattern.
head
crochet with green yarn.
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6: sc,inc,(4sc,inc)*5,3sc (36)
rnd 7-12: (6 rounds) 36sc (36)
rnd 13: (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 14: 3sc,inc,(6sc,inc),3sc (48)
rnd 15: (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 16: 4sc,inc,(8sc,inc)*6,4sc (60)
rnd 17-24: (8 rounds) 60sc (60)
insert safety eyes between rnd 13 and 14, about 10 stitch apart.
rnd 25: (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 26: 3sc,dec,(7sc,dec)*5,4sc (48)
rnd 27: (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 28: 2sc,dec,(5sc,dec)*5,3sc (36)
rnd 29: (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 30: 1sc,dec,(3sc,dec)*5,2sc (24)
stuff with fiber fill
rnd 31: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 32: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 33: (dec)*6 (6)
close the end and cut the yarn.

body

rnd 9: (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10: 4sc,inc,(8sc,inc)*5,4sc (60)
rnd 11-13: (3 rounds) 60sc (60)
rnd 14: (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 15-16: (2 rounds) 54sc (54)
rnd 17: 3sc,dec,(7sc,dec)*5,4sc (48)
rnd 18-19: (2 rounds) 48sc (48)
