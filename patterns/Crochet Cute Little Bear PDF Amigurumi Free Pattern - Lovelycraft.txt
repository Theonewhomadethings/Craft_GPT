materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarndart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2,5 mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5 mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
k: knit (scarf knitting)
p: purl (scarf knitting)
are you ready to make the amigurumi bear pattern?
we start by crocheting the head part of the amigurumi bear toy. white yarn is preferred in the head part.
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3 sc, inc)*5, 2 sc (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. 2 sc, inc, (5 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (42)
rnd 8. (6 sc, inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9-18. (10 rnds) 48 sc (48)
insert the eyes at round 13, 14, 7 sts apart
rnd 19. (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
rnd 20. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 21. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 22. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 23. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 24. blo (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 25. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn, weave in the end.
after completing the head part of the amigurumi toy, let's crochet the legs
leg x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. 3 sc, 3-inc, 3 sc (12)
rnd 4. 12 sc (12)
rnd 5. 3 sc, 3-dec, 3 sc (9)
rnd 6. (2 sc, inc)*3 (12)
rnd 7. 12 sc (12)
rnd 8. (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd 9. 15 sc (15)
rnd 10. (4 sc, inc)*4 (18)
rnd 11. 18 sc (18)
rnd 12. 3 sc
cut the yarn, crochet the 2 nd leg in the same way till rnd 11.
rnd 12. 16 sc
do not cut the yarn, make 6 chains, join in 1 st leg. continue to the body.
after completing the legs of the amigurumi pattern, let's crochet the body part. after the legs are completed, we start the body part without cutting the rope.
body
rnd 1. 9 sc, inc, 8 sc, inc, 4 sc, inc, 8 sc, inc, 9 sc, inc, 4 sc, inc (54)
rnd 2-4. (3 rnds) 54 sc (54)
rnd 5. 9 sc, dec, 24 sc, dec, 17 sc (52)
rnd 6. 52 sc (52)
rnd 7. 8 sc, dec, 24 sc, dec, 16 sc (50)
rnd 8. 50 sc (50)
rnd 9. 7 sc, dec, 24 sc, dec, 15 sc (48)
rnd 10-11. (2 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 12. (6 sc, dec)*3, change to red, (6 sc, dec)*3 (42)
rnd 13-14. 42 sc (42)
rnd 15. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 16-17. (2 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 18. (4 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 19-20. (2 rnds) 30 sc (30)
rnd 21. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 22-24. (3 rnds) 24 sc (24)
rnd 25. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 26. 18 sc (18)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing
we have completed the body part of the amigurumi bear. now we will crochet the sleeves. red thread is preferred for the sleeves. white thread is used in their hands.
arm x 2
start with white yarn
rnd 1. 5 sc in mr (5)
rnd 2. 5-inc (10)
rnd 3-6. (4 rnds) 10 sc (10)
change to red
rnd 7-19. (13 rnds) 10 sc (10)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
after completing the arms of the amigurumi bear pattern, we will crochet the last piece, the ears.
ear x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3-5. (3 rnds) 12 sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing
let's crochet the scarf, the cute accessory of the amigurumi bear.
scarf (knitting)
cast on 40 sts on the needle
rnd 1. 1 k, 1 p, 1 k, 1 p .. until end round
rnd 2-21. (20 rnds) repeat round 1
sew the button on the scarf
congratulations, you have completed the crochet cute little bear.
