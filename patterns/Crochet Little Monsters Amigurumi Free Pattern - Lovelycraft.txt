today i will give you a wonderful cartoon anime character little cute crochet monsters amigurumi pattern. also completely free. these cute little monsters will be a play or sleep companion for your kids. for beginners, they will crochet very easily thanks to the detailed step by step pattern description.

crochet little monsters amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: mes.mignonneries
abbreviations
rnd round
mr magic ring
ch chain
sl st slip stitch
sc single crochet
hdc hald double crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
()* repeat till the end of round
[] total number of stitches in the round
size with the materials above will be about 10 cm.
materials
cotton ricorumi:
-version 1: lila (017), white (001), orange (024), yellow (063)
-version 2: aqua (074), pink (067), white (001), red (028)
crochet hook: 3mm
safety eyes (you may need to use smaller/ larger eyes, depending on hook/ yarn size)
-version 1: 10mm (x1)
-version 2: 4mm (x2)
yarn needle
black and white embroidery thread
piece of thin cardboard
fiberfill for stuffing
scissors
eye
in white.
rnd 1: mr 8 sc (4)
rnd 2: (1sc,inc)* (12)
insert security eye in the mr.
head & body
stuff as you go along your work.
in purple.
rnd 1: mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2: (inc)* (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,inc)* (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 7-9: 36sc (36)
rnd 10: (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 11-14: 42sc (42)
insert the security eyes between rnd 7 and 8. sew to the head.
rnd 15: (5sc,dec)* (36)
rnd 16: (4sc,dec)* (30)
rnd 17: (3sc,dec)* (24)
rnd 18: (2sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 19: (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 20: 24 sc (24)
rnd 21: (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 22-24: 30 sc (30)
rnd 25: (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 26-29: 26sc (36)
rnd 30: (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 31: 42 sc (42)
rnd 32: through back loops only (5sc,dec)* (36)
finish to stuff.
for best stability: draw a circle about 4cm in diametet on a piece of thin cardboard cut it out and slide it into the opening of rnd 32.
rnd 33: (2sc,dec)* (27)
rnd 34: (1sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 35: (dec)* (9)
close and fasten off.
horns (x2)
stuff as you go along your work.
in orange.
rnd 1: mr 4sc (4)
rnd 2: 3sc,inc (5)
in yellow.
rnd 3: 4sc,inc (6)
rnd 4: 5sc,inc (7)
in orange.
rnd 5: inc,5sc,inc (9)
rnd 6: inc,7sc,inc (11)
in yellow.
rnd 7: inc,3sc,dec,4sc,inc (12)
rnd 8: 5sc,dec,4sc,inc (12)
in orange.
rnd 9: 5sc,dec,4sc,inc (12)
rnd 10: 5sc
sew the horns in side of the head between rounds 3 and 6, leaving approx 7 stitches between them.
arms (x2)
in purple.
rnd 1: mr 4sc (4)
rnd 2: (1sc,sc)* (6)
rnd 3-12: 6sc (6)
sew the arms in side of the body.
teeth (x2)
in white.
rnd 1: ch 2, 1 hdc in the second ch from the hook. (1)
embroider the mouth with blakc embroidery thread and sew the theeth.
head & body
stuff as you go along your work.
in blue.
rnd 1: mr 6sc (6)
rnd 2: (inc)* (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,inc)* (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 7-9: 36 sc (36)
rnd 10: (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 11-14: 42 sc (42)
rnd 15: (5sc,dec)* (36)
rnd 16: (4sc,dec)* (30)
rnd 17: (3sc,dec)* (24)
rnd 18: (2sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 19: (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 20: 24 sc (24)
rnd 21-24: 30 sc (30)
rnd 25: (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 26-29: 26 sc (36)
rnd 30: (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 31: 42 sc (42)
rnd 32: through back loops only (5sc,dec)* (36)
finish to stuff.
for best stability: draw a circle about 4cm in diameter on a piece of thin cardboard. cut it out and slide it into the opening of rnd 32.
rnd 33: (2sc,dec)* (27)
rnd 34: (1sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 35: (dec)* (9)
close and fasten off.
eyes (x2)
in white.
rnd 1: mr 4sc (4)
rnd 2: (1sc,sc)* (6)
insert security eye in the mr.
in blue.
rnd 1: mr 6sc (6)
rnd 2: (inc)* (12)
rnd 3-6: 12 sc (12)
rnd 7: (dec)* (6)
rnd 8-13: 6 sc (6)
insert the security eyes between rnd 3 and 4. sew.
dots (x6)
in pink.
rnd 1: mr 4sc (4)
rnd 2: (1sc,sc)* (6)
sew the dots.
arms (x2)
in blue.
rnd 1: mr 4sc (4)
rnd 2: (1sc,sc)* (6)
rnd 3-7: 6sc (6)
sew the arms in side of the body.
tongue
in red.
rnd 1: ch2, 1sc in the second ch from the hook (1)
embroider the mouth with black embroidery thread and sew the tongue.
cute little monsters are now ready..
