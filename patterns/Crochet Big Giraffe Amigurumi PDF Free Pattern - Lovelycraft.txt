abbreviations:
mr= magic ring- crochet single crochet stitches in to the adjustable loop
ch= chain stitch
sc= single crochet stitch
sl= slip stitch
dc= double crochet stitch
inc= increase- make two single crochet stitches in the same stitch
dec=decrease- crochet two stitches together using the decrease method
(4 sc, inc) x 6= repeat the pattern between parentheses 6 times
blo= back loops only
inc3= 3 sc in the same stitch
f/o= fasten off
notes:
do not join rounds unless stated, work in a continuous spiral
use a stitch marker to mark the beginning of each round
all stitches are worked in both loops unless stated otherwise in the pattern

assembly
head and body:
1. pin the horns onto the head approximately between rounds 22 and 23 of the head.
sew the horns onto the head. pin the ears about 4 stitches away from the horns on the side of the head and sew into place. embroider some nostrils with dark gray yarn approximately between rounds 6 and 7 of the head.
2. pin the head to the body for extra support. you may need to add more stuffing as you sew. (make sure the double crochets from round 47 are on the back of the head.)
3. sew the head to the body.
arms:
1. pin the arms onto the side of the body. i do not have particular rounds i place these on. i just make sure they are facing forward and are on the side of the body slightly underneath the beginning of the neck.
2. sew the arms onto the body.
legs:
1. turn the body upside down with the nose facing away from you. pin the legs underneath the body approximately between rounds 5 and 6.
2. sew the legs onto the body.
tail and spots:
1. pin the tail onto the back of the body approximately between rounds 17 and 18.
2. sew the tail onto the body.
3. cut many shapes out of dark gray felt and glue or sew onto the body.
4. cut the ear shapes out of light gray felt and sew or glue them into the ears.
