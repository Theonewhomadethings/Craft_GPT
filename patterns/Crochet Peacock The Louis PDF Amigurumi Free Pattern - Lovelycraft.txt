crochet peacock the louis pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: les.mailles.du.rocher
materials
cotton ricorumi (blue, navy blue, green, beige)
crochet hook size: 3 mm
yarn needle
fiberfill for stuffing
safety eyes (5 or 6 mm)
stitch marker
scissors
abbreviations
rnd: round
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
sl st: slip stitch
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
hdc: half double crochet
()*: repeat till the end of round
[]: total number of stitches in the round
head and body
in blue.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. 6 inc [12]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [18]
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* [24]
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* [30]
rnd 6. (2sc,inc,2sc)* [36]
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)* [42]
rnd 8-15. 42 sc [42]
rnd 16. (5sc,dec)* [36]
rnd 17. (2sc,dec,2sc)* [30]
rnd 18. (3sc,dec)* [24]
rnd 19. (2sc,dec)* [18]
rnd 20-27. 18 sc [18]
rnd 28. (8sc,inc)* [20]
rnd 29. 20 sc [20]
rnd 30. (9sc,inc)* [22]
rnd 31. 22 sc [22]
rnd 32. 9 sc, inc, 2 sc, inc, 9 sc [24]
rnd 33-34. 24 sc [24]
rnd 35. ch 13 (first sc in 2nd ch from hook, mark this sc as the new beginning of the round): inc, 11sc, 1sc where the foundation chain starts, 24 sc on the body, 11sc, 3sc in last stitch [52]
rnd 36. 3 sc in same stitch , 23sc, inc, 3sc, inc, 23sc [56]
rnd 37. 2 inc, 54sc [58]
rnd 38-41. 58 sc [58]
rnd 42. 4 sc, dec, 20 sc, dec, 6 sc, dec, 20 sc, dec [54]
rnd 43-45. 54 sc [54]
rnd 46. (7sc,dec)* [48]
rnd 47. (3sc,dec,3sc)* [42]
rnd 48. (5sc,dec)* [36]
rnd 49. (2sc,dec,2sc)* [30]
rnd 50. (dec)* [15]
rnd 51. (3sc,dec)* [12]
rnd 52. (dec)* [6]
fasten off and close.
embroider the paws with beige yarn.
beak
in beige.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. (2sc,inc)* [8]
rnd 3. (3sc,inc)* [10]
rnd 4. (4sc,inc)* [12]
rnd 5-6. 12 sc [12]
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing to the head.
eye patches
start with navy blue.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
change color to white.
rnd 2. 3 inc, (1sc,1hdc in same stitch), (1dc,ch2,1slst in 2nd ch from hook, 1dc in same stitch), (1hdc, 1sc in same stitch), 1 slst in first sc of rnd 1.
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing.
insert the security eyes and sew the patches to the head.
head feathers
in blue.
ch 2, 1 slst in 2nd ch from hook, (ch 8, 1slst in the foundation chain)*3
fasten off and sew at the top of the head.
add fringes in the chains with navy yarn.
back feathers
in green (make 3)
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. (inc)* [12]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [18]
in blue (make 5)
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. (inc)* [12]
fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing to the back of the body.
tail
in green, make 5 pieces:
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. 6 inc [12]
rnd 3-5. 12 sc [12]
fasten off 4 pieces (leaving a tail for closing the holes between the pieces after joining).
don't cut the last piece, now you will join all of them together.
rnd 6. join to 2nd piece with 1 sc (first of the round), continue crocheting 5 sc around this piece (6sc in total on 2nd piece): join to 3rd piece and crochet 6sc: join to 4th piece and crochet 6sc: on the last (5th) piece, crochet 12sc, then 6sc around 4th piece, 6 sc around 3rd piece, 6sc around 2nd piece, and 12 sc around first piece [60]
rnd 7-10. 60 sc [60]
rnd 11. (4sc,dec)* [50]
rnd 12-14. 50 sc [50]
rnd 15. (3sc,dec)* [40]
rnd 16-17. 40 sc [40]
rnd 18. (2sc,dec)* [30]
rnd 19. 30 sc [30]
rnd 20. (3sc,dec)* [24]
rnd 21. 24 sc [24]
rnd 22. (2sc,dec)* [18]
rnd 23-24. 18 sc [18]
fold and crochet through both sides 9 sc.
sew or glue to the back of the tail.
tail feathers (make 3)
start with navy blue.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
change color to blue.
rnd 2. 3 inc, (1 sc, 1hdc, in same stitch), (1dc, ch2, 1 slst in 2nd ch from hook, 1dc, in same stitch), (1 hdc, 1sc, in same stitch), 1 slst in first sc of rnd 1.
fasten off, sew or glue at the top of each piece of the tail.
