abbreviations
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
dc : double crochet
hdc:half double crochet
dcinc: double crochet increase
sl-st : slip stitch
ch : chain
rnd: round
flo :use the front loops only
blo : use the back loops only
(..) :total number of stitches after the round / row.
() x . times: repeat in structions in parentheses x times

legs ( with skin color ) x 2
begin with the legs, we will make the body by joining two legs.
rnd1: 6sc into the magic ring
rnd 2:(inc)x6 times (12)
rnd 3-10: 12sc
rnd11: (dec) x 2 times, 3sc, (inc)x2 times, 3sc(12)
rnd 12: 12sc
rnd 13: (dec) x 2 times, 3sc, (inc)x2 times, 3sc (12)
rnd 14-19: 12sc
rnd20: 12sc
rnd 21: work blo 12sc
you have finished the first leg. crochet the second leg in the same way.
when you have finished round 21, make 2 single crochets into the stitches of the second leg to reach the middle. it is important to make the tips of the feet look at each other.
do not break yarn ch6 to join the two legs.
body
rnd22:join the two legs by chaining 6(36)
rnd 23-30: 36sc
rnd 31:work blo 36 sc
rnd 32: work blo 36sc
rnd33:work blo 36sc
rnd 34-36: 36sc
rnd 37: (4sc,dec)x6 times (30)
rnd 38-39: 30sc
rnd 40: (3sc, dec)x6 times (24)
rnd41: 24sc
rnd42: (2sc, dec)x6 times (18)
rnd43-45: 18sc
you have finished the body.
do not break the yarn, we will crochet the head.
for the ruffles of the underpants; with white color work into the blo stitches of round 21 and (ch1,1sc) x 12 times. repeat this for two legs.
stuff the body when the pieces get narrowed.

rnd 1: 6sc into the magic ring
rnd 2: inc x 6 (12)
rnd 3: (1sc, inc) x 6 times (18)
rnd 4: (2sc, inc)x6 times (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)x 6 times (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)x 6 times (36)
rnd 7: 36sc

rnd 9: (6sc,inc) x 6 times (48)
rnd 10-15: 48sc
to make the border of the hair:ch2,turn and make single crochet into the 3rd stitch from your hook. crochet all the rounds in the same way.
rnd 16. ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (46sc)
rnd 17.ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitichfrom your hook (44sc)
rnd 18. ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (42sc)
rnd 19. ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (40sc)
rnd 20. ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (38sc)
rnd 21. ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (36sc)
rnd 22. ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (34sc)
rnd 23. ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (32sc)
rnd 24. ch 2,turn sc all around starting from the 3rd stitich from your hook (30sc)
break the yarn leaving a long tail. sew the hair on the head by making the division section come in the middle of face. follow the pictures!
tresses(black)
make 13 tresses
rnd 1:ch17
rnd 2:insert your hook into the 3rd stitch
from your hook andmake (15 hdc)x2 times (30hdc)
sew the 13 tresses on the hair. sew 1 tress on the right side and 1 tress on the left side of the face,put the other tresses side by side and under the sepieces with out leaving stitches between them.follow the pictorial when sewing!
hair band (red)
leave a long tail before making the first chain of the hairband!
rnd 1:ch22
rnd 2: 1sc, 19hdc, 3sc into the stitch on the corner, do not turn keep crocheting on the other side of the chain.
rnd 3:19hdc, 1sc.
break the yarn leaving a long tail.
bow(red)
rnd 1: ch17
rnd 2: 1sc, 1hdc, 3dc, 1hdc, 1sc, 2 sl-st, 1sc, 1hdc, 3dc, 1hdc, 3sc on the last stitch,do not turn ,crochet on the other side of the chain
rnd 3: 1sc, 1hdc, 3dc, 1hdc, 1sc, 2 sl-st, 1sc, 1hdc, 3dc, 1 hdc, 1sc.fasten off.
sew the bowin to the middle of the hair band.
put the hair band with long tails as you see in the pictorials and sew.
inner part of the skirt ( white)
we will crochet the inner skirt into the blo stitches of round 31 on the body. crochet as dc.
rnd1: 2dc, 1dcincaround(48)
rnd2-4. 48dc around
rnd5. 3dc, 1dcinc around (60)
rnd6-10. 60dc around
to make borders of the inner skirt; yarn over and insert your hook into the second stitch from your hook, make 1 dc. make 5 more dc into the same stitch. repeat the same process until the end of the round. there will be 15 pieces in total.
edge of the blouse (blue)
we will crochet the edge of the blouse into the blo stitches of round 33 on the body.
rnd1.36sc
rnd2. 36sc
rnd3. 2sc,2hdc,1dcinc,ch1 ,1dcinc, 2hdc, 12sc, 2hdc, 1dcinc, ch1, 1dcinc, 2hdc, 10sc.. fasten off.
begin crocheting the edge of the blouse leaving a long tail. hide the yarn tails by sewing the mint other stitches of body.
begin crocheting the edge of blouse from the first stitch of the blo stitches of round 33 on the body.
place the pointy ends of the blouse right in to the middle front and back of the body.
there will be some white piece in the blo section close it with your yarn and hide the yarn tails.
upper part of the skirt ( yellow)
we will crochet the upper part of the skirt into the blo stitches of round 32 on the body.
crochet as dc
rnd1: 1dc, 1dcinc around (54)
rnd2-4: 54dc around
rnd5: 2dc, 1dcinc around (72)
rnd6-12: 72dc
after completing the inner and upper part of the skirt, sew the edges of the blouse in to the stitches of upper part of the skirt using blue yarn this will prevent curving of the blouse.
crochet the cloak in to the stitches of ch16 as dc.
ch2 at the beginning of each round and turn.
rnd 1:insert your hookin to the 3rd stitch from your hook and make 14dc
rnd 2: 1dc, 1dcinc around (21)
rnd 3-4: 21 dc
rnd 5: 2dc, 1dcinc around (28)
rnd 6. 28dc..fasten off.
collar of the cloak (white)
start with making 1hdc into one of the stitches which is placed in then arrow part of the cloak. ch2 and turn at the beginnig of the rounds.
rnd 1: make 14hdc in to the stitches of the cloak.
rnd 2: 1 hdc, 1hdcinc a round (21)
rnd 3: 2 hdc, 1hdcinc (28)..fasten off.
laces; ch20 with white yarn and slst in to the stitch between collar and cloak.
make this for boths ides.tie a knot and break the yarn and hide tails.
apple (red)
rnd1:6sc into the magic ring
rnd2: inc around (12)
rnd3: (1sc, inc)x6 times (18)
rnd 4-6. 18sc
rnd 7. (1sc, dec)x6 times (12)
rnd 8. dec around(6)
leaf (green)
rnd1: ch6
rnd2: 1 sl-st, 5sc.fasten off.
to make a hole on the top side of the apple, pull your yarn tight and sew it under side it.
sew leaf and stem in to the hole on the top side. for stem; tie a knot and insert your needle from under side and take it ou rfrom top side of the apple. break yarn leaving a short tail.
