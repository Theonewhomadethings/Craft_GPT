materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2mm-2.5mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
let's start crocheting the amigurumi doll pattern.
we will crochet the head part of the amigurumi baby pattern first.
head
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) * 6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc) * 6 (24)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3 sc, inc) * 5, 2 sc (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc) *6 (36)
rnd 7. 2 sc, inc, (5 sc, inc) * 5, 3 sc (42)
rnd 8-12. (5 rnds) 42 sc (42)
rnd 13. (6 sc, inc) * 6 (48)
rnd 14-17. (4 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 18. (4 sc, dec) * 8 (40)
rnd 19. (3 sc, dec) * 8 (32)
rnd 20. (2 sc, dec) * 8 (24)
rnd 21. (sc, dec) * 8 (16)
rnd 22. (2 sc, dec) * 3, 4 sc (13)
rnd 23-28. (6 rnds) blo 13 sc (13)
rnd 29. 6-dec, sc (7)
insert the eyes at round 13, 14, 8sc apart
cut the yarn. fasten off. now put the neck you have formed inside the doll's head.
after completing the head part of the amigurumi toy, we will crochet the legs.
leg * 2
start with yellow
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (2 sc, inc) * 2 (8)
rnd 3. 8 sc (8)
change color
rnd 4-10. (7 rnds) 8 sc (8)
cut the yarn, crochet the 2nd leg in the same way till rnd 10. do not cut thye yarn, make 2 chains, join in 1st leg. continue to the body.
after crocheting the legs of the amigurumi pattern, we will crochet the body part. we will crochet the dress part separately.
body
rnd 1-7. (7 rnds) 20 sc (20)
rnd 8. (2 sc, dec) * 5 (15)
rnd 9. 15 sc (15)
rnd 10. (sc, dec) *5 (10)
rnd 11. (3 sc, dec) * 2 (8)
rnd 12-16. (5 rnds) 8sc (8)
rnd 13. 4-dec (4)
cut the yarn and weav in the end
let's crochet amigurumi doll's arms and ears
arm * 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-8. (7 rnds) 6 sc (6)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
ear * 2
3 sc in mr (3)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing
let's crochet the amigurumi doll's hair. black yarn is preferred for her hair. if you want, you can use blue, yellow, pink yarn.
hair
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc) *6 (18)
rnd 4. (chain 7, 6 sc on chain) * 6, chain 20, 19 sc on chain, (chain 20, 19-hdc on chain) * 16, chain 20, 19 sc on chain
cut the yarn and weave in the end. wear the hair on the head. use hot glue to stick them together.
let's crochet the yellow dress of the amigurumi doll. you can change the color of her dress as you wish.
dress
chain 12, insert your hook to the first chain o make a circle. start to crochet the dress from the collar.
rnd 1. 12 sc (12)
rnd 2. (sc, inc) * 6 (18)
rnd 3. 3 sc, inc, sc, inc, 6 sc, inc, sc, inc, 3 sc (22)
rnd 4. 3 sc, 2-hdc, hdc-inc, 2-hdc, 6sc, 2-hdc, inc-hdc, 2-hdc, 3 sc (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, chain 6 (skip 6 sts), 6 sc, chain 6 (skip 6 sts), 3 sc (24)
rnd 6-7. 24 sc (24)
rnd 8. 24-dc-inc (48), slst
rnd 9. 48-dc (48), slst
rnd 10. (chain 3, slst in next stitch) * 47
cut the yarn, fasten off.
let's crochet the red hairpin on the top of the amigurumi doll's head.
bow tie
chain 3, 2-hdc into the first chain, chain 2, slst into first chain, chain 2, 2-dc into first chain, chain 2, slst into first chain. wrap the yarn over the middle of the bow tie to make the shape.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi little baby girl with red hairpin pattern.
