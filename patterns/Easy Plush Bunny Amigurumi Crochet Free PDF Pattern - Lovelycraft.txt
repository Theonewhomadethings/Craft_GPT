hello my dears. today we are adding a new one to the bunny amigurumi patterns. today's crochet bunny is a pretty easy pattern. if you want more easy bunny patterns, you can check the bunny amigurumi category. the ears of the crocheted bunny look quite large. however it looks cute. when making crochet bunny amigurumi, we make the parts separately. then we put the pieces together. in this way, you can have this cute amigurumi bunny. now let's crochet bunny amigurumi step by step.
pdf easy plush bunny amigurumi crochet free pattern
designer: __oksana__kameneva__
materials
plush yarn
hook (2.5-body, paws; 3.5-ears)
eyes
spout, or embroider
filler
bunny size 12-15 cm, depending on the yarn
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
tr: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
body and head together.
body
1. 7 sc in mr
2. 7 inc (14)
3. 2sc, (inc, 1 sc)*5, 2 sc (19)
4. 4 sc, (1 sc, inc)*5, 5 sc (24)
5. 24 sc
6. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
7. 18 sc
8. 3 sc, 6 dec, 3 sc (12)
9. 6 dec (6)
head
10. 6 inc (12)
11. (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
12. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
13. 24 sc
14. 24 sc
15. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
16. (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
17. 12 sc
18. 6 dec pull off the yarn.
insert eyes between 14 and 15 row.
arms (2 parts)
1. 6 sc in mr
2. (1 sc, inc)*3 (9)
3. 9 sc
4. 3 sc, 3 dec (6)
5-8. 6 sc (4 rows)
fold the arms in half and crochet 3 sc.
legs (2 parts)
1. 6 sc
2. 6 inc (12)
3. 12 sc
4. 3 sc, 3 dec, 3 sc (9)
5. 4 sc, 1 dec, 3 sc (8)
6-8. 8 sc (3 rows)
fold the legs in half and crochet 4 sc.
tail
1. 6 sc
2. 6 inc (12)
pull off the yarn.
ears (2 parts)
1. 6 hdc
2. 6 inc (12)
3. (1 hdc, inc)*6 (18)
4. 18 hdc
5. (4 hdc, dec)* (15)
6. 15 hdc
7. (3 hdc, dec)*3 (12)
8-9. 12 hdc
10. (dec, 4 hdc)*2 (10)
11. 10 hdc
12. (dec, 3 hdc)*2 (8)
13. 8 hdc
14. (dec, 2 hdc)*2 (6)
15. 6 hdc
fold in half and crochet 4 sc sew all the details.
bunny amigurumi is ready !
