crochet christmas tree amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: mumigurumi
materials
molly yarn (chunky, 100% acrylic, 50m, 2 balls) or any other chunky yarn
4mm hook
polyester fiberfill for stuffing
tapestry needle
stitch markers
abbreviations
ch: chain
sc: single crochet
dc: double crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
sl st: slip stitch
blo: back loop only
flo: front loop only
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2-3: sc around (6)
rnd 4: 6-inc (12)
rnd 5: sc around (12)
rnd 6: (2ch,1sc)*12 in flo (12)
rnd 7: (1sc,1inc)*6 in back loops only of the same round (18)
last increase will be worked into the stitch with the marker.
rnd 8: sc around (18)
rnd 9: (2ch,1sc)*18 in flo (18)
rnd 10: (2sc,1inc)*6 in blo of the same round (24)
rnd 11: sc around (24)
rnd 12: (2ch,1sc)*24 in flo (24)
rnd 13: (3sc,1inc)*6 in blo of the same round (30)
rnd 14: sc around (30)
rnd 15: (2ch,1sc)*30 in flo (30)
rnd 16: (4sc,1inc)*6 in blo of the same round (36)
rnd 17: sc around (36)
rnd 18: (2ch,1sc)*36 in flo (36)
rnd 19: (5sc,1inc)*6 in blo of the same round (42)
rnd 20: sc around (42)
fasten off and stuff your tree.
bottom
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: 6-inc (12)
rnd 3: (1sc,1inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (1sc,1inc)*9 (27)
rnd 5: (2sc,1inc)*9 (36)
rnd 6: (5sc,1inc)*6 (42)
fasten off leaving a long tail for sewing.
final steps
sew the bottom to the tree.
decorate your tree with colorful beads.
congratulations, your tree is ready for christmas.
