hello friends. today i will share a wonderful teddy bear pattern for the weekend. if you want to have a pleasant time at home this weekend, join us and let's make the amazing teddy bear amigurumi together. the legs of the amigurumi are made together with the body. other parts are made separately and then combined with the body. this makes it easier to do. in addition, when you combine small parts, it loses its boringness. now, let's crochet teddy bear amigurumi step by step.
pdf easy plush big bear amigurumi free crochet pattern
designer: budnikova_toys
materials:
yarn: himalaya dolphin baby (more than one skein of main color)
hook size: 4.5 mm
black yarnart jeans yarn for embroidering eye brows and nose
12 mm safety eyes
thin thread in the tone of yarn for sewing details together
sewing needle and scissors
fiberfill
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
tr: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
head
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc)x6 (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 7: (5 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 8: (6 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 9-14: 48 sc (6 rnds)
insert safety eyes between r 9 and r 10.
rnd 15: (6 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 16: (5 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 17: (4 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 18: (3 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 19: (2 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (18)
stuff quite tightly, the head should be the correct round shape..
ears (make 2)
rnd 1: 8 sc in magic ring (8)
rnd 2: (inc) repeat 8 times (16)
rnd 3: 16 sc
rnd 4: (3 sc, inc) repeat 4 times (20)
muzzle
we make a chain of 7ch
rnd 1: we start knitting from the second ch: 5sc, 3sc in 1 loop, we continue knitting on the other side of the chain: 4sc, inc (14sc)
rnd 2: 1 ch, inc, 4 sc, (inc)repeat 3 times, 4 sc, (inc)repeat 2 times (20)
rnd 3: 1 ch, inc, 5 sc, (1 sc, inc) repeat 4 times, 5 sc, inc (26)
rnd 4: 1 ch, inc, 6 sc, (2 sc, inc)repeat 4 times, 6 sc, inc (32)
rnd 5: 32 sc
upper paws
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc) repeat 6 times (12)
rnd 3: (5 sc, inc) repeat 2 times (14)
rnd 4: (3 sc, inc) repeat 3 times, 2 sc (17)
rnd 5: 17 sc
rnd 6: dec, (1 sc, dec) repeat 5 times (11)
rnd 7-11: 11 sc (5 rnds)
rnd 12: 9 sc, dec (10)
rnd 13-14: 10 sc (2 rnds)
rnd 15: 8 sc, dec (9)
rnd 16-17: 9 sc (2 rnds)
fold top of arm flat and 5 sc through both sides across.
similarly, we knit the second paw.
lower legs
we make a chain of 7 ch
rnd 1: from the 2nd ch from hook: 5 sc, 4 sc in last st, turn and crochet on the opposite side of the chain: 4 sc, 3 sc in one st (16)
rnd 2: inc, 4 sc, (inc) repeat 4 times, 4 sc, (inc) repeat 3 times (24)
rnd 3: inc, 1 sc, 4 sc, (inc, 1 sc) repeat 4, 4 sc, (inc, 1 sc) repeat 3 times (32)
rnd 4: blo 32 sc
rnd 5: 32 sc
rnd 6: 7 sc, (dec)x6, 8 sc, (dec)x2 times, 1 sc (24)
rnd 7: 6 sc, (dec)x4 times, 10 sc (20)
rnd 8: 3 sc, (dec)x6 times, 5 sc (14)
rnd 9-13: 14 sc (5 rnds)
rnd 14: 12 sc, dec (13)
rnd 15: 13 sc
rnd 16: 11 sc, dec (12)
rnd 17-19: 12 sc (3 rnds)
fold top of leg flat and 6 sc through both sides across..
body
rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
rnd 2: (inc)x6 times (12)
rnd 3: (1 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4: (2 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5: (3 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 6: (4 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 7: (5 sc, inc) repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 8: 42 sc
rnd 9: 8 sc, 6 sc together with sc of the 1st leg, 14 sc, 6 sc together with sc of the 2nd leg, 8 sc.
rnd 10-11: 42 sc (2 rnds)
rnd 12: (5 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 13-14: 36 sc (2 rnds)
rnd 15: (4 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 16: 30 sc
rnd 17: (3 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 18-19: 24 sc (2 rnds)
rnd 20: (2 sc, dec) repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 21: 3 sc, (5 sc together with sc of the 1st arm, 4 sc, 5 sc together with sc of the 2nd arm, 1 sc
rnd 22: 18sc
stuff, fasten off.
your teddy bear amigurumi toy is ready!
