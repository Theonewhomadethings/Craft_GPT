alice in wonderland amigurumi doll free crochet pattern

abbreviations
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
sl-st : slip stitch
st/sts: stitch/stitches
ch : chain
rnd: round
flo :use the front loops only
blo : use the back loops only
(..) :total number of stitches after the round/ row.
() x . times:repeat in structions in parentheses x times
reverse single crochet: reverse single crochet simply means to crochet from left to right side of the round. you can use this technique to make borders on your works. chain 1 on the right side to turn your work. insert your hook into the right of the stitch you have just worked. make single crochets in this way from left to right and do not forget to stretch your yarn when crocheting. reverse single crochet until the end of the round.

legs(black/white) x 2
begin with legs. we will make body by joining two legs. begin with black and change to white color in every two rounds.
with black color
rnd1:6sc into the magic ring
rnd2: (inc)x 6 times (12)
change to white color, do not forget to change color in every 2 rounds.
rnd3-11: 12sc
rnd12: (dec)x2, 3sc, (inc)x2, 3sc (12)
rnd13: 12sc
rnd14: (dec)x2, 3sc, (inc)x2, 3sc (12)
rnd15-24: 12sc
you have finished the first leg. crochet the second leg in the same way.
when you have finished round 24, make 2 single crochets into the stitches of the second leg to reach the middle.it is important to make the tips of the feet look at each other. use white yarn when you are joining two legs as the underpants are white. stuff the legs after joining.
body
keep crocheting with white
rnd25: join two legs with ch6(36)
rnd26-33: 36sc
rnd34:work blo 36sc
with blue color
rnd35: 36sc
rnd36:work blo 36sc
rnd37-40: 36sc
rnd41: (4sc, dec)x 6 times (30)
rnd42-43: 30sc
rnd44:(3sc, dec)x 6 times (24)
rnd45: 24sc
rnd46:(2sc, dec) x 6 times (18)
with skin color
rnd47-49: 18sc
you have completed the body. do not break the yarn, we will crochet the head.
start stuffing when the decreases begin .
head (skin color)
rnd50: (2sc,inc)x6 times (24)
rnd51:(3sc, inc)x6 (30)
rnd52:(4sc, inc)x6 (36)
rnd53:(5sc, inc)x6 (42)
rnd54:(6sc, inc)x6 (48)
rnd55:(7sc, 1inc)x6 (54)
rnd56-60: 54sc
rnd61: (7sc,dec)x6 (48)
rnd62-66: 48sc
rnd67: (6sc, dec)x6 (42)
rnd68: (5sc,dec)x6 (36)
with yellow color
rnd69: 36sc
rnd70:(4sc,dec)x6 (30)
rnd71:(3sc,dec)x6 (24)
rnd72:(2sc,dec)x6 (18)
rnd73:(1sc,dec)x6 (12)
rnd74: 6dec (6) fasten off.
arm (skin color ) x 2
rnd 1: 5sc into the magic ring
rnd 2: (inc)x5 (10)
rnd 3-5: 10sc
rnd 6: dec, 6sc, dec (8)
rnd 7-17: 8sc
with blue color
rnd 18. work flo inc around (16)
rnd 19-20: 16sc
rnd 21: dec around (8)
rnd 22: (dec)x4 (4) fasten off.
place the security eyes between round 60-61 with a distance of 7 stitches between them. leave a long tail after finishing crocheting the arms. stuff them firmly and sew them to the body by closing the holes. sew the arms on the body where the neck section begins.
do not forget to sew them by hiding the color changes.
hair (yellow)
cut length of 40cm strands(pieces of yarn).
we will place them one by one round the stitches of round 69 where we have crocheted with yellow.
follow the pictorial below!
begin from the front middle of the head.
pull the strands with the help of your hook.(divide hair from the middle to the left and right.)
fold the pieces of yarn in half so they will be equal in length.
after you finish adding hair,take strands of hair from left and right side make a knot on the back with a piece of yellowyarn. you can twist them before making a knot.
use your scissors to make them equal in length.
hair band (black)
make the first chain leaving a long tail, we will sew it on the hair with the help of long tails.
rnd1:ch 22
rnd2:1sc, 19hdc, 3x on the last stitch, do not turn crochet on the other side of the chain.
rnd3: 19hdc, 1sc
bow (black)
rnd1:ch17
rnd2: 1sc, 1 hdc, 3 dc, 1 hdc, 1sc, 2 sl-st, 1sc, 1hdc, 3 dc, 1 hdc, 3sc into the last stitch, do not turn crochet on the other side of the chain.
rnd3: 1sc, 1 hdc, 3 dc, 1 hdc, 1sc, 2 sl-st, 1sc, 1hdc, 3 dc, 1 hdc, 1sc ..fasten off.
sew the bow in to the middle of the hair band.
place the hair band with the help of long tails as in the pictures and sew it. sew them in side the hair on the head.
shoes (red)x2
rnd1:6sc into the magic ring
rnd2: inc around (12)
rnd3: 3sc, inc around (15)
rnd4-6: 15sc
rnd7-8: ch1, turn, 10sc
ch5to make the shoe laces sew it on the opposite side.
inner part of the skirt (white)
we will crochet the inner part of the skirt into the blo stitches of round 34.crochet the skirt as dc
rnd1: 2dc, 1dcinc around (48)
rnd2-3: 48dc
rnd4: 3dc, 1dcinc around (60)
rnd5-7: 60dc
to make borders of the inner skirt; yarn over and insert your hook into the second stitch from your hook, make 1 dc. make 5 more dc into the same stitch. repeat the same process until the end of the round. there will be 15 pieces in total.
begin crochet in gleaving a long tail. hide the yarn tails by sewing the min to the stitches of body.
begin crocheting from the first stitch of blo.
upper part of the skirt (blue)
we will crochet the skirt into the blo stitches of round 36.crochet the skirt as dc.
rnd1:36dcinc (72)
rnd2: 72dc
rnd 3: 3dc, 1dcinc around (90)
rnd4-8: 90dc
rnd9:reverse single crochet around
to make borders of the upper part of the skirt; reverse single crochet all around. if you have any difficulty in making reverse single crochet, you can watch videos on "reverse single crochet" on youtube

rnd1: 6sc into the magic ring

stuff your toadstool stem.
crochet the cap till the last round.do not break the yarn, join the two pieces with single crochet.stuff firmly.
