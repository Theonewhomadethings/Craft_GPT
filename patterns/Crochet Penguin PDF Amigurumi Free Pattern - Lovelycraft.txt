crochet penguin pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: mes.mignonneries
abbreviations
rnd round
mr magic ring
ch chain
sl st slip stitch
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
()* repeat till the end of round
materials
cotton ricorumi: black (060), white (001), grey (080), orange (025), pink (065)
crochet hook: 3mm
6mm safety eyes. you may need to use smaller/ larger eyes, depending on hook/ yarn size.
yarn needle
black embroidery thread
fiberfill for stuffing
scissors
size with the materials above will be about 9 cm.
head & body
black: a
white: b
grey: c
stuff as you go along work.
star in a.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. (inc)* (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 7. 36 sc (36)
rnd 8. (in a: 13sc), (in b: 3sc), (in a: 4sc), (in b: 4sc), (in a: 13sc) (36)
rnd 9. (in a: 12sc), (in b: 5sc), (in a: 2sc), (in b: 5sc), (in a: 12sc) (36)
rnd 10. (in a:(5sc,inc)*2), (in b: 4sc,inc), (in a: 2sc), (in b: 4sc,inc), (in b:(5sc,inc)*2) (42)
rnd 11-12. (in a: 13sc), (in b: 16sc), (in a: 13sc) (42)
rnd 13. (in a: 14sc), (in b: 14sc), (in a: 14sc) (42)
rnd 14. (in a: 15sc), (in b: 12sc), (in a: 15sc) (42)
insert the security eyes between rnd 10 and 11.
rnd 15. (in a:(5sc,dec)*2,1sc), (in b: 5sc,dec,3sc,dec), (in a:(5sc,dec)*2,1sc) (36)
rnd 16. (in a:(4sc,dec)*2,1sc), (in b: (3sc,dec)*2), (in a:(4sc,dec)*2,1sc) (30)
rnd 17. in a (3sc,dec)* (24)
continue in c.
rnd 18. (2sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 19. (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 20. 24 sc (24)
rnd 21. (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 22-24. 30 sc (30)
rnd 25. (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 26-29. 36 sc (36)
rnd 30. (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 31. 42 sc (42)
rnd 32. through back loops only (5sc,dec)* (36)
finish to stuff.
rnd 33. (2sc,dec)* (27)
rnd 34. (1sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 35. (dec)* (9)
rnd 36. (1sc,dec)* (6)
fasten off and close.
beck
in orange
rnd 1. mr, 3 sc (3)
rnd 2. (inc)* (6)
sew the beck between the eyes.
tail
in grey
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)* (9)
rnd 3. 9sc (9)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* (12)
sew the tail in the back of penguin.
foot (make 2)
in black
rnd 1. mr, 3sc (3)
rnd 2. (inc)* (6)
flatten the opening of the foot and sc in next 3 stitches through both layers to close the opening. fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing. sew the feet under the body (6 stitches apart).
wings (make 2)
in grey
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)* (9)
rnd 3. 9 sc (9)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* (12)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* (15)
rnd 6-7. 15 sc (15)
rnd 8. (3sc,dec)* (12)
rnd 9. (2sc,dec)* (9)
flatten the opening of the foot and sc in next 3 stitches through both layers to close the opening. fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing. sew the wings in the side of the body.
earmuffs (make 2)
in pink
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (4)
rnd 2. (inc)* (8)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* (12)
rnd 4-5. 12 sc (12)
to make the headband.
ch 21, 1 sl st in second ch from hook, 19 sl st (20)
attach the band across top of the head. attach round muffs at either side of the band (stuff lightly if desired).
scarf
in pink
ch 65, inc in the second ch from hook, sc in next 62 ch, 3 sc in the last ch. continue on the other side of the ch, 63 sc: join with sl st.
