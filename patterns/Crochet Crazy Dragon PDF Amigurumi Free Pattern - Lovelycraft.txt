crochet crazy dragon pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer: aquariwool
materials
yarn color:
(a) turquoise: alize cotton gold 610 (100g, 330m)
(b) yellow: yarnart jeans 84 (50g, 160m)
(c) orange: yarnart jeans 23 (50g, 160m)
(d) white: yarnart jeans 62 (50g, 160m)
other materials:
crochet hook size 2, 2.5 mm
knitting needles size 3.5- 4 mm
1 pair of safety eyes 6mm
1 wooden button
black and pink thread
tapestry needles, pins, stitch markers, scissors.
the size of the finished amigurumi toy is approximately 13 cm when the specified materials are used.
abbreviations
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
hdc half double crochet
sl st slip stitch
mr magic ring
dc double crochet
tr treble crochet
bobble bobble stitch (4 hdcs in the same st)
blo back loop only
flo front loop only
rnd round
sts stitches
k-knit
p-purl
legs (make 2)
(yarn a)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
break the yarn, make the second leg in the same way, but don't the yarn this time. connect 2 legs together: sc 15 (leg 1)+ sc 15 (leg 2)= 30. move to the body part.
body
(yarn a)
rnd 1. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 2-7. sc 36 (6 rnds) (36)
rnd 8. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 9. sc 30 (30)
rnd 10. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 11. sc 24 (24)
rnd 12. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 13-16. sc 18 (4 rnds) (18)
break the yarn with a long rest. stuff.
tail
(yarn a)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. sc 2, inc 2, sc 2 (8)
rnd 3. sc 3, inc 2, sc 3 (10)
rnd 4. sc 4, inc 2, sc 4 (12)
rnd 5. sc 5, inc 2, sc 5 (14)
rnd 6. sc 6, inc 2, sc 6 (16)
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later, stuff.
arms (make 2)
(yarn a)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-7. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
break the yarn with a long rest. stuff slightly. sew 2 edges together.
head
(yarn a)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10. sc 54 (54)
rnd 11. (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 12-19. sc 60 (8 rnds) (60)
rnd 20. (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 21. sc 54 (54)
rnd 22. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 23. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 24. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 25. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 26. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 27. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later. stuff.
eyes (make 2)
yarn d)
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
rnd 5. (3sc,dec)*3 (12)
break the yarn with a long rest, attach safety eyes, stuff.
eyelids (make 2)
(yarn a)
rnd 1. ch 8, start on the 2nd stitch from the hook.
rnd 2. sc 1, hdc 1, dc 1, (dc 2), dc 1, hdc 1, sc 1.
break the yarn with a long rest to sew later.
nose
(yarn a)
use yarn b, sc 4 into a magic ring (x2)
use yarn a, sc 6 into a magic ring (x2)
attach 2 pieces of color a and color b together.
wing (make 2)
(yarn d)
rnd 1. ch 15 start in the 2nd stitch from the hook, sc 14 turn (14)
rnd 2. ch 1, blo, sc 14, turn (14)
rnd 3-4. ch 1, blo, sc 12, turn (2 rnds) (12)
rnd 5-6. ch 1, blo, sc 10, turn (2 rnds) (10)
rnd 7-8. ch 1, blo, sc 8, turn (2 rnds) (8)
break the yarn, use the yarn a to crochet sc across the wings, use the tapestry needle to sew 2 wings into the back of the body.
thorn
(yarn b)
crochet round 1 with single crochet from tail up to the first half of the head (like photo below).
rnd 2. (skip 2, dc 5 in the same stitch, skip 2), repeat to the end.
scarf
(yarn c, using knitting needles)
rnd 1. cast on 6 st (6)
rnd 2. k6 (6)
rnd 3. p6 (6)
repeat rnd 2 and 3 till you reach 13 cm then cast off, break the yarn, hide the remaining yarn.
use the tapestry needle to attach the wooden button
to the scarf. that's it, congratulations!
use black thread to embroider the mouth.
use pink thread to embroider the cheeks.
