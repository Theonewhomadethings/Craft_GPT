crochet plush strawberry bunny amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: stuffedamigurumi
abbreviations
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
ch chain
slst slip stitch
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
ttc triple crochet
we start the construction of the crochet strawberry bunny from the head first.
head
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 9. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 10-14. 54sc (54)
rnd 15. 12sc,6inc,18sc,6inc,12sc (66)
rnd 16-20. 66sc (66)
rnd 21. 12sc,6dec,18sc,6dec,12sc (54)
rnd 22. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 23. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
fasten off.
after completing the head part of the crocheted bunny, we move on to the next step, the body part.
body
(red yarn)
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (6)
rnd 2. (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3. (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 4. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 5. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 6. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 7. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 8. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 9. (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 10. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 11-18. 54sc (54)
rnd 19. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 20. 48sc (48)
rnd 21. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 22. 42sc (42)
fasten off. leave a long tail for sewing.
after completing the strawberry body, we crochet the bunny's ears and fix them on the head.
ears (make 2)
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4-7. 18sc (18)
rnd 8. (7sc,dec)*2 (16)
rnd 9. 16sc (16)
rnd 10. (6sc,dec)*2 (14)
rnd 11. 14sc (14)
rnd 12. (5sc,dec)*2 (12)
fasten off. leave a long tail for sewing.
leaves
(green yarn)
48ch, on 2nd chain count from hook: slst, sc, hdc, dc, ttc, skip 2ch, (slst, 6ch, slst, hdc, dc, ttc),
skip 2ch () continue to the end.
fill with fiberfill.
assembly pieces as shown by sample. use pink plush to make up. safety eyes size 8 mm.
