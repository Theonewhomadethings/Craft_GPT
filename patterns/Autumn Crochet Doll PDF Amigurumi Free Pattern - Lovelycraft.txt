autumn crochet doll pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: dollishland
abbreviations
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
slst : slip stitch
sc : single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
hdc : half double crochet
hdc-dec: half double crochet decrease
dc: double crochet
dc -inc: double crochet increase
blo: back loop only
flo: front loop only
rnds : rounds
sts : stitch
hook size: 2.00 mm
we start with the legs of the amigurumi crochet doll.
legs (make 2)
rnd 1: start with beige yarn. 6 sc in mr = 6
rnd 2: 2 (2sc, 1 inc)= 8
rnd 3-12: 8sc (10 rnds)
cut the yarn of the first leg. don't cut the yarn of second leg, chain 3, join the first leg.
rnd 13: 8sc, 3sc in the chain, 8sc, 3sc in the chain = 22
we will go on with the body
i prefer not to stuff the legs but you can stuff the legs.
as we complete the crochet baby's legs, we then move on to crocheting her body.
body
rnd 14-18: 22sc (5 rnds), change to white yarn
rnd 19: blo 22sc
rnd 20-22: 22sc (3 rnds)
rnd 23: 5(2sc, 1 dec), 2sc = 17
rnd 24: 17sc
rnd 25: 5 (1sc, 1 dec)= 12
rnd 26-27: 12 sc (2 rnds)
rnd 28: 4 (1sc, 1 dec)= 8
rnd 29-34: 8sc (6 rnds)
rnd 35: 4 dec and cut the yarn
after completing the crochet baby's legs and body, let's make the arms and pin them.
arm (make 2)
rnd 1: start with skin color 6sc in mr = 6
rnd 2: 2(2sc , 1 inc)= 8
rnd 3-8: 8sc ( 6 rnds ), change to white yarn
rnd 9-18: 8sc ( 10 rnds), cut the yarn but leave a long tail to sew the arms.
after completing the arms, we make the crocheted doll's winter coat.
coat
rnd 1: start with mustard color yarn. make 31 chain. start in the 2nd stitch from the hook. 3 (1 inc, 4sc), 3(4sc, 1 inc)= 36
rnd 2-4: 3( 1 ch, turn back, 36sc)( 3 lines)
rnd 5: 1 ch, 5sc, 8ch, skip 8sts , 10sc, 8ch, skip 8 sts, 5sc = 36x
rnd 6-11: 6 (1ch, turn back, 36sc)(6 lines)
rnd 12: 1 ch, 36 dc, don't cut the yarn.
we will make a line of sc around the coat. make 3sc in 1sts on the bottom and top corners.
the arms of the coat (make 2)
insert your hook to the sts of the armpit. make 18sc during 9 rnds. make 18dc for the last round, cut the yarn.
after completing the crochet coat, we will crochet her cute shoes.
shoes (make 2)
rnd 1: make 6 chain, start in the 2nd stitch from the hook, 4sc, 3sc in last stitch, 3sc and 1 inc into the other side of ch = 12
rnd 2: 1 inc, 3sc, 3 inc, 3sc, 2 inc, = 18
rnd 3: make 1 ch,insert your hook to the next sts from the back and don't yarn over the hook and insert your hook to the next sts from the front. yarn over and pull the hook through these 2 stitches and make 1sc. repeat it 17 times more.
rnd 4: 18sc
rnd 5: 4sc, 3 hdc-dec, 8sc = 15
rnd 6: 5( 1sc, 1 dec)= 10
rnd 7: 10sc
rnd 8: blo 10sc
rnd 9: 10sc and cut the yarn.
most of the pattern steps are complete, now let's move on to crocheting the crochet doll's head. don't forget to follow lovelycraft.com for 2 new free amigurumi patterns every day.
head
rnd 1: with skin color 6s in mr = 6
rnd 2: 6 inc= 12
rnd 3: 6 (1sc, 1 inc)= 18
rnd 4: 6 (2sc, linc)= 24
rnd 5: 1sc, 1 inc, 5(3sc, 1inc), 2sc = 30
rnd 6: 6 (4sc, 1 inc)=36
rnd 7: 2sc, 1 inc, 5(5sc, 1 inc), 3sc =42
rnd 8-12: 42sc (5 rnds)
rnd 13: 6 (6sc,1 inc)= 48
rnd 14-17: 48sc (4 rnds)
rnd 18: 8(4sc, 1dec)= 40
rnd 19: 8 (3sc, 1dec)= 32
insert the safety eyes between the round 13 and 14 and there should be 8 sts between the eyes. start to stuff the head.
rnd 20: 8(2sc, 1dec)= 24
rnd 21: 8(1sc, 1dec)= 16
rnd 22: 3(2sc, 1dec), 4sc = 13
rnd 23-28: 13sc (6 rnds)
rnd 29: 6dec, 1sc = 7 and cut the yarn.
push the piece that is shaped at the and of the head into the head by using a pencil or the handle of the hook. apply hair to the head.
after finishing the crochet doll's head part, we make the hat.
hat
rnd 1: start with white yarn, 8sc in mr = 8
rnd 2: 8inc = 16
rnd 3: 8(1sc, 1 inc)= 24
rnd 4: 8(2sc, 1 inc)= 32
rnd 5: 8(3sc, 1 inc) = 40, change to brown yarn
rnd 6: 8(4sc,1 inc)= 48
rnd 7: 8 (5sc, 1 inc)=56, change to white yarn
rnd 8: 8(6sc,1 inc)= 64
rnd 9: 8 (7sc, 1 inc)= 72, change to brown yarn
rnd 10: 8(8sc, 1 inc)= 80
rnd 11: 8(8sc, 1 dec)= 72, change to white yarn
rnd 12: 8(7sc, 1dec)= 64
rnd 13: 8 (6sc, 1dec)= 56, change to brown yarn
rnd 14: 8(5sc, 1dec)= 48, change to white yarn
rnd 15: make 2 ch, 48dc and cut the yarn.
we have completed the crochet hat, now let's embroider a beautiful crochet bag.
bag
rnd 1: start with dark green yarn. make 11 ch, start in the 2nd stitch from the hook, 1sc, 3sc in 1 sts, 6sc, 1 inc. go on the other side of the chain 10 sc= 22
rnd 2: blo 22sc
rnd 4-8: 22sc (5 rnds)
rnd 9: 12sc, 1 ch and turn back, 9sc
rnd 10-13: 4 (1 ch, turn back, 9sc)(4 lines)
rnd 14: 5sc, 5ch, 4sc and cut the yarn.
after completing the crochet bag and other accessories, let's complete the cat part, which is the last step, and finish the pattern.
the cat (head)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr = 6
rnd 2: 6inc = 12
rnd 3: 6(1sc, 1 inc)= 18
rnd 4: 6(2sc,1 inc)= 24
rnd 5: 6(3sc, 1 inc)= 30
rnd 6: 6(4sc, 1 inc)= 36
rnd 7-10: 36sc ( 4 rnds )
rnd 11: 6(4sc, 1 dec)= 30
rnd 12: 6(3sc, 1 dec)= 24
rnd 13: 6(2sc, 1 dec)= 18
start to stuff it.
rnd 14: 6(1sc, 1dec)= 12
rnd 15: 6 dec and cut the yarn.
the cat ears
rnd 1: 6sc in mr
rnd 2: 2 (2sc, 1 inc)=8
rnd 3: 8sc, leave a long tail to sew and cut the yarn.
-sew the ears on the head and between the rounds 13-14.
you can make a round of sc to the flo that is not crocheted of the arms and the body
the crochet doll is now completely ready.
