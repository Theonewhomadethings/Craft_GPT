designer
instagram: kalu.crochet
materials
brown, light blue and white thread
hook 2.0 mm
8mm safety eyes
wool needle
pair of scissors
filling
and lots of love
abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
blo back loop only (you knit only taking the back strand of the stitch).
head
we are going to weave in a spiral with white yarn
rnd 1. mr 6sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc from sc (12)
rnd 3. 1sc, 1inc x6 (18)
rnd 4. 2sc, 1inc x6 (24)
rnd 5. 3sc, 1inc x6 (30)
rnd 6. 4sc, 1inc x6 (36)
rnd 7. 5sc, 1inc x6 (42)
rnd 8. 6sc, 1inc x6 (48)
rnd 9. 7sc, 1.inc x6 (54)
rnd 10-19. 54 sc (10 turns)
we place the eyes in the middle of rounds 15 and 16 with sc apart. and the middle nose of the eyes on round 17 taking 2sc.
rnd 21. 7sc, 1dis x6 (48)
rnd 22. 3sc, 1 dis x6 (42)
rnd 20. 5sc, 1 dis x6 (36)
rnd 21. 4sc, 1 dis x6 (30)
rnd 22. 3sc, 1 dis x6 (24)
rnd 23. 2sc, 1 dis x6 (18)
we fill, cut strand and lose in the fabric.
arms
we knit with brown color
rnd 1. mr 5 sc (5)
rnd 2. 5 inc from sc (10)
rnd 3-13. 10 sc (11 turns)
color change to pink
rnd 8-10. 9 sc (3 turns)
we join the fabric and close with sc. we cut the thread and match the fabric. (it does not have padding) we sew on rounds 14,15 and 16, on the sides of the body.
cream
we are going to knit in white
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc sc (12)
rnd 3. 1 sc, 1 inc x6 (18)
rnd 4. 2 sc, 1 inc x6 (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, 1 inc x6 (30)
rnd 6. 4 sc, 1 inc x6 (36)
rnd 7. 5 sc, 1 inc x6 (42)
rnd 8. (let's weave fans)
we knit 1 sl st, we leave a free stitch and in the next one we work 6 tr, we leave a free stitch and in the next we knit 1 slipped or dwarf stitches, so we form the first fan. (we repeat the above to form each fan, in total there are 10 fans, at the end we cut a long thread and sew to the upper right part of the head).
legs (make 2)
we are going to knit in brown
rnd 1. mr 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc from sc (12)
rnd 3-6. 12 sc (5 turns)
note: at the end of the first leg we cut the yarn and match the fabric.
at the end of the second round we do not cut, we continue knitting a slip stitch and we knit 4 chains, we take a stitch from the first leg and join with a slipped or dwarf stitches.
body
rnd 1. we weave 12 sc on the leg 1, 4 sc on the chains and we continue weaving 12 sc on the second leg and to finish the round. (32)
rnd 2. 7 sc, 1 inc x4 (36)
rnd 3-5. 36 sc (3 turns)
rnd 6. 4 sc, 1 dis x6 (30)
rnd 7-15. 30 sc (9 turns)
rnd 16. 3 sc, 1 dis x6 (24)
rnd 17. 2 sc, 1 dis x6 (18)
rnd 18. 18 sc.
we fill and cut long strand to sew to the head.
skirt
we are going to weave with light blue yarn
we start making a slip knot and weave 46 chains, we leave 6 chains free (to form the buttonhole) and in the next we knit the first stitch of round 1.
rnd 1. 40 sc (1 turns)
we close the last stitch with a slip stitch to the first stitch of the round and work up with 2 chains for the next round.
rnd 2. 40 inc hdc (80)
we close the last stitch with a slip stitch to the first stitch of the round and work up with 2 chains for the next round.
rnd 3. 1 hdc, 1 inc x40 (120)
we cut a long strand and match the fabric. we sew a small button on the first round 1 stitches
accessory
we are going to weave with light blue yarn
we start with a slip knot and weave 13 chains. we leave a free chain and in the next we knit the first stitch to start row 1.
rnd 1. 12 sc (1 turns)
at the end of each round we go up work with a chain and turn the fabric.
rnd 2-6. blo 12 sc (5 turns)
we cut the thread and lose in the fabric.
we take new thread and we roll it in the middle of the piece to form the knot of the bow, we knot and leave a thread to sew on the head, just on the cream.
