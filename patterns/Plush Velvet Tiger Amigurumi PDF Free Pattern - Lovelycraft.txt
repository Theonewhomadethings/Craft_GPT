friends, today i will share a very cute tiger pattern. you can find the materials used in the description section. you can get a similar look when you use the same materials. i can say that the crochet tiger pattern is quite cute. especially the mouth and nose part looks very cute. the inner parts of the ears and the white paws add a different reality. as for the eyes, you need to do some tightening. now, the crochet tiger amigurumi is ready! now let's crochet tiger amigurumi step by step.
crochet plush tiger amigurumi pdf free pattern
designer: tashkatoys
materials
hook 4.5 mm or according to your density
eyes 12mm
himalaya dolphin baby or yarnart dolce
finished toy size 17cm
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
head
we crochet with white yarns
1rnd: 6 mr
2rnd: inc*6 = 12 sc
rnd: (2 sc, inc)*4 = 16 sc
4rnd: 2 sc, inc*4, 10 sc = 20 sc
5rnd: change yarn color to orange and crochet 20 slst
6rnd: 5 sc, inc*4, 11 sc = 24 sc
7- 11rnd: 24 sc (5 rows)
12rnd: (2 sc, dec) 6 = 18 sc
13rnd: (1 sc, dec) 6 = 12 sc
14rnd: dec*6
we insert eyes between 6 -7 next on review 7 sc we stretch and embroider the nose.
ears
4 parts: 2 white, 2 orange
1rnd: 3 mr, ch and turn
2rnd: 1 sc, 2 sc in one loop, 1 sc, turn
3rnd: 4 sc
connect two parts and crochet sc on three sides sew on at a distance of 4-5 loops between 10-11 rows.
arms
1rnd: 6 mr
2-4 rnd: 6 sc (3 rows)
5rnd: 2 sc, dec, 1 sc, inc =6 sc
6-7rnd: 6 sc
fold in half and crochet 3sc
legs
we crochet with white yarns
1rnd: 6 mr
2rnd: inc*6 = 12 sc
3rnd: change the color of the yarn and crochet (5 sc, inc)*2 = 14 sc
4rnd: 14 sc
5rnd: 3sc, dec*4, 3sc = 10sc
6rnd: 3sc, dec*2, 3 sc = 8sc
7-8rnd: 8 sc (2 rows)
9rnd: (2 sc, dec)*2 = 6 sc
10rnd: 1 sc, fold in half and crochet 3 sc
body
1rnd: 6 mr
2rnd: inc*6 = 12 sc
3rnd: (1 sc, inc)*6 = 18 sc
4rnd: 18 sc
5rnd: !we crochet the tail! : 16 sc, 6 ch and into the second loop from the hook 1slst, 4sc (so that there is no hole in the next loop, we do another 1 sc), 1 sc together with the tail (connect the last loop of the tail with the body), 1 sc
6rnd: !we crochet legs!: 1 sc, 3 sc with leg, 6 sc, 3 sc with leg, 5 sc = 18 sc
7rnd: 18 sc
8rnd: (1 sc, dec)*6 = 12 sc
9 10rnd: 12 sc
11rnd: (4 sc, dec)*2 = 10 sc
12rnd: !we crochet our hands!: 2 sc, 3 sc with hands, 2 sc, 3 sc with = 10 sc
13rnd: 3 sc, turn and 3 sc (we make a slight rise so that the head looked slightly down when stitching)
