crochet giraffe keychain free amigurumi pattern
designer
instagram: handmadebylacey
materials
2mm hook
6mm eyes
let's start from the head part to make our cute crochet giraffe keychain.
head
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: 1sc,inc (18)
rnd 4: 2sc,inc (24)
rnd 5: 3sc,inc (30)
rnd 6: 4sc,inc (36)
rnd 7-12: sc in all
attache safety eyes between row 9-10 with 6 st apart.
rnd 13: 4sc,dec (30)
rnd 14: 3sc,dec (24)
rnd 15: 2sc,dec (18)
rnd 16: 1dec,10sc,1dec,4sc (16)
we started the making of the giraffe keychain from the head, after we finished, let's move on to crocheting the body.
body
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (inc)*6 (12)
rnd 3: 1sc,inc (18)
rnd 4: 2sc,inc (24)
rnd 5: 3sc,inc (30)
rnd 6-10: sc in all
rnd 11: 4sc,dec (25)
rnd 12: 3sc,dec (20)
rnd 13: sc in all
rnd 14: 3sc,dec (16)
rnd 15-16: sc in all
now that we've completed the head and body part of the giraffe keychain, let's make the arms.
arm (make 2)
rnd 1: 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-8: sc in all
after finishing the arms of the key chain, let's work the legs beautifully with crochet.
legs
ch 5, start in second ch from hook
rnd 1: 3sc, 3sc in last ch, continue working an oval round, 2sc, inc.
rnd 2: inc, 2sc, 3inc, sc, 2inc
rnd 3: 4sc, 3dec, 4sc, dec
rnd 4: 4sc, 2dec, 4sc
rnd 5-10: sc in all
rnd 11: 3sc, dec
the arms and legs of the giraffe amigurumi pattern are now ready, now we are making the nose.
nose
ch 7 and start in second ch from hook
rnd 1: inc, 4sc, 3sc in last ch, continue working in oval round, 4sc, 1sc in last.
rnd 2: (inc)*2, 4sc, (inc)*3, 4sc, inc
rnd 3: sc in all
the next stage of giraffe amigurumi is the ears, let's complete them carefully and beautifully.
ears
rnd 1: 4sc in ring
rnd 2: 1sc,inc (6)
rnd 3: 1sc,inc (9)
rnd 4: 2sc,inc (12)
rnd 5-6: sc in all
let's make 3 patches to make the points on the giraffes and fix them on the amigurumi model.
patches (make 3)
rnd 1: 10 sc in ring
finally, the horns of the amigurumi giraffe are left, let's crochet them well and fix them to the head of the amigurumi mold.
horns (make 2)
start with brown
rnd 1: 6sc in ring
rnd 2: inc (12)
rnd 3: sc in all
rnd 4: dec (6)
change to yellow
rnd 5-7: sc in all
that's it, the crochet giraffe keychain pattern is now completely ready. don't forget to send us the photos of the keychains you made.
