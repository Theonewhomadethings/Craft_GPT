hello my dears. today i will share a lalafanfan amigurumi pattern made with velvet yarn. as you know, lalafanfan is a very popular character lately. when using these materials, the size of the finished toy is 20 cm. please note that the size of your toy depends on the density of your crocheting and the size of the hook. legs, body and head are crocheted in one piece. handles are tied. the beak and cheeks are sewn on. don't forget to follow me for more free amigurumi patterns. now let's crochet lalafanfan amigurumi step by step.
pdf plush lalafanfan amigurumi free crochet pattern
designer: konyshevatoys
materials
1. plush yarn (composition 100 gr / 120 m):
himalaya dolphin yarn: 80302 (80313) -1 skein
himalaya dolphin yarn: 80309 on the cheeks
himalaya dolphin yarn: 80318 (80316)
on the beak you can take absolutely any color of yarn.
2. hook 3.5 (or which you usually crochet)
3. eyes 12 mm on a secure mount
4. filler
5. yarn vita soso (or equivalent) for assembly
6. needle for stitching parts
7. scissors
8. markers
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
tr: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
hands (2 parts)
fill only the bottom of the hands.
1. 6 sc in mr (6)
2. 6 inc (12)
3-12. 10 rows 12 sc (12)
cut the yarn, fasten and hide. we will sew the arms into the body.
cheeks (2 parts)
we will stuff when sewing.
1. 6 sc in mr (6)
2. 6 inc (12)
3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
4. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
5-6. 2 rows 24 sc (24)
cut the yarn, fasten and hide.
beak
1. 6 sc in mr (6)
2. 6 inc (12)
3. 6 sc, 6 hdc (12)
cut the yarn, fasten and hide.
we form the beak: 6 sc from the bottom, 3 hdc on the sides.
legs (2 parts)
stuff tightly as you crochet.
1. 6 sc in mr (6)
2. 6 inc (12)
3. (sc, inc) *6 (18)
4-8. 5 rows 18 sc (18)
cut the yarn, fasten and hide.
we do not cut the yarn on the right leg, we will connect it from behind. next we will crochet the body.
body
stuff tightly as you crochet.
9. 18 sc left leg, 18 sc right leg (36)
10. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
11-16. 6 rows 42 sc (42)
17. (5 sc, dec) 6 (36)
18-20. 3 rows 36 sc (36)
21. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
22-24. 3 rows 30 sc (30)
25. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
in the 26th and 27th row we crochet our hands. we divide the hand into 2 parts of 6 sc. make sure your arms are symmetrical.
26. 3 sc, 6 sc on the inside of the left hand along with the body, 6 sc, 6 sc on the inside of the right hand along with the body, 3 sc (24)
27. 3 sc, 6 sc in remaining left hand sts, 6 sc, 6 sc in remaining right hand sts, 3 sc (24)
28. 24 sc (24)
we do not cut the yarn, then we will crochet the head.
head
stuff tightly as you crochet.
29. 24 inc (48)
30. (7 sc, inc)*6 (54)
31-38. 8 rows 54 sc (54)
insert eyes between 37-38 rows, between eyes 4-6 sc.
39. (7 sc, dec)*6 (48)
40. (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
41. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
42. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
43. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
44. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
45. (sc, dec) 6 (12)
46. 6 dec, close the hole.
cut the yarn, fasten and hide.
assembly
sew the beak between 35-37 rows, forming the beak into a triangle. sew cheeks between 30-37 rows.
