let's start crocheting the amigurumi cat pattern. first we will crochet the head part.
head
rnd 1. start 8 sc in a magic ring (8)
rnd 2. inc in all 8 st (16)
rnd 3. (sc in next st, inc) rep 8 times (24)
rnd 4. sc in all 24 st (24)
rnd 5. (sc in next 2 sts, inc) rep 8 times (32)
rnd 6. sc in all 32 st (32)
rnd 7. (sc in next 3 sts, inc) rep 8 times (40)
rnd 8. sc in all 40 st (40)
rnd 9. (sc in next 4 sts, inc) rep 8 times (48)
rnd 10 16. sc in all 48 st (48)
rnd 17. (sc in next 4 st, dec) rep 8 times (40)
rnd 18. (sc in next 3 st, dec) rep 8 times (32)
rnd 19. (sc in next 2 st, dec) rep 8 times (24)
insert the safety eye between rounds 12 and 13 with an interspace of 14 stitches. embroider the nose. stuff with fiberfill.
after completing the head part of the amigurumi cat, let's crochet the body part. we will crochet the legs of the esparanza cat in this part.
body (change colon)
rnd 20-22. sc in all 24 st (24)
rnd 23. (sc in next 5 sts, inc) rep 4 times (28)
rnd 24-25. sc in all 28 st (28)
rnd 26. (sc in next 6 sts, inc) rep 4 times (32)
rnd 27-28. sc in all 32 st (32)
rnd 29. (sc in next 7 sts, inc) rep 4 times (36)
rnd 30-36. sc in all 36 st (36)
divide the legs into 18 stiches each. fill as you go.
* rnd 37 -38. sc in all 18 st (18)
rnd 39. (sc in next 4 st, dec) rep 3 times (15)
rnd 40-41. sc in all 15 st (15)
rnd 42. (sc in next 3 st, dec) rep 3 times (12)
rnd 43-46. sc in all 12 st (12)
rnd 47. dec 6 times (6)
close with tapestry needle. make the other *
let's crochet amigurumi cat ears. it is crocheted using light brown yarn.
ears
rnd 1. start 6 sc in a magic ring (6)
rnd 2. (sc in next st, inc) rep 3 times (9)
rnd 3. (sc in next 2 sts, inc) rep 3 times (12)
rnd 4. (sc in next 3 sts, inc) rep 3 times (15)
rnd 5. (sc in next 4 sts, inc) rep 3 times (18)
rnd 6. sc in all 18 st (18)
let's crochet the arms and tail of the amigurumi cat
arms
rnd 1. start 5 sc in a magic ring (5)
rnd 2. inc in all 5 st (10)
rnd 3-17. sc in all 10 st (10)
tail
rnd 1. start 7 sc in a magic ring (7)
rnd 2-30. sc in all 7 st (7)
let's crochet the collar part of the amigurumi cat pattern. you can crochet using any color yarn you want.
necklace
rnd 1. ch 26 st
rnd 2. 3 ch in each st (72)
rnd 3. 3 ch in each st (216)
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi esparanza cat pattern.
