materials
safety eyes
hook no:2

abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
tr: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
ears:
1. 7sc in mr
2. 7inc (14)
3. 14slst leave the yarn for sewing, cut the thread.

1. 6sc in mr
2. 6inc (12)
3. (3sc, 3inc)*2 (18)
4. 18sc leave the yarn for sewing, cut the thread
head:
1. 6sc in mr
2. 6inc(12)

4. (2sc, inc)*6 (24)

6. (2sc, inc)*10 (40)
7-10. 40sc
11. (4sc, inc)*8 (48)
12-16. 48sc
17. (6sc, dec)*6 (42)

19. (4sc, dec)*6 (30)
20. (sc, dec)*10 (20)
21. (8sc, dec)*2 (18)
fasten off, long leave the tail for sewing.
eyes
between 12 and 13 rows, at a distance of 7 loops
tummy:
1. 6sc
2. 6inc (12)
3. (sc, inc)*6 (18) leave the tail for sewing, cut the thread
arms:
first color:
1. 6sc in mr
2. 6inc (12)

change main color
4. 15sc
5. (sc, dec)*5 (10)

7-15. 8sc
4sc in half, leave the tail for sewing, cut the thread
legs:
first color:
1. 6sc in mr

4. 18sc

7-10. 12sc
11. (3sc, inc)*3 (15) fasten the thread on the first leg, cut it off. on the second leg, knit an additional 5-6 sc so that the legs look in one direction, go to knitting the body

2ch
