emperor crochet penguin pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: les.mailles.du.rocher
materials
cotton ricorumi: grey 004, white 001, black 060
crochet hook: 2,5 or 3 mm
security eyes: 5 or 6 mm
yarn needle
fiberfill for stuffing
black embroidery thread
stitch marker
scissors
abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
sl st slip stitch
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
()* repeat till the end of round
[] total number of stitches in the round
rnd round
wings (make 2)
in grey
rnd 1. mr, 5sc [5]
rnd 2. 5 sc [5]
rnd 3. 5 inc [10]
rnd 4. 10 sc [10]
rnd 5. (4sc,inc)* [12]
rnd 6-10. 12 sc [12]
rnd 11. (4sc,dec)* [10]
rnd 12. 10 sc [10]
rnd 13. (3sc,dec)* [8]
fold and crochet through both sides 3 sc.
the wings will be crocheted directly to the body.
legs & body
stuff as you go along your work.
in grey
make 2 legs:
rnd 1. mr, 7 sc [7]
rnd 2. 7 inc [14]
rnd 3. 14 sc [14]
fasten off the first leg.
don't cut the second. make a chain 4 and join to the first leg with an sc.
this sc will be the first sc of the rnd.
rnd 4. on first leg, 1 sc (join), 13 sc, 4 sc on the chain, 14 sc on second leg, 4 sc on the other side of the chain [36]
rnd 5. 36 sc [36]
all the increases and decreases should be on the sides of the body. if not, add or undo some stitches.
rnd 6. 7 sc, inc, 16 sc, inc, 11 sc [38]
rnd 7-12. 38 sc [38]
rnd 13. 8 sc, dec, 16 sc, dec, 10 sc [36]
rnd 14-16. 36 sc [36]
rnd 17. 8 sc, dec, 16 sc, dec, 8 sc [34]
rnd 18-19. 34 sc [34]
rnd 20. 8 sc, dec, 15 sc, dec, 7 sc [32]
in next rnd you will insert the wings.
rnd 21. 7 sc, 3 sc through wing and body, 13 sc, 3sc through wing and body, 6 sc [32]
change to black
rnd 22. 8 sc, dec, 13 sc, dec, 7 sc [30]
tail
in grey
rnd 1. mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)* [6]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [9]
rnd 4-5. 9 sc [9]
head
black: b
white: w
start in b
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. 6 inc [12]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [18]
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* [24]
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* [30]
rnd 6. (2sc,inc,2sc)* [36]
rnd 7-10. (in b: 11sc), (in w: 5sc), (in b: 2sc), (in w: 5sc), (in b: 13sc) [36]
rnd 11-12. (in b: 11sc), (in w: 12sc), (in b: 13sc) [36]
continue in b
rnd 13. (2sc,dec,2sc)* [30]
fasten off and leave a tail to sew.
beak
in black
rnd 1. mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)* [6]
rnd 3. 6 sc [6], 1 slst
fasten off and sew to the head.
feet (make 2)
in black
rnd 1. mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)* [6]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [9]
rnd 4-5. 9 sc [9]
flatten the foot, crochet through both sides: (1sc, ch 2, 1sc in same stitch)*3, 1 slst.
