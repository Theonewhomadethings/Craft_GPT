pdf crochet valentine bear's amigurumi free pattern
designer: littleapril.designs
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase (2 sc in one stitch)
dec decrease (make 2 stitches in one stitch)
st stitches
tr treble crochet
dc double crochet
hdc half double crochet
slst slip stitch
note: please put the eyes at round 23 (size: 10mm)
legs (make 2)
rnd 1. mr: 6 sc
rnd 2. 6 inc
rnd 3.(sc, inc) x 6
rnd 4-7. 18 sc (4 rnds)
start with 2nd leg and make 9 chains join with 1st leg (18sc) then back with 9sc and
18sc in 2nd leg (9 + 18 + 9 + 18).
body
rnd 1-27. 54 sc (27 rnds)
rnd 28. (7sc, dec) x 6
rnd 29. (6sc, dec) x 6
rnd 30. (5sc, dec) x 6
rnd 31. (4sc, dec) x 6
rnd 32. (3sc, dec) x 6
rnd 33. (2sc, dec) x 6
head
rnd 1. mr: 6 sc
rnd 2. 6 inc
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x 6
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) x 6
rnd 5. (3sc, inc) x 6
rnd 6. (4sc, inc) x 6
rnd 7. (5sc, inc) x 6
rnd 8. (6sc, inc) x 6
rnd 9. (7sc, inc) x 6
rnd 10. (8sc, inc) x 6
rnd 11. (9sc, inc) x 6
rnd 12. (10sc, inc) x 6
rnd 13. (11sc, inc) x 6
rnd 14-22. 78sc (9 rnds)
rnd 23. (12sc, inc) x 6
rnd 24. (13sc, inc) x 6
rnd 25. (14sc, inc) x 6
rnd 26. 96sc
rnd 27. (14sc, dec) x 6
rnd 28. (13sc, dec) x 6
rnd 29. (12sc, dec) x 6
rnd 30. (11sc, dec) x 6
rnd 31. (10sc, dec) x 6
rnd 32. (9sc, dec) x 6
rnd 33. (8sc, dec) x 6
rnd 34. (7sc, dec) x 6
rnd 35. (6sc, dec) x 6
rnd 36. (5sc, dec) x 6
rnd 37. (4sc, dec) x 6
rnd 38. (3sc, dec) x 6
rnd 39. (2sc, dec) x 6
ears (make 2)
rnd 1. mr: 6 sc
rnd 2. 6 inc
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x 6
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) x 6
rnd 5-6. 24sc (2 rnds)
rnd 7. (2sc, dec) x 6
muzzle (whie yarn)
rnd 1. mr: 6 sc
rnd 2. 6 inc
rnd 3. (sc, inc) x 6
rnd 4. (2sc, inc) x 6
rnd 5-6. 24sc (2 rnds)
arms (make 2)
rnd 1. mr: 6 sc
rnd 2. 6 inc
rnd 3-19. 12sc (17 rnds)
scarf
rnd 1. start with 87 chains
rnd 2. (blo) work in the opposite direction. start with the third stitch. hdc until the last stitch (85)
rnd 3-4. (blo) 85 hdc (2 rounds) (85)
heart (2 pieces)
rnd 1. start with mr, 2 chains
rnd 2. 3tr, 4dc, tr, 4dc, 3tr
rnd 3. sc in chain space, 2hdc in next st, 3hdc in next st, 2hdc in next st, hdc in next 3sts, 2hdc in next st, (hdc, dc, hdc) in next st, 2hdc in next st, hdc in next 3sts, 2hdc in next st, 3hdc in next st, 2hdc in next st, (sc, slst) in chain space.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi bear pattern.
