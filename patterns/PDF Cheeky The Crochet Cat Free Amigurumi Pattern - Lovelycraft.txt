cheeky the crochet cat free amigurumi pattern
designer
instagram: francuccicrochet
ravelry: silvia francucci
the finished amigurumi is 10 cm tall.
materials
hobbii cotton kings 8/4 yarn:
black 03
light pink 15
white 01
red 10
2mm hook
tapestry needle
pins
scissors
stuffing material
stitch markers
abbreviations
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
rnd: round
st: stitch
sl st: slip stitch
sc: single crochet
hdc: half double crochet
dc: double crochet
htc: half treble crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
dec of 3: take 3 loops whendecreasing instead of 2
flo: front loop only
blo: back loop only
fo: fasten off
(): repeat for the times indicatedin brackets
[]: work in the same stitch
when working in rows, always doa turning ch and start from 2ndch from hook, never count thatch as a stitch
always use a st marker at thebeginning of a new r
work tight
when you do inc and dec, if notspecified, they are always sc
head
(black)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6inc (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (sc,inc,sc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: 24sc (24)
rnd 6: (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 7: 30sc (30)
rnd 8: (2sc,inc,2sc)*6 (36)
rnd 9-13: 36sc (36)
rnd 14: (2inc,16sc)*2 (40)
rnd 15: sc,2inc,18sc,2inc,17sc (44)
rnd 16: (4dec,14sc)*2 (36)
rnd 17: (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 18: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 19: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
start stuffing
rnd 20: (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 21: 6dec (6)
sl st and fo, close the little hole.
body
(black)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6inc (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (sc,inc,sc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6-11: 30sc (30)
rnd 12: (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 13: 24sc (24)
rnd 14: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 15-16: 18sc (18)
sl st and fo, leave long end for sewing.
stuff
sew the body to the head (pic 3).
stuff a bit more before closing.
ears (make 2)
(black) (pic 4)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: 9sc (9)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*3 (12)
rnd 5: 12sc (12)
rnd 6: (3sc,inc)*3 (15)
sl st and fo, leave long end for sewing.
tail
(black)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6inc (12)
rnd 3-5: 12sc (12)
rnd 6: 2dec,4sc,inc,3sc (11)
rnd 7: 2dec,3sc,inc,3sc (10)
rnd 8: 2dec,2sc,inc,3sc (9)
rnd 9: 2dec,2sc,inc,2sc (8)
rnd 10-14: 8sc (8)
rnd 15-19: dec,2sc,inc,3sc (8)
rnd 20: dec,2sc,inc,sc,dec (7)
sl st and fo, leave end for sewing.
the tail should naturally curve.
collar
ch 18, join in a circle while it is around the neck of the cat and fo.
assembly
sew ears between rnd 3-4 at the sides of the head. use pins to clearly see where they're going to be sewn and to be sure they are symmetrical.
sew the tail to the body as shown in pics. 9-10.
embroidery
your cat is ready! congratulations!
