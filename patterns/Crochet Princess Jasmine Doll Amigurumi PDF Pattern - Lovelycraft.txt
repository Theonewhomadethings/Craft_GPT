hello my dears. today i will share princess jasmine amigurumi pattern. i think you all know princess jasmine. i hope i'm not wrong. an easy to make amigurumi. however, her hair is designed in some detail. in addition, the construction is explained in a video. if you wish, you can learn more about how it is done by watching the video. he chose a color that is very close to the original color, i think it looks very cute. now let's crochet princess jasmine amigurumi.
pdf crochet princess jasmine doll amigurumi free pattern
designer: aliskotoys
materials
markers
scissors
filler
eyes
yarn
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
hands: (2pcs)
1) 6sc in mr
2 12) 6sc (11rows)
we crochet for both sides together 2sc, ch, cut the yarn leaving a long end for sewing.
hair:
1) 6sc in mr
2) 6inc (12)
3) (1sc, inc) repeat 6 times (18)
4) (2sc, inc) repeat 6 times (24)
5) (3sc, inc) repeat 6 times (30)
6) (4sc, inc) repeat 6 times (36)
7 10) 36sc (4rows)
first curl- 85ch, second loop from the hook 84sc, sl-st in the loop of the base, from which began to crochet ch
second curl- 85ch, second loop from the hook 84sc, sl-st in the loop of the base, from which began to crochet ch
third curl- 85ch, second loop from the hook 84sc, sl-st in the next loop (see video for more details)
fourth curl-85ch, second loop from the hook 84sc, sl-st into the loop of the base, from which began to crochet ch (see more details in the video), slst, cut the yarn leaving a rather long tip of the yarn, for sewing on a wig and curls.
legs: (2pcs)
shoe color:
1) 6sc in mr
2) 6inc (12)
3) 12sc
4) 3sc, 3dec, 3sc (9)
change yarn to body color
5) blo 9sc
6) 9sc
change the yarn to the color of the panties
7) 9inc
8 10) 18sc (3rows)
11) (1sc, dec) repeat 6 times (12)
12) (1sc, inc)repeat 6 times (18)
13 16) 18sc (4rows)
on the first leg we make slst, cut the yarn.
on the second leg we crochet another 6 sc, we do not cut the yarn.
we stuff all the details as we crochet.
body:
1) from the leg we crochet 2ch, we connect with 1 leg. 18sc on the first leg, 2sc on ch, 18sc on the second leg (40) put a marker
2 3) 40sc (2rows)
4) we make one decrease from each side (total two decreases in a circle)(38)
5) we make one decrease in the back and front (two decreases in the circle) (36)
6) (4sc, dec) repeat 6 times (30)
change yarn to body color
7) blo+ 30sc
8) (3sc, dec) repeat 6 times (24)
9 11) 24sc (3rows)
change the yarn to the color of the clothes
12 15) 24sc (4sc)
change the yarn to the color of the body. we do not cut the yarn from the clothes, we throw it forward (watch the video)
16) blo (2sc, dec) repeat 6 times (18)
17) (1sc, dec) repeat 6 times (12)
18) 12sc
head:
crocheted from the body, with the same yarn
19) 12inc (24)
20) (2sc, inc) repeat 8 times (32)
21) (7sc, inc) repeat 4 times (36)
22 27) 36sc (6rows)
we insert eyes between 23 and 24 rows, at a distance of 8sc.
28) (4sc, dec) repeat 6 times (30)
29) (3sc, dec) repeat 6 times (24)
30) (2sc, dec) repeat 6 times (18)
31) (1sc, dec) repeat 6 times (12)
32) 6dec (6)
we tighten the hole.
see the video for further design of the doll.
