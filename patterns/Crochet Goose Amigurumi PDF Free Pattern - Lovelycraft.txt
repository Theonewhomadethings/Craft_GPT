easy crochet goose amigurumi pdf free pattern
designer: les.mailles.du.rocher
materials
cotton ricorumi (white, orange)
crochet hook : 3 mm yarn needle
fiberfill for stuffing security eyes (5 or 6 mm)
black embroidery thread
stitch marker
scissors
abbrevations
rnd : round
mr : magic ring
ch : chain
sl st : slip stitch
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
hdc : half double crochet
blo : back loops
flo : front loops
0* : repeat till the end of round
[ ] : total number of stitches in the round
head
in white
rnd 1: mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2 : (inc)* [12]
rnd 3 : (1 sc, inc)* [18]
rnd 4 :18 sc [18]
rnd 5 : (2 sc, inc)* [24]
rnd 6 : 24 sc [24]
rnd 7 : (3 sc, inc)* [30]
rnd 8 : (2 sc, inc, 2 sc)* [36]
rnd 9 : (5 sc, inc)* [42]
rnd 10 13 : 42 sc [42]
rnd 14 : (5 sc, dec)* [36]
rnd 15 : (2 sc, dec, 2 sc)* [30]
rnd 16 : (3 sc, dec)* [24]
insert the security eyes btw rnds 10 & 11 and stuff.
rnd 17 : (2 sc, dec)* [18]
rnd 18 24 : 18 sc [18]
rnd 25 : (2 sc, inc)* [24]
rnd 26 : (3 sc, inc)* [30]
rnd 27 : (2 sc, inc, 2 sc)* [36]
rnd 28 : ch 4 (first sc in 2nd ch from hook, mark this stitch as the beginning of the round), crochet 3 sc along the chain, 36 sc around the body, 3 sc along the chain [42]
rnd 29 31 : 42 sc [42]
rnd 32 : (5 sc, dec)* [36]
rnd 33 : (2 sc, dec, 2 sc)* [30]
rnd 34 : (3 sc, dec)* [24]
rnd 35 : (2 sc, dec)* [18]
rnd 36 : (1 sc, dec)* [12]
rnd 37 : (dec)* [6]
fasten off and close.
beak
in orange
rnd 1 : mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2 : (2 sc, inc)* [8]
rnd 3 : (3 sc, inc)* [10]
rnd 4 : (4 sc, inc)* [12]
rnd 5 : (5 sc, inc)* [14]
rnd 6 :14 sc [14]
fasten off and leave a tail for sewing.
wings
in orange
rnd 1: mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2 : (inc)* [12]
rnd 3 : (1 sc, inc)* [18]
fold in half and crochet 9 sc through both sides. fasten off and leave a tail for sewing.
legs
in orange
rnd 1: mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2 : (1 sc, inc)* [6]
rnd 3 : (1 sc, inc)* [9]
rnd 4 5 : 9 sc [9]
flatten the foot and crochet 4 sc through both sides.
