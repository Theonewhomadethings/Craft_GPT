crochet plush penguin free pdf amigurumi pattern
designer: julia__musatova
materials
yarn dolphin baby 80321,1,5 skeins
yarn dolphin baby 80301 (tummy)
yarn dolphin baby 80317 (beak paws)
yarn gazzal baby cotton3443 (scarf)
crochet hook 3 mm
button eyes 15m
fiberfill
the finished toy size is 20 cm.
working time-2 days
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch: chain
lp: loop
body
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12sc)
rnd . (1sc, inc)*6 (18sc)
rnd 4. (2sc, inc)*6 (24sc)
rnd 5. (3sc, inc)*6 (30sc)
rnd 6. (4sc, inc)*6 (36sc)
rnd 7. (5sc, inc)*6 (42sc)
rnd 8. (6sc, inc)*6 (48sc)
rnd 9. (sc, inc)*6 (54sc)
rnd 10-16. 54sc (7 rnds)
rnd 17. (sc, dec)*6 (48sc)
rnd 18-19. 48sc (2 rnds)
rnd 20. (15sc, inc)*3 (51sc)
rnd 21-23. 51sc (3 rnds)
rnd 24. (16sc, inc)*3 (54sc)
rnd 25-27. 54sc (3 rnds)
rnd 28. (17sc, inc)*3 (57sc)
rnd 29-30. 57sc (3 rnds)
rnd 31. (18sc, inc)*3 (60sc)
rnd 32-36. 60sc (5 rnds)
rnd 37. (13sc, dec)*4 (56sc)
rnd 38. (6sc, dec)*7 (49sc)
rnd 39. (5sc, dec)*7 (42sc)
rnd 40. (4sc, dec)*7 (35sc)
rnd 41. (3sc, dec)*7 (28sc)
rnd 42. (2sc, dec)*7 (21sc)
rnd 43. (1sc, dec)*7 (14sc)
rnd 44. (dec)*7 (7sc)
after the 40th row we stuff.
cut the thread, leave 20 cm.
loops tighten the thread, fix, cut.
tummy
we crochet with white yarn in rotary rows.
rnd 1. 12 ch (+2ch lifting)
rnd 2. 12 sc
rnd 3. inc, 10sc, inc (14sc)
rnd 4. 14 sc
rnd 5. inc, 12sc, inc (16sc)
rnd 6-12. 16 sc (1rnds)
rnd 13. dec, 12sc, dec (14sc)
rnd 14-16. 14 sc (3rnds)
rnd 17. dec, 10sc, dec (12sc)
rnd 18. 12 sc
rnd 19. inc, 10sc, inc (14sc)
rnd 20-22. 14 sc (3rnds)
rnd 23. dec, 10sc, dec (12sc)
rnd 24. dec, 8sc, dec (10sc)
next, crochet 2 semicircles.
rnd 25. 1sc, 1hdc, 1dc, 1hdc, sl st, sl st, 1 hdc, 1dc, 1 hdc, 1sc (10 loops)
next, we tie the part in a circle sc.
sew the white part to the blue thin yarn.
wings
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*6 (12sc)
rnd 3. 12 sc
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)*3 (15sc)
rnd 5-6. 15 sc (2rnds)
rnd 7. (4sc,inc)*3 (18sc)
rnd 8-9. 18 sc (2rnds)
rnd 10. (5sc,inc)*3 (21sc)
rnd 11-12. 21 sc (2rnds)
rnd 13. (6sc,inc)*3 (24sc)
rnd 14-15. 24 sc (2rnds)
rnd 16. (2sc,dec)*6 (18sc)
fold the crocheting in half and connect (close) the wing.
sew the wings to the body one row below the neck.
tail
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. (1sc, inc)*3 (9sc)
rnd . (2sc, inc)*3 (12sc)
rnd 4. (3sc, inc)*3 (15sc)
rnd 5. (4sc, inc)*3 (18sc)
rnd 6. (5sc, inc)*3 (21sc)
rnd 7. (6sc, inc)*3 (24sc)
rnd 8. 24sc
paws
rnd 1. 7 sc in mr
rnd 2. (inc)*7 (14sc)
rnd -6. 14sc (4 rnds)
rnd 7. (sc, dec)*2 (12sc)
rnd 8. (4sc, dec)*2 (10sc)
rnd 9. (3sc, dec)*2 (8sc)
fold the crocheting in half and connect (close).
beak
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr
rnd 2. (1sc,inc)*2 (6sc)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)*3 (9sc)
rnd 4. 9 sc
stuff a little
scarf
cast on 10 stitches on needles 3 and crochet about 40cm in stockinette stitch.
assembly
sew the wings of their tail to the body.
sew on the pictula.
sew beak eyes.
we embroider eyelashes.
tie a scarf.
