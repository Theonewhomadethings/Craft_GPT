materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm-2,5 mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5 mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
empecemos a tejer el patron de muneco amigurumi.
head
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3 sc, inc)*5, 2 sc (30)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7. 2 sc, inc, (5 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (42)
rnd 8-12. (5 rnds) 42 sc (42)
rnd 13. (6 sc, inc)*6 (48)
rnd 14-17. (4 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 18. (4 sc, dec)*8 (40)
rnd 19. (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 20. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
rnd 21. (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 22. (2 sc, dec)*3, 4 sc (13)
rnd 23-28. (6 rnds) blo 13 sc (13)
rnd 29. 6 dec, sc (7)
insert the safety eyes between round 13, 14 and 8 sts apart
cut the yarn. leave a long tail for sewing.
legs x 2
start with black
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (2 sc, inc)*2 (8)
rnd 3-5. (3 rnds) 8 sc (8)
change to white. rounds 6, 8, 10 are in white. rounds 7, 9 are in black
rnd 6-10. (5 rnds) 8 sc
rnd 11. 6 sc end round here
cut the yarn.
crochet leg 2 in the same way till round 10. crochet 11 sc more to reach the crochet.
do not cut the yarn, chain 2, join to leg 1 and keep going on to the body with white yarn.
body
rnd 1-4. (4 rnds) 20 sc (20)
change to skin color
rnd 5. blo 20 sc (20)
rnd 6-8. (3 rnds) 20 sc (20)
rnd 9. (2 sc, dec)*5 (15)
rnd 10. 15 sc (15)
rnd 11. (sc, dec)*5 (10)
rnd 12. (3 sc, dec)*2 (8)
rnd 13-18. (6 rnds) 8 sc (8)
rnd 19. 4 dec (4)
cut the yarn, leave a long tail for sewing.
arm x 2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-8. (7 rnds) 6 sc (6)
do not stuff the arms. cut the yarn and fasten off.
ear x 2
rnd 1 4 sc in mr (4)
cut the yarn. leave a long tail for sewing.
skirt (under dress)
(cotton fiber, hook size 2,00 mm)
insert the hook to blo round on the body, chain 2
rnd 1. 20 dc, inc (40), slst
rnd 2. 40 hdc (40), slst
rnd 3. (chain 4, slst in the next stitch)*39
congratulations, you have completed the little doll amigurumi.
