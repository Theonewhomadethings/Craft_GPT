materials and tools
yarn art jeans 160m/ 50gr
71 for a bear (main)
03 milk for the muzzle
hook 2mm (b)
safety eyes d:6mm
filler, scissors, pins.
thin thread for the nose of dark brown color
the size of the finished toy, taking into account the specified materials, is 3,54 inches(9cm)
abbreviations
mr magic ring
ch chain
sc single crochet
inc increase (two sc in one stitch)
dec decrease (two stitches crocheted together)
we start from the head and body of the amigurumi mini teddy bear pattern with crochet.
head and body
crochet with the main color.
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6-10. 30sc (5rnds)
insert the eyes between 8 and 9 rows, at a distance of 3-4 stitch.
rnd 11. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 12. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 13. (sc,dec)*6 (12)
start stuffing
rnd 14-15. 12sc (2rnds)
rnd 16. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 17-18. 18sc (2rnds)
rnd 19. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 20-23. 24sc (4rnds)
rnd 24. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 25. (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 26. 6dec
pull the hole by the front half loops, fasten the thread and cut it off, hide the end inside the
part.
after completing the head and body of the amigurumi teddy bear pattern with crochet, let's make the ears.
ears (2 pcs)
crochet with the main color.
rnd 1. 6sc in mr
fasten the thread and cut it off, leaving the end for sewing. sew the ears between 4 and 6 rows.
after crocheting the ears of the amigurumi teddy bear pattern step by step, we can move on to knitting the arms and legs, friends.
arms and legs
crochet with the main color. we do not fill in the deteils.
rnd 1. 6sc in mr
rnd 2. (2sc,inc)*2 (8)
rnd 3-7. 8sc (5rnds)
fold the part in half and crocheted it for both sides 4sc. fasten the thread and cut it off, leaving
the end for sewing. sew the arms between the 16 and 17 rows.
rnd 1. 6sc in mr
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3-4. 12sc (2rnds)
rnd 5. 3sc, dec, 3sc (9)
rnd 6. 4sc, dec, 3sc (8)
rnd 7-8. 8sc (2rnds)
fold the part in half and crocheted it for both sides 4sc. fasten the thread and cut it off,
leaving the end for sewing. sew the legs between the 23 and 24 rows.
