materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2mm- 2.5 mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5 mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
leg*2
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8- inc (16)
rnd 3-6. (4 rnds) 16 sc (16)
cut the yarn, make the 2 nd leg in the same way. do not cut the yarn. make 4 chains. join in 1 st leg and continue to the body.
body
rnd 1. (4 sc, inc)*8 (48)
rnd 2-8. (7 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 9. (10 sc, dec)*4 (44)
rnd 10-13. (4 rnds) 44 sc (44)
rnd 14. (9 sc, dec)*4 (40)
rnd 15-17. (3 rnds) 40 sc (40)
rnd 18. (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 19-20. (2 rnds) 32 sc (32)
rnd 21. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
don't cut the yarn and continue to the body
head
rnd 1. flo 24- inc (48)
rnd 2-3. (2 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 4. (5 sc, inc)*8 (56)
rnd 5-10. (6 rnds) 56 sc (56)
insert the eyes between round 8-9, 8 sts apart.
rnd 11. (5 sc, dec)*8 (48)
rnd 12-15. (4 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 16. (4 sc, dec)*8 (40)
rnd 17. 40 sc (40)
rnd 18. (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 19. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
rnd 20. (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 21. 8- dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end
ear*2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3-11. (9 rnds) 9 sc (9)
rnd 12. (sc, dec)*3 (6)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
arm*2
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6- inc (12)
rnd 3-15. (13 rnds) 12 sc (12)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
scarf
cast on 5 stitch
rnd 1. 5k
rnd 2. 5p
until you get 18-20 cm length ot the scarf
snow bunny is ready.
