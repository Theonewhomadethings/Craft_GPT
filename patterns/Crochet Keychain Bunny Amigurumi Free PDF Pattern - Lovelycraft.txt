baby bunny amigurumi who loves carrots, you can make this amigurumi as a keychain. the image of the toy is very cute, it is knitted wrapped in a carrot. the ears of the toy are slightly large and rounded. his eyes and red nose are knitted flush. the color of the crochet bunny is white, which made this toy look cuter. the color of the carrot is orange and the leaves of the carrot are knitted green, making the toy more cute.
pdf crochet keychain bunny amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: anan.amilove
ravelry: anan.amilove
material
yarn: jeans art 03, 23, 69.
hook: size 2.5mm
two 6mm safety eyes
abbreviations:
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
sl st: slip stitch
ch: chain
head
rnd1: 6 sc in the magic (6)
rnd2: 6 inc (12)
rnd3: (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd4: (inc, 2 sc)*6 (24)
rnd5: (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd6: 1 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (36)
rnd7: 36 sc (36)
rnd8: (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd9-13: 42 sc (42)
rnd14: (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd15: 2 sc, dec, (4 sc, dec)*5, 2 sc (30)
rnd16: (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd17: 1 sc, dec, (2 sc, dec)*5, 1sc (18)
rnd18: (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
fasten off the yarn. stuff the head.
put in the eyes between rows 11-12, leaving 6 stitch space between them.
ear
rnd1: 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd2: (1sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd3: (inc, 2 sc)*3 (12)
rnd4: (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd5: 1sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*2, 3 sc (18)
rnd6-9: 18 sc (18)
rnd10: (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd11: 12 sc (12)
rnd12: (2 sc, dec)*3 (9)
fasten off the yarn, leaving a tail for sewing. do not stuff.
fold the top of the ear, sew the 2 opposed stitches.
body
rnd1: 6 sc in the magic (6)
rnd2: 6 inc (12)
rnd3: (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd4: (inc, 2 sc)*6 (24)
rnd5: (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd6: 1 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (36)
rnd7-9: 36 sc (36)
rnd10: (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd11: 30 sc (30)
rnd12: (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd13: (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
fasten off the yarn, leaving a long tail for sewing.
stuff the body.
arm
rnd1: 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd2: (1 sc, inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3-7: 9 sc (9)
fasten off the yarn, leaving a tail for sewing. do not stuff.
fold the top of the arm, sew the 2 opposed stitches.
leg
rnd1: 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd2: 6 inc (12)
rnd3: (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd4-5: 15 sc (15)
rnd6: 4 sc, 3 dec, 5 sc (12)
rnd7: 3 sc, 3 dec, 3 sc (9)
fasten off the yarn, leaving a tail for sewing.
stuff the leg.
fold the top of the leg, sew the 2 opposed stitches.
carrot
rnd1: 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd2: 6 inc (12)
rnd3: 12 sc (12)
rnd4: (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd5: 15 sc (15)
rnd6: (4 sc, inc)*3 (18)
rnd7: 18 sc (18)
rnd8: (5 sc, inc)*3 (21)
rnd9-11: 21 sc (21)
rnd12: (5 sc, dec)*3 (18)
rnd13: (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 14: 6 dec (6)
fasten off the yarn.
stuff the carrot.
leaves
(make 9 ch, start in the second ch from the hook : 8 sl st )*3
tail
rnd1: 6 sc in the magic ring (6)
rnd2: 6 inc (12)
rnd3: (3 sc, inc)*3 (15)
rnd4: (3 sc, dec)*3 (12)
fasten off the yarn, leaving a tail for sewing.
stuff the tail.
