required materials and tools
(when crocheting the specified yarn, the height of the finished toy is 32 cm)
himalaya dolphin baby yarn in black and white;
yarnart jeans in black and red;
hook 3.5 for plush yarn and 1.7 for cotton;
eyes 13 mm;
white felt;
nako paris white for a chupchik;
synthetic fluff or holofiber;
scissors, marker, glue (for eyes)
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
hands: (2 parts)
1) 6 sc in mr (6)
2) 6 inc (12)
3) (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
4) (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
5-6) (24)
7) (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
8) (4 sc, dec)*3 (15)
9-10) (15)
11) (3 sc, dec)*3 (12)
12-16) 5 rows (12)
17) 10 sc, fold foot in half and work 6 sc behind both sides.
ears: (2 parts)
1) 6 sc in mr
2) 6 inc (12)
3) (3 sc, inc)*3 times (15)
4) 15 sc
5) (1sc, dec)*6 (9)
fold and crochet 4 sc on both sides, fold and sew again.
eyes: (2 parts)
we crochet in black.
1) 6 sc in mr
2) 6 inc (12)
3) (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
4) (2 sc, inc)*6 (24)
5) (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
6) (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
nose
we crochet in red.
1) 4 ch, starting from the second loop from the hook 2 sc, 3 sc in one loop, 1 sc, inc (8)
2) inc, 1 sc, 3 inc, 1 sc, 2 inc (14)
3) 1 sc, inc, 1 sc, (1 sc, inc)*3, 1 sc (1 sc, inc)*2 (20)
4) without changes (20)
5) 1 sc, dec, 1 sc (1 sc, dec)*3, 1 sc (1 sc, dec)*2 (14)
tail
1) 6 in mr (6)
2) 6 inc (12)
3) (12)
4) 6 dec (6)
forelock
dial on a chain of the required number of air loops and tie it in a circle 3-4 times. make a fleece and paint with a light green colored pencil.
legs: (2 parts)
we start crocheting in black.
6 ch. from the 2nd loop from the hook we crochet:
1) 4 sc, 3 sc in 1 loop, 3 sc, inc (12)
2) inc, 3 sc, 3 inc, 3 sc, 2 inc (18)
3) 1 sc, inc, 3 sc (1 sc, inc)*3, 3 sc (1 sc, inc)*2 (24)
4) 2 sc, inc, 3 sc (2 sc, inc)*3, 3 sc (2 sc, inc)*2 (30)
5-7) (30)
8) 6 sc (1 sc, dec)*6, 6 sc (24)
9) 6 sc, 6 dec, 6 sc (18)
10-12) (18)
13) 15 sc, cut the yarn.
in the second leg, do not tie 3 loops in row 13. change the color of the yarn to white and then crochet the body.
14) 3 ch and connect the legs making increments at the joints, on the sides and in front of 3 increments in a row (tummy) (51)
15) 41 sc, inc, 3 sc, inc, 4 sc, inc (54)
16-19) 4 rows (54)
20) (7sc, dec)*6 (48)
21) (48)
22) (6 sc, dec)*6 (42)
23) (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
24) (36)
25) (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
26-27) (30)
28) 3 sc, dec, 2 sc, change the color of the yarn to black, 1 sc, dec, (3 sc, dec)*4 (24)
29) 7 sc, put a marker, now from here will be the beginning of the row.
next 20 sc, take the hand and crochet 5 sc with the hand, mark the beginning of the row, 8 sc, 5 sc with the hand, 11 sc, change the color of the yarn to white (24) continue crocheting the head.
head
1) inc in each loop (48)
next, we crochet 3 rows for the front half loops.
2) (7 sc, inc)*6 (54)

21) decreases before closing, pull off the hole, hide the yarn.
