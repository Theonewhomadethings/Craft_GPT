we start from the head part of our kitty unicorn mermaid amigurumi pattern with crochet.
head
start with white color
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6inc (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: (2sc,inc)*8 (32)
rnd 6: (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 7: (4sc,inc)*8 (48)
rnd 8: 48sc (48)
rnd 9: (5sc,inc)*8 (56)
rnd 10-11: 56sc (56)
rnd 12: (6sc,inc)*8 (64)
rnd 13-20: 64sc (64)
rnd 21: (6sc,dec)*8 (56)
rnd 22: (5sc,dec)*8 (48)
rnd 23: (4sc,dec)*8 (40)
rnd 24: (3sc,dec)*8 (32)
rnd 25: (2sc,dec)*8 (24)
rnd 26: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing.
after crocheting the head part, we move on to the construction of the ears.
ear (make 2)
start with white color
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 3: sc,inc,sc change to pink color make sc,inc,sc change to white color make sc,inc,sc (12)
rnd 4: 4sc, change to pink color make 4sc, change to white color make 4sc (12)
rnd 5: 3sc, inc, change to pink color make 3sc, inc, change to white color make 3sc,inc (15)
rnd 6: 2sc, inc, 2sc, change to pink color make 2sc, inc, 2sc, change to white color make 2sc, inc, 2sc. (18)
rnd 7: 6sc, change to pink color make 6sc, change to white color make 6sc. (18)
fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing.
once we have completed the ears of the kitty unicorn mermaid pattern, we can start crocheting her body.
body
using yellow color
rnd 1: 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2: 8inc (16)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5: 32sc (32)
rnd 6: (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
change to pink color
rnd 7: 40sc (40)
rnd 8: blo 40sc (40)
rnd 9-10: 40sc (40)
change to blue color
rnd 11: (4sc,inc)*8 (48)
rnd 12: blo 48sc (48)
rnd 13-14: 48sc (48)
change to violet color
rnd 15: (4sc,dec)*8 (40)
rnd 16: blo 40sc (40)
rnd 17: 40sc (40)
change to white color
rnd 18: (3sc,dec)*8 (32)
rnd 19-21: 32sc (32)
rnd 22: (2sc,dec)*8 (24)
rnd 23-24: 24sc (24)
rnd 25: (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 26: 18sc (18)
fasten off.
after crocheting her body, let's embroider her arms and join them with her body.
continue work on flo
rnd 8: (slst,1ch)*40. fasten off.
rnd 12: (slst,1ch)*48. fasten off.
rnd 16: (slst,1ch)*40. fasten off.
arm (make 2)
using white color
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-4: 6inc (12)
rnd 5-10: (sc,dec)*4 (8)
fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing.
after completing the arms of the kitty unicorn mermaid amigurumi pattern, let's crochet the bow bra step.
bow bra
using violet color
make 4ch, start at the 4th chain from hook, 3dc, 3ch, slst at the same first chain, 4ch, 3dc, 3ch, slst. fasten off.
gather bow by wrapping some same color yarn around the center tightly to cinch together, fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing.
after crocheting the bow bra, let's move on to the horn part and the pattern is gradually completed.
horn
using blue color
rnd 1: make 2ch, start at the 2rd ch from hook, 4sc (4)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*2 (6)
rnd 3: 6sc (6)
change to pink color
rnd 4: (sc,inc)*3 (9)
rnd 5-6: 9sc (9)
change to yellow color
rnd 7-9: 9sc (9)
fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing.
after completing the horn step with crochet, let's crochet the tail part and complete our pattern.
tail (make 2)
using yellow color
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6sc (6)
rnd 3: 6inc (12)
rnd 4: 12sc (12)
rnd 5: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 6-7: 18sc (18)
rnd 8: (4sc,dec)*3 (15)
rnd 9: (3sc,dec)*3 (12)
rnd 10: 12sc (12)
rnd 11: (2sc,dec)*3 (9)
rnd 12: (sc,dec)*3 (6)
fasten off and leave a long tail for sewing.
