legs and body
start with beige color.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. (inc)* [12]
rnd 3-19. 12 sc [12]
in rnd 20, take the white yarn of the panties and continue with this color.
rnd 20. 12 sc [12]
stuff firmly.
fasten off the first leg.
crochet the second leg in the same way, but after rnd 20 don't cut the yarn. keep the stitch marker in the same place.
rnd 21. continue 6 sc on the second leg (if the color change is in front of you after those 6 sc, you can add some stitches and move the stitch marker of same number of stitches, so that the color change is at the back).
make a ch 6 and join to the first leg with 1 sc (choose the 6th stitch of the leg so that the color change is at the back), 11 sc on remaining stitches of the first leg, 6 sc (in ch 6), 6 sc on the second leg [36]
the beginning of the round is on the right side of the body.
rnd 22. 6 sc, 6 sc on the other side of the ch, 5 sc, inc, 17 sc, inc [38]
rnd 23. 6 sc, (1sc,inc)*3, 26 sc [41]
rnd 24. 41 sc [41]
rnd 25. 6 sc, (inc,2sc)*3, 26 sc [44]
rnd 26. 44 sc [44]
in rnd 27, take the beige yarn of the skin and continue with this color.
rnd 27-30. 44 sc [44]
in rnd 31, take the coral yarn of the dress and continue with this color.
rnd 31. (dec,20sc)* [42]
rnd 32. blo- 42 sc [42]
rnd 33. 10 sc, (dec,2sc)*2, 24 sc [40]
rnd 34. (dec,18sc)* [38]
rnd 35. 38 sc [38]
stuff firmly and continue to stuff as you go along with your work.
rnd 36. (dec,17sc)* [36]
rnd 37. (dec,4sc)* [30]
rnd 38. 30 sc [30]
rnd 39. (dec,3sc)* [24]
rnd 40. 24 sc [24]
in rnd 41, take the beige yarn of the skein and continue with this color.
rnd 41. blo- (dec,2sc)* [18]
rnd 42. 18 sc [18]
fasten off.
sew the head to the body.
in flo of rnd 31, legs on the top: take the yarn of the dress.
rnd 1. 42 sc [42]
rnd 2. (3sc,inc,3sc)* [48]
rnd 3. (7sc,inc)* [54]
rnd 4. (4sc,inc,4sc)* [60]
rnd 5. (9sc,inc)* [66]
rnd 6. (5sc,inc,5sc)* [72]
rnd 7. (11sc,inc)* [78]
rnd 8. (6sc,inc,6sc)* [84]
rnd 9. 84 sc [84]
rnd 10. (skip 1 sc, 5 dc in the same stitch, skip 1 sc, 1 sl st)* [84]
in flo of rnd 40, take the yarn of the dress and crochet: 24 sl st.
optional: with beige yarn, make a pompon for the tail.
arms (make 2)
start with beige color.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. (inc)* [12]
rnd 3-5. 12 sc [12]
rnd 6. (4sc,dec)* [10]
rnd 7-15. 10 sc [10]
in rnd 16, take the yarn of the dress and continue with this color.
rnd 16. 10 sc [10]
rnd 17. blo- 10 sc [10]
rnd 18-20. 10 sc [10]
fold and crochet 5 sc through both sides to close.
in flo of rnd 16, hand on the top: take the yarn of the dress and crochet 10 sc.
fasten off and leave a long thread for assembling.
sew the arms on the body btw rnds 41 and 42.
head
in beige.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. (inc)* [12]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [18]
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* [24]
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* [30]
rnd 6. (2sc,inc,2sc)* [36]
rnd 7. (5sc,inc)* [42]
rnd 8. 42 sc [42]
rnd 9. (3sc,inc,3sc)* [48]
rnd 10-15. 48 sc [48]
rnd 16. (7sc,inc)* [54]
rnd 17-21. 54 sc [54]

rnd 25. (3sc,dec)* [24]

fasten off.

in beige.

rnd 2. (inc)* [12]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [18]
rnd 4. 18 sc [18]
rnd 5. (5sc,inc)* [21]

in pink.

shoes (make 2)
in light green.
make a ch 6.
crochet around the ch. start on the 2nd stitch from hook.
rnd 1. inc, 3 sc, 4 sc in last stitch, 3 sc on the other side of the ch, inc [14]
rnd 2. 2 inc, 3 sc, 4 inc, 3 sc, 2 inc [22]
rnd 3. 2 inc, 8 sc, inc, 1 sc, inc, 8 sc, inc [27]
rnd 4. blo- 27 sc [27]
rnd 5. 9 sc, 2 dec, 1 sc, 2 dec, 9 sc [23]
rnd 6. 8 sc, 5 dec, 5 sc [18]
rnd 7-10. 18 sc [18]
rnd 11. 18 sl st [18]
fasten off and hide the thread.
take white yarn in flo of rnd 3 (opening of the shoe facing you) and crochet 27 sc.
apron
in white.
make a ch 13. crochet in rows back and forth.
row 1-12. 12 sc [12] ch 1, turn
row 13. 2 dec, 4 sc, 2 dec [8] ch 1, turn
row 14. 8 sc [8] ch 1, turn
row 15. dec, 4 sc, dec [6] ch 1, turn
row 16. 6 sc [6] ch 1, turn
row 17. dec, 2 sc, dec [4]
crochet sc around to make a border, making an increase in the corners and finish with 1 sl st in first stitch of row 17.
pocket
in white.
make a ch 9.
crochet in rows back and forth.
row 1-5. 8 sc [8] ch, turn.
sew the pocket to the apron.
straps and belt
for the straps: on each side of upper part of the apron, take the white yarn and make a ch 40.
the chains are tied around the neck.
for the belt: on each side in the middle of the apron, take the white yarn and make a ch 30. the chains are tied around the waist.

flowers
12 in yellow and 8 in white.
rnd 1. in a mr (ch 1, 2 sc, ch 2, 1 sl st)x3
fasten off and hide the thread.
leaves (make 5)
in green.
make a ch 4. crochet around the ch. start on the 2nd stitch from hook: 1 hdc, 3 sc in same stitch, 1 dc, 2 sc in same stitch, 1 sl st.
fasten off and hide the thread.
sew or glue the flowers and leaves on the soil.
