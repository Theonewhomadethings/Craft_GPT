materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2 mm- 2.5 mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
safety eyes size 3 mm and 5 mm
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
inc increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec decrease (invisible decrease)
blo back loop only
slst slip stitch
molang
body
start with white
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (2 sc, inc)*8 (32)
rnd 5. sc, inc, (3 sc, inc)*7, 2 sc (40)
rnd 6. (4 sc, inc)*8 (48)
rnd 7-8. (2 rnds) 48 sc (48)
rnd 9. (5 sc, inc)*8 (56)
rnd 10-13. (4 rnds) 56 sc (56)
insert the eyes at round 12, 13. 9 sc apart.
rnd 14. (6 sc, inc)*8 (64)
rnd 15. 64 sc (64)
rnd 16. (15 sc, inc)*4 (68)
rnd 17-26. (10 rnds) 68 sc (68)
rnd 27. (15 sc, dec)*4 (64)
rnd 28-29. (2 rnds) 64 sc (64)
rnd 30. (6 sc, dec)*8 (56)
rnd 31. 56 sc (56)
rnd 32. (5 sc, dec)*8 (48)
rnd 33. (4 sc, dec)*8 (40)
rnd 34. (3 sc, dec)*8 (32)
rnd 35. (2 sc, dec)*8 (24)
rnd 36. (sc, dec)*8 (16)
rnd 37. 8-dec (8)
cut the yarn and weave in the end
ear*2
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. (sc, inc)*2 (6)
rnd 3-4. 6 sc (6)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
leg*4
rnd 1. 6 sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-4. (3 rnds) 6 sc (6)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
piupiu
start with yellow yarn
rnd 1. 8 sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3.(sc, inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. 24 sc (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. 30 sc (30)
rnd 7. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 8-14. (7 rnds) 36 sc (36)
rnd 15. (4 sc, dec)*6 (30)
rnd 16. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 17. (2 sc, dec)*6 (18)
rnd 18. (sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 19. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end
wing*2
rnd 1. 4 sc in mr (4)
rnd 2. 4-inc (8)
rnd 3. (3 sc, inc)*2 (10)
rnd 4. 10 sc (10), slst
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing
molang and puipui are ready.
