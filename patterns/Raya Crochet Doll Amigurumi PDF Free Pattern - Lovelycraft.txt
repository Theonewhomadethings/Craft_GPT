raya doll amigurumi crochet free pattern
designer
instagram: e.m.i_creations
materials
50gram cotton 8ly yarn
(colours tan. mustard. green. red. black 4 brown)
2.5mm crochet hook
wm safety eyes
fibre fill stuffing
tapestry needle
stitch marker
fabric glue
dmc embroidery thread (colour black)
(soliloquy id="12576)
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
trc: triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc: double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst: slip stitch
ch: chain
..in: make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo: crochet into front loops only
blo: crochet into back loops only
legs x 2 (red 4 brown yarn)
starting with brown yarn.
rnd 1 mr 6sc (6)
rnd 2 6inc (12)
rnd 3 blo. (12)
rnd 4 12sc (12)
change to red yarn.
rnd 5 12sc slst (12)
fasten a weave in loose ends.
repeat steps for second leg. but do not fasten. you will join the two legs to start the body.
after crocheting the legs of the amigurumi doll, we crochet the body.
body (green, brown, tan and mustard yarn)
change yarn to green.
continuing from the second leg. chain 3. you will now connect the second leg with the first.
start crocheting in the first leg
rnd 6 12 in the first leg, 3sc in chain. 12sc in second leg. 3sc in chain (3o)
rnd 7-8 30sc (30)
change to brown yarn
rnd 9 30sc (30)
change to mustard yarn
rnd 10 13 30sc (30)
rnd 14 flo. (3sc. dec) x 6. slst into first stitch of round (24)
fasten weave in loose ends.
starting with the tan yarn. you will now crochet the neck. working in the back loops of round 13. connect yarn to one of the back loops and chain 1.
rnd 14 blo 30sc. slst in first stitch of round (30)
rnd 15 chain 1. (3sc. dec) x 6 (24)
fasten and leave a long tail for sewing to head. stuff the body.
tip: before filling the body. make sure the ends are together to tighten them loosely. stitches due to color changes.
we finish the part of the body of the amigurumi stripe pattern. now we crochet the head part.
head (tan yarn)
rnd 1 mr 6sc (6)
rnd 2 6inc (12)
rnd 3 (1sc. inc) x 6 (18)
rnd 4 (2sc. inc) x 6 (24)
rnd 5 (3sc. inc) x 6 (30)
rnd 6 (4sc. inc) x 6 (36)
rnd 7 (5sc. inc) x 6 (42)
rnd 8 14 42sc (42)
rnd 15 (5sc. dec) x 6 (36)
rnd 16 (4sc. dec) x 6 (30)
rnd 17 (3sc. dec) x 6 (24)
fasten and weave in loose ends.
insert eyes between rounds 11 and 12. 5 stitches apart. start stuffing the head.
embroider the eye lashes and brows to the head with black thread. embroider the nose with tan yarn a weave in any loose ends.
we crochet the arms of the amigurumi doll.
arms x 2 (tan and brown yarn)
starting with tan yarn.
rnd 1 mr 6sc (6)
rnd 2 (2sc. inc) x 2 (8)
change yarn to dark brown.
rnd 3 8sc (8)
change yarn to tan.
rnd 4 (2sc. dec) x 2 (6)
rnd 5 6sc (6)
fasten and leave a long tail for sewing.
do not stuff the arm s sew the opening of the arms closed. leave the remaining tail to sew to body.
we crochet the ears and the cape of the amigurumi stripe pattern. red yarn is preferred for the cape.
cape (red yarn)
you will now crochet in rows and not in rounds for the cape.
chain 23. start crocheting from the 2nd chain from hook.
row 1 9 22sc chain 1 and turn (22)
row 10 22sc and chain 12 (34)
fasten and leave a long tail at end of chain for sewing weave in other loose ends.
ears x 2 (tan yarn)
rnd 1 mr 4sc (4)
fasten and leave a long tail for sewing.
let's crochet the hair of the amigurumi doll.
hair (black yarn)
starting with a long strand. you will use this to sew two hair strands later.
rnd 1 mr 6sc (6)
rnd 2 6inc (12)
rnd 3 (1sc, inc) )( 6 (18)
rnd 4 (2sc, inc) x 6, slst (24)
start working on the strands (std) of hair
std 1 2 chain 20. crochet in 2nd chain from hook, 19slst, slst in next stitch (19)
std 3 chain 10. crochet in 2nd chain from hook 9slst. slst in next stitch (9)
std 4 23 chain 20. crochet in 2nd chain from hook. 19sc. slst in next stitch (19)
std 24 chain 10. crochet in 2nd chain from hook 9slst. slst in next stitch (9)
fasten a weave in loose ends except for the middle thread. you will be using this to sew two strands of hair.
we crochet the hat of the amigurumi pattern.
hat (mustard yarn)
rnd 1 mr 4sc (4)
rnd 2 (1sc, inc) x 2 (6)
rnd 3 (1sc, inc) x 3 (9)
rnd 4 9inc (18)
rnd 5 18sc (18)
rnd 6 (1sc. inc) x9 (27)
rnd 7 (2sc. inc) x9 (36)
rnd 8 (3sc. inc) x9 (45)
rnd 9 11 45sc (45)
rnd 12 flo 45inc (90)
fasten and weave in loose ends.
assembly
1 sew head to body.
2 sew arms to round 13 and weave in loose ends.
3 sew ears to head. between round 10 and 12.
4 wrap cape around the neck and sew in place.
5 glue hair to head (if you do not have glue. you can sew the hair down). strand 3 and 24 will be glued in front of the ears. lift every second strand of hair between strands 4 and 23 up. you will now glue the remaining strands down and use pins to secure hair until glue dries. repeat same process for the remaining strands between strand 4 and 23. strand 14 2 will be ripped to the top of the head. place one strand of hair over the other strand. glue down the hair and sew strands down.
6 place hat on head.
congratulations, you have completed the stripe amigurumi pattern.
