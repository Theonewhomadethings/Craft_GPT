crochet sea lion billy pdf amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: les.mailles.du.rocher
materials
cotton ricorumi: grey 079, light grey 004, white 001, black 060
crochet hook: 2,5 or 3 mm
security eyes: 5 or 6 mm
yarn needle
fiberfill for stuffing
black embroidery thread
stitch marker
scissors
abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
rnd round
ch chain
()* repeat till the end of round
[] total number of stitches in the round
head & body
stuff as you go along your work.
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. 6-inc [12]
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* [18]
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* [24]
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* [30]
rnd 6. (2sc,inc,2sc)* [36]
rnd 7. 36 sc [36]
rnd 8. (5sc,inc)* [42]
rnd 9-15. 42 sc [42]
rnd 16. ch 13 (first sc in 2nd ch from hook, mark this stitch as the beginning of the round),crochet 12 sc along the chain, 1 sc the stitch where the chain fondation starts, 42 sc,11sc and 1 inc along the chain [68]
rnd 17. 2inc, 64sc, 2inc [72]
rnd 18. 3inc, 66sc, 3inc [78]
rnd 19-20. 78 sc [78]
insert the security eyes between rnds 4 and 5.
rnd 21. 4sc, dec, 1sc, dec, 60sc, dec, 1sc, dec, 4sc [74]
rnd 22. 4sc, dec, 1sc, dec, 22sc, dec, 8sc, dec, 22sc, dec, 1sc, dec, 4sc [68]
rnd 23. 4sc, dec, 1sc, dec, 20sc, dec, 6sc, dec, 20sc, dec, 1sc, dec, 4sc [62]
rnd 24. 4sc, dec, 50sc, dec, 4sc [60]
rnd 25. (4sc,dec,4sc)* [54]
rnd 26. (7sc,dec)* [48]
rnd 27. (3sc,dec,3sc)* [42]
rnd 28. (5sc,dec)* [36]
rnd 29. 18 dec [18]
rnd 30. (1sc,dec)* [12]
rnd 31. 6 dec [6]
fasten off and close.
snout
make 2 circles:
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc [6]
rnd 2. 6-inc [12]
fasten off the first circle. leave a tail to hide the little hole after joining.
don't cut the second and join to the first with 1 sc (new beginning of the rnd).
rnd 3. on first circle, 1sc (join), 11sc, 12sc on the second circle [24]
rnd 4. 24 sc [24]
finish with 1 sl st. fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing.
embroider the nose with black thread. add some white lines.
sew the snout under the eyes, approx from rnd 6 to rnd 10.
teeth (make 2)
rnd 1. mr, 4 sc [4]
rnd 2. 4 sc [4]
rnd 3. 1inc, 4sc [5]
rnd 4-6. 5 sc [5]
leave a long tail and sew on the bottom of the snout.
without the teeth, it will look like a seal, choose the design you prefer.
front flippers (make 2)
rnd 1. mr, 5sc [5]
rnd 2. 5 sc [5]
rnd 3. 5 inc [10]
rnd 4. 10 sc [10]
rnd 5. (4sc,inc)* [12]
rnd 6-8. 12 sc [12]
rnd 9. (4sc,dec)* [10]
rnd 10. (3sc,dec)* [8]
don't stuff.
fold and close by crocheting 4 sc through both sides.
sew or glue to the body.
hind flippers (make 2)
rnd 1. mr, 5 sc [5]
rnd 2. 5 sc [5]
rnd 3. 5 inc [10]
rnd 4-7. 10 sc [10]
fold and close by crocheting 5 sc through both sides. fasten off.
bring out the thread at the center of mr and sew to the corners of the tail.
