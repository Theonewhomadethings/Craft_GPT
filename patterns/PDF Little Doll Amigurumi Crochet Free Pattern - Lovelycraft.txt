materials
yarn: sport or dk light weight yarn. (yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2mm- 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
safety eyes size 5mm
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
flo front loop only
slst slip stitch
head
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6-inc (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5: sc, inc, (3sc,inc)*5, 2sc (30)
rnd 6: (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 7: 2sc, inc, (5sc,inc)*5, 3sc (42)
rnd 8-12: (5rnds) 42sc (42)
rnd 13: (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 14-17: (4rnds) 48sc (48)
rnd 18: (4sc,dec)*8 (40)
rnd 19: (3sc,dec)*8 (32)
rnd 20: (2sc,dec)*8 (24)
rnd 21: (sc,dec)*8 (16)
rnd 22: (2sc,dec)*3, 4sc (13)
rnd 23-28: (6rnds) blo 13sc (13)
rnd 29: 6-dec, sc (7)
insert the eyes at round 13,14 8sc apart cut the yarn. fasten off. now put the neck you have formed inside the doll's head.
leg (make 2)
start with yellow
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: (2sc,inc)*2 (8)
rnd 3: 8sc (8)
change color
rnd 4-10: (7rnds) 8sc (8)
cut the yarn, crochet the 2nd leg in the same way till rnd 10. do not cut the yarn, make 2 chains, join in 1st leg. continue to the body.
body
rnd 1-7: (7rnds) 20sc (20)
rnd 8: (2sc,dec)*5 (15)
rnd 9: 15sc (15)
rnd 10: (sc,dec)*5 (10)
rnd 11: (3sc,dec)*2 (8)
rnd 12-16: (5rnds) 8sc (8)
rnd 13: 4-dec (4)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
arm (make 2)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2-8: (7rnds) 6sc (6)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
ear (make 2)
rnd 1: 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2: 6-inc (12)
rnd 3: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4: (chain 7, 6sc on chain)*6, chain 20, 19sc on chain, (chain 20, 19-hdc on chain)*16, chain 20, 19sc on chain.
cut the yarn and weave in the end. wear the hair on the head. use hot glue to stick them together.
dress
chain 12, insert your hook to the first chain on make a circle. start to crochet the dress from the collar.
rnd 1: 12sc (12)
rnd 2: (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 3: 3sc, inc, sc, inc, 6sc, inc, sc, inc, 3sc (22)
rnd 4: 3sc, 2-hdc, hdc-inc, 2-hdc, 6sc, 2-hdc, inc-hdc, 2-hdc, 3sc (24)
rnd 5: 3sc, chain 6 (skip 6sts), 6sc, chain 6 (skip 6sts), 3sc (24)
rnd 6-7: 24sc (24)
rnd 8: 24-dc-inc (48),slst
rnd 9: 48-dc (48), slst
rnd 10: (chain 3, slst in next stitch)*47
cut the yarn, fasten off.
bow tie
chain 3, 2-hdc into the first chain, chain 2, slst into first chain, chain 2, 2-dc into first chain, chain 2, slst into first chain. wrap the yarn over the middle of the bow tie make the shape.
