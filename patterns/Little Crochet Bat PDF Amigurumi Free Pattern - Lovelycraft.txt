little crochet bat amigurumi free pattern
designer
instagram: mes.mignonneries
abbreviations
rnd: round
mr: magic ring
ch: chain
sl st: slip stitch
sc: single crochet
dc: double crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
()*: repeat till the end of round
(): total number of stitches in the round
materials
cotton ricorumi: dark grey (080), pink (065)
crochet hook: 3 mm
6 mm safety eyes. you may need to use smaller/ larger eyes, depending on hook/ yarn size.
yarn needle
black embroidery thread
piece of thin cardboard
fiberfill for stuffing
scissors
size with the materials above will be about 9 cm.
head & body
stuff as you go along your work.
in dark grey
rnd 1. mr, 6sc (6)
rnd 2. (inc)* (12)
rnd 3. (1sc,inc)* (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 5. (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 7-9. 36 sc (36)
rnd 10. (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 11-14. 42 sc (42)
insert the security eyes between rnd 10 and 11.
embroider the cheeks in pink embroidery thread and nose with black one.
rnd 15. (5sc,dec)* (36)
rnd 16. (4sc,dec)* (30)
rnd 17. (3sc,dec)* (24)
rnd 18. (2sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 19. (2sc,inc)* (24)
rnd 20. 24 sc (24)
rnd 21. (3sc,inc)* (30)
rnd 22-24. 30 sc (30)
rnd 25. (4sc,inc)* (36)
rnd 26-29. 36 sc (36)
rnd 30. (5sc,inc)* (42)
rnd 31. 42 sc (42)
rnd 32. through the back loop only (5sc,dec)* (36)
finish to stuff.
for best stability. draw a circle about 4 cm in diameter on a piece of thin cardboard.
cut it out and slide it into the opening of rnd 32.
rnd 33. (2sc,dec)* (27)
rnd 34. (1sc,dec)* (18)
rnd 35. (dec)* (9)
close and fasten off.
ears (make 2)
in pink
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc (6)
rnd 2. (3sc,sc)* (12)
rnd 3. sc, (3sc in same stitch, 3sc)x 2, 3sc in same stitch, 2 sc (18)
in dark grey
rnd 4. 2 sc, (3 sc in same stitch, 5sc)x 2, 3 sc in same stitch, sc 3 (24)
rnd 5. 3 sc, (3 sc in same stitch, 7sc)x 2, 3 sc in same stitch, 4 sc (30)
sew the ears in side of the head, between rounds 5 and 12, leaving approx 5 stitches between them.
wings (make 2)
in dark grey
rnd 1. mr, 3 ch, 15 dc, slst into ch space (16)
rnd 2. 3 ch, dc in same ch space, (dc, dc-inc)x7, dc, slst in ch space (24)
rnd 3. 3 ch, dc in same ch space, (2 dc, dc-inc)x7, 2dc, slst in ch space (32)
rnd 4. 3 ch, dc in same ch space, (3 dc, dc-inc)x7, 3dc, slst in ch space (40)
rnd 5. 3 ch, dc in same ch space, 3ch, 2dc in the same space, (4 dc, dc-inc, 2 ch, dc-inc)x7, 4 dc, slst in ch space (80)
stitch together through the back loops only.
sew the wings to the back of the body.
