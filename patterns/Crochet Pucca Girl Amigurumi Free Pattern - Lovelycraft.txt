crochet pucca girl amigurumi free pattern
designer: beary_bearnita_
materials
yarn: sport or dk light
weight yarn. (i used yarnart jeans)
crochet hook: 2mm- 2.5mm
black embroidery thread
fiberfill
yarn needles
abbreviation
mr-magic ring
sc-single crochet
hdc-half double crochet
dc-double crochet
inc-increase (2 single crochet in 1 stitch)
dec-decrease (invisible decrease)
flo-front loop only
slst-slip stitch
leg*2
start with black
rnd 1. 5sc in mr (5)
rdn 2. 5-inc (10)
rnd 3-4. (2rnds) 10sc (10)
rnd 5. (4sc,inc)*2 (12)
rnd 6-10. (5rnds) 12sc (12)
cut the yarn, make the 2nd leg in the same way.
don't cut the yarn. make 3 chains. join in 1st leg and
continue to the body.
we start with the legs of the pucca doll, which is an anime character.
body
rnd 1. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 2-6. (5rnds) 36sc (36)
rnd 7. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 8-10. (3rnds) 30sc (30)
rnd 11. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 12. blo 24sc (24),slst
cut the yarn. leave long tail for sewing.
after embroidering the crocheted pucca doll's legs, let's move on to crochet her body.
skirt
insert the hook to the blo round of the body.
(rnd12). start to crochet the skirt in red.
rnd 1. (2sc,inc)*8 (32)
rnd 2-3. (2rnds) 32sc (32)
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)*8 (40)
rnd 5-10. (6rnds) 40sc (40)
slst. cut the yarn and weave in the end.
after crocheting her body, we are making the pucca doll's crochet wonderful dress.
head
start with skin color
rnd 1. 8sc in mr (8)
rnd 2. 8-inc (16)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*8 (24)
rnd 4. (3sc,inc)*6 (30)
rnd 5. (4sc,inc)*6 (36)
rnd 6. (5sc,inc)*6 (42)
rnd 7. (6sc,inc)*6 (48)
rnd 8. (7sc,inc)*6 (54)
rnd 9. (8sc,inc)*6 (60)
rnd 10-19. (10rnds) 60sc (60)
rnd 20. (8sc,dec)*6 (54)
rnd 21. (7sc,dec)*6 (48)
rnd 22. (6sc,dec)*6 (42)
rnd 23. (5sc,dec)*6 (36)
rnd 24. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
rnd 25. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
rnd 26. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 27. (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 28. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
we've finished her crochet dress, now it's time to crochet her head, and then we'll do her beautiful hair.
hair
start with black
rnd 1. 7sc in mr (7)
rnd 2. 7-inc (14)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*7 (21)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*7 (28)
rnd 5. sc,inc, (3sc,inc)*6,2sc (35)
rnd 6. (4sc,inc)*7 (42)
rnd 7. 2sc,inc, (5sc,inc)*6,3sc (49)
rnd 8. 49sc (49)
rnd 9. (6sc,inc)*7 (56)
rnd 10. 56sc (56)
rnd 11. 3sc,inc, (7sc,inc)*6,4sc (63)
rnd 12-21. (10rnds) 63sc (63)
rnd 22. 10sc, 43hdc, 10sc (63), slst
cut the yarn and weave in the end.
after crocheting her hair, we move on to making the buns of her hair.
hair bun*2
rnd 1. 6sc in mr (6)
rnd 2. 6-inc (12)
rnd 3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. (2sc,inc)*6 (24)
rnd 5-8. (4rnds) 24sc (24)
rnd 9. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
rnd 10. (sc,dec)*6 (12)
rnd 11. 6-dec (6)
cut the yarn, leave long tail for sewing.
amigurumi pucca doll is almost ready, now let's crochet the sleeves, then we will make the sleeves of the dress.
arm*2
start with skin color
rnd 1. 5sc in mr (5)
rnd 2-7. (6rnds) 5sc (5)
change to red
rnd 8-10. (3rnds) 5sc (5)
slst. cut the yarn.
sleeve*2 (in red)
insert your hook to the front loop on the last round
of the hand. (pics)
rnd 1. 2sc,inc,sc,inc (7)
rnd 2-3. (2rnds) 7sc (7)
rnd 4. 3sc,inc,2sc,inc (9)
rnd 5-8. (4rnds) 9sc (9)
cut the yarn, weave in the end.
wear the hair on head and sew to the body.
sew the hair balls to the head.
use red yarn to make hair tie.
embroidery eyebrows at round 10; eyes at round
15,4sc apart; mounth at round 18.
we have completed the sleeves of the crochet dress, the pucca amigurumi doll is completely ready. you have a great animated character.
