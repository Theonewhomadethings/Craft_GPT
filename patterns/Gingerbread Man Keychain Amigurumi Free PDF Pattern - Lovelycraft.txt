hello friends, today i will share the gingerbread man pattern. this pattern is pretty easy. the crochet gingerbread man pattern is designed to be used as a key chain. so it has pretty simple details. as you can imagine, an amigurumi used as a key chain also needs to be durable. therefore, while making amigurumi, it is necessary to design it as simply as possible. as you can see, this gingerbread man amigurumi is designed just the way i described it.
a few areas of the gingerbread man amigurumi have defining elements. first of all, the lines of her clothes were made clear by using blue thread. eyes and buttons are made using small beads. now let's crochet the gingerbread man amigurumi step by step.

materials
yarn
scissors
felt
paint
abbreviations
mr: amigurumi magic ring
sc : single crochet
inc : increase
dec : decrease
tr : triple crochet/ treble crochet
st: stitch
dc : double crochet
hdc: half double crochet
slst : slip stitch
ch : chain
..in : make increase into same stitch as many as the number which is given in front of "inc" abbreviation (exp; 3inc, 4inc..).
flo : crochet into front loops only
blo : crochet into back loops only
the toy is crocheted with a single piece.
we start crocheting from the legs.
leg 1
1. 5sc in mr (5).
2. 5 inc (10).
3-6. 10 sc (10).
we finish crocheting, cut the thread.
we crochet the second leg in the same way, but we do not cut the thread.
we connect the legs.
7. 10 sc on the first leg; 10 sc on the second leg (20).
8-11. 20 sc (20).
next, we will crochet arms.
i make a 1sc forward offset to keep the arm straight.
but! it is possible that you do not need to do it, it all depends on how you crochet (tilt, thread tension). the number of loops in a row does not change.
12. 5 sc; 3 ch; 3 sc on the other side three ch; 10 sc; ch; 3 sc on the other side three ch; 5 sc (32).
13. 7 sc; 2 inc; 14 sc; 2 inc; 7 sc (36).
14. 36 sc (36).
we stuff a toy
15. 7 sc; 2 dec; 14 sc; 2 dec; 7 sc (32).
head
making another loop forward displacement.
16. 2 sc; 12 skip loops; 4 sc; 12 skip loops; 2 sc (8).
17. 8 inc (16).
18. 1sc; 1 inc (24).
19-22. 24 sc (24).
23. 1sc; 1 dec (16).
stuff the head
24. 8 dec (8).
pull off the hole
we fill the arms and sew the holes. we embroider the pattern and the mouth on the gingerbread.
sew on or glue the eyes.
we hook the keychain fastening.
your gingerbread is ready!
