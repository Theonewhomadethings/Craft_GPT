yarn
scheepjes softfun

3 and 2,5 mm hook
9 mm safety eyes
polyester fiberfill for stuffing
tapestry needle
note
you can use any yarn of similar thickness, just make sure it's the same for all parts
work in continuous rounds, do not join or turn unless noted
start with an adjustable ring ("magic ring")
use stitch markers to indicate the beginning of each row
it's important you select the right hook; try to use another hook size if there are holes between stitches
abbreviations
ch: chain
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
dc: double crochet
slst: slip stitch
blo: back loops only
let's start making our amigurumi toy for halloween
let's crochet the body part of the free pattern
body
we will start with the legs.
round 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
round 2: 6 inc (12)
rounds 3-4: sc around (12)
fasten off. make the 2nd leg using the same pattern. do not fasten off, as we will use this yarn. we will now go on crocheting the body.
round 5: in this round, we will connect the legs in a circle, ending with 32 sts in total. with the 2nd leg on your hook 4 ch and connect with the 1st leg with a sl st. now sc around the 1 st leg (12 sc) and work 1 sc in each stitch along the chain. then 12 sc around the second leg and 4 sc along the chain again. count to make sure you have 32 sts in total.
round 6: (3 sc, 1 inc) x 8 (40)
rounds 7-8: sc around (40)
round 9: (4 sc, 1 inc) x 8 (48)
rounds 10-16: sc around (48)
round 17: (6 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (42)
round 18: sc around (42)
round 19: (5 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (42)
round 20: sc around (36)
round 21: (4 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (30)
round 22: (3 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (24)
stuff firmly. we will now go on crocheting the head.
round 23: 24 inc (48)
rounds 24-26: sc around (48)
round 27: (7 sc, 1 inc) x 6 (54)
rounds 28-32: sc around (54)
attach safety eyes approximately between rounds 30-31.
round 33: (7 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (48)
round 34: sc around (48)
round 35: (6 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (42)
round 36: sc around (42)
round 37: (5 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (36)
round 38: sc around (36)
round 39: (4 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (30)
round 40: (3 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (24)
round 41: (2 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (18)
round 42: (1 sc, 1 dec) x 6 (12)
round 43: 6 dec
fasten off, weave in ends.
you have completed the body part of the amigurumi mummy pattern. let's crochet the next process arms
arms
round 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
round 2: 6 inc (12)
round 3: (2 sc, 1 dec) x 3 (9)
rounds 4-10: sc around (9)
leave a tail for sewing.
let's crochet the black hair of the amigurumi pattern. in this section, you can change the hair color of the amigurumi pattern.
hair
with black yarn. take 10 cm piece of yarn, pull a loop, then take the ends of your yarn and pull through the loop and tighten. make a few more, until you've got the thickness you want. trim the length.
let's crochet the little mummy amigurumi pattern wrapping bandage
bandage
make a long chain, enough to wrap the toy around. then starting from the second chain from the hook double crochet in every stitch to the end. cut the yarn and hide the ends.
wrap the toy with the bandage. you can also sew it on with a few stitches.
let's crochet the little lantern, which is the most beautiful accessory of the little mummy amigurumi pattern.
lantern
we will start from the bottom with black yarn.
round 1: 8 sc in magic ring (8)
round 2: 8 inc (16)
round 3: (1 sc, 1 inc) x 8 (24)
round 4: sc around in blo (24)
round 5: (1 sc, 1 dec) x 8 (16)
change to yellow or golden yarn.
round 6: sc around in blo (16)
rounds 7-10: sc around (16)
fasten off. we will now make the top part.
round 1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
round 2: (2 sc, 1 inc) x 2 (8)
round 3: (1 sc, 1 inc) x 4 (12)
round 4: (2 sc, 1 inc) x 4 (16)
round 5: (1 sc, 1 inc) x 8 (24)
round 6: sc around (24)
fasten off, leaving a long tail. sew both parts together and attach a piece of thin aluminium wire for the handle.
congratulations. you have completed the little mummy amigurumi pattern. your best halloween amigurumi toy is ready.
