the character among us entered our lives as a video character brought to life by a game company and was welcomed by many young people, including children. the designer, on the other hand, created a cute crochet amigurumi toy inspired by the character of the video game.
behind our white amigurumi toy, there is a backpack and a beautiful carrot attached to it. you can paint your cheeks with blush as you wish. from a distance, it resembles a bunny character. i leave you with the pattern to complete this cute crochet amigurumi pattern. have fun.. stay tuned for more free crochet amigurumi patterns.
bunny among us amigurumi crochet fre pattern
designer
instagram: rin crochet
first leg
1. mr 6sc (6)
2. 6inc (12)
3. (sc,inc)*6 (18)
4-6. 18sc (18)
fasten off, cut the yarn.
crochet the 2nd leg and make ch 1.
7. 18+1+18+1 (38)
8-9. 38sc (38)
10. (17sc,dec)*2 (36)
11-13. 36sc (36)
14. (4sc,dec)*6 (30)
15. 30sc (30)
16. (3sc,dec)*6 (24)
17. 24sc (24)
18. (2sc,dec)*6 (18)
19. 18sc (18)
20. (sc,dec)*6 (12)
21. 6dec (6)
fasten off & weave in the end.
helmet- blue
make ch 5,
1. 2nd ch from hook 3sc, inc, 2sc, inc (9)
2. inc, 2sc, 2inc, 3sc, inc (13)
3. (black) inc, 4sc, 2inc, 5sc, inc (17)
fasten off & sew it between r13- 16
backpack
make ch 9,
1. 2nd ch from hook,
7sc, inc, 6sc, inc (17)
2. inc, 6sc, 2inc, 7sc, inc (21)
3-7. 21sc (21)
8. dec, 7sc, 2dec, 6sc, dec (17)
fasten off and sew it.
ear x2
1. mr 6sc (6)
2. 6inc (12)
3-5. 12sc (12)
6. (2sc,dec)*3 (9)
fasten off & sew it on the head.
and backpacker among us amigurumi character is ready!! ?
