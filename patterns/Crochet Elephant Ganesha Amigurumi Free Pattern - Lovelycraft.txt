crochet elephant ganesha amigurumi pattern
finished toy is around 30 cm height.
designer: @kediperiatolyesi
copyright owner: lovelycraft.com
materials
kartopu amigurumi / blue (color code k551)
kartopu amigurumi / red (color code k150)
kartopu amigurumi / white (color code k010)
kartopu amigurumi / yellow (color code k330)
kartopu amigurumi / purple (color code k1709)
kartopu amigurumi / pink (color code k787)
kartopu amigurumi / green (color code k1390)
10 mm safety eye (black)
for embroidery: black thread
fiberfill
pins
size 2.5mm or 3mm crochet hook
stitch marker
scissors
abbreviations
ch: chain
sc: single crochet
inc: increase
dec: decrease
slst: slip stitch
hdc: half double crochet
dc: double crochet
tr: triple crochet
st/sts: stitch/stitches
blo: back loops only
flo: fornt loops only
bobble: bobble stitch
( ): the parentheses at the end of the row give the total number of stitches of that round.
[ ]x.. : repeat instructions x times
legs (use blue color and make 2
rnd 1 start 6sc in a magic ring and join with blue slst. use your stitch marker. (6) please look through "techniques section" to learn about "magic ring" join slst. do not forget to use your stitch marker.
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3-7 sc in all 12 sts (5 rounds)
make heel part
rnd 8 ch 1, turn, sc in next 7st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (7)
rnd 9 ch 1, turn, sc in next 5st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (5)
rnd 10 ch 1, turn, sc in next 3st (skip 1 st and go in 3rd st) (3)
join foot part and crochet leg
rnd 11 ch 1, turn, sc in next 3st, (sideways) sc in next 2st, (in front) sc in next 5st, (sideways) sc in next 2st (12)
rnd 12-22 sc in all 12 sts (11 rounds)
body (use blue color)
continue crochet the body with blue yarn.
rnd 23 ch 6 and join to legs (36)
rnd 24 sc in all 36 sts
rnd 25 [sc in the next 5 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 26 sc in all 42 sts
rnd 27 [sc in the next 13 sts, inc] repeat 3 times (45)
rnd 28 sc in all 45 sts
rnd 29 [sc in the next 14 sts, inc] repeat 3 times (48)
rnd 30-31 sc in all 48 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 32 [sc in the next 14 sts, dec] repeat 3 times (45)
rnd 33 sc in all 45 sts
rnd 34 [sc in the next 13 sts, dec] repeat 3 times (42)
rnd 35 sc in all 42 sts
rnd 36 [sc in the next 5 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 37-38 sc in all 36 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 39 [sc in the next 4 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 40 sc in all 30 sts
rnd 41 [sc in the next 3 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 42-44 sc in all 24 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 45 [sc in the next 2 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 46 sc in all 18 sts
body is finished. fasten off and leave a long tail.
head (use blue color)
place the safety eyes between 17 and 18 with 9 stitches gap between them.
rnd 1 6sc in a magic ring and join with blue slst. use your stitch marker. (6)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4 [sc in the next 2 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5 [sc in the next 3 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 6 [sc in the next 4 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (36)
rnd 7 [sc in the next 5 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (42)
rnd 8 [sc in the next 6 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (48)
rnd 9-16 sc in all 48 sts (8 rounds)
rnd 17sc in the next 9 sts, (inc) repeat 3 times, sc in the next 21 sts, (inc) repeat 3 times, sc in the next 12 sts (54)
rnd 18 sc in the next 10st, inc, sc in the next 3st, inc, sc in the next 22st, inc, sc in the next 3st, inc, sc in the next 12st (58)
rnd 19-20 sc in all 58 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 21 sc in next 9st, (dec) repeat 4 times, sc in next 21st, (dec) repeat 4 times, sc in next 12 st (50)
rnd 22 sc in next 10st, dec, sc in next 23st, dec, sc in next 13st (48)
rnd 23 sc in all 48 sts
rnd 24 [sc in the next 4 sts, dec] repeat 8 times (40)
rnd 25 [sc in the next 3 sts, dec] repeat 8 times (32)
rnd 26[sc in the next 2 sts, dec] repeat 8 times (24)
rnd 27 [sc in the next 1 sts, dec] repeat 6 times (18)
shape the head
ear (use blue color yarn and make 2)
rnd 1 6sc in a magic ring and join with blue slst. use your stitch marker. (6)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3 [sc in the next st, inc] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4 [sc in the next 2 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5 [inc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 21 sts (27)
rnd 6 sc in the next st, [inc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 23 sts (30)
rnd 7 sc in the next 3 sts, [inc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 24 sts (33)
rnd 8 sc in the next 5 sts, [inc], sc in the next 27 sts (34)
rnd 9 sc in the next 6 sts, [inc], sc in the next 27 sts (35)
rnd 10 sc in the next 8 sts, [inc], sc in the next 15 sts, [dec], sc in the next 9 sts (35)
rnd 11 sc in the next 8 st, [inc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 15 sts, [dec], sc in the next 7 sts (37)
rnd 12 sc in all sts (37)
rnd 13 sc in the next 8 st, [dec] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 15 sts, [dec], sc in the next 6 sts (33)
rnd 14 sc in the next 8 st, [dec] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 19 sts (30)
rnd 15 sc in the next 7 st, [dec] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 11 sts, [dec], sc in the next 4 sts (26)
rnd 16 sc in the next 6 st, [dec] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 10 sts, [dec], sc in the next 4 sts (23)
rnd 17 sc in the next 4 st, [dec] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 7 sts, [dec] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2 sts (18)
rnd 18 sc in all sts (18)
trunk (use blue color yarn)
rnd 1 start 4sc in a magic ring and join with blue slst (4)
rnd 2 inc in all sts (8)
rnd 3-10 sc in all 8 sts (8 rounds)
rnd 11 sc in the next 7 sts, [inc] (9)
rnd 12 sc in all 9 sts
rnd 13 sc in the next 8 sts, [inc] (10)
rnd 14 sc in the next 9 sts, [inc] (11)
rnd 15 sc in all 11 sts
rnd 16 sc in the next 10 sts, [inc] (12)
rnd 17 sc in the next 2 st, [dec] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2 sts, [inc] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2 sts (12)
rnd 18-19 sc in all 12 sts (2 rounds)
rnd 20 sc in the next 2 st, [dec] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2 sts, [inc] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2 sts (12)
rnd 21-23 sc in all 12 sts (3 rounds)
rnd 24 [sc in the next sts, inc] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 25 sc in all 18 sts
rnd 26 [sc in the next 2 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 27 sc in all 24 sts
rnd 28 sc in the next 11 sts, [hdcinc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 10 sts (27)
please look through the "techniques section" to learn about hdc stitches.
arms (use blue color yarn and make 4)
rnd 1 start 6sc in a magic ring and join with blue slst
rnd 2 inc in all sts (12)
rnd 3-7 sc in all 12 sts (5 rounds)
rnd 8 [dec], sc in the next 4sts, [dec], sc in the next 4sts (10)
rnd 9-12 sc in all 10 sts (4 rounds)
rnd 13 [dec] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2sts, [inc] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2sts (10)
rnd 14 sc in all 10 sts
rnd 15 [dec] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2sts, [inc] repeat 2 times, sc in the next 2sts (10)
rnd 16-21 sc in all 10 sts (6 rounds)
rnd 22 [dec] repeat 5 times (5)
trunk / ear / arms sewing stage
crown (use yellow color yarn)
rnd 1 start 10dc in a magic ring
rnd 2 inc in all dc (20dc)
rnd 3 [dc in the next sts, inc] repeat 6 times (30dc)
rnd 4 ch 1, sc in the next 15 sts, ch 8, sc in the next 15 sts (30)
rnd 5 [ch 2, sc in the next sts] repeat 15 times, [into ch8; sc in the next 2sts, dc in the next 3 sts, tr in the next 2 sts, ch1, tr in the next 2 sts, dc in the next 3 sts, sc in the next 2 sts], [ch 2, sc in the next sts] repeat 15 times
crown hill (use yellow color yarn)
rnd 1 start 6sc in a magic ring
rnd 2 inc in all sc (12)
rnd 3 [sc in the next sts, inc] repeat 6 times (18)
rnd 4 [sc in the next 2 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (24)
rnd 5 [sc in the next 3 sts, inc] repeat 6 times (30)
rnd 6-8 sc in all 30 sts (3 rounds)
crown accessory (use purple color yarn)
rnd 1 ch31 starting from the second stitch from the hook; [hdc3inc] repeats 30 times (90)
pants (use white color)
rnd 1 ch49
rnd 2 starting from the second stitch from the hook; sc in the next 48 sts, slst
rnd 3-7 ch 2, dc in next 48st (5 rounds)
split the pants from legs. after finish the first leg fasten off yarn and join the yarn with sl-st at other side for
second leg. do the same steps to create pants leg separations.
rnd 8 ch 2, dc in next 24st, slst
**the waist part of the pants turn 48sc with purple rope.
pants accessory (use white color)
rnd 1 ch10
rnd 2 starting from the third stitch from the hook; dc in the next 6 sts, dcinc, (for corner part; dc in the next 3 sts, ch1, dc in the next 3 sts), dcinc, dc in the next 6 sts
change to purple colour
rnd 3 sc in the next 8 sts, [inc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next sts, [inc] repeat 3 times, , sc in the next 7sts, [inc] repeat 3 times, sc in the next 3sts, inc (36)
waistcoat (use green color)
rnd 1 ch39
rnd 2 starting from the fourth stitch from the hook; tr in the next 36 sts
rnd 3-7 ch3, tr in the next 36 sts (5 rounds)
we separate the arms (8-9th rows to the right and left)
rnd 8 ch3, tr in the next 3 sts
rnd 9 ch3, tr in the next 3 sts. cut the rope
we make the back of the vest
after 3 rows on the right front, we leave 9 stitches and start the 10th stitch by making ch3.
rnd 1 ch3, tr in the next 12 sts
rnd 2 ch3, turn
to combine the arm and back
starting right at the front; sc in the next 3 sts, ch3, sc in the next 12 sts, sc in the next 3 sts
legs accessory (use yellow color and make 2)
rnd 1 ch21
rnd 2 starting from the second stitch from the hook; sc in the next 2 sts, [1boble stitch, sc in the next 2sts] repeat 6 times
rnd 3 ch1, turn, sc in the next 20 sts
arms accessory (use yellow color and make 4)
rnd 1 ch16
rnd 2 [sc in the next sts, ch3] repeat 15 times
