we start with the arms and legs to make the anne shirley amigurumi doll pattern. then, let's crochet the body part. we will make the head part of the amigurumi pattern after the body part is completed. the light brown hat and orange hair of the amigurumi pattern are very cute. green yarn is preferred for the dress of the toy. white yarn is used for the apron of the amigurumi pattern. brown yarn was used for the shoes of anne shirley amigurumi pattern. light color yarn is used for shoelace. you can make the eyebrows of the amigurumi pattern using orange yarn after the toy is completed. you can change the hair color of the amigurumi pattern. don't forget to add the anne shirley amigurumi pattern to your favorite list.

abbreviations
mr magic ring
sc single crochet
inc increase
dec decrease
blo back loop
flo front loop
sl slip stitch
hdc half double crochet
dc double crochet
tc treble crochet
rnd round
materials
cotton or acrylic yarn in the following colors: light peach (for the skin), olive green, brown, light brown, rust or orange (for the hair) and white
3.5 mm crochet hook
stuffing
wooden chopstick to support the head
10 mm security eyes
fill the doll as you go, the arms don't need to be filled
let's start crocheting our amigurumi pattern
let's crochet the arms and legs of our amigurumi doll.
arms
light peach yarn
rnd 1. mr, 6 sc
rnd 2-3. 6 sc
change to olive green yarn
rnd 4-10. 6 sc
make 1 sl and close the arm with 2 sc. make the other arm and save them for later to be joint on body's round 31
legs
brown yarn
rnd 1. mr, 8 sc
rnd 2-4. 8 sc
change to white yarn
rnd 5. 8 sc
rnd 6-17. 8 sc
change to olive green
rnd 18. 8 sc
finish off the first leg. make another leg and do not cut the yarn as we will join the legs to start the body
the arms and legs of our toy are complete. now let's crochet the body part.
body
olive green yarn
continue from the second leg
rnd 19. 2 sc, chain 4, join the legs, 8 sc, 4 sc (on the chain 4 we just made), 6 sc (24)
rnd 20. 24 sc (this includes the remaining loops of the chain 4)
rnd 21-22. 24 sc
rnd 23. dec, 9 sc, dec, 11 sc (22)
rnd 24. blo 22 sc
rnd 25. dec, 9 sc, dec, 9 sc (20)
rnd 26. dec, 8 sc, dec, 8 sc (18)
rnd 27. 18 sc
rnd 28. (dec, 4 sc)*3 (15)
rnd 29. 15 sc
rnd 30. (dec, 3 sc)*3 (12)
rnd 31. 12 sc (12) join the arms with 2 sc, make sure they are even on the sides with 4 stitches between them.
rnd 32. blo 12 sc
rnd 33. 6 dec (6)
rnd 34. 6 sc
go back to round 32 where we left the front loops free and make 12 sc with olive green yarn to make the dress collar.
after completing the body part of the amigurumi pattern, let's crochet the head part
head
light peach yarn
continue from the neck (rnd34)
rnd 1. 6 sc (12)
rnd 2. 12 inc (24)
rnd 3. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 4. 2 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*5, 2 sc (36)
rnd 5. (5 sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 6. 3 sc, inc (6 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (48)
rnd 7-14. 48 sc
place the eyes between rounds 8-9, 8 stitches apart. embroider the nose in round 7 and the eyebrows between rounds 10-11
rnd 15. 3 sc, dec, (6 sc, dec)*5, 3 sc, (42)
rnd 16. (5 sc, dec)*6 (36)
rnd 17. 2 sc, dec, (4 sc, dec)*5, 2 sc (30)
rnd 18. (3 sc, dec)*6 (24)
rnd 19. 1 sc, dec (2 sc, dec)*5, 1 sc (18)
rnd 20. (1 sc, dec)*6 (12)
rnd 21. 6 dec (6)
close by sewing the front loops of the last round and pull to tighten the yarn.
let's crochet the free amigurumi doll's dress.
dress
olive green yarn
go back to round 24 of the body where we left the front loops free. insert the yarn on any stitch in the back, chain 1 and on that same stitch go on.
rnd 1. 5 sc, inc, 10 sc, inc, 5 sc (24)
rnd 2. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 3. 30 sc
rnd 4. (4 sc, inc)*6 (36)
rnd 5-9. 36 sc
rnd 10. flo (1 sc, chain 1)*36
finish off and weave.
if you want the dress to be longer just make a few more rounds of 36 sc. you can make a round of sl to from a belt. white yarn go to the last round of the dress, insert the hook on the back loops left and make (chain 4, 1 sc)*36 all the round long.
let's crochet the hair of the cute amigurumi toy
hair
rust /orange yarn
rnd 1. mr 6 sc
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. 1 sc, inc, (2 sc, inc)*5, 1 sc, inc (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. 2 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*5, 2 sc (36)
rnd 7. (3 sc, inc)*9 (45)
rnd 8. 22 sc, inc, 21 sc, inc (47)
rnd 9-12. 47 sc
rnd 13. 23 sc, inc, 22 sc, inc (49)
rnd 14-17. 49 sc
rnd 18. 12 sc, 1 hdc, 5 dc, 1 tc, 4 dc, 1 sc, 1 sl, 1 sc, 4 dc, 1 tc, 5 dc, 1 hdc, 12 sc, 1 sl
leave a long tail to sew the wig to the head or you can glue it with a glue gun. to do the braids cur 12 pieces of rust yarn about 30 cm (11 inches). grab two strands, fold them in half and insert them on round 18 following the next.
directions: count 7 stitches from the last hdc (make on round 18) and place 2 should have 12 strands (4 on each stitch) to make a braid. braid the doll's hair and secure them with a knot.
we have come to the part where we will do the most enjoyable crochet. let's crochet the white apron of the amigurumi pattern.
apron
white yarn
work in rows, before starting a new row make a turning chan for single crochet and 2 turning chains for double crochet. chain 19, skip the last chain made and start.
row 1. 2 sc, chain 4, skip 4 stitches in the main chain, 6 sc, chain 4, skip 4 stithes in the main chain, 2 sc (18)
row 2-7. 18 sc
row 8. inc in dc (36)
row 9-11. 36 dc
sew a button on the left edge in row 6 and chain 14 on the right edge, join with a sl in the same stitch.
we have completed the apron of the amigurumi pattern. now let's crochet the hat she will wear while walking in the flower gardens.
hat
light brown yarn
rnd 1. mr 6 sc
rnd 2. 6 inc (12)
rnd 3. (1 sc, inc)*6 (18)
rnd 4. 1 sc, inc, (2 sc, inc)*5 (24)
rnd 5. (3 sc, inc)*6 (30)
rnd 6. 2 sc, inc, (4 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (48)
rnd 7. 5 (sc, inc)*6 (42)
rnd 8. 3 sc, inc (6 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc (48)
rnd 9. blo 48 sc
rnd 10. 48 sc
rnd 11. flo (7 sc, inc)*6 (54)
rnd 12. 4 sc, inc, (8 sc, inc)*5, 3 sc, sl (60)
finis off and weave.
you can embroider or crochet a row with darker brown to male the hat look prettier. to finish with some last few details, you can embroider or draw freckles with a marker.
congratulations, you have completed the amigurumi doll pattern.
